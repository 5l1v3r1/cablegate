SUBJECT: GOK CONGRATULATES IRAQI PEOPLE FOR APPROVING 
CONSTITUTION 
 
REF: STATE 199224 
 
(U) On October 30, the Council of Ministers (Cabinet), during 
one of its bi-weekly meetings, congratulated the Iraqi people 
for approving the constitution and expressed hope that this 
"achievement" would put Iraq on the road to gaining stability 
and security.  The Cabinet also hoped the constitution would 
bolster Iraqi reconstruction and development efforts.
