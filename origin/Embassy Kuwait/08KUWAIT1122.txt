SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: LEVERAGING FATF STATEMENT ON 
IRAN INTO NATIONAL ACTIONS 
 
REF: STATE 115523 
 
 ¶1.  (SBU)  During a meeting on October 30 with the Prime 
Minister and Foreign Minister, Deputy Secretary of Treasury 
Robert Kimmitt delivered reftel message.  The Kuwaitis 
offered no substantive response. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  In a meeting with Undersecretary Ministry of 
Finance Khalifa M. Hamada, on November 6, Econcouns delivered 
reftel points, reiterating the  Financial Action Task Force's 
(FATF) concern of the terrorism finance threat emanating from 
Iran and of the U.S. plan to impose preventive measures in 
the coming weeks to further protect itself from Iran's 
illicit finance threat.  U/S Hamada said he would review the 
information presented to him and contact Econcouns in two 
weeks to arrange a follow-up meeting.  Post will report any 
substantive response septel.
