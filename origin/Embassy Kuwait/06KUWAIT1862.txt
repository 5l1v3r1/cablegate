SUBJECT: KUWAITI FM CONGRATULATES NEW IRAQI GOVERNMENT;

Subsequent to the May 20 congratulatory messages from Kuwaiti 
Amir Shaykh Sabah Al-Ahmed Al-Jaber Al-Sabah to Iraqi 
President Jalal Talabani and Prime Minister Nuri al-Maliki 
(ref A), Deputy Prime Minister and Foreign Minister Shaykh 
Dr. Mohammed Al-Sabah Al-Salem Al-Sabah sent a letter to 
Iraqi FM Hoshyar Zebari praising the formation of the new 
government.  According to press reports, Shaykh Dr. Mohammed 
wrote that government formation "clearly shows Iraq's 
ambitions for a strong united nation which treats its 
citizens with equality and dignity."  He also invited Zebari 
to visit Kuwait for bilateral talks on how to increase 
cooperation and strengthen ties between the two countries.
