SUBJECT: DIPLOMATIC NOTE REGARDING USE OF KASOTC DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 33103 
 
(SBU) On March 1, Ambassador delivered to FM Abdulilah 
Al-Khatib the diplomatic note (reftel) regarding the King 
Abdullah Special Operations Training Center (KASOTC).  Khatib 
undertook to provide a diplomatic note in response. Post also 
provided copies of reftel note to the Chairman of the 
Jordanian Armed Forces, General Khalid Sarairah, and to Major 
General Mohammed Tayseer and Brigadier General Mohammed 
Farghal, who are the main Jordanian military contacts for 
this project.
