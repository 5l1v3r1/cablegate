SUBJECT: FRIENDS OF IRAQ CONFERENCE: INVITATION DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 50592 
 
Political Counselor delivered reftel invitation May 13 to 
Jamal Shamayleh, Director of the Foreign Minister's private 
office, and flagged the imminent registration deadline. 
Shamayleh did not offer an immediate response, but said the 
MFA would review the letter and make a decision regarding 
attendance.  On May 15, Defense Attache Office delivered the 
invitation and accompanying details to Jordan Armed Forces 
(JAF) General Headquarters (GHQ) for the attention of the 
Chief of Defense General Sarayreh.  Post anticipates that the 
JAF will also receive the invitation via the Jordanian Senior 
National Representative at CENTCOM.  Post will follow up to 
encourage high-level attendance.
