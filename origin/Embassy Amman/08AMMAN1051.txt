SUBJECT: Jordan's Nomination for the International Transit and 
Transshipment Conference in Tangier 
 
REF: STATE 27128 
 
¶1.  The Government of Jordan has nominated Colonel Hassan Mahmoud Al 
Otoum, Director of Petra Police, to participate in the International 
Transit and Transshipment Conference to be held in Tangier, Morocco, 
May 20-22, 2008.
