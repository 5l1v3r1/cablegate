SUBJECT: INVITATIONS TO MEFTA TRADE AND INVESTMENT 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: A. STATE 149519 B. MONROE-BEHRENS 9/12 EMAILS 
 
¶1. Ambassador conveyed reftel talking points on the November 
3 MEFTA Trade and Investment Conference to Minister of 
Commerce and Industry Hassan Fakhro during a September 12 
meeting for visiting Commerce DAS Holly Vineyard.  Fakhro 
welcomed the conference, asked about U.S. company 
participation, and looked forward to more details about 
participants as they became available.  In a meeting on 
September 13 with senior officials at the Economic 
Development Board, EDB Deputy Director Zakaria Hijris 
confirmed that Bahrain's U.S.-Bahrain FTA Implementation 
Committee would contribute $15,000 towards the cost of the 
conference. 
 
¶2. Per ref B, the Ambassador has sent a letter to King Hamad 
formally inviting him to be a keynote speaker at the event.
