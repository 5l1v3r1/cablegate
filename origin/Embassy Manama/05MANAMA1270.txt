SUBJECT: CONDOLENCE MESSAGE FROM BAHRAIN'S CROWN PRINCE TO 
THE VICE PRESIDENT 
 
 
 ¶1.  Embassy received a condolence cable from His Highness 
Shaikh Salman Bin Hamad Al-Khalifa, Crown Prince and 
Commander-in-Chief of the Bahrain Defence Force addressed to 
Vice President Dick Cheney.  Subject of the cable is the 
Hurricane Katrina disaster.  No original will follow. 
 
¶2.  Begin Text: 
 
3rd September 2005 
 
The Hon. Dick Cheney 
Vice President 
Washington, D.C. 
 
Dear Mr. Vice President: 
 
Please accept my sincere condolences for the terrible loss of 
lives and destruction caused by Hurricane Katrina in the 
southern Gulf Coast of the United States. You and the people 
of the United States of America have our deepest sympathies 
as you overcome this terrible tragedy, and our thoughts are 
with the victims and their families at this sad time. 
 
SALMAN BIN HAMAD AL-KHALIFA 
Crown Prince and Commander-in-Chief 
of the Bahrain Defence Force 
 
End text. 
MONROE
