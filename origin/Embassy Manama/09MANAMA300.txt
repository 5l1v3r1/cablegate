SUBJECT: BAHRAIN RECOGNIZES KOSOVO INDEPENDENCE 
 
(SBU) MFA Bilateral Relations Director Dhafer Al Umran 
informed poloff on May 20 that the Bahraini government had 
officially recognized Kosovo's independence.  According to 
MFA, Bahrain becomes the sixtieth government to recognize 
Kosovo as an independent state.
