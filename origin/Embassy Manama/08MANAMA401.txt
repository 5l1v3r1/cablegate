Classified By: CDA Christopher Henzel for reasons 1.4 (b) and (d). (SBU) MFA Bilateral Relations Director Dr. Dhafer Al-Umran confirmed to A/DCM on June 11 that Ambassador Abdullah Abdulatif Al-Khalifa (Bahrain's ambassador in Geneva) will represent the GOB at the June 23 reconstruction conference in Vienna. He added that Al-Khalifa has instructions to coordinate with the U.S. delegation to ensure that the conference is a success and sends a strong message of support for PM Siniora's government. ********************************************* ******** Visit Embassy Manama's Classified Website: XXXXXXXXXXXX********************************************* ******** HENZEL
