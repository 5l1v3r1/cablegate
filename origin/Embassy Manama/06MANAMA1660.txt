DCM delivered reftel demarche September 14 to MFA Assistant 
Undersecretary for Coordination and Follow Up Shaikh Abdul 
Aziz Bin Mubarak Al Khalifa regarding the Community of 
Democracies Ministerial at the UNGA September 20.  Shaikh 
Abdul Aziz said he would pass the information along to the 
Bahraini delegation attending the General Assembly.  He 
remarked that Foreign Minister Shaikh Khalid Bin Ahmed Al 
Khalifa will be addressing the UNGA on that day.
