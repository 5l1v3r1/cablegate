SUBJECT: FOREIGN MINISTER SAUD AL FAISAL TO REPRESENT 
SAUDIS AT ICI MEETING 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 50497 
     ¶B. SECSTATE 47346 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  Per ref a, in a 17 April meeting, Foreign Minister 
Prince Saud al Faisal reported to Iraq Special Coordinator 
Satterfield, Treasury Deputy Director Nugent, Charge 
D'Affaires Gfoeller, and ADCM Rundell that he would represent 
the Saudi government at the May 3 International Compact for 
Iraq (ICI) meeting.  He did not provide specific information 
on debt relief or foreign assistance announcements Saudi 
Arabia planned to make at the meeting. 
 
¶2.  (U)  Per ref b, on 16 April, CDA Gfoeller delivered a 
demarche and non-paper on the May 3 ICI meeting to the MFA's 
Director of the Western Department, Ambassador Khalid 
al-Jindan.  Ambassador al-Jindan promised to pass the 
information to the appropriate Saudi authorities, but did not 
have any immediate comment.  Post will work to provide 
further  technical information regarding possible Saudi 
announcements on debt relief or development assistance to 
Iraq after April 21 when Ministry of Finance interlocutors 
return from the Spring IMF/World Bank meetings in Washington. 
 
 
¶3.  (U)  Also on 16 April, Congen Jeddah Pol/Econ Chief 
Houssami delivered the ref B demarche and talking points to 
Islamic Development Bank Technical Advisor Aljomaih, in the 
office of IDB President Dr. Ahmed Mohammed Ali.  Advisor 
Aljomaih had no immediate comment, but promised to report 
President Ali's comments shortly.  ConJen Jeddah will report 
septel.
