SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON U.S. OPPOSITION TO IRANIAN 
CANDIDIATE FOR UNDP BOARD 
 
REF: SECSTATE 01500 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche on January 8, to Vesna 
Vukovic, head of the MFA's UN office regarding the Iranian 
candidate for the President of the UNDP Executive Board.  She 
indicated that the Croatian Foreign Ministry would review our 
position and Post will report any substantive response in a 
septel.  The Acting POL Chief also spoke with Ambassador 
Mario Nobilo, Director for Multilateral Affairs, the same day 
regarding this issue, and he said he understood the U.S. 
concerns about Iran becoming President of the UNDP Executive 
Board.
