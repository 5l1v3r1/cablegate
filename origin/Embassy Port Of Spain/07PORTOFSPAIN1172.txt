¶1.  (SBU) PolChief met December 3 with MFA Multilateral 
Affairs official Kathleen Seenarine presented ref (a) points, 
requesting the GOTT vote against, or at least abstain, on 
Resolutions 61/22, 61/23, and 61/116.  PolChief especially 
emphasized the resolution mentioned in ref (b), urging, at a 
minimum, an abstention. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Ms. Seenarine listened attentively to our points, 
remarking she was familiar with all the resolutions.  She 
promised that our concerns would be given close attention. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Comment:  We will remain in close touch with MFA 
officials on these resolutions pushing for a definitive 
response.  Any substantive information will be reported.
