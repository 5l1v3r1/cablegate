In response to reftel, the Dutch MFA reported GoNL military 
contributions for the period encompassing July 15, 2003 to 
July 15, 2004 at approximately 90 million euros.  This does 
not/not include costs incurred for an additional 101 troops 
deployed in January 2004 to aid in security sector reform 
(SSR).  That figure was unavailable in time for the May 4 
deadline.
