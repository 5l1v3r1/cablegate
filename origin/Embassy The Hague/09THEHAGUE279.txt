(SBU) The Dutch want more details before giving funding for 
the Ethiopian logistics unit deploying to Darfur. Post 
delivered reftel A points to key Dutch MFA contacts. 
Bastienne Karel, a policy officer for Sudan in the 
Peacebuilding and Stabilization Unit, responded that the 
logistics proposal was "too weak" to fund given "shortcomings 
in our stability fund."  With a project proposal and more 
details, however, the Dutch are willing to "see if there are 
possibilities."
