Post delivered ref B talking points talking points and non- 
paper 24 February to the UAE Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA).  Consular Chief raised the issue with UAE MFA Office 
Director for Consular Affairs, Ambassador Abdul Jalil, on 
February 28.  Ambassador Abdul Jalil provided Consular Chief 
with a copy in Arabic of a UAE Diplomatic Circular stating 
the UAE recognizes the Interim Travel Document (ITD). 
Operative text follows: 
 
Begin text 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the United Arab Emirates 
presents its best compliments to all diplomatic missions 
accredited to the UAE. 
 
The Ministry is honored to advise all foreign missions in 
the UAE that the relevant authorities of the UAE have agreed 
to recognize the Travel Document issued by the Coalition 
Provisional Authority in Iraq and have also agreed to the 
probability of granting entry visas to holders of such 
travel documents who are traveling to the UAE for visiting 
purposes only. 
 
 
End Text
