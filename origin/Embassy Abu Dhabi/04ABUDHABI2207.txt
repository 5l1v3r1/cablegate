SUBJECT: UAE - Iraq Embassy implements visa regime 
 
Ref: Baghdad 14 
 
Iraq's Embassy in Abu Dhabi and Consulate in Dubai 
received instructions from the Iraqi Foreign Ministry 
on July 2 detailing visa issuance procedures (per 
reftel).  The first visa applications are being 
processed under the new visa regime, Iraqi Embassy 
Counselor Abdul Razzaq Ahmed told Polchief July 4.
