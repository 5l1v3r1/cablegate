SUBJECT: BELGIUM: DONOR SUPPORT FOR THE SOMALIA TFG 
FOLLOWING AGREEMENT WITH PRICEWATERHOUSECOOPERS (PWC) 
 
REF: STATE 79853 
 
 (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel points to Isabelle 
Maduda-Lukebamoko, Attach for Sub-Saharan Africa at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on August 5.  Maduda-Lukebamoko 
was not aware of the agreement reached between Somalia's 
Transitional Federal Government and PWC.  She said that the 
GOB will follow through on the USD 646,831 pledged by Belgium 
to the African Union Mission in Somalia (AMISOM).  She said 
she has no control over the timing of delivery of the 
assistance, but she estimates the GOB will disburse it 
sometime during the third quarter of 2009.
