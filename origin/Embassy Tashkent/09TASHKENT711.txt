SUBJECT: Uzbekistan Confirms Participation in Economic Cooperation 
Conference 
 
REF: STATE 39779; TASHKENT 617 
 
(SBU) On May 8, Uzbekistan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs informed 
Post via a diplomatic note that it will participate in the May 
13-14 economic cooperation conference on Afghanistan in Pakistan. 
The head of Uzbekistan's diplomatic mission in Pakistan will 
attend.
