SUBJECT: CONGEN CURACAO PLANS FOR DAY OF SOLIDARITY WITH CUBAN 
PEOPLE 
 
REF: A. STATE 45792, B. STATE 46997 
 
To commemorate the Day of Solidarity with Cuban People, Post 
plans to use the materials provided by the Department as 
follows: (1) to provide links to the solidarity day website on 
the Post website; (2) to circulate and have available copies of 
the Petition for Political Prisoners in Cuba; (3) to provide 
information on the Damas de Blanco in the consular waiting room 
and the Post webpage; (4) to place the Op-Ed in local newspapers 
and on the Post webpage; (5) to provide the relevant information 
to Foreign Affairs Offices for Aruba and the Netherlands 
Antilles and other key contacts; and (6) to place a static 
display of Department-provided materials on the Day of 
Solidarity in the CAC and consular waiting rooms.
