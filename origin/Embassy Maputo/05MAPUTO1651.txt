SUBJECT: MOZAMBIQUE: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR DECEMBER 15 
IRAQ ELECTION 
 
REF: STATE 225860 
 
¶1. On December 17, Deputy Econ/Pol Chief delivered reftel 
talking points to Faruque Faquira, Head of Department, 
Directorate for International Organizations and Conferences 
(DOIC), Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Faquira gave no 
substantive response. 
 
¶2. The Mozambican media ran a few short "general interest" 
pieces on the election in Iraq, but has not reported any 
official GRM reaction, and neither the president nor his 
government have made any public statements of support for the 
December 15 Iraq elections.
