SUBJECT: POLISH CANDIDATE UP FOR RE-ELECTION TO UNHRC WITH 
WEDGWOOD 
 
REF: STATE 085722 
 
PolOff delivered reftel demarche and diplomatic note seeking 
support for Ruth Wedgwood's re-election to the U.N. Human 
Rights Committee on June 12 to Iwona Marczyk of the MFA's 
U.N. Department. Marczyk indicated we could expect a response 
to our dipnote in the next one to two months. Marczyk also 
informed us that we will also receive a request from the 
Polish government for support of their candidate, Roman 
Wieruszewski, who is also up for re-election this year.
