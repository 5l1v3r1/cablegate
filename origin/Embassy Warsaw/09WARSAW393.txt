SUBJECT: POLISH BUDGET MEETS TRANSPARENCY REQUIREMENTS FOR 
CONTINUED FMF/IMET ASSISTANCE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 28885 
 
The Polish government's budget process meets the transparency 
requirements of the Foreign Operations and Related Programs 
Appropriations Act (SFOAA), 2009.  The government is expected 
to continue to receive U.S. foreign assistance using FY09 
funds (both FMF and IMET).  The government of Poland's budget 
is publicly available, including breakdowns of monthly 
revenue and expenditures.  These breakdowns, along with 
budget projections, related statistics, state agency plans, 
and draft budget submissions are readily available on-line.
