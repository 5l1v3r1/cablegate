SUBJECT: REQUEST HUMAN RIGHTS VETTING FOR ISRAEL DEFENSE 
FORCES REPRESENTATIVE ATTENDING JUNE 4-26 2008 SEMINAR IN 
U.S. 
 
¶1. (SBU) The Israeli Defense Forces (IDF) have nominated the 
following individual for participation in a National Defense 
University Near East/South Asia (NESA) Center for Strategic 
Studies' Executive Seminar, scheduled to run in the U.S. June 
4-26, 2008.  Embassy Tel Aviv has no derogatory information 
in its records on the individual, or any other indication 
that he has been involved in gross violations of human rights. 
 
COL Dan Efrony 
Deputy Military Advocate General 
Israel Defense Forces 
Date of Birth:  17 November 1958 
Israeli Passport Number:  10512121 
 
¶2. (SBU) ACTION REQUEST:  Embassy Tel Aviv requests the 
Department vet the individual for human rights, utilizing 
appropriate USG databases, and confirm that we may proceed 
with processing him for participation in the NESA Seminar. 
(NOTE:  Please be advised of the short timeline for 
completing this vetting. END NOTE.)
