SUBJECT: NON-PAPER ON CHINA TRANSMITTED TO GOI 
 
REF: STATE 99000 
 
¶1. On September 22, Econoff passed non-paper transmitted 
reftel to a representative of Udi Levi, Director of the 
National Security Council's (NSC) Bureau of Counterterrorism 
Finance.  On the same day, Poloff transmitted the non-paper 
to Haim Waxman, Director of the MFA's Department of 
Non-Proliferation, and Roey Gilad, Director for Export 
Controls, also at the MFA. 
 
¶2. Econoff informed GOI that the USG was in the process of 
passing the information to other friendly countries that 
might have the ability to act upon it.  Udi Levi's 
representative expressed great appreciation for the 
information itself and especially for the USG's initiative to 
encourage its partners to act.  He told econoff that the GOI 
would continue to share as much information as possible about 
Sino-Iranian relations, whether open source or classified. 
He was supportive of taking strong measures to disrupt any 
attempts by the parties to improve relations.
