GOLDSTONE REPORT FINDINGS 
 
REF: STATE 112828 -- DEMARCHE REQUEST ON GOLDSTONE 
     REPORT 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloff conveyed reftel demarche points to Mr. Jamal 
Sharifuddin Johan, Principal Assistant Secretary in the MFA's 
Political, Security, and Disarmament Unit of the Multilateral 
Political Division, on November 3.  Jamal thanked Poloff for 
providing the U.S. perspective on the Goldstone Report, but 
said that the GOM will "definitely" support its findings when 
raised in the U.N. General Assembly. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Comment: The GOM, as a stalwart in the OIC and NAM, 
has a long and consistent history of supporting UN measures 
critical of Israel.  We do not foresee the GOM changing its 
position on this issue.  End Comment.
