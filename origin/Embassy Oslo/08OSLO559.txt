SUBJECT: NORWEGIANS WILL DONATE: GEORGIA DONORS CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 109212 
 
On October 14 we delivered reftel points to MFA Global 
Affairs Senior Advisor Henrik Foyn Skjerve.  Deputy Foreign 
Minister Raymond Johansen will lead the GON's Donors 
Conference delegation. Although the GON will donate, Skjerve 
stressed that no final sum has been decided upon.  He noted 
that the GON is currently considering a 10 million euro 
donation.
