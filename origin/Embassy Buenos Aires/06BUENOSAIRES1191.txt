SUBJECT: ARGENTINA: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR IRAQI 
GOVERNMENT FORMATION COMPLETION 
 
REF: STATE 81632 
 
¶1.  (SBU) DCM, accompanied by PolCouns, delivered reftel 
points seeking public GoA support of the Iraqi political 
process to Argentina MFA acting Undersecretary for Foreign 
Policy Luis Cappagli on May 23. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Cappagli concurred that the government formation 
process represented an "important step" in achieving security 
and stability in Iraq.  He added it was important that the 
territorial integrity of Iraq be preserved and that the 
formation of the new government should contribute to that 
objective.  Cappagli gave no indication that any GoA public 
statement on the new Iraqi government would be forthcoming.
