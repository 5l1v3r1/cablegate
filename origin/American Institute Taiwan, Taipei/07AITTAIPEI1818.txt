¶1. (SBU) Per reftel request, AIT/T contacted the ultimate consignee, 
Professor Huei Wang, the foreign intermediate consignee, Li 
Chao-chieh, and the National Taiwan University (NTU).  The NTU 
Personnel Office stated that Prof. Wang Huei is on its faculty but 
not Li Chao-chieh. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Prof. Wang confirmed that he ordered the subject 
amplifiers.  He stated that Li Chao-chieh is his student who was 
assisting him in locating suppliers for the amplifiers.  Prof. Wang 
further stated that the amplifiers would be used for various 
millimeter-wave testing and research.
