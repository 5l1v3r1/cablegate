SUBJECT:  Taiwan to Set up Trade Representative Office 
 
 
¶1.  Taiwan's Executive Yuan (EY) will set up a Taiwan Trade 
Representative (TTR) Office, likely in December 2006.  The TTR will 
be responsible for all trade negotiations and is modeled on USTR, 
according to Connie Chang, a special aide to former Economic 
Minister Morgan Huang. 
 
¶2.  The TTR will be headed by Vice Premier Tsai Ing-wen, with 
Economic Minister Steve Chen serving as Deputy.  Initially, the TTR 
will have a staff of seven senior negotiators, including four 
negotiators transferred from the Bureau of Foreign Trade and one 
each from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Council of Agriculture,
