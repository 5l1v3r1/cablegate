SUBJECT: PERMISSION TO BE ABSENT FROM AIT (PERSONAL AND 
OFFICIAL TRAVEL) 
 
 
¶1.  Director Stephen M. Young requests approval to be absent 
from AIT on annual leave Thursday, May 22 through Monday, 
June 2, and on consultations in Washington, D.C. June 3-9. 
He will depart Washington on June 10 and return to Taipei on 
Wednesday, June 11 at 1730. 
 
¶2.  Deputy Director Robert S. Wang will be Acting Director 
during this period.
