SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON NATANZ FEP CEREMONY DELIVERED 
 
REF: State 45880 
 
(SBU) On April 9, Poloff delivered reftel points on Iran's Natanz 
Fuel Enrichment Plant ribbon-cutting ceremony to MFA West Asia and 
Africa Department Iran Desk Officer Nguyen Quynh Phuong.  According 
to Phuong, her Department was not consulted about the issue.  She 
had no further information about whether or not a GVN representative 
would attend the ribbon-cutting ceremony, but pledged to pass the 
points to her leadership.
