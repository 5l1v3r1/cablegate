Poloff met with Ministry of Foreign Affairs International 
Organizations First Secretary Ngo Thi Lien March 10 to 
deliver reftel demarche.  Lien said the GVN is considering 
signing the Third Additional Protocol.  She said she is 
unaware of any changes in GVN policy that would affect the 
GVN's current support for the Protocol, but noted that the 
GVN does not yet have a final official position on signing 
it.  Lien promised to advise the Embassy when the GVN has 
made its decision.
