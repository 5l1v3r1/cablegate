SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON WESTERN SAHARA TALKS 
 
REF: STATE 88765 
 
(SBU) On August 20, Poloff met with MFA IO Section Chief Do Hung 
Viet to stress, per reftel, the importance of an early resumption of 
talks on Western Sahara and to urge Vietnam to support the continued 
tenure of SYG Special Envoy Peter Van Walsum.  Viet said that 
Vietnam understands the responsibility of the international 
community to support negotiations and shares an interest in 
facilitating a political solution acceptable to both sides of the 
conflict.  He took note of the U.S. position on Van Walsum and 
promised to report the substance of the demarche to his superiors in 
the MFA and to Vietnam's mission at the United Nations.
