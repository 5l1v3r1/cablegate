(SBU) Poloff delivered ref B demarche to Leila Villatoro, 
Deputy Director for UN Affairs at the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs (MFA), and Elisa Oliva, Second Secretary at the MFA, 
in advance of the International Organization for Migration's 
(IOM) upcoming elections in Geneva.  Oliva stated that the 
GOG had not decided which candidate it would support as the 
IOM's new Director General.  Pol/Econ Counselor also 
discussed Ambassador Swing's candidacy with Vice Foreign 
Minister Lars Pira.  Pira commented that while he was aware 
the elections would be held on June 18, the GOG had not yet 
made a decision regarding the IOM DG candidacy.
