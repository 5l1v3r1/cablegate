SUBJECT: KAZAKHSTAN: ENCOURAGING SUPPORT FOR THE IRAQ 
TRANSITIONAL GOVERNMENT 
 
Ref:  State 79107 
 
¶1. (SBU) Drawing on reftel points, EBO Astana principal 
officer on May 11 conveyed to Zhandos Asanov, director of 
the MFA's Department of Europe and Americas, the request for 
Kazakhstan to support the Iraqi Transitional Government. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Asanov responded positively and said that he would 
convey the request to the Department of Middle East Affairs.
