SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON IMPLEMENTING THE DUAL USE 
ITEM PROVISION OF UN SECURITY COUNCIL (UNSC) RESOLUTION 1803 
 
REF: STATE 29464 
 
¶1. ESToff delivered the points in ref, regarding the dual use 
item provision contained in UNSCR 1803, to Japanese Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs Non-Proliferation, Science, and Nuclear 
Energy Division officer Isami Takada on March 25.  The MOFA 
official appreciated receiving the non-paper and agreed to 
share it with colleagues in MOFA's Second Middle East 
Division, responsible for this issue. 
 
¶2.  On March 28, Poloff met with MOFA's Second Middle East 
Division Principal Deputy Director Motosada Matano and Iran 
Unit Chief Takashi Kamada.  Matano confirmed the Second 
Middle East Division has the lead on UNSCR 1803.  He noted 
Japan is fully aware of the provisions of UNSCR 1803, and 
like other Iran-related resolutions, intends to comply fully. 
 Kamada said the GOJ plans to issue a formal statement by May 
¶2.
