SUBJECT: ENCOURAGING SENIOR-LEVEL PARTICIPATION IN THE PRT 
CONFERENCE IN PRAGUE 
 
REF: STATE 3595 
 
Embassy Poloff delivered reftel points encouraging GOJ 
participation in the January 25-27 Provincial Reconstruction 
Team (PRT) Conference in Prague to MOFA Second Middle East 
Division Deputy Director Kunihiko Higashi on January 14. 
Higashi expressed Japan's interest in the conference but 
noted that the GOJ had not made a decision on its attendance 
or level of participation.  Higashi promised to keep post 
informed about Japan's participation in the conference.
