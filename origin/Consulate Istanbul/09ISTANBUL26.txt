SUBJECT: SHORTAGE OF HOTEL ROOMS IN ISTANBUL MARCH 16-22. 
 
Consulate General Istanbul requests that Washington transmit 
the following cable as an ALDAC. 
 
Consulate General Istanbul advises travelers that due to the 
Fifth World Water Forum hotel rooms in Istanbul will be 
extremely limited from March 16 ) 22.  Travelers who plan to 
attend the Fifth World Water Forum should state this clearly 
in the country clearance request. Country clearance for 
travel un-related to the World Water Forum will only be 
granted for Mission essential travel. 
Wiener
