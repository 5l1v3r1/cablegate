SUBJECT: MEXICO ON POSSIBLE IRAN PRESIDENCY OF UNDP 
 
REF: STATE 1500 
 
(SBU) Poloff conveyed points January 8 on USG concerns over a 
possible Iranian presidency of the Executive Board of the 
United Nations Development Program (UNDP) to Julian Juarez 
Cadenas, the Deputy Director General for UN-Related Security 
and Legal Issues at the Foreign Secretariat (SRE).  Juarez 
said that he was unaware of Iran's candidacy or a possible 
decision as early as January 9 and would confer with his 
colleagues immediately about the matter.
