SUBJECT: IMI: INVITATION FOR BID - URBAN WATER SUPPLY AND 
SANITATION PROJECT IN YEMEN 
 
¶1. The Urban Water Supply and Sanitation Project of the 
Ministry of Water and Environment is inviting international 
companies for the drilling and construction of ten boreholes 
in Al-Sabeen and the surrounding area.  The works consists 
of drilling ten boreholes at a depth of between 600-900m, 
supplying and installing casings and screens, and pumping 
tests and logging. 
 
¶2. All submitted bidders should have the following: 
 
  a.   The maximum allowable number of joint venture partners 
     is 2 
  b.   Experience in drilling water boreholes depth more than 
     900m 
c.   The minimum required annual volume of construction 
works for the    successful bidder in any of the last five 
years shall be US$ 2 million 
  d.   The minimum of liquid assets and/or credit facilities 
     net of other contractual commitments of successful bidder 
     shall be US$ 1.5 million 
 
¶3. A complete set of bidding documents may be purchased by 
interested bidders at the address below upon payment of a 
non refundable fee of USD 100 or its equivalent in a freely 
convertible currency. 
 
¶4. Bids must be delivered to the address below at or before 
12:00 noon on Saturday November 12, 2005.  Late bids will be 
rejected.  Bids will be opened physically at 12:00 noon on 
Saturday November 12, 2005 in the presence of the bidders' 
representatives who choose to attend in person. 
 
¶5. All bids shall be accompanied by a bid security of 
US$150,000 valid for 150 days from bid opening date. 
 
¶6. Interested eligible bidders may obtain further 
information from: 
 
      Office of the Project Director 
      Urban Water Supply and Sanitation Project 
      Ministry of Water and Environment 
      Hadda, near Shammar Trading Center, flat no. 7 
      Sanaa, Republic of Yemen
