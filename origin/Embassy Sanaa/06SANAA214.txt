SUBJECT: THANK YOU MESSAGE FROM FOMIN AL-QIRBI TO SECRETARY 
RICE 
 
The following message was submitted to post from Minister of 
Foreign Affairs Dr. Abu Bakr Al-Qirbi to Secretary Rice.  The 
letter is dated January 17, 2006 and was received on January 
21, 2006.  The signed original will follow. 
 
Begin text: 
 
Dear Secretary Rice, 
Thank you for your kind greetings to me and the people of the 
Republic of Yemen on the occasion of Eid Al Adha.  I take 
this opportunity to wish you and the American people 
prosperity and progress and greater achievements in 
maintaining world peace. 
 
With my best regards. 
 
Sincerely, 
Dr. Abu Bakr Al-Qirbi 
Minister of Foreign Affairs 
of the Republic of Yemen 
 
End Text.
