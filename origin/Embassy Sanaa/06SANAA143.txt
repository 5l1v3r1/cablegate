SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON PROVISION OF BANKING 
SERVICES TO NORTH KOREAN GOVERNMENT AGENCIES 
 
REF: SECSTATE 921 
 
On January 28, Econoff delivered reftel demarche to Abdullah 
al-Olafi, Sub-Governor for the Banking Supervision Sector at 
the Central Bank of Yemen.  Olafi said that he had not heard 
of Banco Delta Asia or any associated front companies, but 
would release a circular to commercial banks asking them to 
report any relevant transactions.  Olafi promised to share 
with post any new information on this matter.
