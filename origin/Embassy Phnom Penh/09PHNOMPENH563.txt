¶1.  (SBU) Per reftel, information on Cambodian high-level attendance 
at UNGA is as follows: 
 
¶A.  Attendance of the Head of State 
King Norodom Sihamoni does not plan to attend. 
 
¶B.  Attendance of Head of Government 
Prime Minster Hun Sen does not plan to attend. 
 
¶C.  Attendance of Foreign Minister 
Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of Foreign Affairs and 
International Cooperation Hor Namhong plans to attend. 
 
He arrives in New York on September 25 and departs New York 
September 26. 
He will be present for the General Debate on September 25-26. 
Interpretation is not required. 
 
¶D.  Attendance of other VIP's 
No other VIP's plan to attend. 
 
¶E.  Attendance of the Foreign Ministry official responsible for IO 
affairs 
H.E. Ouch Borith, Secretary of State, Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
and International Cooperation, plans to attend. 
Interpretation is not required. 
 
H.E. Kao Kim Hourn, Secretary of State, Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
and International Cooperation, and Advisor to the Prime Minister, 
plans to attend. 
Interpretation is not required.
