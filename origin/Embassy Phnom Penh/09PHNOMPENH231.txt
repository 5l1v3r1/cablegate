¶1.  This is an action cable. Please see Paragraph Six. 
 
¶2.  Reftel is guidance for screening nominees for U.S.-sponsored 
training programs. 
 
¶3.  The individual listed below will attend the SYMPOSIUM ON EAST 
ASIAN SECURITY (SEAS) is a program to reinforce security cooperation 
and support for counter-terrorism activities within the East 
Asia/Pacific Region.  To comply with the Leahy amendment relative to 
U.S.-sponsored security assistance training, the U.S. Embassy Phnom 
Penh conducted local human rights vetting for the following 
individual. 
 
NOMINEE: 
 
NAME: POR, PHAK (Male) 
DPOB: May 10, 1975; Phnom Penh 
RANK: Brigadier General 
POSITION: Assistant to Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of 
Interior Sar Kheng; Director of the Ministry of Interior Department 
of International Relations 
 
 
¶4.  U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, 
as of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above-mentioned individual. 
 
¶5.  The above individual will attend the Symposium on East Asian 
Security (SEAS) scheduled for June 5-29, 2009 in Honolulu, Hawaii. 
This program to reinforce security cooperation and support for 
counter-terrorism activities within the East Asia/Pacific region. 
 
¶6.  Please verify whether the Department possesses any credible 
information that the individual named above has been implicated in 
gross violations of human rights.
