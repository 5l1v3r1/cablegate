Invitation and proposed agenda for the June 17-19 Iraq 
Conference Meeting in Abu Dhabi were passed to both the EU 
Council and the EU Commission.  Both institutions today 
advised that they will not be attending the conference.  EU 
officials will be in Iraq June 17-19 negotiating the EU-Iraq 
Trade and Cooperation Agreement (TCA).
