SUBJECT: THE GAMBIA: PRESIDENT'S POLICY ADDRESS ON IRAQ 
 
REF: STATE 3592 
 
¶1. (SBU) In meeting with Vice President Isatou Njie Saidy, 
Ambassador briefed on the President's new Iraq strategy and 
provided text of his remarks.  The Ambassador urged the GOTG 
to consider expressing publicly its support for the 
President's strategy and for the Iraqi government's efforts 
on behalf of national stability, economic development, and 
national reconciliation. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Vice President Njie Saidy took the Ambassador's 
presentation on board and indicated that the GOTG would 
carefully review the President's statement and consider our 
request.  She remarked, "it is essential to resolve the Iraqi 
situation," but otherwise made no substantive comment.
