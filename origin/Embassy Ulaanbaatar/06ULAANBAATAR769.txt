SUBJECT: Mongolia to Vote for Guatemala in UNSC Battle 
 
 
Sensitive But Unclassified -- Not for Internet distribution 
 
(SBU) On October 5, Vice Foreign Minister Tsolmon told then Charge 
that Mongolia would vote for Guatemala in the forthcoming vote for 
UNSC elected seats.  Tsolmon stated that Mongolia's mission at the 
United Nations would formally convey this to Guatemala by a 
diplomatic note within the next few days.
