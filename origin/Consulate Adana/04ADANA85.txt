SUBJECT: SOUTHEAST TURKEY PRESS SUMMARY JUNE 18, 2004 
 
 
¶1.      This is the Southeastern Turkey press summary for June 18, 
¶2004.  Please note that Turkish press reports often contain 
errors or exaggerations; AmConsulate Adana does not vouch for 
the accuracy of the reports summarized here. 
 
POLITICAL, SECURITY, HUMAN RIGHTS 
 
¶2.      Cumhuriyet / During ex-Kurdish MPs visit in Bitlis, people 
were banned by the police from talking to them.  It is reported 
that police also closed some roads to prevent people from 
joining in the meeting. 
 
¶3.      Milliyet, Ozgur Gundem / In Guclukonak, Sirnak, during a 
raid by the alleged terrorists, a Turkish soldier was killed. 
 
¶4.      Sabah / During the operations in Mus, two village guards 
were wounded. 
 
¶5.      Sabah Guney / Workers' Party Adana Youth Branch gathered 
in front of their party building to protest the coming Istanbul 
NATO Summit. 
 
¶6.      Ozgur Gundem / People's Party Forces (HPG) announced that 
they would not be active unless an operation was launched 
against them.
