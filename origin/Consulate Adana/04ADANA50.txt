POLITICAL, SECURITY, HUMAN RIGHTS 
 
 
Milli Gazete, Vakit,  / Diyarbakir Governorate sued Ahmet 
Guvener, a Turkish Christian, for building a church illegally. 
U.S Ankara Embassy Economic Counselor Scott Marciel visited the 
Diyarbakir Public Prosecutor on April 22 to get information 
about the case. Allegedly, Guvener is reported to have received 
approximately $80,000 from European countries for the building 
of the church. 
 
 
Ulkede Gundem / In Van, Jandarma detained 91 people who entered 
Turkey illegally.  The detainees are from Pakistan, Bangladesh, 
and Afghanistan. 
 
 
Bolge, Turkiye, Zaman / The Young Turkish Youth Parliament will 
be inaugurated on May 19, 2004.  Members had their first meeting 
in Tarsus.
