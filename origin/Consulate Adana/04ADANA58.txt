SUBJECT: SOUTHEAST TURKEY PRESS SUMMARY MAY 7, 2004 
 
¶1.      This is the Southeastern Turkey press summary for May 7, 
¶2004.  Please note that Turkish press reports often contain 
errors or exaggerations; AmConsulate Adana does not vouch for 
the accuracy of the reports summarized here. 
 
 
POLITICAL, SECURITY, HUMAN RIGHTS 
 
 
¶2.      Bolge, Evrensel / Workers' Party members, DEHAP and 
Socialist Platform members gathered at Adana's Inonu Park to 
protest against growing Islamic fundamentalism in the 
government, and the situation in Turkish prisons. 
 
 
 
 
CULTURE and TOURISM 
 
 
¶3.      Hurriyet Cukurova / In Samandag, Hatay province, where a 
large Christian community resides, Samandag District head Semir 
Aydin announced that he is going to start an Annual 
International Easter Festival.
