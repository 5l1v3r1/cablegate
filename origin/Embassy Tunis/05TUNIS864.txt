SUBJECT: TUNISIA: MILITARY GRANT ASSISTANCE: LEGAL 
REQUIREMENTS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 05896 
 
On April 13, Embassy received a diplomatic note from the 
Tunisian Ministry of Foreign Affairs in response to subject 
action request delivered on January 28 and again on April 8. 
Translation of the note reads as follows: The Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Tunisia presents its 
compliments to the Embassy of the United States of America 
and in reference to its Note Verbale No 506 of April 8, 2005 
relating to the Foreign Assistance Act of 1961 between the 
Government of Tunisia and the United States of America, has 
the honor to inform that the National Defense Ministry does 
not object to replacing the sentence "under the military 
assistance program" of the second paragraph, section 505 (f) 
of the Agreement, with the sentence "on a grant basis from".
