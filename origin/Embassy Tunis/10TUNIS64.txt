(SBU) The Tunisian press reported that on January 25 the 
Tunisian delegation to the United Nations donors meeting in 
New York announced that President Ben Ali had decided to 
donate $1,000,000 to the UN emergency relief fund for Haiti. 
The donation was separately confirmed to us January 26 by 
Hatim Landolsi, Deputy Director for the Americas Desk at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
