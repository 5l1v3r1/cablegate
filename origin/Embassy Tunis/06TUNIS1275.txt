SUBJECT: WEDGWOOD CANDIDACY FOR U.N. HUMAN RIGHTS COMMISSION

On May 26, Ambassador delivered reftel demarche and dipnote 
on seeking support for the U.S. candidate for the U.N. Human 
Rights Commission (Ruth Wedgwood) to MFA Director General for 
the Americas and Asia Ghazi Jomaa.  Jomaa said that there 
were many candidates, but that he would pass the information 
to the MFA Director General for International Organizations.
