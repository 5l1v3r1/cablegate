SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: PRE-LICENSE END-USE CHECK 
ON LICENSE 050197949 
 
¶1. This is an Action Request. See paragraph 4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a pre-license check on license 050197949 for the export of 
ammunition to Georgia.  Post is requested to complete this 
Blue Lantern check within 30 days.  Lack of response to a 
Blue Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for Request:  The ammunition in this transaction is 
of very significant caliber and capability.  This request is 
for Post to establish the bona fides of this transaction and 
verify the exact quantity of ammunition ordered by the 
Ministry of Internal Affairs.  Post is also requested to 
determine the role of Mamuka Kakabadze, who was authorized by 
a MOIA Letter of Authorization to take part in this 
transaction but does not appear on the license application. 
 
¶4. Action Request: Post is requested to establish the bona 
fides of this transactions by making inquiries to 
representatives of the Georgian Ministry of Internal Affairs. 
 Post is requested to verify the exact quantity of ammunition 
ordered by MOIA and determine Mamuka Kakabadze's role in this 
transaction.  The following points are included for guidance: 
 
(To Ministry of Internal Affairs) 
 
--Can you confirm that MOIA has ordered the ammunition listed 
below?  Please verify the exact quantity MOIA ordered. 
 
--According to the Letter of Authorization No. 114039/402, 
MOIA authorized Mr. Mamuka Kakabadze to "use the services of 
M5 International Company."  Who is Mr. Mamuka Kakabadze and 
what is his role in this transaction?  Is he an employee of 
the Government of Georgia?  If not, what services is he 
providing and how is he being compensated? 
 
In its reply, Post is encouraged to comment on any 
information pertinent to this transaction such as security of 
facilities, end-use, and concerns about the entities involved.
