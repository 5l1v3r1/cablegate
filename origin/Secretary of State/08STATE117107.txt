¶1. This is an Action Request. See paragraphs 2 - 4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on license 050055911 for the export of 
carbines to Lebanon. Post is requested to complete this Blue 
Lantern check within 45 days. Lack of response to a Blue 
Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for Request: To confirm that the carbines listed 
below were received by the Lebanese Internal Security Forces. 
 
¶4. Action Request: Embassy Beirut should make the appropriate 
inquiries with officials of the Lebanese Internal Security 
Forces to determine that the carbines listed below were 
received. In its reply, Post may also comment on any other 
information pertinent to these transactions such as security 
of facilities, concerns about the entities involved, and 
irregularity of quantity and/or models.
