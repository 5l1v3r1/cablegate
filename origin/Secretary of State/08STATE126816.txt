SUBJECT: OPEN SKIES TREATY REIMBURSEMENT

with Turkey over the Russian Federation the week of 4 August.

the U.S. agreed to pay the Government of Turkey no more than 
$5,000 U.S. dollars as reimbursement for our share of the 
expenses.
