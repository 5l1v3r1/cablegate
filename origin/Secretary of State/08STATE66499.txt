SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN POST-SHIPMENT END USE CHECK ON

¶1.  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
post-shipment check for the export of GloTape (infrared 
reflective material) to New Zealand via Australia.  Post is 
requested to complete this Blue Lantern check within 45 days. 
 Lack of response to a Blue Lantern check will affect pending 
and future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3.  Reason for request: Sensitive commodity with potential 
for diversion.  This is a check on the intermediary parties 
involved in the transaction. 
 
¶4.  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, New Zealand 
Defense Force, and confirm its receipt of the exact quantity 
and specific type of commodity listed below.  END ACTION.
