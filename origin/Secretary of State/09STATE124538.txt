¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in 
conducting a post-shipment check on license 050170346 for the 
export of an International Traffic in Arms 
(ITAR)-controlled VHF transceiver and directional gyro to 
Indonesia.  Post is requested to complete this Blue Lantern 
inquiry within 45 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern 
inquiry will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: Sensitive commodities at risk for 
diversion; Unfamiliar consignee (limited licensing history), 
Pt. Putra Chandra Lestari (PCL); Ship to address stated on 
purchase order is a freight forwarder located in Singapore 
(ultimate end-user is the Indonesian Air Force); Payment 
terms stated on purchase order are C.O.D. (Cash On Delivery). 
 
¶4. ACTION: The Department requests that Post confirm that the 
foreign end-user, the Indonesian Air Force (IAF), has 
received the transceiver and gyro and that the items are 
being used for the purpose stated on the license application. 
 In addition, the Department requests that Post make 
inquiries with appropriate officials to establish the bona 
fides of PCL in the transaction and its relationship with the 
IAF. 
 
END ACTION.
