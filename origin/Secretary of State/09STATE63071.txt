¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Posts' assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on license 050094716 for the export of 
International Traffic in Arms Regulations (ITAR) - controlled 
signal amplifiers to Saudi Arabia.  Posts are requested to 
complete this Blue Lantern inquiry within 45 days.  Lack of 
response to a Blue Lantern inquiry will affect pending and 
future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: To substantiate bona fides of foreign 
parties involved in this transaction and confirm order/ 
receipt with the end-user. 
 
¶4. Action Pretoria:  The Department requests that Post make 
inquiries with Foreign Consignee RF Design to verify its role 
in this transaction.  Emphasize that the unauthorized 
re-export or retransfer of United States Munitions List 
(USML) items is strictly prohibited. 
 
Action Riyadh:  Post is requested to confirm that End User 
King Abdul Aziz City for Science and Technology (KACST) has 
received the USML items listed below in full and that they 
are being used for the purpose stated on the license. 
 
END ACTION.
