SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN PRE-LICENSE END USE CHECK ON 
APPLICATION 05-050101889 AND 05-050111984 
 
¶1.  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
pre-license check for the export of riot control munitions to 
Egypt.  Post is requested to complete this Blue Lantern check 
within 30 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern check 
will affect pending and future licenses involving parties to 
this license. 
 
¶3.  Reason for request: large quantity of riot control 
munitions. 
 
¶4.  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, the Egypt 
Ministry of Interior, and verify its request for the exact 
quantities and specific types of riot control munitions 
listed on both licenses below.  Also request the Ministry of 
Interior to confirm the role of El Gindy Company Trading & 
Import, the Foreign Intermediate Consignee on these two 
transactions.  END ACTION.
