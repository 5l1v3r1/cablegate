SUBJECT: SERBIA TO ABSTAIN ON THE THREE UNGA RESOLUTIONS WITH 
ANTI-ISRAEL BIAS 
 
REF: State 159386 
 
(U) Vanja Radonjic Rakic, of Serbia's MFA United Nations 
Directorate, told poloff, December 12, that Serbia plans to abstain 
during the December 17 vote on the Special Committee to Investigate 
Israeli Practices.  Rakic also said that Serbia abstained during the 
December 10 vote on the resolution for the Committee on the Exercise 
of Inalienable Rights of the Palestinian People as well as the 
Division for Palestinian Rights within the UN Secretariat.
