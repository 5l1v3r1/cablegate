SUBJECT: NICARAGUA WILL PRESS PANAMA AND COSTA RICA ON 
UNIVERSALIZATION OF FRAMEWORK TREATY ON DEMOCRATIC SECURITY 
IN CENTRAL AMERICA 
 
REF: STATE 9539 
 
In his meeting with the Ambassador on January 20, Foreign 
Minister Norman Caldera agreed that it makes "great sense" to 
urge Costa Rican and Panamanian ratification of the Framework 
Treaty.  Caldera promised to work on the issue, starting at 
the inauguration of new Honduran President Zelaya.
