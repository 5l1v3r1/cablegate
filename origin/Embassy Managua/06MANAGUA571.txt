¶1. The final composition of Nicaragua's delegation to the 
CICTE Sixth Regular session has not been confirmed by the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs, but will likely be led by Vice 
Foreign Minister Javier Williams Slate.  At the technical 
level, the GON will send Myriam Vasquez, the Vice Director 
for Sovereignty, Territory and International Legal Issues. 
 
¶2. Nicaragua ratified the CICTE Convention via Decree number 
47 (2003), which came into effect on June 10, 2003.
