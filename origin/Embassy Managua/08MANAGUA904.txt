SUBJECT: NICARAGUA FILLS 2008 SUGAR QUOTAS 
 
REF: STATE 71823 
 
In response to reftel action request, Nicaraguan authorities 
have confirmed in writing that they have filled both the WTO 
and CAFTA-DR quotas for sugar exports to the United States 
during FY2008: 
 
WTO Quota 
--------- 
--10,927.57 metric tons shipped November 4, 2007 
--7,555.12 metric tons shipped January 29, 2008 
--2,810.68 metric tons shipped July 1, 2008 
 
CAFTA-DR Quota 
-------------- 
--11,783.28 metric tons shipped November 4, 2007 
--8,147.43 metric tons shipped January 29, 2008 
--2,948.43 metric tons shipped July 1, 2008 
 
The Nicaraguans did not request information about quota 
reallocation.
