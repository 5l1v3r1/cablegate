SMALL SCALE FACILITY ARRANGEMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 32563 
 
Reftel points were delivered to MFA office of 
Nonproliferation on March 1.  On March 7, poloff spoke with 
Etienne Sur, the MFA Chemical Weapons Convention Desk 
Officer, who said that he transmitted the information to the 
Federation for Unique Small Scale Facilities in France.  He 
said that the Federation appreciated the comments and 
suggestions contained in reftel points and is currently 
taking them into consideration.  He said that they will 
provide feedback through appropriate channels when finished.
