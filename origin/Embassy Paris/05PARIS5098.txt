SUBJECT:  VISIT OF FEDERAL TRAINING SHIP GOLDEN BEAR - 2005 
TRAINIING CRUISES 
 
REF.  STATE 20779 
 
(U) On 19 May, Embassy received a diplomatic note dated 21 
July granting clearance to the Golden Bear to call at 
Papeete on 8 to 11 August.  The text of the note reads as 
follows:  "The French Foreign Ministry presents its 
compliments to the U.S. Embassy and referring to its request 
of no. 337 of 21 July 2005 and has the honor to convey the 
fact that the French authorities have given their agreement 
to the calls of the vessel "Golden Bear" at Papeete from 8 
to 11 August 2005. (Complimentary close)."
