SUBJECT: FRENCH RESPONSE TO SAFE HAVEN DEMARCHE

Classified By: Political Minister-Counselor Josiah Rosenblatt, reasons 
1.4 (b) and (d). 
 
(C) PolOff delivered reftel demarche to MFA Desk Officer for 
Nicaragua Isabelle de Boisgelin on October 27.  Boisgelin 
indicated that the process of revoking any existing visas or 
freezing any assets was a complicated one that, if 
implemented, would involve a number of different bureaus and 
ministries.  She noted that she had disseminated the reftel 
points and was coordinating with interlocutors within the 
MFA.  She pledged to keep us informed of any developments.
