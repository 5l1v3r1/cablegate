SUBJECT: FRANCE CIVAIR:  MULTIPLE DESIGNATIONS 
 
REF. STATE 95168 
 
Embassy informed the French Government by diplomatic note of 
designations as cited reftel.  On 18 June, Embassy received 
a diplomatic note from the foreign ministry which "takes 
note of the designations of Air America Inc., Island Bird 
Inc., Clair Aero Inc. and Servecios Profesionales Inc. to 
fly non-scheduled flights under conditions prescribed in the 
bilateral U.S.-French agreement on air services and welcomes 
these operators who have heretofore not been registered."
