SUBJECT: DELIVERY OF DEMARCHE REQUESTING FRANCE'S SUPPORT 
FOR U.S. CANDIDATE FOR THE UN COMMITTEE ON THE ELIMINATION 
OF RACIAL DISCRIMINATION 
 
REF: STATE 157655 
 
(U) Post delivered on November 19 an advance copy of reftel 
demarche and supporting documents (including bio info on U.S. 
candidate Pierre-Richard Prosper) to Jacques Pellet, Deputy 
Office Director equivalent for Humanitarian and Social 
Affairs at the MFA.  The diplomatic note formally requesting 
France's support for Prosper's candidacy for election to the 
UN Committee on the Elimination of Racial Discrimination was 
delivered to the MFA on November 27.  We will follow up with 
the MFA again before the January 18, 2008 election date, 
unless we receive, in the interim, a response to our note.
