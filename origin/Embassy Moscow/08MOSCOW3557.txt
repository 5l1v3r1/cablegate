SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: POST-SHIPMENT VERIFICATION: UAB ARNIKA, 
DRUSKININKAI (VILNIUS), LITHUANIA, LICENSE NO. NLR 
 
REFTEL: USDOC 06830 
 
¶1. Unauthorized disclosure of the information provided below is 
prohibited by Section 12C of the Export Administration Act. 
 
¶2. Reftel 1 requested a Post-shipment verification to determine the 
legitimacy and reliability of the end-user, UAB Arnika, Gardino g. 
55, LT-66191, Drushkininkai (Vilnius), Lithuania. The company is 
listed on BIS license application: NLR as the ultimate consignee of 
one (1) seismograph 24 channel, style no. SMART-SE24 with spares and 
accessories for surveying. ECCN 6A999. Controlled for anti-terrorism 
(AT) reasons. The exporter is Geometrics, 2190 Fortune Drive, San 
Jose, CA  95131. 
 
¶3. On November 11, 2008 through December 5, 2008, Export Control 
Attach Peter Liston made contact with various entities within UAB 
Arnika, via facsimile, email and by telephone.  ECO Liston advised 
UAB Arnika of the Export Administration Regulations as they pertain 
to Dual Use commodities exported from the United States under a 
Commerce license.  UAB Arnika was advised the ECO would like to 
conduct a EUC at their office regarding the seismograph 24 channel, 
style no. SMART-SE24 with spares and accessories they received from 
the U.S. on or about February 1, 2007. 
 
¶4. ECO Liston spoke with UAB Arnika General Manager Roland 
Geremimas, who advised ECO Liston that the person responsible for 
the seismograph 24 channel, style no. SMART-SE24 with spares and 
accessories was Alexey Kuzmin, and provided a contact telephone 
number (+7-9067-468-242) for Kuzmin in Russia. ECO Liston called the 
contact number and spoke with Roland LNU.  Roland LNU provided ECO 
Liston with Alexey KuzminQs Russian mobile number, +7-921-27-35-671. 
 ECO Liston called and spoke with Alexey Kuzmin, who advised that he 
purchased the seismograph 24 channel, style no. SMART-SE24 with 
spares and accessories from Geometrics and then caused it to be 
diverted to Russia and sold to the Kolskiy Mining Institute in 
Apatit, Russia,  Kuzmin stated that the POC at the Kolskiy Mining 
Institute is Maxim Svetlov, email: mms@mail.ru. 
 
¶5. Alexey Kuzmin advised ECO Liston that he no longer has any 
paperwork regarding shipping the seismograph 24 channel, style no. 
SMART-SE24 with spares and accessories from Lithuania to Russia. 
 
¶6. Throughout the protracted communications with UAB Arnika, ECO 
Liston was told by Mr. Roland Geremimas, General Manager of UAB 
Arnika, that he would meet with the ECO in his office during the 
week of December 2, 2008.  GM Geremimas told ECO Liston that he 
would advise later of the exact time and date.  GM Geremimas 
eventually refused to meet with ECO Liston. 
 
¶7. Recommendations: Post cannot/cannot recommend UAB Arnika, 
Druskonikai (Vilnius), Lithuania or Alexey Kuzmin, Russia as 
reliable recipients of sensitive U.S. origin commodities.
