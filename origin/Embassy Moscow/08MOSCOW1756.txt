SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: POST-SHIPMENT VERIFICATION: ZAO 
MP DIAGNOST, MOSCOW, RUSSIA, LICENSE NO. NLR 
 
 
¶1. Unauthorized disclosure of the information provided 
below is prohibited by Section 12C of the Export 
Administration Act. 
 
¶2. Reftel 1 requested a post-shipment verification to 
determine the legitimacy and reliability of the end- 
user, ZAO MP Diagnost, Moscow, Russia. The company is 
listed on BIS license application: NLR as the ultimate 
consignee of One (1) M67D Optical Pyrometer, product 
no. 14300-D2300C, and accessories.  ECCN - 0x000 - no 
license required.  The exporter is Mikron Infrared 
Incorporated, 16 Thornton Road, Oakland, NJ  07436. 
 
¶3. On June 18, 2008, Export Control Attach Peter 
Liston and RSO Investigative Assistant Joseph Tacke 
conducted the requested post-shipment verification 
with ZAO MP Diagnost, Okruzhnoy Proezd, Bldg 15, Room 
10, Moscow, Russia. The export control team met with 
Alexander Zozlov, Director; Michael Petrushenkov, 
Executive Director; and Vladimir Rouban, Sales 
Manager. 
 
¶4. ZAO MP Diagnost was founded in 1991 as measuring 
and testing equipment distributor specializing in 
infrared and ultrasonic testing of heat and heat 
sources.  ZAO MP Diagnost is listed in Dunn and 
Bradstreet's Who's Who (NO. 401404).  The company 
employs approximately 20, with operations in Moscow 
and St Petersburg.  95% of ZAO MP Diagnostic's 
customer base is located within the Russian 
Federation.  ZAO MP does not export to countries where 
the United States has trade restrictions in place. 
ZAO MP Diagnost advised the Export Control Team that 
they purchased two (2) M67D Optical Pyrometers, not 
one as indicted in reftel. 
 
¶5. The M67D Optical Pyrometers in reftel are used for 
non-contact temperature measurement.  The M67D uses 
infrared temperature measurement technology and can 
measure the temperature of a heat source through hot 
gas.  It is used in various industries including 
metallurgical plants and glass factories.  Sales 
invoices provided to the Export Control Team by ZAO MP 
Diagnost accounted for the commodity in reftel.  The 
invoices are available upon request.  There are no 
indications of impropriety and ZAO MP Diagnost company 
representatives answered all questions related to the 
subject commodity. 
 
¶6. ZAO MP Diagnost Moscow is located in a multi-office 
complex with secure, card-controlled access.  There is 
manned security and the Export Control Team was 
allowed full access to all areas of the company's 
operations. 
 
¶7. Recommendations: Post recommends ZAO MP Diagnost, 
Moscow, Russia as reliable recipients of sensitive 
U.S. origin commodities.
