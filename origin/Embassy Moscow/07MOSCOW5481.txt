SUBJECT: GICNT: DETECTION ARCHITECTURE MODEL GUIDELINES 
NONPAPER PASSED 
 
REF: STATE 156602 
 
¶1. (SBU)  We met November 21 with Senior Counselor Vladimir 
Prokhorov of the MFA's New Challenges and Threats Department 
to present reftel nonpaper regarding the proposed January 22 
Detection Architecture Model Guidelines conference. 
Prokhorov said that he would pass the nonpaper to relevant 
agencies and work to get a response by December 7. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Prokhorov informed us that Deputy Foreign Minister 
Kislyak had transferred responsibility for coordination on 
the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism (GICNT) 
from the MFA Security and Disarmament Department (DVBR) to 
the New Challenges and Threats Department.
