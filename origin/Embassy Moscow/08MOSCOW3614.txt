SUBJECT: RUSSIA RESPONSE ON ANTARCTIC TREATY ANNIVERSARY AND 
INTERNATIONAL POLAR YEAR 
 
REF: STATE 109133 
 
On December 1, Post delivered reftel talking points to Vasiliy 
Titushkin of the MFA Legal Department, the Ministry's lead action 
officer on Antarctic Treaty issues.  Titushkin advised us that 
Russia supports the proposed date and format of the Antarctic Treaty 
Consultative Meeting.  He said that the Government of Russia is 
still considering the makeup of its delegation with a view toward 
participation at the minister level, and that his office would be 
able to provide additional information at a later date.
