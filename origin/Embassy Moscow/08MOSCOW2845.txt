We delivered reftel points to MFA DVBR Advisor Sergey 
Kashirin September 23.  DATT also delivered points to MOD 
UVS.  Kashirin indicated that Moscow was familiar with U.S. 
policy on nuclear weapons and said he looked forward to 
reading the paper.  He had no other questions, but used the 
opportunity to reiterate Moscow's request for a draft text on 
a post-START agreement as soon as possible.
