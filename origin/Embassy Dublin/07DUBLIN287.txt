(SBU) In response to Post's earlier reftel demarche, Janice 
McGann, Third Secretary in the Department of Foreign Affairs 
(DFA) UN/Conflict Resolution office (formerly, the 
UN/International Terrorism office) informed Post on April 13 
that she was circulating information to the Irish Central 
Bank and Departments of Justice and Finance on the U.S. 
designation of Iran's Defense Industries Organization (DIO). 
She committed to contact Post if the GOI discovered any 
activities in, or connections to, Ireland involving the DIO.
