(SBU) On June 27, PolOff followed-up with MFA International 
Organizations Human Rights Officer Janio Tunion on MDA 
demarche (reftels A and B) of June 6 (reftel c).  Tunion told 
PolOff that his office is busy consolidating its position on 
UN Commission on Human Rights reform, but had not yet made a 
final decision on a response to the Swiss Circular note. 
Tunion said that the GOP supported "in principle" holding a 
conference to adopt the Third Additional Protocol and to 
include the society of Magen David Adom in the Red Cross/Red 
Crescent societies.  Tunion had no further information as of 
July 5.  Embassy will report any further developments to the 
Department.
