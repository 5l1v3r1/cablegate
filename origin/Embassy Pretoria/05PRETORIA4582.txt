SUBJECT: INTERNATIONAL MARITIME ORGANIZATION - DEMARCHE IN 
SUPPORT OF U.S. CANDIDACY 
 
REF: SECSTATE 207080 
 
The SAG has not yet responded to demarche delivered by 
PolCounselor November 14 to DFA DDG for Multilateral Affairs 
Ambassador S.G. Nene and DFA USA Director Vollenhoven and by 
EconOff November 15 to Transportation Department Chief 
Director for Maritime Regulations Nosibo Sobekwa.  Emboffs 
informed them of the US candidacy and requested support for 
the reelection of the United States to the IMO Council in 
Category A. On November 17, PolCounselor reminded Vollenhoven 
that a SAG reply was still pending.
