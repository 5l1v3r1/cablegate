SUBJECT: SOUTH AFRICA FEDERAL AIR MARSHALL SERVICE DIPNOTE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 188876 
 
 ¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, post delivered on November 30 Federal 
Air Marshal Service dipnote and proposed Air Marshal 
agreement received 17 November to South Africa Department of 
Foreign Affairs Nokwazi Mtshali. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post received October 23 via e-mail the proposed Air 
Marshal agreement from TSA's (Transportation Security 
Administration) John Halinski (Rome). The agreement was sent 
to South Africa Department of Transport Bilateral Affairs 
Chief Johann Bierman and to South African Civil Aviation 
Authority Security Chief Mongezi India.  Mr. India tabled the 
matter at the November 9 meeting of the South Africa Aviation 
Security Committee, chaired by Biermann.  Mr. Biermann told 
econoff November 24 that good progress was being made on 
reviewing the proposed agreement but that the review would 
not be completed before U.S. carrier Delta Airline's 
inaugural flight to South Africa December 5.
