SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO URUGUAY ON AUSTRALIAN 
AMENDMENT TO CUBA EMBARGO RESOLUTION 
 
REF: STATE 184156 
 
Classified By: Charge D'Affaires James D. Nealon 
for reasons 1.4 (B) and (D) 
 
(C) Per instructions reftel, on the mid-morning of November 
8, Political Counselor delivered subject demarche to the 
MFA's Acting Director of Multilateral Affairs Beatriz Nunez. 
However, it was not until a separate meeting later that 
afternoon with Nunez and Director General for Political 
Affairs Alvaro Gallardo that any substantive commentary was 
received.  At that meeting, Gallardo indicated that the MFA 
had not/not issued any instructions for the Uruguayan 
delegation in New York to support the Australian amendment or 
to give an Explanation of Vote condemning the human rights 
abuses of the Cuban government. Gallardo also noted that, 
such decisions are now being taken in concert with our 
Mercosur partners.""
