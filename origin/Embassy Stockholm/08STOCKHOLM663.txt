SUBJECT: STOCKHOLM DELIVERS CERTIFICATE OF REVOCATION (SIMON 
ENGLISH, CECILIO EDUARDO) 
 
REF: STATE 103325 
 
(SBU) Per reftel, Post hand delivered a letter to the Embassy of 
Panama informing Panamanian Ambassador to Sweden Cecilio Eduardo 
Simon English (DPOB: 01-OCT-1951, Panama) that his B1/B2 visa has 
been revoked under INA 212(A)(3).  The Ambassador's office 
acknowledged receipt of the notice and subsequently called the 
Consul to seek clarification.  Consul referenced the text of the 
Certificate of Revocation.  Consul also advised the Panamanian 
Embassy that Ambassador Simon English may attempt to establish his 
eligibility for future travel to the United States by applying for a 
visa at the U.S. Embassy if he so desires.  The Panamanian Embassy 
indicated that they would seek further information through their 
mission in Washington, DC.
