SUBJECT: SWEDEN: GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT 
NUCLEAR TERRORISM 
 
REF: STATE 37720 
 
ECON Counselor delivered reftel demarche April 15 to Niklas Wiberg, 
Deputy Director in the Security Policy Department's 
Counter-Terrorism Unit at the Swedish Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA).  Wiberg commented that it was interesting to learn that the 
U.S. is encouraging Bahrain to consider hosting/proposing an 
activity in 2008-2009.  Post has also shared reftel demarche with 
Mats Nystrom, Bahrain desk officer; Asa Gustafsson, Deputy Director, 
Department for Disarmament and Non-Proliferation; and Jorgen 
Persson, Deputy Director, Department for Disarmament and 
Non-Proliferation at the MFA.
