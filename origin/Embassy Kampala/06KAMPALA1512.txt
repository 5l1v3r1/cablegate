(SBU)  Poloff delivered points per reftel on the UNSC 
resolution draft on Lebanon to Amb. Patrick Mugoya, head of 
the Americas Department at the Ugandan Foreign Ministry on 
August 7.  Mugoya was the highest level MFA official in town. 
 Mugoya stated that Uganda would support any effort to stop 
the fighting in Lebanon and urged that the U.S. pressure both 
sides of the conflict to cease hostile operations.
