SUBJECT: Chile: Demarche on President's Announcement on the Way 
Forward in Afghanistan/Pakistan 
 
REF: STATE 122731 
 
(SBU) The Ambassador delivered reftel demarche, previewing the 
President's announcement on the way forward in Afghanistan and 
Pakistan, to Acting Foreign Minister Angel Flisfisch on December 1 
(FM Fernandez was on travel).  After the Ambassador emphasized the 
importance of this announcement to the U.S. Administration, 
Flisfisch promised to discuss it with FM Fernandez and explore 
whether Chile could issue a statement on the announcement.
