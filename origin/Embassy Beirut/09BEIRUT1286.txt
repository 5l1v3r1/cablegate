SUBJECT:  DEMARCHE RESPONSE: LEBANON NOTIFIED OF CENTCOM 2010 GULF 
REGION COMMUNICATIONS CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 122229 
 
¶1. Post notified Minister of Telecommunications Charbel Nahas and 
Chairman of the Telecommunications Regulatory Authority (TRA) Dr. 
Kamal Shehadi on December 3 that the 2010 Gulf Region Communications 
Conference (GRCC) will be held inAmman, Jordan, on February 21-23, 
2010, and requsted that they save the date.  We also informed them 
that official invitations will be delivered by mid-December.  Both 
Nahas and Shehadi wanted to review a draft agenda before committing 
to attend. 
 
¶2. ACTION REQUEST: Post would appreciate receiving a draft agenda or 
more substantive information as soon as possible to forward to our 
Lebanese interlocutors.
