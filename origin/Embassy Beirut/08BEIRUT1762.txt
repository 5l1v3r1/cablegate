SUBJECT: LEBANON: NO SUBSTANTIVE RESPONSE TO US PROPOSAL TO 
HARMONIZE UN CONDITIONS OF SERVICE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 129988 
 
 (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel points and text of the USG 
proposal to harmonize United Nations conditions of service to 
Oussama Khachab, Head of the Americas Desk at the Lebanese 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs on December 12.  Khachab had no 
immediate response, but reported on December 15 that he had 
forwarded the proposal to the Ministry's Department of 
International Organizations and to the Lebanese mission to 
the UN.
