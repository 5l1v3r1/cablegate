SUBJECT: LEBANESE RESPONSE TO U.S. CANDIDATE FOR ITU RADIO 
REGULATORY BOARD 
 
REF: STATE 51211 
 
On April 26, econoff delivered reftel diplomatic note 
concerning the candidacy of Julie Napier for the 
International Telecommunication Union (ITU) Radio Regulations 
Board to Ghady El-Khoury, Head of the Americas Desk, at the 
Lebanese Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  El-Khoury did not know 
at this time if Lebanon would support the U.S. candidate.  He 
said if the Arab League supported a candidate, Lebanon would 
likely support that candidate.  El-Khoury said the decision 
would likely be made at the Ministerial level.  He promised 
to raise Napier's candidacy with Foreign Minister Fawzi 
Salloukh as soon as possible.
