SUBJECT: IRAQ:  CSW CANDIDACY 
 
 
¶1.  Mission has received a note from the Permanent Mission of 
the Republic of Iraq to the United Nations requesting USG 
support for its candidature for membership on the Commission 
on the Status of Women (CSW).  The elections will be held 
during the resumed organizational session of the Economic and 
Social Council on May 10-11, 2006. 
 
¶2.  Mission will respond that careful consideration will be 
given by the United States to the candidature of the 
Permanent Mission of the Republic of Iraq.
