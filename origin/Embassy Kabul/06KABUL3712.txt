¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Mr. George Kiley, Professional 
Staff Member of the Senate Committee on Armed Services 
(SASC), Mr. Derek J. Maurer, PSM, SASC, Mr. Michael J. 
Mccord, PSM, SASC, Ms. Evelyn N. Farkas, PSM, SASC, Col.

equipment condition, maintenance and reset requirements; 
deployment and redeployment operations; and Force Protection 
initiatives.  Control Officer is Christopher Buck, Deputy 
PolMil Counselor, email address: BuckC@state.gov, telephone: 
011-937-010-8160 or cell number 011-937-021-563, and Aaron 
Jensen, Political Officer, email address: 
JensenAW2@state.gov, telephone 011-937-010-8175 or cell 
number 011-937-990-79291.  All travelers should bring USG ID 
for identification purposes and to obtain an Embassy ID.

escorts and lodging.

clearance.
