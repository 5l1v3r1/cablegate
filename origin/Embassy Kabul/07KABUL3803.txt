SUBJECT: NATO SENIOR CIVILIAN REPRESENTATIVE IN AFGHANISTAN 
VACATING HIS POST 
 
¶1. (SBU) The NATO Senior Civilian Representative in 
Afghanistan, Ambassador Daan Everts of the Netherlands, has 
circulated a letter to the Afghan Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
and the diplomatic community announcing that he will be 
concluding his duties and vacating his posting in Afghanistan 
by the end of December. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post has scanned the letter and e-mailed it to 
SCA/A, EUR/RPM, and USNATO.
