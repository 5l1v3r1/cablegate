SUBJECT: GOVERNMENT OF AFGHANISTAN'S RESPONSE TO INVITATION 
ON LITERACY AND HEALTH HOSTED BY MRS BUSH 
 
REF: STATE 126167 
 
¶1.  (U) Per Reftel, Post again approached the Government of 
the Islamic Republic of Afghanistan to extend the invitation 
to the proposed luncheon to be hosted by Mrs. Bush to the 
spouse of Afghan President Hamid Karzai. 
 
¶2.  (U) Late September 20, Post received a response from the 
Protocol Unit of the Presidential Palace, Dr. (Mrs.) Karzai 
regretted that she would not be able to attend the luncheon 
as she was not accompanying President Karzai on this trip to 
the United States.  Palace Protocol relayed Dr. Karzai's 
greetings to Mrs. Bush and "expressed her heartfelt thanks" 
for the invitation.
