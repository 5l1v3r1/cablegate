SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST: ANA OFFICERS SELECTED FOR IMET

¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 2. 
 
¶2. In order to comply with Leahy vetting requirements, this cable 
forwards the names and biodata of three Afghan National Army (ANA) 
officers who have been selected to participate in a Marshal Center 
sponsored seminar on counterterrorism. The seminar will be held May 
23 - August 10, 2007 in Garmisch, Germany.  Post does not possess 
any derogatory information on these individuals.

Rank: Brigadier General
