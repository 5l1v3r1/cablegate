SUBJECT: GERMANY:  NO PLANS TO ATTEND IRAN'S NUCLEAR ENERGY 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 112229 
 
(SBU) Germany has no plans to attend Iran's First 
International Conference on Nuclear Power Plants, 
Environmental and Sustainable Development.  Econoff met with 
Dr. Joerg Polster, German MFA Desk Officer for NSG issues and 
delivered reftel points.  Dr. Polster stated that he had not 
previously heard of the conference and that the MFA had no 
plans to send anyone.  Polster also stated he would inform 
other ministries of the USG position.  Polster stated he did 
not know if any representatives from Germany's nuclear 
industry were planning to attend.
