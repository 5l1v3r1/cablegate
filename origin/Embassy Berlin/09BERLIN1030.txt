SUBJECT: AUSTRALIA GROUP DEMARCHE: ESTABLISHING MINIMUM 
SURFACE COATING THICKNESS FOR LINED CHEMICAL EQUIPMENT 
 
REF: STATE 88010 
 
(SBU) On August 25, EconOff delivered ref non-paper to MFA 
Export Control Division Desk Officer, Nancy Reck, regarding a 
US Australia Group (AG) proposal to establish a minimum 
fluoropolymer surface coating thickness for lined chemical 
equipment.  Reck said that she would deliver the non-paper to 
AG experts at the German Federal Office of Economics and 
Export Control (BAFA) and would request feedback before the 
AG Plenary next month.  Post will report any substantive 
German response septel.
