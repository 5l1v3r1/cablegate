SUBJECT: ONCE AGAIN CHAVEZ TAKES HUGE ENTOURAGE TO UNGA 
 
REF: A. STATE 101170 
 
     ¶B. CARACAS 01575 
 
1.(SBU)  As of September 21, the U.S. Embassy in Caracas has 
approved visas for 233 delegates to attend the 62nd United 
Nations General Assembly in New York.  So far, the delegation 
includes President Chavez, his Chief of Staff, Hugo Cabezas, 
Foreign Minister Nicolas Maduro, Minister of Communication 
William Lara, various Vice Foreign Ministers, a large 
contingent of technical staff, members of the Venezuelan 
Armed Forces, and seven Cuban doctors. 
 
¶2. (SBU)  The military part of the delegation includes 
doctors, flight crew, security and protocol people, but no 
senior military officials of note.  Minister of Communication 
Lara will be accompanied by his press director and the 
requisite staff of photographers, journalists, and technical 
support.  Foreign Minister Nicolas Maduro will be accompanied 
by at least four Vice Ministers.  The Foreign Ministry will 
also bring its own protocol people, photographers and 
translators.
