SUBJECT: NAM DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO RWANDAN OFFICIALS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 176594 
 
Reftel talking points were delivered August 17 to the 
Director of the Americas Division, Augustin Rutikara, in 
Rwanda's Ministry of Foreign Affairs in Cooperation 
(MINAFFET).  Rutikara said he would deliver the talking 
points immediately to the Secretary General.  There was no 
immediate response, but Rutikara reiterated the GOR's support 
of the Proliferation Security Initiative (PSI).
