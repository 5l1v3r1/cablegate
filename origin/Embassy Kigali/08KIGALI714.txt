SUBJECT: RWANDA UNAMID DEPLOYMENT READINESS: REPORT #3 
 
REF: KIGALI 668 
 
(SBU)   The UN Pre-Deployment Visit (PDV) team arrived in 
Kigali October 7, and began preliminary discussions with 
Rwanda Defense Force (RDF) personnel October 8; Mission DATT 
also attended.  Discussions centered on unit readiness and 
deployment schedules, including potential airlift 
bottlenecks.   The team then proceeded to Gako Training 
Center to begin assessment of the first of the four 
battalions be deployed to Darfur.  The PDV team intended to 
assess training, equipment, and operational performance in 
the field.  Two more battalions will be evaluated October 9, 
and the final battalion October 10.  The team will conduct 
outbriefings also on October 10, and depart the 11th.
