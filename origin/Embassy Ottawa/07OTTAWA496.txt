SUBJECT: CANADA TO SUPPORT US POSITION ON PARTNERSHIP FOR

REF: STATE 28310 
 
(SBU) We delivered reftel demarche to Department of Foreign 
Affairs and International Trade (DFAIT) Human Security Policy 
Advisor Jeff Senior on March 9, and followed up with the 
Assistant Deputy Minister Drew Fagan on March 10.  Senior 
told us on March 14 that Canada was finalizing a "positive 
response" that would support the proposal to house the PDG at 
the OECD.  Senior said that Canadian officials would soon 
convey their detailed response directly to U.S. officials at 
the OECD.
