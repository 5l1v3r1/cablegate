SUBJECT: AECL REACTOR RESTARTED 
 
REF: OTTAWA 2260 AND PREVIOUS 
 
Atomic Energy of Canada Limited (AECL) has confirmed that its 
National Research Universal (NRU) reactor at Chalk River, 
Ontario, returned to service early Sunday (December 16) 
morning.  AECL anticipates that medical isotopes will begin 
to be removed from NRU for processing and distribution 
December 20.
