SUBJECT: CANADA SUPPORTS U.S. ITSO POSITION 
 
REF: STATE 68520 
 
(U) On July 7, in response to reftel points, Jim MacKenzie, 
Senior Policy Analyst, International Telecommunications 
Policy, Industry Canada, stated that Canada agrees with the 
United States and will oppose the call for arbitration. 
MacKenzie said Santiago Reyes-Borda, the head of the Canadian 
delegation and Director of International Telecommunications 
Policy and Coordination, has already met with Steve Lett and 
Doug May of the U.S. delegation to express support for the 
U.S. position.
