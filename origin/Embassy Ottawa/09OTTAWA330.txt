¶1. (U)  Embassy welcomes the visit of Staffdel Casey to 
Ottawa on April 30 and May 1 (reftel). 
 
¶2. (U)  The administrative point of contact is Deputy 
Political Counselor Kurt van der Walde, (o) 613-688-5242, BB 
613-698-5018, unclass email address: VanderwaldeK@state.gov 
 
¶3.  (U)  Staffdel has accommodations at the Fairmont Chateau 
Laurier, 1 Rideau Street, Ottawa, phone: 613-241-1414.  The 
room rate is C$228.06 and includes tax and breakfast.  The 
latest cancellation  without penalty is 6 pm on day of 
arrival. 
 
¶4.  (U)  Guidance on U.S. Government owned laptop computers 
and other electronic equipment (for visitors planning to 
bring computers into Mission Canada facilities): 
 
-- if not already addressed by the country clearance request, 
RSO and ISSO must be advised of and concur with proposed use 
of computers within our facilities; 
 
-- connection of laptop computers to Department of State 
systems is strictly prohibited; 
 
-- computers cleared for use in controlled access areas (CAA) 
must have proper classification markings and are to remain in 
place until post departure of visitor; computers to be used 
in CAA for period of more than 30 days must have their 
computer shipped my diplomatic courier; 
 
-- personally owned computers are not permitted within 
Mission Canada facilities; and, 
 
-- cellular phones and personal digital assistants (PDA) are 
prohibited in CAA areas. 
 
¶5.  (U)  Mission Canada posts are considered medium threat 
level for general crime, but all visitors are requested to 
maintain appropriate vigilance.  Official travelers are also 
encouraged to review security briefing materials available 
from the Regional Security Office.  Visitors should keep a 
low profile, be alert to signs of surveillance, and vary 
routes.
