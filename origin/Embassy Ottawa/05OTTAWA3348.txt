SUBJECT: PROPOSED AGENDA ITEMS FOR UK-HOSTED MEETING OF THE 
G-8 BIOTERRORISM EXPERTS GROUP (BTEX) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 203478 
 
¶1. (U) Post delivered ref points to Sebastien Beaulieu, 
Deputy Director for Counter-Terrorism in the International 
Crime and Terrorism Division (ICT) of Foreign Affairs Canada 
(FAC). While Canada will not/not be submitting any proposals 
for the December 12 BTEX meeting in London, Beaulieu thanked 
us for our efforts.  Canada looks forward to receiving USG 
proposals. 
 
¶2. (U) Beaulieu assured us that someone from his division 
would lead the Canadian delegation.  He undertook to 
enoourage the participation of other divisions and agencies, 
including FAC's nonproliferation office, Public Safety and 
Emergency Preparedness Canada (PSEPC) and Public Health 
Agemcy of Canada (PHAC). 
 
¶3. (U) Post will report any additional information, as 
appropriate.
