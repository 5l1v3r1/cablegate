SUBJECT: GEORGIA - ABKHAZIA AND SOUTH OSSETIA  PARTICIPATION IN UN 
DISCUSSIONS 
 
REF: STATE 100791 
 
Drawing on points contained in reftel, Charge d'affaires sought 
Libyan support to prevent Abkhazian and South Ossetian 
participation in UNSC meetings in a September 23 discussion with 
MFA Secretary for Americas Affairs Ahmed Fituri.  Fituri made no 
substantive response, but promised to inform appropriate 
officials of the USG position.  He noted that the head of the 
MFA's International Organization Affairs Department, Hindi 
Abdulatif Hindi, is currently in New York for the UNGA, and that 
FM Abderrahman Shalgham had not/not traveled to New York to head 
Libya's delegation to the UNGA as had been previously 
anticipated.
