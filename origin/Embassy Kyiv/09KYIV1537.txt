REFTELS: REQUEST #06210022; DATED 7/15/2009 
 
¶1. Unauthorized disclosure of the information provided below is 
prohibited by Section 12C of the Export Administration Act. 
 
¶2. Reftel requested a post-shipment verification to determine the 
legitimacy and reliability of the end-user, SATURN, KYIV, UKRAINE. 
The company is listed on BIS license application: 06210022 as the 
ultimate consignee of twelve (12) wideband MMIC VCO electronic 
integrated circuits.No ECCN given. The licensee is Hittite Microwave 
Corp., 20 Alpha Road, Chelmsford, MA 01824. 
 
¶3. On September 1st, 2009, CS Kyiv SCO Richard Steffens and LES 
Ruben Beliaev conducted the requested post-shipment verification at 
the offices of SATURN, KYIV, UKRAINE. The CS Kyiv team met with Mr. 
Yuri Muskin, Deputy Director General for Open Joint Stock Company 
Saturn. 
 
¶4. SATURN was created in 1968 and has 570 employees.  SATURN 
produces metrology test devices for radio relay, TV, satellite 
communications for the Ukrainian government agencies, China and CIS 
countries. SATURN participates in all of the major 
telecommunications exhibitions in Ukraine. SATURN deputy director 
advised the CS Kyiv team that SATURN imports mainly from these U.S. 
companies: Hittite Microwave Corp. SATURN deputy director stated 
that during the course of doing business with U.S. exporters of 
licensable dual use commodities, SATURN has become very familiar 
with export compliance procedures and requirements. 
 
¶5. SATURN has shown CS Kyiv team purchase order for the reftel 
commodity. The purchase order indicated that the reftel commodity 
was purchased through Ukrainian company Biacom Ltd. The terms and 
conditions were standard business practice. The purchase order shows 
that the end user of the reftel commodity is Saturn. 
 
¶6. The CS Kyiv team was shown reftel commodity and told that the 
reftel commodity will be used for the end use for which it was 
licensed. The commodity was at the consignee's facility as indicated 
in reftel. There were no indications of impropriety and the 
consignee's answers were not evasive. 
 
¶7. Recommendations: Post recommends SATURN, KYIV, UKRAINE, as a 
reliable recipient of sensitive U.S. origin commodities.
