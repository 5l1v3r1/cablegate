SUBJECT: OMAN PUBLIC STATEMENT SUPPORTS USG INITIATIVE TO 
IRAN 
 
REF: A. MUSCAT 868 
     ¶B. SECSTATE 87682 
 
¶1. (SBU) Per ref A assurance from FM Yusuf bin Alawi, the 
Omani Ministry of Foreign Affairs issued a public statement 
June 1 welcoming the USG's May 31 proposal to open 
conditional negotiations with Iran (ref B).  The statement, 
which follows, was reported by the government-owned Oman News 
Agency (ONA), and carried by all leading local media. 
 
¶2. (U) Begin informal translation of the ONA report: 
 
"The Sultanate has expressed its welcome to the new U.S. 
stand regarding direct participation in the talks between the 
European Troika and the Islamic Republic of Iran regarding 
the Iranian nuclear dossier.  The statement added that the 
Sultanate of Oman sees in the new U.S. stand an encouraging 
step towards starting a serious effort to find a diplomatic 
solution and reduce the tension with the Islamic Republic of 
Iran and work with it toward building an atmosphere of trust. 
 Building an atmosphere of trust will help find new 
directions towards resolving the Middle East crisis.  We are 
confident that the Islamic Republic of Iran will cooperate 
with the international efforts to reach a peaceful solution 
to the crisis."
