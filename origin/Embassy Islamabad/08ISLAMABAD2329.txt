Kamran Akhtar, Director of Disarmament at the MFA, concerning 
Pakistan's recognition of and partnership with Jordan and 
India as new partners in the Global Initiative to Combat 
Nuclear Terrorism (GI).  The information was well received, 
and the POC for GI at the MFA is Mr. Khalid Aziz Babar, 
Additional Secretary for United Nations and Economic 
Coordination, Office telephone: 092-051-920-5494.
