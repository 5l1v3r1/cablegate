On August 18, Andorran Foreign Minister Xavier Espot sent the 
Charge a diplomatic note accompanied by a draft Tax 
Information Exchange Agreement (TIEA) for transmission to 
U.S. authorities.  The Minister reiterated the GOA's interest 
(reported ref A and repeated in an August 6 call by the 
Minister to the Charge) in concluding a TIEA with the U.S. 
Post has forwarded the draft text to State/EUR/WE and 
Treasury/OTP and requests guidance for a response.
