¶1.   POST WISHES TO SUBMIT AND/OR UPDATE THE FOLLOWING

¶2.   DAILY PRESS OF JUNE 16, 2005 REPORTED THAT THE 
  SPANISH POLICE ARRESTED FIVE MOROCCAN NATIONALS IN 
  BARCELONA AND MADRID, FOR THEIR ALLEGED LINKS TO THE 
  MADRID 11 MARCH 2004 TERRORIST ATTACKS. INVESTIGATIONS 
  BEING CONDUCTED ON PERPETRATORS PRESENTLY AT LARGE, LED 
  TO THE ARREST OF THE FOLLOWING INDIVIDUALS: 
 
¶3.   NAME: EL IDRISSI, MOHAMED 
 
  -    GENDER: MALE 
-    DPOB: SEPT. 1, 1976, BENI AMARTE, MOROCCO 
-    CLASS: POSITIVE 
-    ARRESTED IN VILLAVERDE, MADRID, SPAIN 
-    EL IDRISSI HELPED THE MARCH 11 PEPETRATORS WITH 
LOGISTICS AND AIDED THE ESCAPE OF SOME OF THEM. HE 
PURCHASED A MOBILE TELEPHONE FOR AHMED AFALAH, FATHER 
OF MOHAMED AFALAH, A SUSPECT WHO MANAGED TO ESCAPE AND 
WAS SMUGGLED OUT OF SPAIN TO BELGIUM.  MOHAMED AFALAH 
CALLED HIS FATHER FROM SYRIA AND LATER FROM IRAQ TO 
INFORM HIM OF HIS DECISION TO COMMIT A SUICIDE ATTACK 
IN IRAQ.  ACCORDING TO INVESTIGATORS HE MAY HAVE 
CARRIED OUT A SUICIDE ATTACK BETWEEN MAY 12-19 OF 2005. 
 
¶4.   NAME: ESSABAR, ABDENERI 
 
  -    GENDER: MALE 
-    DPOB: MARCH 31, 1965, FEZ, MOROCCO 
-    CLASS: POSITIVE 
-    ARRESTED IN MADRID 
-    ALSO ACCUSED OF COLLABORATING WITH TERRORISTS. 
 
¶5.   OTHER ARRESTED WILL BE REPORTED IN SEPTEL BY
