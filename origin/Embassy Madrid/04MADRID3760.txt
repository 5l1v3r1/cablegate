¶1.   THE VISAS VIPER COMMITTEE MET ON SEP. 27, 2004, AS PART 
  OF THE MADRID INTERAGENCY COUNTERTERRORISM WORKING GROUP AND 
  WAS CHAIRED BY THE CHARGE, ROBERT MANZANARES.  THE CHARGE 
  EXPRESSED HIS APPRECIATION FOR THE CONTINUING STRONG 
  COOPERATION AND COMMITMENT TO THE PROGRAM BY ALL AGENCIES AT 
  POST. 
 
¶2.   DURING THE MONTH OF SEPTEMBER 2004, MADRID SUBMITTED 
  FIVE VISAS VIPER CABLES IDENTIFYING SEVENTEEN INDIVIDUALS, 
  TWO SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ETA AND FIFTEEN SUSPECTED MEMBERS 
  OF ISLAMIST TERRORIST GROUPS LINKED TO AL-QAEDA. 
 
¶3.   CABLES REFERENCE NUMBER: MADRID 03489, 03490, 03491, 
  03639, 03728. 
 
¶4.   IN ADDITION, MADRID HAS SUBMITTED ONE TIPPIX MEMORANDUM 
  CONTAINING ONE PHOTOGRAPH OF A TERRORIST SUSPECT. 
 
MANZANARES
