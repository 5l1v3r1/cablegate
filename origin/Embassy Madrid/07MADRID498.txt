SUBJECT: SPAIN STILL SUPPORTS U.S.-EU OPEN SKIES PLUS 
AGREEMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 29781 
 
¶1. (SBU) EconCouns reconfirmed on March 15 that the GOS will 
vote in favor of the U.S.-EU Open Skies Plus Agreement. 
Civil aviation authorities also noted to EconOff on March 15 
that Spain will do everything possible to push the UK to take 
a more conciliatory approach. 
 
¶2. (U) Iberia Airlines remains a fervent supporter of the 
Agreement.
