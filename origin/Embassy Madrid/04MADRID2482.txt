¶2.   DAILY "EL PAIS" OF JUNE 10, 2004, REPORTED THAT 
  FRENCH AUTHORITIES EXTRADITED THE ETA MEMBER, JUAN LUIS 
  AGUIRRE LETE TO SPAIN, AFTER SERVING A SENTENCE IN 
  FRANCE FOR CONVICTED FELONS ASSOCIATION FOR TERRORIST 
  PURPOSES.  HE WAS PREVIOUSLY TEMPORARILY EXTRADITED TO 
  SPAIN FROM AUG. 2002 UNTIL FEB. 2003, TO RESPOND IN THE 
  PENDING INDICTMENTS FOR THE MURDER OF SPANISH SOCIALIST 
  PARTY LEADER FERNANDO MUGICA, AND SPANISH ARMY GENERAL 
  DIONISIO HERRERO.  AT THAT TIME, AGUIRRE LETE RECEIVED 
  A 30 AND 66 AND A HALF YEARS SENTENCES RESPECTIVELY. 
  (MAXIMUM TO BE SERVED IN SPAIN IS 30 YEARS).  THIS 
  TIME, AGUIRRE LETE WILL BE TRIED FOR HIS PARTICIPATION 
  IN THE HIJACKING OF PRISON OFFICER JOSE ANTONIO ORTEGA 
  LARA.
