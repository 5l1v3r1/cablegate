SUBJECT: SPAIN: UN COMMISSION ON THE STATUS OF WOMEN (CSW) 
-- RESOLUTION ON AFGHAN WOMEN 
 
REF: STATE 9810 
 
Poloff delivered reftel talking points to Antonio Garcia 
Roger, MFA Assistant to the Deputy Director General of Human 
Rights on January 18.  Garcia Roger transmitted our position 
on a resolution on Afghan women to Jose Antonio de Ory Peral, 
MFA Deputy Director General of Human Rights, via telephone 
and fax.  De Ory Peral was attending EU Council Working Group 
on Human Rights (COHOM) meetings in Brussels and did not have 
an immediate comment.  He promised to get back to us later on 
Spain's views.
