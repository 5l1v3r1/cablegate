(SBU) On June 5, poloff delivered reftel demarche regarding 
the U.S.-nominated candidate for the United Nations 
International Civil Service Commission (ICSC) election, to 
take place during the 64th UN General Assembly in October or 
November 2009, to Spanish MFA Subdirector General for the UN 
Marcelino Cabanas.  Mr. Cabanas responded that he is 
personally familiar with Ms. Myers' qualifications, but that 
Spain has not yet made a decision as to its vote.
