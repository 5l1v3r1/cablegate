¶2. DAILY PRESS OF APR. 16, 2005, REPORTED THAT THE 
SPANISH NATIONAL COURT EXAMINING JUDGE FOR THE MARCH 
11TH TERRORIST ATTACKS, ORDERED THE UNCONDITIONAL 
IMPRISONMENT OF SOUFIANE RAIFAK, MOROCCAN CITIZEN. 
RAFIAK WAS PREVIOUSLY ARRESTED AND QUESTIONED FOR HIS 
ALLEGED INVOLVEMENT IN THE MARCH 11TH TERRORIST 
ATTACKS, FOR WHICH HE PLEADED INNOCENT, ALTHOUGH HE 
ADMITTED TO OTHER CRIMINAL ACTIVITIES.  ADDITIONAL 
INFORMATION HAS REVEALED THAT RAIFAK IS ASSOCIATED WITH 
SOME OF THE MARCH 11TH PERPETRATORS AND PLAYED A KEY 
ROLE IN THE PURCHASE OF THE EXPLOSIVES USED IN THE 
ATTACKS.  RAIFAK HAS AN EXTENSIVE RECORD FOR MULTIPLE 
STREET CRIMES, SUCH AS ROBBERIES AND DRUG TRAFFICKING, 
AND WAS PRESENTLY BEING RETAINED BY POLICE PENDING A 
COURT ORDER FOR HIS DEPORTATION TO MOROCCO.
