  JUNE 2004, POST HAS RECEIVED INFORMATION FROM THE 
  SPANISH AUTHORITIES INDICATING AS THE POSSIBLE 
  IDENTIFICATION OF FORMER BARCELONA-BASED EXTREMIST 
  ABDORRAHMAN AS MOROCCAN NATIONAL FILALI OUALI.  SPANISH 
  AUTHORITIES BELIEVE THAT KHALAD ABIDI, AKA "ABBAS", 
  ASSOCIATE ABDORRAHMAN WHO FLED SPAIN FOR THE UNITED 
  KINGDOM (UK) CIRCA MAY 1, 2004, MAY BE IDENTIFIABLE 
  WITH MOROCCAN NATIONAL OUALI, FILALI, DPOB: FEB. 15, 
  1976, TANGIERS, MOROCCO, HOLDER OF SPANISH FOREIGNERS 
  IDENTIFICATION CARD NUMBER X04583773.  OUALI'S LAST 
  KNOWN RESIDENCE WAS CALLE MARQUES DE SANT MORI, 106, 
  FLOOR 4, APARTMENT 4A, BADALONA, BARCELONA, SPAIN.  THE 
  SPANISH POLICE CONTINUE THEIR INVESTIGATION OF THE 
  BARCELONA-BASED CELL ASSOCIATED WITH ABIDI AND 
  ABDORRAHMAN.
