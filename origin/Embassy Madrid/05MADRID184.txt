SUBJECT: SPAIN:  UN COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS (CHR), 
VARIOUS RESOLUTIONS 
 
REF: STATE 9812 
 
We delivered reftel talking points to Antonio Garcia Roger, 
MFA Assistant to the Deputy Director General of Human Rights 
on January 18.  Garcia Roger transmitted talking points on 
resolutions to come up during the spring 2005 Commission on 
Human Rights meeting by fax and telephone to MFA Deputy 
Director General of Human Rights Jose Antonio de Ory Peral. 
De Ory Peral was attending EU Council Working Group on Human 
Rights (COHOM) meetings in Brussels and did not have an 
immediate comment.  He promised to notify us of Spanish 
positions at a later date.
