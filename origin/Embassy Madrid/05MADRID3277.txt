SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST FOR TRAVEL OF TSA 
OFFICIAL 
 
 
 ¶1.  The Transportation Security Administration (TSA) 
requests country clearance for the travel of Christine Sarkes 
Sasseville, International Program Analyst, International 
Programs and Multilateral Liaison, for travel to Rabat and 
Casablanca for the dates of 24-28 September 2005. 
 
¶2.  The purpose of travel is to meet with the Director 
General of the Arab Civil Aviation Commission (ACAC), Mr. 
Mohammed El Alj, in Rabat, to discuss International Civil 
Aviation Organization (ICAO) and USG support for an ICAO/ACAC 
sponsored conference of civil aviation experts to be held in 
Abu Dhabi in February 2006.  Mr. Jalal Haidar, ICAO Regional 
Officer, Aviation Security, Middle east and Africa, will 
travel separately to facilitate the meeting. 
 
¶3.  No post assistance is required.  Following are traveler's 
particulars: 
    Christine Sarkes Sasseville 
    International Program Analyst 
    TSA/International Programs/Multilateral Liaison 
    Official Passport Number 801993121 
    Security clearance:  Secret 
 
¶4.  TSA greatly appreciates post support.  If post requires 
further coordination, please contact Christine Sasseville at 
571-227-1021.
