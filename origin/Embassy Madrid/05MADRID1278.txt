SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER MONTHLY REPORT: FY05 - MARCH 
 
REF: STATE 058520 
 
¶1.   THE VISAS VIPER COMMITTEE MET ON 04 APRIL 2005, AS PART 
  OF MADRID INTERAGENCY COUNTERTERRORISM WORKING GROUP AND WAS 
  CHAIRED BY THE CHARGE, ROBERT MANZANARES.  THE CHARGE 
  EXPRESSED HIS APPRECIATION FOR THE CONTINUING STRONG 
  COOPERATION AND COMMITMENT TO THE PROGRAM BY ALL AGENCIES AT 
  POST. 
 
¶2.   DURING THE MONTH OF MARCH 2005, MADRID SUBMITTED 
  THIRTEEN VISAS VIPER CABLE IDENTIFYING TWENTY-SEVEN 
  INDIVIDUALS, TWENTY-TWO SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ETA AND FIVE 
  SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ISLAMIST TERRORIST GROUPS LINKED TO AL- 
  QAEDA. 
 
¶3.   CABLES REFERENCE NUMBER: MADRID 0858, 0925, 0979, 0981, 
  01011, 01025, 01179, 01181, 01182, 01183, 01187, 01203, 
  ¶01204. 
 
¶4.   IN ADDITION, POST HAS SUBMITTED FIVE TIPPIX MEMORANDUMS 
  CONTAINING TWENTY-FOUR PHOTOGRAPHS OF SUSPECTS.
