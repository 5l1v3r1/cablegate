SUBJECT: SPANISH CIO FINDS WAY TO INCLUDE 2 NGOS IN OSCE 
MEETING 
 
REF: SECSTATE 39276 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel points on the importance of 
including two NGOs in the 29-30 March meeting of the OSCE 
Supplementary Human Dimension meeting to MFA OSCE 
Chairmanship coordinator Ambassador Jose Angel Lopez Jorrin 
on March 28.  Lopez Jorrin consulted, then replied that Spain 
has resolved the situation.  Both the Russian-Chechen 
Friendship Society and the Turkmenistan Helsinki Initiative 
groups will attend.  Lopez Jorrin said that Spain fully 
agrees that the Turkmenistan group should participate.  The 
Friendship Society will be able to participate by virtue of 
its Finnish NGO registration. 
 
¶2. (SBU) On a different issue, Lopez Jorrin said that the 
tentative dates for the OSCE Chairmanship conference related 
to Islam and tolerance issues in Cordoba this year are 9-10 
October.
