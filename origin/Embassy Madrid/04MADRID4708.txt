¶2.   DAILY SPANISH PRESS "EL PAIS" OF DEC. 10, 2004 
  REPORTED THAT THE FOLLOWING INDIVIDUALS WERE ARRESTED 
  BY THE SPANISH POLICE ON DEC. 8, 2004, IN THE SPANISH- 
  FRENCH BORDER LOCATION OF IRUN, GUIPUZCOA, SPAIN. 
  POLICE INVESTIGATION OF MOBILE TELEPHONES AND SIM CARDS 
  USED BY THE PERPERTRATORS OF THE MARCH 11TH TERRORIST 
  ATTACKS REVEALED THAT THE ARRESTED ARE CLOSELY RELATED 
  TO AT LEAST FIVE OF THEM.  DURING THE ARREST POLICE 
  SEIZED A LARGE VOLUME OF DOCUMENTATION WHICH IS BEING 
  ANALYZED.  ACCORDING TO THE POLICE, THE ARRESTED ARE 
  ALSO SUSPECTED OF COLLECTING FUNDS FOR JIHAD AND OF 
  PROVIDING EXTREMISTS WITH FALSE IDENTIFICATION AND 
  OTHER DOCUMENTS.
