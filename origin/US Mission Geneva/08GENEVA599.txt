SUBJECT: JCIC-XXXII: OTHER PARTIES' DELEGATION LISTS 
 
¶1.  This is JCIC-XXXII-003. 
 
¶2.  Paragraphs 3-5 below contain the texts of the official 
translations of the delegation lists of the Belarusian, 
Russian and Ukrainian Delegations to JCIC-XXXII.  The 
Kazakhstani Delegation did not arrive until July 18, 2008 and 
no delegation list was provided.
