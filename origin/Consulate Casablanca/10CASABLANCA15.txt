SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR JANUARY 2010 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met February 1, 2010.  The meeting was 
chaired by DCM with CG, CONS, RAO, LEGATT, DHS, DAO, RSO, POL, ECON, 
and PD in attendance.  Discussion centered on implementation of new 
requirements in the Visas Viper program in the light of the 
terrorist attempt on Christmas 2009. 
 
¶2. Casablanca has the following submission for the January 2010 
reporting period: 
 
OUDRISS, Jamal, born 24 July 1987 in Oujda, Morocco, PPT T78443. 
Father's name Ahmed Oudriss, mother's name Maghnia.  Oudriss 
departed February 5, 2006 for Syria and is suspected of travelling 
to support fighting against coalition forces in Iraq.  More 
information has been reported septel.
