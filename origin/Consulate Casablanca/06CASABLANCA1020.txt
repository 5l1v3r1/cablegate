SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR AUGUST 2006 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1. (U) Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met September 5, 2006.  The meeting was 
chaired by the Acting DCM and attended by representatives from CONS, 
RAO, RSO, POL, ECON, PAS, DAO and LEGATT.
