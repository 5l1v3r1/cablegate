REF:  HQ ICE IAO 291014Z OCT 07, HQ ICE IAO 051732Z MAR 08 
 
¶1. Below please find details of Embassy Jakarta's nominees for the 
South East Asia Regional Bulk Cash Smuggling Workshop to be held in 
Semarang, Indonesia on April 21-24, 2008, jointly funded with 
Australia.  If you require additional information on the nominee's 
educational or work history, their CVs are available on request. 
Embassy Jakarta awaits fund cite cable to prepare travel arrangement 
and disburse per diem for participants from the Attorney General's 
Office, the Indonesian National Police, the Immigration Office, and 
the Counter Terrorism Coordination Desk.  Australia will fund the 
remaining participants, from Customs and the Financial Intelligence 
Unit.
