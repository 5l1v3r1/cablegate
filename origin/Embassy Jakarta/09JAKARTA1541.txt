SUBJECT: INDONESIAN RESPONSE ON NUCLEAR SECURITY SUMMIT 
KICKOFF MEETING 
 
REF: 100338Z SEP 09 WHITE HOUSE 
 
Per reftel, Dep Pol/C contacted Roy Soemirat of the 
Department of Foreign Affairs Directorate for International 
Security and Disarmament to request that the GOI select a 
Sherpa to attend the November 2009 and February 2010 Nuclear 
Security Summit preparatory meetings.  Soemirat said 
Indonesia had not yet selected a Sherpa.  He noted 
Indonesia's commitment to the goals of the upcoming summit 
and said he would pass the non-paper message on the Vienna 
Kickoff Meeting to the Indonesian delegation at the 
International Atomic Energy Agency General Conference.  The 
delegation is led by Vice Foreign Minister Triyono Wibowo.
