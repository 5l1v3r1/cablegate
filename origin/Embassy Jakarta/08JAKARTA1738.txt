SUBJECT: COMMUNITY OF DEMOCRACIES:  U.S. PRIORITIES 
 
REF: SECSTATE 97313 
 
¶1.  (U) On September 13, Dep/Pol/C discussed U.S. priorities 
for the upcoming meeting of the Community of Democracies with 
Cicilia Rusdiharini, Deputy Director for North American 
Affairs at the Department of Foreign Relations.  Dep/Pol/C 
emphasized that the United States took the CD dialogue 
seriously and urged that DEPLU consider U.S. positions 
carefully.  Dep/Pol/C left a lightly edited version of reftel 
discussion as a nonpaper. 
 
¶2.  (U) Rusdiharini welcomed the U.S. points and said that 
she would convey the U.S. positions to the offices that would 
represent Indonesia in the September 26 meeting on the 
margins of the United Nations General Assembly.
