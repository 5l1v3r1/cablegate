SUBJECT: AUSTRALIAN DEMARCHE TO IRANIANS URGING THE RELEASE 
OF DETAINED AMERICAN, MS SABERI 
 
REF: A. A. STATE 30046 
     ¶B. B. CANBERRA 00326 
 
Classified By: Clasified by: Political Counselor, James F. Cole, for re 
asons 1.4 (b) and (d) 
 
 ¶1. (S/NF) Further to Ref B, April 2, Hugh Jeffry, Director 
of Iran Section,  Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade 
told Poloff that, in response to Ref A request,  the GOA had 
delivered a message to the Iranian ambassador, Mahmoud 
Movahhedi, in Canberra requesting the freeing of the American 
citizen,  Ms Roxana Saberi,  for humanitarian reasons. 
¶2. (S/NF) Ambassador Movahhedi accepted the message and 
promised to return a formal response in the coming days. He 
also mentioned that he had been informed by Tehran that Ms 
Saberi was to be visited in prison by her parents on 
April 2. The Iranian response will be provided septel.
