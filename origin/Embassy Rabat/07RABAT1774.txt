SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT 
NUCLEAR TERRORISM - NONPAPER ON DETECTION ARCHITECTURE 
MODEL GUIDELINES DOCUMENT. 
 
REF: STATE 15662 
 
Sensitive But Unclassified.  Protect Accordingly. 
 
(1)  (SBU) On November 21, Poloff delivered reftel 
demarche--and accompanying non-paper--concerning the U.S. 
proposal for the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear 
Terrorism (GI) to develop a Nuclear Detection Architecture 
Model to the Moroccan MFA's Head of the United Nations 
Division Azzedine Farhane. 
 
 
(2)  (SBU) Farhane welcomed the U.S. initiative and said that 
the MFA would review the proposal favorably and get back 
Poloff with any comments/amendments on the non-paper. 
Farhane asked that the U.S. table the plan of the Global 
Nuclear Detection Architecture proposal, and associated 22 
January meeting in Washington D.C., at the next GI seminar 
being held in Rabat on 5-7 February for endorsement by the GI 
members.
