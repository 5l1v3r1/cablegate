SUBJECT: MOROCCO: CLARIFICATION OF ACTIVITIES AT THE U.S. 
NEVADA TEST SITE 
 
REF: STATE 52643 
 
(SBU) Per reftel, Poloff met April 13 with Abdelatif Sadafi, 
MFA Legal Affairs Division officer, to deliver diplomatic 
note #111 providing clarification concerning a forthcoming 
large high explosive experiment at the U.S. Nevada test site. 
 Sadafi thanked poloff for the embassy's transparency on this 
issue and promised to pass the diplomatic note to the 
appropriate authorities at the National Center for Scientific 
and Technical Research (CNRST).  He pledged to relay to 
poloff any questions or concerns from CNRST.  Post will 
report any reaction septel.
