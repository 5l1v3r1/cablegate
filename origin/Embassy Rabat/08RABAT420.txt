SUBJECT: MOROCCO CONSIDERS U.S. REQUEST FOR SHAPING OSLO 
PROCESS DRAFT TEXT 
 
REF: STATE 47101 
 
 On May 9, 2008, Poloff provided Moroccan Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs Counselor to the Director General for Bilateral 
Relations Karim Halim with a summary of the U.S. position on 
the ongoing Oslo process and encouraged the Government of 
Morocco to support reftel recommended amendments to the Oslo 
process draft text on cluster bombs.  Halim said that he 
would pass the information to relevant members of his 
ministry and give it full consideration.
