SUBJECT: REALLOCATIONS OF GWOT FUNDS

Sensitive but Unclassified.  Please Protect Accordingly. 
 
Poloff informed MFA Bilateral and Counterterrorism Affairs 
Officer Karim Halim of the reallocation of $1.8 million in 
funds previously programmed for border control training and 
equipment (reftel) during an October 20 meeting.  Halim 
acknowledged the notice and affirmed that Morocco looks 
forward to continued cooperation with the USG in other CT and 
non-proliferation activities.
