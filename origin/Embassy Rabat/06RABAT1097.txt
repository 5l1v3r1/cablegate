SUBJECT: MOROCCO: DEMARCHE FOR PROFESSOR WEDGWOOD'S 
RE-ELECTION TO U.N. HUMAN RIGHTS COMMITTEE 
 
REF: STATE 85722 
 
¶1.  Per reftel, poloff delivered diplomatic note number 183 
on June 6 to MFA Director of UN and International 
Organizations Affairs Nasser Bourita requesting GOM support 
for the Ruth Wedgwood's re-election to the U.N. Human Rights 
Committee.  Poloff also left a copy of Wedgwood's CV.  The 
diplomatic note had previously been sent to the MFA on May 
¶31.  While he could make no immediate commitment, Bourita 
pledged to carefully consider the issue of GOM support for 
Wedgwood.
