¶2. (SBU) On December 28, econoff passed points coveyed in 
reftel to Abdellatif Bendahane, Director of African Affairs, 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Bendahane was unaware of the 
Liberia forum, but spoke very positively about it.  He added 
that Morocco was planning to send an advisory team to Liberia 
within the next three months to discuss electricity and 
microcredit cooperate endeavors that would possibly lead to a 
visit by King Mohammed VI later in 2007.  Bendahane promised 
to circulate information about the forum to appropriate GOM 
officials, and said he would most likely lead Morocco's 
delegation.  Econoff supplied Bendahane copies of the forum's 
announcement, invitation letter, and agenda.
