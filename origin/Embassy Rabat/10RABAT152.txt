SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: MANAGING PART ONE OF THE 
CONFERENCE ON DISARMAMENT 
 
REF: STATE 13698 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche on February 22 to MFA 
Head of the United Nations Division Redouane Hussaini. 
Hussaini stated that Morocco, like the U.S., would like to 
obtain agreement on the program of work before the end of the 
current Conference on Disarmament session.  Morocco likewise 
views progress on the program of work as important toward 
creating a positive, productive atmosphere ahead of the 
Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty Review Conference in May, he 
said.  According to Hussaini, Morocco currently is holding 
consultations with several members of the Conference on 
Disarmament in Geneva and would like to see the negotiations 
for the Fissile Material Cutoff Treaty commence.
