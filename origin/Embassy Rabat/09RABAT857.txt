(SBU) Poloff delivered ref A demarche to Moroccan Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs Head of Security and Disarmament Bouchaib 
Eloumni October 14.  Eloumni stated that Morocco plans to 
attend both the Sous-SHERPAS meeting in Washington, DC in 
November and the SHERPAS meeting in Tokyo, Japan in December. 
 Moroccan attendees will be from the Moroccan Embassies in 
Washington and Tokyo, respectively, unless otherwise 
notified.  POC for these events will be Eloumni, email: 
eloumni@maec.gov.ma. Poloff also notified Eloumni of 
approaching deadline for submitting suggestions to the 
Possible Summit Outcomes paper per ref A.  Eloumni replied 
that he would look into this.
