¶1.  (SBU) On March 4, Post delivered REFTEL letter from Acting 
National Marine Fisheries Service (NMFS) Assistant Administrator 
James Balsiger to Aldemir Gregolin, Brazil's Minister of Aquiculture 
and Fishing.  Post followed up by informing Osvaldo Barbossa, the 
International Affairs Advisor for the Secretariat of Aquiculture and 
Fishing, and Fernando Coimbra, the Director of the Ministry of 
Exterior Relations' Environment Division. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) On March 12, Barbossa provided an update, saying that the 
Brazilian government has been studying the matter and would be 
preparing its response.  When Post receives a response it will pass 
on to Washington.
