SUBJECT: BRAZIL PREFERS SUSPENSION OF ISS ARRANGEMENT 
 
 
Â¶1.  Post received a Diplomatic Note from Brazil's Foreign Ministry 
on June 29, 2007, referring to the Government of Brazil's position 
about the bilateral International Space Station Arrangement (ISS). 
The Diplomatic Note is as follows: 
 
"The Ministry of External Relations complements the United States of 
America Embassy and has the honor to refer to Diplomatic Note number 
189 dated May 23, 2007, in which the Embassy transmits the desire of 
the Government of the United States of America to terminate or 
suspend the Complementary Adjustment in relation to the Brazilian 
participation in the project and construction of the International 
Space Station. 
 
2)  Regarding this matter, the Ministry of External Relations 
transmits the Government of Brazil's position of agreeing to the 
suspension of the Complementary Adjustment in reference to the 
Brazilian participation in the International Space Station, with the 
objective of evaluating future opportunities of additional 
cooperation in this area."
