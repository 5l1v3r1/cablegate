SUBJECT:  BRAZIL- DEMARCHE DELIVERED -OFAC NARCOTICS KINGPIN ACT 
DESIGNATION OF THREE SENIOR VENEZUELAN OFFICIALS 
 
REFTEL  A) State 97316 B) Brasilia 1226 
 
¶1. (SBU) On September 18, 2008 ECONOFF delivered reftel demarche to 
the Council for the Control of Financial Activities (COAF) at the 
Ministry of Finance.  COAF officials Joaquim da Cunha Neto and 
Bernardo Mota took delivery of the document, which outlined the 
reasons behind the designation of three senior Venezuelan officials 
under the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin Designation Act for materially 
assisting the narcotics trafficking activities of the Revolutionary 
Armed Forces of Colombia. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) COAF officials explained that they would continue to 
cooperate with the U.S. government at a technical level on issues of 
illicit financing and narcotics trafficking.  However, COAF 
officials cautioned that, given the political implications of the 
matter, any decision to proceed against the Venezuelan officials 
would have to be made at the highest levels of the Brazilian 
government.
