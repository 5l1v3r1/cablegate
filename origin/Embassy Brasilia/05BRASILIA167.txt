(S) Emboff delivered Reftel prenotification of USG intent to 
nominate Muhsin Fadhi and Sulayman Khalid Darwish to the UNSC 
1267 list to Luiza Lopes da Silva of the Foreign Ministry's 
Office for the Combat of Illicit International Activities and 
to Joaquim Cunha of Brazil's Financial Intelligence Unit 
(COAF).  Cunha undertook to search COAF's databases and share 
the list with relevant GoB entities that work with COAF, 
including the Central Bank and intelligence agency (ABIN), 
for them to conduct similar searches.  Da Silva likewise 
promised to follow up.
