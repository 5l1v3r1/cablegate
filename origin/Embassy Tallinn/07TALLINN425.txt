(SBU) On June 19, we delivered reftel points to Riho 
Kruuv, MFA Director for Strategic Goods and Export 
Controls, Marin Mottus, MFA Director for the Middle 
East, and Reen Liivat, MFA U.S. Desk Officer, to notify 
the GOE of four additional designated Iranian entities 
and two individuals under E.O 13382 ("Blocking the 
Property of Weapons of Mass Destruction Proliferators 
and their Supporters").  Kruuv thanked us for the 
information and said that the GOE would forward any 
information that comes its way on any of these entities 
or individuals.
