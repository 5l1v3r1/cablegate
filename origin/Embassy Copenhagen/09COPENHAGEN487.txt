SUBJECT: PIRACY OFF SOMALIA:  WORKING GROUP 2 INVITATION, 
NOV. 26-27 IN COPENHAGEN 
 
REF: EMAIL BELL-LANDSIDLE 10/29/2009 
 
¶1.  Embassy has received an invitation for the United States 
to send a delegation to the fourth meeting of The Working 
Group on Legal Issues (WG2) of the Contact Group on Piracy 
off the Coast of Somalia (CGPCS), to take place on November 
26-27 at the Radisson SAS Scandinavia Hotel in Copenhagen. 
The invitation is from WG2 Chair Ambassador Thomas Winkler, 
who is MFA's Under-Secretary for Legal Affairs. 
 
¶2.  Delegations are asked to limit their participation to a 
maximum of three delegates, and to inform MFA about their 
participation no later than November 11.  Point of contact is 
given as Ms. Mette Juul Trepka, email:  mettre(at)um.dk, with 
cc's requested to tironi(at)um.dk and sabteg(at)um.dk.  For 
its part, Embassy would appreciate earliest possible 
notification of arrivals/departures and any support requested. 
 
¶3.  The invitation with accompanying information packet has 
been been emailed to Jennifer Landsidle of L.
