Office of UN Affairs, on August 1 regarding the Egyptian 
delegation to the 62nd UN General Assembly.  Refaat said that 
plans are still being made, but provided the following 
preliminary information on the Egyptian delegation: 
 
¶A. Attendance of the Head of State. 
 
-- No Head of State attendance. 
 
¶B. Attendance of the Head of Government. 
 
-- No Head of Government attendance. 
 
¶C. Attendance of the Foreign Minister. 
 
-- Ahmed Aboul Gheit, Minister of Foreign Affairs 
 
-- Arrival and departure dates not yet known 
 
-- Spouse travel not yet known, but she usually does not 
attend. 
 
-- No interpretation necessary

-- Not yet known

Relations 
 
-- Arrival and departure dates in New York not yet known
