SUBJECT: EGYPT TO BACK USG ON SINGLE MEMBER STATE VETO 
ISSUE IN GENEVA 
 
REF: STATE 197285 
 
Econoff today spoke with the Director of International 
Relations for the GOE Ministry of Communications and 
Information Technology, per reftel demarche, asking whether 
the GOE will support the U.S. position on the Single Member 
State Veto issue at the Telecommunication Standardization 
Advisory Group meeting in Geneva next week.  The Director 
confirmed that the GOE plans to send a representative from 
the National Telecom Regulatory Authority to Geneva, who will 
be prepared, in accordance with the demarche request, to back 
the U.S. position.
