SUBJECT: EGYPTIAN ATTENDANCE AT THE JUNE 23 CONFERENCE ON 
RECONSTRUCTION OF LEBANON'S NAHR AL-BARID REFUGEE CAMP 
 
REF: SECSTATE 60216 
 
¶1. (SBU) We delivered points in reftel June 9 to Ayman 
Zeineldeen, Deputy Assistant Minister for Arab Affairs. 
Zeineldeen confirmed that Egypt would attend the June 23rd 
conference in Vienna on reconstruction of Lebanon's Nahr 
al-Barid refugee camp.  In a June 11 meeting, MFA Senior 
Advisor Hossam Zaki told DCM that the Egyptian delegation 
would "probably" be led by Minister for International 
Cooperation Faisa Aboul Naga. 
 
¶2. (U) We will advise when the Egyptian delegation is 
finalized.
