SUBJECT: ALBANIA ON DRAFT UNSC RESOLUTION ON LEBANON 
 
REF: STATE 128442 
 
(SBU) Per reftel, Ambassador delivered demarche urging 
support for the U.S.-French draft UNSC resolution on Lebanon 
to Deputy Foreign Minister (responsible for multilateral 
affairs) Edith Harxhi.  Harxhi offered the full support of 
the GoA for the draft resolution and the international 
community's efforts generally to resolve the crisis.  Harxhi 
expressed particular interest in the resolution's 
humanitarian aspects.  Ambassador noted that the draft 
resolution's call for "the international community to take 
immediate steps to extend its financial and humanitarian 
assistance to the Lebanese people" was significant as it 
would have the authority of the Security Council.
