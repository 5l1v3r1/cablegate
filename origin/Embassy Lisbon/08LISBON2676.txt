SUBJECT: GOP NOT ATTENDING IRAN'S UPCOMING NUCLEAR 
CONFERENCE ON 30 NOVEMBER 2008 
 
REF: SECSTATE 112229 
 
Portugal shares U.S. concerns about Iran's upcoming nuclear 
conference as outlined in reftel and will not participate in 
Iran's event.  MFA Deputy Director for Disarmament and 
Security Joao Lago added that Portugal's Former President 
Jorge Sampaio will attend in his capacity as the UN High 
Representative on the Alliance of Civilizations, but stressed 
that Sampaio would not be representing Portugal in any way.
