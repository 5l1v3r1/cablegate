SUBJECT: PRC RESPONDS TO INDONESIA EARTHQUAKE 
 
 
China provided Indonesia with USD 2 million in cash, RMB 10 
million in materials and equipment and an emergency 
assistance team in response to the May 27 earthquake, MFA 
Asian Affairs Department Deputy Division Director He Wei told 
poloff on May 31.  The 44 person team, which took a chartered 
plane to the affected area, consists mostly of doctors and 
nurses along with medical equipment and supplies.  The team 
included "specialists" sent to assist the Indonesians 
estimate the damage and the rebuilding costs.  He Wei said he 
expects China to play a role in the reconstruction phase, 
following this initial immediate response to the humanitarian 
needs.  The PRC Embassy has not received any reports of death 
or injury of PRC citizens or overseas Chinese.
