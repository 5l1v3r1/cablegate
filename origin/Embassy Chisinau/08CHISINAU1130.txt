SUBJECT:  CONGRATULATORY MESSAGE FOR PRESIDENT- 
ELECT OBAMA FROM PRESIDENT VORONIN OF MOLDOVA 
 
REF:STATE 118192 
 
¶1. (U) Embassy has received the following press 
release from the Office of President Vladimir 
Voronin to President-elect Barack Obama. 
 
¶2. (U) Begin text: 
 
Press Service of the President of the Republic of 
Moldova 
Press release 
 
The President of the Republic of Moldova, Vladimir 
Voronin, congratulated Barack Obama on the 
occasion of his election as President of the 
United States of America. 
 
The message says: 
 
"On behalf of the Moldovan people and on my own 
behalf, I would like to express to you the warmest 
congratulations on the occasion of the brilliant 
victory gained during the election for the high 
position of President of the United States. 
 
I carefully followed the development of the 
electoral campaign in the United States. I must 
say that the truly historical choice made by the 
American people is a choice in favor of change, 
progress and prosperity, based on the fundamental 
principles of democracy. 
 
I avail myself of this occasion to reaffirm the 
willingness of the Republic of Moldova to further 
strengthen and deepen friendship and cooperative 
relations with your country in various fields, as 
well as to set up new bridges of mutually 
advantageous collaboration. 
 
Wishing you success in further activity in your 
responsible position of the President of the 
United States of America, please accept, Your 
Excellency, the assurances of my highest 
consideration." 
 
2008-11-05 
 
End Text.
