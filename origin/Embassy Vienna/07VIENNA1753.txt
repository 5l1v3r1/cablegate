¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, post submitted information on domestic USG 
designations of four additional Iranian entities and two individuals 
under E.O. 13382 to Elisabeth Ottawa, Terrorism Financing 
Coordinator in the Financial Markets Directorate of the Ministry of 
Finance (MoF).  Post also sent reftel information to Alfred Lejsek, 
Head of the MoF's Financial Markets Unit, as well as to the Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs' International Security Cooperation Office. 
 
¶2. (SBU) In a follow-up, Katharina Senk from the MoF's Financial 
Markets Unit, told us that the MoF had circulated the information to 
all banks and financial institutions in Austria.
