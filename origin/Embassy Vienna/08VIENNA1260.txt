¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, post submitted information on USG designations 
of an additional 5 Iranian entities and individuals under E.O. 13382 
to Elisabeth Ottawa, Terrorism Financing Coordinator in the 
Financial Markets Directorate of the Ministry of Finance (MoF). 
Post also sent reftel information to Alfred Lejsek, Head of the 
MoF's Financial Markets Unit, as well as to the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs' International Security Cooperation Office. 
 
¶2. (SBU) In a September 2 follow-up, Elisabeth Ottawa told us that 
the MoF had circulated the information to all banks and financial 
institutions in Austria.
