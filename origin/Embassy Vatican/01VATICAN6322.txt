SUBJECT: BIO INFO FOR GIUSEPPE PINTO - NEW VATICAN 
AMBASSADOR TO SENEGAL AND MAURITANIA 
 
¶1.   THE POPE HAS APPOINTED ITALIAN PRIEST, MONSIGNOR 
GIUSEPPE PINTO, AS NUNCIO (AMBASSADOR) IN SENEGAL AND 
APOSTOLIC DELEGATE IN MAURITANIA. PINTO IS CURRENTLY THE 
ARGENTINA-CHILE DESK OFFICER AT THE VATICAN FOREIGN OFFICE. 
HE WAS BORN IN 1952 AND ORDAINED A PRIEST IN 1978. MONSIGNOR 
                       UNCLASSIFIED 
 
PAGE 02        VATICA  06322  061608Z 
PINTO HOLDS A DOCTORAL DEGREE IN CANON (CHURCH) LAW, AND 
ENTERED THE VATICAN'S DIPLOMATIC SERVICE IN 1984. HE HAS 
SERVED AT THE VATICAN'S MISSIONS IN PAPUA NEW GUINEA AND IN 
ARGENTINA. AS WELL AS HIS NATIVE ITALIAN, PINTO SPEAKS 
FRENCH, SPANISH AND ENGLISH. MONSIGNOR PINTO WILL BE 
ORDAINED AN ARCHBISHOP PRIOR TO TAKING UP HIS APPOINTMENT. 
 
¶2.   NOTE: AN APOSTOLIC DELEGATE IS THE PAPAL REPRESENTATIVE 
TO THE CATHOLIC CHURCH IN A COUNTRY WITH WHICH THE HOLY SEE 
HAS NOT ESTABLISHED FORMAL DIPLOMATIC RELATIONS. END NOTE.
