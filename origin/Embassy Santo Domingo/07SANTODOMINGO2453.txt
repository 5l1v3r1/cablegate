SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON CUBA EMBARGO RESOLUTION DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 148506 
 
¶1. POLOFF delivered Reftel talking points on the annual UN 
General Assembly resolution introduced by Cuba condemning the 
U.S. economic embargo to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 
Deputy Foreign Minister Alejandra Liriano made no substantive 
response, but rather pledged to immediately inform Foreign 
Minister Morales of the U.S. position.  We will report any 
subsequent Dominican response.
