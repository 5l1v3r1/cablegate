SUBJECT: MDA DEMARCHE: DOMINICAN REPUBLIC WILL SUPPORT 
 
REF: STATE 104272 
 
(SBU) Embassy delivered reftel demarche on June 10 and 
repeated it June 15 to the MFA division of UN/OAS and 
international conference affairs.  On June 22, deputy 
director Rhadys Abreu de Polanco informed us that the 
Dominican Republic will support the Swiss proposal for a 
diplomatic conference to adopt a third additional protocol to 
open the door for full membership for Magen David Adom (MDA) 
in the International Red Cross/Red Crescent Movement.  The 
Dominican Republic will reply accordingly to the Swiss 
circular note that made the proposal.
