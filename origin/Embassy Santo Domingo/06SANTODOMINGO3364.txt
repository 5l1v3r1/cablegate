SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON UNGA RESOLUTIONS ON LEBANON DELIVERED

¶1. POLOFF delivered Reftel talking points on UNGA Resolutions 
on the Situation of Human Rights and Children in Lebanon to 
the Ministry of Foreign Relations.  Ambassador Michelle 
Cohen, Dominican Chief of United Nations and Organization of 
American States Affairs (Assistant Secretary-equivalent), 
took our views on board but had no immediate reply.  We will 
report any subsequent Dominican response.
