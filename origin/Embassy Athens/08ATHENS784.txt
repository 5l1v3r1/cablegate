¶1. (SBU) DepPolCouns passed reftel demarche on the June 23 
Vienna conference on Reconstruction of Lebanon's Nahr al-Baid 
Refugee Camp to MFA A6 Directorate for Middle East Affairs 
deputy head Giorgos Ayfantis. Ayfantis said FM Bakoyannis 
intended to attend the Vienna conference, as well as the 
Donors' Conference for Palestine on June 24 in Berlin. 
 
¶2. (SNU) A/PolCouns confirmed Bakoyannis' intention to attend 
both conferences in a conversation with officials in the FM's 
Diplomatic Cabinet.
