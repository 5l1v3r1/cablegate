SUBJECT: BURKINA FASO: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON IMPLEMENTING UN SECURITY 
COUNCIL RESOLUTION ON IRAN 
 
REF: STATE 105173 
 
 (SBU) On October 6, PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Sylvain 
Yameogo, Acting Director of International Organizations at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Yameogo noted that UN Security Council 
Resolution 1835 follows the same themes as previous resolutions on 
Iran.  Yameogo highlighted the issues of travel restrictions, 
technological information sharing, and financial sanctions as being 
among the most pertinent issues for discussion within the 
international community and at the national level.  Yameogo said he 
would study the points presented in the U.S. non-paper, pass on any 
necessary information to Burkina Faso's permanent mission to the UN 
in New York, and would notify Post if he has any new information on 
their position to share.
