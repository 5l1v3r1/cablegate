SUBJECT: MALAWI - MUTHARIKA TO ATTEND SADC SUMMIT ON ZIMBABWE 
 
REF: LUSAKA 412 
 
On April 10, Malawi State House Press Officer Chikumbutso Mtumodzi 
confirmed that President Mutharika will attend the Southern African 
Development Community Summit concerning Zimbabwe on April 12. 
Mtumodzi said the President will depart on the morning of April 12 
and is expected to return the same evening.
