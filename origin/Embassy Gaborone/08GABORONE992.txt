SUBJECT: GOB TO RESPECT DESIGNATION OF GULF-BASED AL-QAIDA 
FINANCIERS 
 
REF: STATE 113231 
 
 ¶1. (SBU) PolOff met with the Director of the Department of 
Multilateral Affairs in Botswana's Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs and International Cooperation Mmamosadinyana Punkie 
Josephine Molefe November 12. PolOff discussed the UN 
Security Council 1267 Sanctions Committee approval to add 
three Gulf-based al-Qaida financiers: Khalifa Muhammad 
TurkiAl-Subaiy, Abd Al-Rahman Muhammad Jaffar Ali, and Adil 
Muhammad Mahmud Abd Al-Khaliq.  Further, PolOff informed the 
Director that the United States designated these individuals 
domestically on June 5, and urged the GOB to take action in 
accordance with its obligations under relevant UN Security 
Council Resolutions. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The Director said that there will be a GOB 
multi-agency counterterrorism meeting on Friday and she will 
take this information with her to the meeting to ensure all 
interested parties are informed. She added that Botswana will 
respect the designation of the three Gulf-based al-Qaida 
financiers. Director Molefe also stressed that Botswana 
considers its obligations under international agreements very 
important.
