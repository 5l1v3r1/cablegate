SUBJECT: FINLAND "POSITIVE" ABOUT U.S. IMO CANDIDACY

Â¶1. (SBU) In response to the demarche on re-election of the 
U.S. to the IMO Council in category "A," Ann-Christine 
Kivela, Secretary for International Affairs at the Finnish 
Maritime Administration said Finland "acknowledges the United 
States will be seeking re-election to Category 'A' of the 
Council of the International Maritime Organization..." and 
that "Finland will give the most careful consideration to 
this candidacy."  She continued by saying Finland has a 
"traditionally positive attitude" toward U.S. candidacy. 
Paavo Wihuri, Director of Maritime Safety and Security at the 
Finnish Maritime Administration, will lead the Finnish 
delegation in London.
