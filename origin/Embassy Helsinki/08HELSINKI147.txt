SUBJECT: FINLAND: IMPLEMENTING THE DUAL USE ITEM PROVISION 
OF UNSCR 1803 
 
REF: STATE 29464 
 
(SBU) PolChief delivered reftel demarche to Tarja Pesamaa, 
Deputy Director of the Finnish MFA's Office of 
Nonproliferation, Arms Control and Disarmament April 1. 
Pesamaa emphasized that the GoF intends to vigorously 
implement the dual use provisions of UNSCR 1803 and all 
previous Resolutions related to Iran.  Finnish companies are 
"well informed and pro-active in seeking clarification" from 
the MFA on transactions regarding dual use items, she said, 
and the GoF "exercises utmost vigilance" on any requests 
regarding Iran.  The GoF also has established a working group 
that includes MFA, Customs and Ministry of Trade and Industry 
representatives in order to provide further safeguards. 
Finland welcomes the EU process aimed ensuring implementation 
among all Member States, she added.
