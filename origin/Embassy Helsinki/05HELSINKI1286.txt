SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO FINLAND - CONDEMNING THE ASSASSINATION 
OF GEBRAN TUENI 
 
REF: STATE 223709 
 
Per reftel, Pol Chief delivered the statements of the 
President and the Secretary regarding the assassination of 
Gebran Tueni to Eija Rotinen, the Finnish MFA's Director (A/S 
equivalent) for the Middle East and Asia.  Rotinen said that 
the GoF was deeply troubled by the assassination and 
expressed gratitude for the U.S. statements.  She noted that 
Finland would not be issuing a separate statement but agreed 
fully with the EU position outlined by the UK Presidency.
