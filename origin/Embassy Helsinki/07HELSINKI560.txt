(U) The Finnish Ministry of Foreign Affairs has provided the 
following information regarding expected high level 
attendance at the 62nd UNGA in September.  All information is 
subject to change.

portions of General Assembly Debate.  Possible side 
trips to Washington and elsewhere TBD.
