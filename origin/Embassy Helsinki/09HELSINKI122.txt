On March 25, PolOff, Republic of Korea Counselor Suk-kyoon 
Chung, and Russian Counselor German V. Parfenov delivered the 
Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism (GICNT) 
Workshop invitation and Exercise Planning Group (EPG) 
background to Outi Hyvarinen, Counselor, Finnish MFA Unit for 
Arms Control, Disarmament and Non-Proliferation. Hyvarinen 
said that she would pass the information to STUK (Finnish 
Radiation and Nuclear Safety Authority) since based upon 
materials presented by Mr. Chung, it would require 
expert-level discussion.  Hyvarinen noted that Government of 
Finland supports the initiative and participation will depend 
on resources.
