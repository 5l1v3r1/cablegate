SUBJECT: TURKEY'S PLANS FOR COMMUNITY OF DEMOCRACIES 
NOVEMBER 14-17 MINISTERIAL 
 
REF: STATE 151024 
 
¶1. (U) We delivered reftel points to Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs Human Rights Department Head Hasan Ulusoy on November 
6, 2007.  Ulusoy reported that Mr. Yalcin Kaya Erensoy, 
Turkey's Ambassador to Senegal, will represent Turkey at the 
November 14-17 Mali Ministerial. 
 
¶2. (U) Ulusoy told us the GOT has not yet formulated any 
particular initiatives to put forward at the roundtables.  He 
said Turkey looks forward to sharing its own experiences on 
the struggle against terrorism, the compatibility of 
Democracy and Islam, and the creation of a vibrant civil 
society.  Ambassador Erensoy plans to work with CD 
participants to create mechanisms for further cooperation on 
these important issues.
