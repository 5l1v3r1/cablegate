SUBJECT:  TURKEY'S ASSISTANCE TO PAKISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 182566 
 
In response to reftel demarche seeking an update on donor pledges 
and expenditures related to the South Asia earthquake, GOT MFA 
officer Sebnem Incesu insisted that Turkey's pledge to Pakistan 
totaled $150 million, of which official and NGO humanitarian 
assistance actually expended totaled $100 million.  She said that 
$40 million of the pledge for reconstruction projects was not yet 
disbursed.
