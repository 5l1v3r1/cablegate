¶1. This is an action cable.  See paragraph 4. 
 
¶2. The Turkish Government has formally offered an 
assistance package totaling $2.5 million for Hurricane 
Katrina, including $1.5 million cash donation sent to 
the American Red Cross, and USD 1 million of in-kind 
assistance to be determined (Ref A). 
 
¶3. With respect to in-kind assistance the GOT could most 
quickly provide (drawing from Ref B list), MFA passed 
the following list to Econoff on September 15: 
(Most quickly and easily:) 
-large tents 
-first-aid kits 
-cleaning supplies 
 
(Next most quickly:) 
-small generators 
-bedding: sheets and blankets 
-baby formula and diapers 
 
¶4.  Embassy requests Washington's guidance on how to 
respond to the GOT's offer and additional information, 
which came from a coordination meeting today of the 
Turkish TGS, MFA, Red Crescent, and TEMA (Turkey 
Emergency Management Agency).
