non-paper via diplomatic note to MFA Head of Multilateral 
Economic Affairs, Yasemin Irtem, and to Ministry of Foreign 
Trade (MFT) Head of WTO Department, Aylin Bebekoglu.  The GOT 
is not a food aid donor or recipient.  Post will report on 
any subsequent response from the GOT on this issue.
