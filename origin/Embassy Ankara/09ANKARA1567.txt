SUBJECT: TURKEY: TOKYO PLEDGE IN PROGRESS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 108183 
 
(SBU) On October 22, we delivered reftel points to MFA South 
Asia Division Head Emre Karagol.  Turkey pledged USD 100 
million at the April 2009 Tokyo Donor's Conference.  USD 10 
million was given as a cash grant, and the rest will be 
allocated to special projects.  The projects, which have been 
proposed by Pakistan, are being reviewed by the Turkish MFA 
and the Turkish international development agency, TIKA.  TIKA 
will administer the projects.  We are seeking a meeting with 
TIKA to develop information on the projects proposed by 
Pakistan.
