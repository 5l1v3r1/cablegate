SUBJECT: SIGNING OF AIR TRAFFIC CONTROL MEMORANDUM OF UNDERSTANDING 
BETWEEN THE ISLAMIC REPUBLIC OF IRAN AND THE REPUBLIC OF IRAQ 
 
 
On November 18th an ICAA delegation from the Republic of Iraq 
entered into an agreement with the Islamic Republic of Iran to allow 
air travel between Baghdad, Iraq and Tehran, Iran.  Mahan Air is the 
only designated air carrier from Iran, and Iraqi Airways is the only 
air carrier from Iraq authorized to conduct flights between Baghdad, 
Iraq and Tehran, Iran.  The only route of flight authorized is G202. 
 The Countries have agreed to meet again in six months to review 
and/or refine the procedures outlined in the MOU.
