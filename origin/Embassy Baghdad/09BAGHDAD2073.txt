SUBJECT: RESPONSE TO BLUE LANTERN LICENSE 050092728 
 
REF: A. STATE 35834 
     ¶B. STATE 31007 
 
¶1.  (U) This is a response to STATE 35384 regarding BLUE 
LANTERN end use check on license 050092728. 
 
¶2.  (U) Post has contacted the Multi-National Security 
Transition Command in Iraq (MNSTC-I) J-4 and they confirm the 
receipt of DTC Case 050092728, to include 7,500 M&P9 Pistols, 
9mm, from Smith & Wesson Corp via email channels.  The 
weapons arrived, and serial numbers have been logged and 
reported to LOGSA. The weapons are currently at the Taji 
National Depot Complex, the supply depot in Taji, north of 
Baghdad that, per MNSTC-I policy statement #03-08, served as 
the principle depot for distribution for all U.S.-provided 
defense articles for the Iraqi Security Forces (ISF).  The 
Iraqi Security Assistance Mission (ISAM, formerly SAO) is 
also aware of the receipt and was informed via phone and 
email channels. 
 
¶3.  (U) Verification was completed based on agreed procedures 
after joint DOS Blue Lantern/DOD Golden Sentry team visit in 
February (ref B).  Post's POL-MIL section worked directly 
with MNSTC-I J4 and ISAM to coordinate verification and 
confirm receipt in compliance with NDAA Section 1228.
