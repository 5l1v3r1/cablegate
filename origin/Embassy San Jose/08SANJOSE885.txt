SUBJECT: COSTA RICA SUPPORTS US CANDIDACY FOR 
INTER-AMERICAN COMMISSION OF WOMEN (CIM) 
 
REF: 112981 
 
¶1.  On November 7, we delivered reftel demarche via 
diplomatic note to Lilian Rodriguez in the MFA's Office of 
Candidacies.  No substantive response was received at that 
time. 
 
¶2.  However, on November 12, we received Dip Note 
DGPE/P111-C/221-11-08, dated November 7, which announced 
Costa Rica,s support for Dr. Wanda Jones as a member of the 
Executive Committee of the CIM.  The Note (sent to 
Department), attributed the GOCR,s support to "the excellent 
ties of friendship and cooperation that unite both (of our) 
countries."
