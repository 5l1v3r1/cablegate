SUBJECT: COSTA RICAN REPRESENTATION AT WHTI MEETING 
 
REF:  SECSTATE 132219 
 
On July 18 and 21, 2005, Econoff communicated with Vice 
Minister of Public Works and Transportation, Maria Lorena 
Lopez, regarding the upcoming Western Hemisphere 
Transportation Initiative (WHTI) meeting.  She stated that 
she will represent Costa Rica at the meeting in Brazil and 
has an interest in rural road projects.  Note: She stated 
that Costa Rica leads the Rural Road group of the WHTI.
