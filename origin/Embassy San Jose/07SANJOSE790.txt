SUBJECT: COSTA RICA: RESPONSE TO DEMARCHE ON NPT PREPCOM 
 
REF: STATE 54147 
 
On April 25, Poloff sent talking points in reftel to Carlos 
Cordero, head of the Disarmament, Terrorism, and Organized 
Crime section within the Foreign Policy Division of the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA).  Cordero replied that he 
would forward these talking points to the MFA official in 
Vienna who would represent Costa Rica in the PREPCOM I 
meeting beginning on April 30.  Cordero gave no indication of 
the GOCR's position on PREPCOM I issues.
