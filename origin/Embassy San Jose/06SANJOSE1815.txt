SUBJECT: COSTA RICA: DEMARCHE ON UN HIGH LEVEL DIALOGUE 
DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 122513 
 
 ¶1. (SBU) On August 16, PolCouns and Poloffs met with Carlos 
Cordero, Director of Multilateral Policy at the Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs (MFA) and Guisella Sanchez, head of the Human 
Rights Division at the MFA, to discuss talking points from 
reftel delivered to Cordero on August 4. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Sanchez indicated that the GOCR has not issued a 
formal position on the creation of a new UN forum on 
migration, but in general, believes, as does the USG, that 
regional discussions on migration are preferable to 
international forums of the type envisioned by the Secretary 
General.
