SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: CANDIDACY OF DAVID WALKER FOR 
UN INDEPENDENT AUDIT ADVISORY COMMITTEE (IAAC) 
 
REF: STATE 146857 
 
¶1. On October 24, PolOff met with Montserrat Vargas 
Solorzano, Director of the Candidacies and United Nations 
Specialized  Organizations Section of the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs (MFA) to request GOCR support of Mr. Walker's 
candidacy and to deliver his biographical data. 
 
¶2. Vargas indicated that the GOCR would give all due 
consideration to this candidacy and would forward the 
biographical information to their UN delegation.
