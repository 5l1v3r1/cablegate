SUBJECT: GHANA: DELIVERY OF DEMARCHE ON IRAN'S ATTEMPTS TO 
EXPAND BANKING RELATIONSHIPS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 132635 
 
 
¶1. (SBU) ECONOFF delivered subject demarche to Paul Acquah, 
Governor of the Bank of Ghana, and Lionel Van Lare Dosoo, 
Deputy Governor of the Bank of Ghana. Governor Acquah said 
that no banking license applications have yet been received 
at the Bank of Ghana from any Iranian entities.  The 
officials did not indicate the Bank of Ghana,s disposition 
toward the possibility of any future Iranian applications. 
 
¶2. (SBU) In response to REFTEL points, Governor Acquah stated 
his belief that these matters would likely be discussed at 
the next Inter Governmental Action Group  Against Money 
Laundering (GIABA) meetings, expected in early 2009. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Post will monitor the financial sector for any 
developments regarding Iranian banking activities in Ghana 
and advise the Department.
