SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST - KATHERINE HADDA TO BANGKOK 
 
 
¶1. (SBU) Embassy requests country clearance for Political-Economic 
Counselor Katherine B. Hadda to travel to Bangkok, Thailand from 
July 19-21, 2006.  The purpose of Ms. Hadda's visit is to attend 
bilateral meetings that will take place in Bangkok between U.S. and 
New Zealand government officials. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Ms. Hadda will arrive at Bangkok airport on Wednesday, July 
19 at 12:15 on TG 990 from Auckland.  She will depart Friday, July 
21 at 17:30 on TG 989 for Auckland. 
 
¶3. (U) No embassy assistance is requested.  Post apologizes for the 
late request.
