SUBJECT: NEW ZEALAND PARTICIPATION IN PSI OPERATIONAL 
EXPERTS GROUP MEETING 
 
 
On March 7, Taha McPherson, of the Security Policy Division 
at the New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade 
(MFAT) told P/E Counselor that Ambassador Dell Higgie, 
Ambassador for Counter-Terrorism and Director of MFAT's 
Security Policy Division, will attend the April 11-12 
Proliferation Security Initiative Operational Experts Group 
meeting in Miami.
