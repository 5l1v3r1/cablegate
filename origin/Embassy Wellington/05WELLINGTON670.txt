¶1. (U) Poloff requested reftel information from Valerie 
Meyer, Deputy Director, United Nations, Human Rights and 
Commonwealth Division (UNHC), Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
Trade (MFAT). 
 
¶2. (SBU) Because New Zealand will hold general elections on 
September 17, New Zealand will not send a high-level 
representative to the UNGA and Summit.  New Zealand's lead 
representative will be Ambassador Rosemary Banks, NZ's 
Permrep to the UN.  Joan Mosely, Director of UNHC/MFAT, may 
also attend. 
 
¶3. (U) Meyer said that this information is also being 
directly provided to USUN by NZ's UN Mission.
