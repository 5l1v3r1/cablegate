SUBJECT:  NEW ZEALAND - DEMARCHE DELIVERED: U.S. CANDIDACY TO ICAO 
COUNCIL 
 
REF:  State 115443 
 
1.) Reftel information regarding the U.S. Candidacy for ICAO Council 
was delivered August 18, 2007 to New Zealand's Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs and Trade (MFAT) and the Ministry of Transportation (MOT). 
The Ministry of Transportation informed the Embassy that it has the 
statutory responsibility for administering NZ's participation in 
ICAO.  Although there was no immediate reaction by GNZ officials to 
the U.S. candidacy, post will forward any response or comments we 
receive after MOT has had sufficient time to consider the request. 
 
2.) In regards to the second question in reftel, the NZ delegation 
to the upcoming ICAO assembly will consist of: 
 
-- Glen-Marie Burns, Manager Aviation MOT (Chief Delegate), 
-- Kathy Perreau, Manager Environmental Sustainability MOT, 
-- Peter Davey, Manager Policy & International, Civil Aviation 
Authority; and 
-- Leslie Macintosh, Chief Legal Counsel CAA.
