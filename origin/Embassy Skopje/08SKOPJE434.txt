TERRORISM UPDATE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 69620 
 
¶1. (U)  This message contains an action request in paragraph 
¶3. 
¶2.  (U)  PolOff informed MFA Director for Multilateral 
Affairs Svetlana Geleva, per reftel, that India and Jordan 
are new partner nations to the Global Initiative to Combat 
Nuclear Terrorism (GI).  In response to reftel's request for 
information, Geleva noted that she would remain Macedonia's 
GI Point of Contact. 
¶3.  (U)  Geleva noted that Macedonia is improperly 
identified as the Former Yugoslav Republic of Macedonia on 
the State Department's list of GI partner nations at 
www.state.gov/t/isn/105955.htm   Action request:  Please 
update the list of GI partner nations list at the above 
referenced website to "Macedonia" or "Republic of Macedonia" 
-- the names by which as the U.S has formally recognized the 
country since November 4, 2004.
