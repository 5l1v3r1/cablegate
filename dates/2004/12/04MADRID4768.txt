SUBJECT: SPANISH CONGRESS RATIFIES S AND T AGREEMENT 
EXTENSION 
 
¶1.  This is an action request for OES/STC -- see para 3. 
 
¶2.   Embassy has received Spanish Foreign Ministry Note 
Verbal 385/2.3, dated December 14, 2004, informing us that 
the Spanish lower house and Senate had ratified the January 
20 and February 2, 2004 exchange of notes extending the 
validity of the October 27, 1994 bilateral Education, 
Culture, and Science Cooperation Agreement.  The note 
indicates that this congressional ratification was the final 
legal step Spain needed to accomplish to bring the agreement 
extension into legal force in Spain.  The note concluded by 
requesting a formal note verbal from the U.S. side regarding 
our efforts to bring the agreement extension into legal force 
in the U.S. 
 
¶3.  ACTION REQUEST:  Please provide via front channel cable 
or EMail (forderk@state.gov) the text of a note verbal we can 
use to respond to the GOS request. 
MANZANARES
