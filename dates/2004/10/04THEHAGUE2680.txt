SUBJECT: DUTCH ON OSCE ELECTION MISSION IN UKRAINE 
 
REF: STATE 220961 
 
 PolOff spoke with Frank van Beuningen, Dutch MFA OSCE Policy 
Officer, concerning reftel demarche on October 15.  Van 
Beuningen said the Netherlands had received a similar request 
from ODIHR and was planning to dispatch 25 observers for both 
round of the upcoming Ukranian elections.  According to van 
Beuningen, this was the extent of what the Dutch could 
provide as they have a limited pool of language-qualified 
persons available.
