SUBJECT: SOUTHEAST TURKEY PRESS SUMMARY JUNE 11, 2004 
 
This is the Southeastern Turkey press summary for June 11, 2004. 
 Please note that Turkish press reports often contain errors or 
exaggerations; AmConsulate Adana does not vouch for the accuracy 
of the reports summarized here. 
 
POLITICAL, SECURITY, HUMAN RIGHTS 
 
Turkiye / Two security guards were killed during an attack on 
Gercus provincial district residences.  It is reported that 
there were two attackers and the Batman Governorate started an 
investigation on the incident. 
 
Ozgur Gundem / In Diyarbakir, Batman, Adana and Mersin, people 
gathered to celebrate the release of DEP MP's, including Leyla 
Zana.
