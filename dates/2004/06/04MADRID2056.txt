¶1.  Embassy communicated with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA) regarding the change in date to June 6 for bunkering of 
the State University of New York (Suny) Maritime College 
Training Ship Empire State in Ceuta. 
 
¶2.  Embassy received a response June 2 from the MFA.  An 
informal translation follows: 
 
      This is to communicate that there is no problem in 
changing the date for bunkering in Ceuta of the referenced 
ship (T.S. Empire State) to June 6, 2004. 
 
End translation.
