¶2.   ACCORDING TO CBP REPORTING, ON MAY 26, 2004, AT 
  GEORGE BUSH INTERNATIONAL AIRPORT, TEXAS, ALVARO BORRAS 
  COMPDERA ARRIVED FROM MEXICO CITY, PRESENTED A SPANISH 
  PASSPORT AND APPLIED FOR ADMISSION UNDER THE VISA 
  WAIVER PROGRAM.  DURING THE CBP INTERVIEW, BORRAS 
  COMPDERA ADMITTED TO AND POSSESSED DOCUMENTS SUPPORTING 
  DIRECT LINKS TO AL QAEDA THROUGH OPERATIVE 
  ASSOCIATIONS, TERRORIST GROUPS, AND ASSOCIATED MOSQUES. 
  IN COORDINATION WITH THE JTTF AGENT, CBP OFFICERS 
  DETERMINED THAT BORRAS COMPDERA WAS INADMISSIBLE TO THE 
  U.S. BASED ON THE POSSIBILITY THAT HE MIGHT ENGAGE IN 
  TERRORIST ACTIVITIES AND ALLOWED HIM TO WITHDRAW HIS 
  APPLICATION FOR ADMISSION.  BORRAS COMPDERA WAS 
  RETURNED TO MEXICO CITY ON MAY 28, 2004.
