SUBJECT:  U.S. ICAO Council Candidacy Demarche 
 
Ref: State 129598 
 
Econoff transmitted reftel points requesting support for 
U.S. re-election to the Council of the International 
Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) to MFA Aviation 
Affairs Director Bekir Uysal and to the Transport 
Ministry's Civil Aviation General Director Topa Toker. 
Uysal said that MFA was considering our demarche, and 
would provide further information on the GOT position as 
well as on the Turkish delegation to the ICAO Assembly 
meeting as it becomes available.
