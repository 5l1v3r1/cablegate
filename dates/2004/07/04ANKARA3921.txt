(U) Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Turkish MFA Deputy 
General Directorate for International Political Organizations 
(IO) on July 13. IO office undertook to convey points to 
relevant sections within the MFA for evaluation and comment. 
Our IO interlocutor noted that the GOT has not yet developed 
its own UNGA agenda, or a timeline for its release, but 
asserted the MFA will forward Turkey's UNGA initiatives to 
Post when a draft is approved.
