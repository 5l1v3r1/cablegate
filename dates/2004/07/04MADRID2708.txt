¶2.   ON JULY 14, 2004, FRENCH POLICE ARRESTED TWO 
  SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF THE TERRORIST ORGANIZATION ETA. 
  THE POLICE GAVE CHASE TO THE SUSPECTS AFTER THEY DROVE 
  THROUGH A POLICE CONTROL AND WERE LATER DETAINED IN THE 
  NEARBY CITY OF SAINTES, CLOSE TO MARSEILLE, FRANCE. 
  THEY WERE DRIVING A CAR WITH FALSE LICENSE PLATES AND 
  HAD THEIR BABY WITH THEM.  THEY WERE ALSO IN POSSESSION 
  O TWO AUTOMATIC PISTOLS, FALSE IDENTIFICATION CARDS, A 
  FALSE POLICE I.D. CARD AND OTHER DOCUMENTS BEARING 
  ETA'S ANAGRAM.  SPANISH POLICE BELIEVE THE ARRESTED ARE 
  PART OF THE ETA'S LOGISTIC NETWORK.  THEY BOTH HAVE 
  SEVERAL PENDING INDICTMENTS AT THE SPANISH NATIONAL 
  COURT FOR TERRORIST RELATED ISSUES.
