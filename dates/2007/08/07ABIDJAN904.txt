SUBJECT: COTE D'IVOIRE DELEGATION TO UNGA 
 
REF: ABIDJAN 808 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Ambassador Bernard Tanoh-Boutchoue, Director of the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) United Nations and 
International Organizations Department, told Poloff August 27 
that Minister of Foreign Affairs Youssouf Bakayoko will head 
Cote d'Ivoire's delegation to the United Nations General 
Assembly.  Tanoh-Botcoue said the delegation would number 
about 25 persons, with approximately 15 traveling from 
Abidjan. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Tanoh-Boutchoue said the MFA has contacted the 
Ministers of Interior, Culture, and Environment regarding 
attendance at the High Level Dialogues on Interfaith and 
Intercultural Relations and Climate Change that will take 
place on the margins of the UNGA, but no decision has yet 
been made.
