SUBJECT: IRAQ: CONSULTATIONS ON AND ROLLOUT OF POLICY 
 
 
¶1. (SBU) Beginning Friday, January 5, President Bush and 
the Secretary will be consulting members of Congress on 
the USG's Iraq policy; the White House will consult select 
international partners prior to an announcement. However, 
no posts will be asked to pre-brief the policy; guidance 
will be sent to posts only after the President's 
announcement is made. This announcement will be made in a 
few days.
