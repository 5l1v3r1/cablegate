SUBJECT: EXTRANCHECK: POST SHIPMENT VERIFICATION: MACAU 
CIVIL AVIATION AUTHORITY 
REF: A) USDOC 05927 B) D357592 
 
1.Unauthorized disclosure of the information provided 
below is prohibited by Section 12C of the Export 
Administration Act. 
 
¶2. In Reftel A, the Office of Enforcement Analysis 
(OEA) of the Bureau of Industry and Security (BIS) 
requested that Export Control Officer (ECO) Philip 
Ankel conduct a post-shipment verification (PSV) visit 
to the Macau Civil Aviation Authority, Centro Com. 
Cheng Feng 18, Macau. The items in question are four 
ISAP POD 100 uncooled thermal imaging cameras with a 
100 MM F/L lens valued at USD 98,000. The Export 
Control Classification Number (ECCN) is 6A003 and the 
items were exported pursuant to export license D357592. 
The exporter was Industrial Security Alliance Partner 
(ISAP). 
 
¶3. At the request of Export Control Officer (ECO), 
Philip Ankel, FCS Commercial Assistant Sandy Lai 
contacted the Civil Aviation Authority to arrange the 
PSV.  At that time, Mr. Lo Veng Tong, Technical Officer 
indicated that the cameras had not yet been installed 
and that it is anticipated the cameras will be 
installed in late February.  At the recommendation of 
OEA, the ECO is closing this PSV for now and will 
contact the Civil Aviation Authority in February to see 
whether installation has occurred and request a follow 
up cable at that time.
