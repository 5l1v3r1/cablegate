SUBJECT: CROATIA SUPPORTIVE ON IRAQ 
 
REF: STATE 3592 
 
¶1. (SBU) Charge delivered reftel demarche to Croatia's Assistant 
Minister of Foreign Affairs for Bilateral Cooperation Ivica Tomic, 
who said Croatia supports President Bush's effort to ensure a 
positive future for Iraq.  He added that Croatia will continue to 
contribute to Iraq's progress according to its capability, noting 
such projects as Croatia's contribution of trainers to the Jordan 
police training facility, its hospitalization efforts for Iraqi 
children, and its appointment of a non-resident ambassador to 
Baghdad. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Charge also briefed Presidential Foreign Policy Advisor 
Budimir Loncar, who was very encouraged that the Iraqi government is 
prepared to address the destabilization efforts of Iran and Syria 
with the governments of those countries.
