SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST: ANP GENERAL OFFICER 
 
¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 3.

forwards the name and biodata of a brigadier general in the Afghan 
National Police who has been selected for appointment as chief of

information available on this individual and has found no derogatory 
information.  The United Nations Assistance Mission to Afghanistan

evidence of gross human rights violations.

Interior can move ahead with this urgent appointment.
