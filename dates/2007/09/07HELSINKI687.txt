(SBU) PolChief delivered U.S. proposals clarifying MTCR 
Annex material/propellant controls (Ref A) and the white 
paper on high energy density fuels (Ref B) to Tarja 
Pesamaa, A/Director of the Finnish MFA's Unit for Arms 
Control, Non-Proliferation and Disarmament Sept. 6. 
Regarding reftel A issues, Pesamaa thanked us for sharing 
the US proposals and said that if Finnish technical experts 
had any questions or input, they would submit those 
directly to US experts through MTCR channels well before 
the Nov. 1 TEM.  Regarding the specific US questions in 
Reftel B, Pesamaa said that Finnish experts would provide 
the answers in the next few weeks, and that she would 
inform the Embassy once they had done so.
