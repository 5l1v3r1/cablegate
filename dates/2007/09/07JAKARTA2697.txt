SUBJECT: INDONESIA SUPPORTS USG CANDIDATE FOR UN COMMITTEE 
AGAINST TORTURE 
 
REF: A) JAKARTA 7626 B) STATE 130973 
 
The Department of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU) informed poloff 
September 24 that Indonesia would support U.S. candidate 
Felice Gaer for reelection to the UN Committee Against 
Torture. The decision was made by the Directorate General for 
Multilateral Affairs in response to our September 17 demarche 
(ref A). The other two candidates Indonesia will support are 
Claudio Grossman (Chile) and Luis Gallegos Chi Riboga 
(Equador).
