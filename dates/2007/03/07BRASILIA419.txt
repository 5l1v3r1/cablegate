SUBJECT:  TWO USGS MOUS WITH BRAZILIAN UNIVERSITIES APPROVED BY 
GOVERNMENT OF BRAZIL FOR SIGNATURE 
 
REF: (A) 06 STATE 131940, (B) 06 BRASILIA 1275 
 
¶1.  Reftel authorizes Post to seek Government of Brazil (GOB) 
authorization to sign two previously negotiated Memorandum of 
Understanding, between the U.S. Geological Survey (USGS) and the 
Geoscience Institute of the University of Brasilia and USGS and the 
Federal University of Rio Grande do Norte. 
 
¶2.  In a letter received February 28, 2007, the Ministry of Science 
and Technology indicated that it had analyzed the MOUs and does not 
oppose the signing of the documents. The letter indicated that there 
is a need to standardize the text of both MOUs in Article V, which 
refers to Intellectual Property.  The Article V in the MOU should be 
in accordance with the terms established in the U.S.-Brazil S&T 
agreement. (For your reference, the S&T agreement will be sent to 
you electronically.) 
 
¶3.  The letter concludes by saying that the universities have the 
autonomy to sign their own international documents.  It was signed 
by Paulo Rogerio Goncalves, Director, International Relations, MCT. 
 
 
¶4.  This office will be happy to facilitate a signing, or USGS and 
the universities may choose to organize the signing independent of 
Embassy Brasilia. 
 
¶5.  Congratulations on this long await authorization.
