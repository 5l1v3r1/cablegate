SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST: ANA IMET Candidates for Training at 
U.S. Army Recruiting School 
 
¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 2. 
 
¶2. In order to comply with Leahy human rights vetting requirements, 
this cable forwards the names and biodata of ten Afghan National 
Army (ANA) officers and interpreters designated to attend training 
at the U.S. Army Recruiting School at Fort Jackson, SC under the 
IMET program in July 2007.  Post has made a good faith effort to 
ensure that the individuals with one name actually only have one 
name.  Post has reviewed the information available on these 
individuals and has found no derogatory information.  Combined 
Security Transition Command - Afghanistan (CSTC-A) has reviewed the 
information available on these individuals and has found no 
derogatory information.  Post requests Department's review of these 
candidates and response by June 15, 2007.
