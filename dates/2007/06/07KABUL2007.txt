¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 2.

this cable forwards the names and biodata of fifteen Presidential 
Protection Service (PPS) officers nominated to attend the improvised 
explosive devices (IED) recognition and awareness course at Camp 
Watan, a DS/ATA facility in Kabul, Afghanistan, July 21-31, 2007. 
Post has reviewed the information available on these individuals and 
has found no derogatory information.  Post requests Department's 
review of these candidates and response by July 5, 2007.
