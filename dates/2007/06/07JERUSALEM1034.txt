SUBJECT: LAUNCH OF "PALESTINIAN ECONOMY" SITE ON STATE 
OPENNET 
 
¶1.  CONSULATE GENERAL JERUSALEM IS PLEASED TO ANNOUNCE THE 
LAUNCH OF "PALESTINIAN ECONOMY," A NEW COMMUNITY WEB SITE 
FACILITATED BY THE DEPARTMENT'S KNOWLEDGE MANAGEMENT TEAM. 
THE WEB SITE IS A COLLECTION OF IN-DEPTH INFORMATION ON THE 
PALESTINIAN ECONOMY, DESIGNED TO HELP FACILITATE INFORMATION 
SHARING WITH U.S. GOVERNMENT EMPLOYEES WORLDWIDE WHO WORK ON 
PALESTINIAN ECONOMIC ISSUES. 
 
¶2.  THE SITE HOSTS NUMEROUS REPORTS FROM THE WORLD BANK, UN 
AGENCIES, IMF, PALTRADE, AND OTHER ORGANIZATIONS THAT CLOSELY 
FOLLOW THE PALESTINIAN ECONOMY.  THE MOST RECENT ENTRIES ARE 
POSTED ON THE MAIN PAGE, AND THE ARCHIVES ARE SEARCHABLE AND 
FILED BY TOPIC.  POST ENCOURAGES READERS TO SUBSCRIBE TO THE 
SITE IN ORDER TO RECEIVE NOTIFICATIONS OF NEW CONTENT.  WE 
HOPE THAT THE "PALESTINIAN ECONOMY" COMMUNITY WILL BECOME 
YOUR FIRST DESTINATION WHEN SEARCHING FOR OFF-THE-SHELF 
STATISTICS AND INFORMATION. 
 
¶3.  YOU CAN VISIT THE SITE ON THE OPENNET SYSTEM AT:
