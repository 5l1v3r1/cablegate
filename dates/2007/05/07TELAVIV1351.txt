¶3. (SBU) Responding to ref B information on the 3rd GI 
Conference scheduled to take place June 11-12 in Astana, 
Kazakhstan, Propper said that it is likely -- but not yet 
confirmed -- that MFA Deputy Director General for Strategic 
Affairs Ambassador Miriam Ziv will lead the Israeli 
delegation to conference.  Propper promised to confirm 
Israel's participation and details as soon as possible. 
 
¶4. (SBU) Propper expressed appreciation for the information 
in refs C and D, observing that there are now 25 countries 
involved in the Global Initiative.  He asked that we keep 
Ambassador Ziv, Dr. Lederman and him informed of GI 
developments, and said that he looked forward to receiving 
from Washington the most current version of the GI Plan of 
Work.
