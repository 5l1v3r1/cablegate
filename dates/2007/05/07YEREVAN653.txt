SUBJECT: ARMENIA WILL PARTICIPATE IN OSCE PPP CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 62142 
 
Dziunik Aghadjanian, head of the MFA's International 
Organizations Department, told us Armenia will send two 
representatives to the OSCE Public-Private Partnership 
Conference in Vienna.  They are: Gnel Nalbandian, the deputy 
executive director of Public TV, and Hovsep Sargsian, a 
member of the Prosecutor General's staff.
