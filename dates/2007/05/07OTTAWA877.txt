SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON OECD ENLARGEMENT AND FINANCING REFORM 
DELIVERED TO CANADA 
 
REF: STATE 62464 
 
¶1. (SBU) EconMinCons and Econof delivered points in reftel to 
Wendy Clark, Head of the Department of Foreign Affairs and 
International Trade's OECD Office.  Clark said that the USG's 
views on all issues in reftel are very much in line with 
Canadian views.  She said that Canada especially agrees that 
the accession process for Russia will likely be lengthy. 
Clark said that Canada is overall very comfortable with the 
USG's positions on the issues in reftel. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Clark also said that Canada would be willing to work 
with the United States to discuss draft language related to 
these issues.
