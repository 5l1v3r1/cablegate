SUBJECT: TURKEY SUPPORTS PARTNERSHIP FOR DEMOCRATIC

REF: STATE 90018 
 
(SBU) We delivered reftel points to the Head of Department at 
MFA responsible for OECD issues.  She reiterated Turkey's 
support for the Partnership for Democratic Governance (PDG) 
and said Turkey would support SecGen Gurria's proposal to 
house the PDG secretariat at the July 12 OECD Council 
meeting.  She was not aware of any decision to provide 
financial support to the PDG secretariat.
