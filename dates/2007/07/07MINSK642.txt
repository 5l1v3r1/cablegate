SUBJECT: ISA DEMARCHE DELIVERED, IRANIAN FM VISITS MINSK 
 
REF: STATE 97439 
 
¶1. (SBU) On July 20, Acting Pol/Econ Chief delivered reftel points 
to Oleg Anisimov, Counselor in the North American Division. 
Anisimov promised to pass the information along to the Asia-Africa 
Department and the International Organizations Department.  He also 
offered to attempt to facilitate a meeting with oil industry 
policymakers, although he noted Belarusneft officials would not be 
able to affect strategic questions.  [Note: Post is seeking a 
meeting with officials from Belarusneft's parent company, 
Belneftekhim.  End note.] 
 
¶2. (U) Separately, Iranian Foreign Minister Mottaki visited Minsk 
July 21.  Mottaki told journalists a session of the 
Iranian-Belarusian intergovernmental commission would meet in Tehran 
in August to discuss joint projects in the oil sector and other 
commercial cooperation.
