SUBJECT: INDONESIAN PARTICIPATION IN NPT PREPCOM 
 
REF: STATE 105585 (NOTAL) 
 
¶1. (U) We discussed reftel issues o  July 25 with Andy 
Rachmianto, Deputy Director frr WMD Issues in the Directorate 
for Internationa  Security and disarmament at the Indonesian 
Department of Foreign Affairs.  Rachmianto stated that, 
barring any unforeseen developments, Desra Percaya, Director 
for International Security and Disarmament, was expected to 
remain the chief player in Jakarta for nonproliferation 
issues, including the Nuclear Nonproliferation Treaty (NPT). 
 
¶2. (SBU) Rachmianto said Indonesia planned to participate in 
the upcoming General Conference meetings in Vienna in 
September and the First Committee of the UN General Assembly 
in New York in October.  Most likely either he or Percaya 
would attend. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Rachmianto expressed appreciation for the points on 
U.S. disarmament actions (noted reftel).
