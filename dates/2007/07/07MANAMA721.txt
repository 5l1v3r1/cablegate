SUBJECT: BAHRAIN: DRAFT GCC+2 JOINT STATEMENT 
 
REF: STATE 104838 
 
Sensitive but unclassified; please protect accordingly.  Not 
for Internet distribution. 
 
¶1. (SBU) Econoff July 29 previewed with MFA Director of 
Bilateral Relations Dr. Dhafer Al-Umran reftel draft joint 
statement for the GCC 2 meeting in Sharm el-Sheikh.  Dr. 
Dhafer welcomed receipt of the draft and said he would 
immediately relay it to Minister of Foreign Affairs Shaikh 
Khalid Bin Ahmed, who will break his vacation in France to 
head the Bahraini delegation. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Dhafer confirmed to Econoff July 30 that Shaikh 
Khalid had received the draft statement.  Dhafer promised to 
provide any feedback as soon as possible ahead of the July 31 
GCC 2 meeting.
