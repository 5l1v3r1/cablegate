SUBJECT: DRC: DEMARCHE DELIVERED - U.S. NATIONAL 
INTELLIGENCE ESTIMATE ON IRAN'S NUCLEAR PROGRAM 
 
REF: STATE 162558 
 
¶1. (U) PolOff delivered reftel demarche on December 7 to 
Alice Kimpembe Bamba, Minister Plenipotentiary in the 
International Organizations Office of the Congolese Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs. 
 
¶2. (U) Kimpembe thanked PolOff for sharing the information on 
Iran's nuclear program.  She said that the DRC supports the 
peaceful resolution to the dispute over the program.  She 
said that the DRC would consider our request to urge Iran to 
suspend its uranium enrichment, reprocessing, and heavy 
water-related activities.
