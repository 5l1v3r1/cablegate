SUBJECT: DESIGNATION OF IRAN'S DEFENSE INDUSTRIES 
ORGANIZATION UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: STATE 40909 
 
On April 2 we delivered reftel demarche and nonpaper to 
Vladimir Prokhorov of the MFA's New Challenges and Threats 
Department, notifying the GOR that the U.S. had designated 
Iran's Defense Industries Organization as a WMD proliferator 
under Executive Order 13382.  On April 12, Prokhorov 
responded that the GOR was considering a review of Iran's DIO 
activity.  We also delivered reftel nonpaper to Aleksandr 
Deyneko, of the MFA's Department for Disarmament and Security 
Affairs.
