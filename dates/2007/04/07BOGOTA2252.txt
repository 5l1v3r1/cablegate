SUBJECT: DELIVERY OF DESIGNATION FOR IRAN'S DIO 
 
REF: SECSTATE 40909 
 
This cable confirms delivery of the non-paper to GOC 
designating Iran's Defense Industries Organization under E.O. 
13382 (reftel).  The non-paper was delivered to the Colombian 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and to Colombia's financial 
intelligence unit, Unidad de Investigacion y Analysis 
Financiera (UIAF), on March 30, following public announcement 
in the U.S.  The GOC had no immediate response to the 
designation.
