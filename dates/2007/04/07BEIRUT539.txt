SUBJECT: LEBANON: NO DELEGATION FROM BEIRUT TO WORLD 
METEOROLOGICAL ORGANIZATION CONGRESS 
 
REF: STATE 47359 
 
Polchief conveyed reftel points to Oussama Khacchab, Americas 
and Multilateral Affairs officer at the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs.  After consulting with his colleagues over two days, 
Khacchab replied that the GOL was not focused on the upcoming 
World Meteorological Organization Congress and has no plans 
to send a delegation to Geneva to attend.  The Lebanese 
delegation to international organizations in Geneva will 
represent Lebanon at the Congress and will take USG positions 
into consideration.
