SUBJECT: DESIGNATION OF IRANIAN ENTITIES AND INDIVIDUALS 
 
REF: STATE 148605 
 
(SBU) On October 26, POL Chief delivered reftel fact sheet 
and non-paper to Ambassador Ellen Nee-Whang, Chief Director 
and number three official in Ghana's MFA, and urged the GOG 
to consider similar steps.  Nee-Whang expressed appreciation 
for USG consultations with Ghana on Iran sanctions.  She 
stressed that Ghana is committed to supporting the existing 
UNSC resolutions on Iran, and said the October 25 
designations appear consistent with those resolutions.  She 
stressed that Ghana favors efforts that will encourage 
dialogue and a diplomatic solution to these issues.
