¶1.  ConGen Munich contact Josef Priller, of Sitec's sales 
department, regarding the questions outlined in reftel.   Priller 
provided the following answers: 
 
a) Sitec's customers:  Sitec's typical customers are commercial 
aircraft manufacturers located worldwide. 
 
b) Ties to China:  Sitec has no direct business relationship with 
firms in China.  Since May 2001 Sitec has been a subsidiary of 
Accuron Technologies, a Singapore-based conglomerate.  Since 1995 
Accuron has run an aerospace component operation in China in the 
Export Processing Zone of the Suzhou Industrial Park, which produces 
airfoils, engine mounts, and case modules.  However, Sitec and 
Accuron's operation in China do not trade parts with each other. 
 
c) U.S. export controls:  Sitec management understands the 
restrictions of the U.S. Munitions List. 
 
¶2.  Post found no indication that Sitec is acting in a manner 
inconsistent with U.S. export controls, or was not forthcoming in 
its answers to our questions.
