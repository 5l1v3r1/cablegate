SUBJECT: INDONESIA DEMARCHE DELIVERED: HAMAS AND FATAH 
AGREE IN PRINCIPLE TO NATIONAL UNITY GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 16453 
 
We delivered reftel demarche to the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs' Middle East and International Security and 
Disarmament Directorates.  In a statement read to poloff, the 
Middle East Directorate said, "The Government of Indonesia 
welcomes and supports the Mecca Agreement between Fatah and 
Hamas, and expects it will be implemented though the 
formation of a national unity government in the near future. 
The GOI considers the agreement to be an important 
achievement and strategic move, and hopes the international 
community, and especially the Quartet, will give the 
agreement positive consideration."  Local media have also 
reported that Foreign Minister Hassan Wirajuda said the 
Government of Indonesia has congratulated the Palestinian 
authority, "especially Hamas, which succeeded in forming a 
unity government.  We hope that the process of dialogue and 
peace with Israel will move forward better than before."
