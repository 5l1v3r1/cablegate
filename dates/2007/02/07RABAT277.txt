¶2.  (SBU) Per reftel, Poloff on February 9 informed Azzedine 
Farhane, Chief of the United Nations Division at the Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs, of the U.S.-Russia joint outreach plan 
for seven prospective partner nations to endorse the Global 
Initiative Statement of Principles following the Ankara 
meeting.  Farhane welcomed the initiative, adding that 
Morocco is fully supportive of efforts to enlarge GI 
membership in order to make the Initiative as robust and 
comprehensive as possible.  Farhane pledged to expeditiously 
pass the information to MFA Secretary General Omar Hilale, 
who will be heading the Moroccan delegation in Ankara.
