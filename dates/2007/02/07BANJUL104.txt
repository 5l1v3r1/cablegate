SUBJECT: THE GAMBIA: IAEA REPORT ON IRANIAN COMPLIANCE WITH 
UNSCR 1737 
 
REF: STATE 23001 
 
¶1. (SBU) Ambassador made reftel demarche February 26 to 
Foreign Affairs Secretary of State (Foreign Minister) Bala 
Garba Jahumpa, stressing Iran's failure to comply with UNSCR 
1737 and ongoing defiance of the international community over 
its nuclear activities.   Ambassador urged the GOTG's support 
for international efforts to dissuade Iran from its continued 
defiance and secure its cooperation with the IAEA and 
compliance with UNSCR 1737. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Jahumpa took Ambassador's presentation on board, but 
made no comment.   We will report any substantive GOTG 
response via septel.
