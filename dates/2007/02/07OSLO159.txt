SUBJECT: NORWAY SUPPORTS IGAD SUMMIT 
 
REF: STATE 18712 
 
On February 15 we delivered reftel points to Kjell Harald 
Dalen, the MFA's Special Envoy to Somalia.  He noted that 
Norway supports a Kenyan-hosted IGAD summit with respect to 
the state of the North-South Comprehensive Peace Agreement 
(CPA) implementation.  The Norwegians agree that the February 
20 IGAD meeting is too soon, and have shared this view with 
the Kenyans.
