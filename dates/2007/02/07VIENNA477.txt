¶1.  (SBU) On February 20, post conveyed reftel information on the 
domestic USG designations of three additional Iranian entities to 
Elisabeth Ottawa, Terrorism Financing Coordinator in the Ministry of 
Finance's (MoF) Financial Markets Directorate.  Post also passed the 
information to the MFA's International Security Cooperation Office 
and to the Ministry of Economics' Directorate General for Foreign 
Trade Policy. 
 
¶2. (SBU) In a follow-up with the MoF's Financial Markets Unit, 
Angelika Schloegel told us that she passed the information to the 
MoF's Export Guarantee Division, which in turn informed the 
Kontrollbank (Ex-Im Bank equivalent).
