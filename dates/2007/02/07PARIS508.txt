SUBJECT: FRANCE: NOMINEES FOR CONFERENCE IN EUROPE ON 
MODERN INSURGENCY 
 
REF: STATE 12490 
 
¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, Embassy Paris nominates the following 
individuals to participate in the March 26-27 European 
conference entitled, "A Comprehensive Approach to Modern 
Insurgency: Afghanistan and Beyond." 
 
a.) Philippe Carre, A/S-Equivalent Director for Strategic 
Affairs, Security and Disarmament, MFA.  If not available, 
post recommends his deputy, Francois Richier, PDAS-Equivalent 
for Strategic Affairs, Security and Disarmament, MFA 
 
b.) Pierre Thenard, DAS-Equivalent for Security in the Bureau 
of Strategic Affairs, Security and Disarmament, MFA 
 
c.) Jean de Ponton d,Amecourt, Director for Strategic 
Affairs, MOD 
 
d.) Bertrand Besancenot, Diplomatic Advisor to the Minister, 
MOD 
 
e.) Jean-Bernard Vernon, Counselor to the Chief of Strategy, 
Post Conflict Reconstruction, French Development Agency 
 
f.) Herve Magro, DAS-Equivalent for Democratic Governance, 
Bureau of Policy and Development, General Directorate for 
Cooperation and International Development, MFA  (Note: This 
is the USAID-Equivalent) 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post has not yet identified a candidate from the 
National Police but will convey the nominee septel.
