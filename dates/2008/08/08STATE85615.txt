------------------------------------------ EVACUATION AUTHORITY AND DEPARTMENT POLICY ------------------------------------------ 
Â¶1. Effective the date of this telegram August 9,2008 I hereby approve authorized departure (AD)of eligible family members from Tbilisi to Yerevan. Eligible family members who are currently outside of Tbilisi and unable to safely return may proceed to the nearest contiguous country. Members of Households (MOHs) who are U.S. citizens are treated as private American citizens for evacuation purposes. Pursuant to the Department of State's "no double standard" policy, private American citizens should be offered the same evacuation opportunities/assistance as members of the official American community, when appropriate and feasible.
