SUBJECT: South China SEZ Blog Now Online at Communities @ State 
 
¶1. U.S. Consulate General Guangzhou is pleased to announce its 
newest communication tool -- South China SEZ -- an interactive blog 
hosted on the OpenNet's Communities @ State website.  South China 
SEZ is a "special exchange zone" on the economic and political 
issues affecting the Pearl River Delta (PRD) and other parts of the 
four provinces that make up Guangzhou's consular district -- 
Guangdong, Fujian, Guangxi and Hainan. 
 
------------------------- 
What's in South China SEZ 
------------------------- 
 
¶2. Topics discussed on South China SEZ will include economic 
conditions, trade, intellectual property rights (IPR), local 
politics, labor, human rights, health issues, energy and the 
environment as they relate to the consular district's diverse 
population of 225 million people.  Postings will feature Qily press 
summaries, weekly excerpts from South China's online chatrooms, 
selections from and supplements to our front-channel cable 
reporting, links to media articles and informal commentary on 
developments in South China.  The postings will be archived by 
subject, and readers can subscribe online to receive email 
notifications of new content. 
 
----------------------------- 
Where to find South China SEZ 
----------------------------- 
 
¶3. We invite you to log on and check out South China SEZ at: 
http://www.intelink.gov/communities/state/sou thchinasez 
Read about the latest developments in South China and post your own 
comments.
