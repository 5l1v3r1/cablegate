SUBJECT: AT LEAST 16 INJURED, DRIVER KILLED IN NEW 
BULLDOZER ATTACK 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Local press and police report at least 19 people 
were injured (one seriously) when a Palestinian drove a heavy 
front-loader into several cars (overturning one) on King 
David Street at approximately 1400 local.  A Border Policeman 
and armed civilian shot and killed the driver, Ghassan Abu 
Tir, from Umm Tuba (East Jerusalem), who is reportedly a 
relative of jailed Hamas PLC member Muhammad Abu Tir. 
 
¶2.  (U) PA President Mahmud Abbas (Abu Mazen), who was 
meeting with Israeli President Shimon Peres in Jerusalem when 
the attack occurred, told local press that he condemns the 
attack. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) There have not been any claims of responsibility 
for the attack as of 1630 local.
