(SBU)  On July 23, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade 
(MOFAT) Disarmament and Non-proliferation Deputy Division 
Director Lee Sang-soo informed Econoff that the ROKG had 
recently designated the Iranian entities listed in reftel. 
Lee added that the Ministry of Strategy and Finance monitors 
USG terrorist finance designations via the internet on a 
daily basis and had designated these particular entities two 
days after the fact sheet was posted on the State Department 
web-site.
