SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON NAM FOREIGN MINISTERS MEETING: OMAN 
 
REF: STATE 74879 
 
(SBU) Pol-Econ Chief delivered reftel demarche on July 16 to 
Humeid al-Maani, Office Director for Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs Secretary General Sayyid Badr al-Busaidi.  Maani 
confirmed that Sayyid Badr would represent Oman at the 
meeting of non-aligned movement (NAM) foreign ministers in 
Tehran later in the month, but provided no substantive 
response to the demarche.  He stated that he would pass our 
message directly to Sayyid Badr for consideration.
