SUBJECT: ISRAEL: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON NEW U.S. POLICY ON 
CLUSTER MUNITIONS 
 
REF: STATE 73257 
 
PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Rodica Radian-Gordon, MFA 
Director for Arms Control on July 16.  The Defense Attache 
Office also passed the demarche to the office of IDF/J5 
General Amir Eshel on July 17.  Radian-Gordon said the GOI 
was already aware of the new cluster munitions policy and 
would be discussing the impact and details of this policy in 
both the regular, bilateral cluster munitions working group 
(CMWG) meetings and during the strategic dialogue taking 
place with Undersecretary Rood later on July 16 (septel).
