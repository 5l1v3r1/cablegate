SUBJECT: IRAN -- DESIGNATION OF ADDITIONAL ENTITIES 
 
REF: STATE 76596 
 
¶1.  (U) This message is Sensitive but Unclassified--Please 
handle accordingly. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) SUMMARY:  Mission has reviewed the designations of 
11 additional Iranian entities with the GOI.  Our contact 
said he would follow up, circulating the information through 
the government's interagency process.  END SUMMARY. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) On July 23, Dep/Pol/C reviewed reftel designations 
with Andy Rachmianto, Deputy Director for International 
Security and Disarmament at the Department of Foreign 
Relations.  Dep/Pol/C said the July 8 designations were done 
under U.S. law.  That said, the designations were also fully 
in compliance with UN Security Council Resolutions 1737, 1747 
and 1803, which required all UN member states to freeze the 
assets of entities and individuals listed in the annexes of 
those resolutions. 
 
¶4.  (SBU) Per reftel, Dep/Pol/C urged the Indonesian 
government to take steps--including placing a freeze--should 
assets be available for any of the entities. 
 
¶5.  (SBU) Rachmianto said Indonesia was also in the process 
of implementing the three UNSCR's.  He said he would review 
the U.S. information carefully and circulate it through the 
GOI interagency process.
