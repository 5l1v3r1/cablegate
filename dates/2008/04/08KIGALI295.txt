SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON DARFUR EQUIPMENT AND 
TRAINING PROGRAM 
 
REF: STATE 41413 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  DATT and Pol/Econ Chief delivered reftel demarche 
on April 25 to General Patrick Nyamvumba, J4 at the Ministry 
of Defense.  PAE contractor Riaan Slabbert and AF/SPG 
contractor Bob Goosey accompanied.  USG team presented a 
training and support services list to General Nyamvumba, 
which the General greeted with great enthusiasm, noting that 
it tracked very closely with an equipment list submitted by 
the Rwanda Defense Forces several months ago.  Mission POC 
for this program are: the Defense Attache's Office, Major Ron 
Miller and Sergeant First Class Jonathan Platt. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Mission responds as follows to reftel paragraph 8 
items "a" through "d": 
 
a) Delivery of diplomatic note:  delivered 
b) Host government POC:  General Patrick Nyamvumba, J4 
(logistics chief). 
c) Customs and VAT exemptions:  General Nyamvumba pledged to 
work to provide this as soon as possible. 
d) Storage warehouse:  General Nyamvumba offered in principle 
to provide secure space without charge at Kanombe Military 
facility, near to Kigali's international airport, subject to 
inspection by PAE and agreement on twenty-four access 
procedures.
