(U) External Economic Relations Advisor to the General Director of 
Ulan-Ude Aviation Plant Sergey Kritsky informed us on April 9 that 
his company would withdraw the reftel application.  According to 
Kritsky, a new application for the commodity would be submitted with 
the Russian Federal Customs Service, Siberian Department, Buryatia 
Branch listed as the end-user.  He also told us that, contrary to 
reftel, the helicopter on which the infrared searchlight would be 
installed is owned by the Russian Federal Customs Service.  When 
giving background on the transaction in question, Kritsky named a 
Russian company, Avia Service Intel as the foreign intermediate 
consignee.
