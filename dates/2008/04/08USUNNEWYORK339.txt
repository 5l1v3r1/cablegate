SUBJECT: 63RD UNGA - ICJ ELECTION - SOMALI REPUBLIC 
CANDIDATE - DR. ABDULQAWI AHMED YUSUF 
 
¶1.  USUN has received a diplomatic note from the Permanent 
Mission of the Somali Republic requesting support for Dr. 
Abdulqawi Ahmed Yusuf, candidate from the Transitional 
Federal Government of Somalia to the International Court of 
Justice (ICJ) at elections to be held during the 63rd UNGA. 
 
¶2.  USUN has forwarded copy of Somali Diplomatic Note and CV 
to L/UNA and will respond with the standard diplomatic note 
of noncommittal acknowledgment.
