SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED:  U.S. PROPOSED PAPERS FOR THE 
NPT PREPCOM 
 
REF: STATE 43044 
 
We delivered reftel U.S. proposed papers and introductory 
non-paper April 24 to MFA Disarmament Department Director Aly 
Sirry and Arab League Disarmament Department Director Wael 
Al-Assad.  Both said they would study the papers for 
discussion with the U.S. delegation at the upcoming NPT 
PrepCom in Geneva.  Sirry said he would send the U.S. 
proposed papers immediately to the Egyptian Mission in 
Geneva.
