SUBJECT: RWANDA SUPPORTS US POSITION AT NPT PREPCOM 
 
REF: A. A: SECSTATE 45218 
     ¶B. B: KIGALI 301 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche on the Nuclear 
Non-Proliferation Treaty (NPT) Preparatory Committee meeting 
to Ben Rutsinga, Director for International Organizations in 
the Rwandan Ministry of Foreign Affairs, on May 2.  Rutsinga 
expressed his government's continuing support for the NPT and 
for the areas of USG engagement outlined in demarche talking 
points, and said he would share them with the Rwandan 
delegation in Geneva. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Rutsinga also stated it was likely that an African 
nation besides Zimbabwe would put forward a nominee to chair 
the 2009 NPT Preparatory Committee, but implied that Rwanda 
would not (reftel B).
