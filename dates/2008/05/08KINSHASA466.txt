SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: 60-DAY REPORT TO UN SECURITY 
COUNCIL ON UNSCR 1803 
 
REF: STATE 48615 
 
Poloff delivered reftel demarche and a copy of the USG's 
60-day report on May 21 to Alice Kimpembe of the MFA's 
International Organizations directorate.  Kimpembe thanked 
poloff for the paper, acknowledging U.S. engagement on the 
issue.  She affirmed DRC support for UNSCR 1803 and all 
related initiatives.  She did not have any information on the 
DRC's 60-day report, but promised to inquire into this with 
the chief of the International Organizations directorate.
