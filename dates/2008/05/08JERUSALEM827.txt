SUBJECT: JENIN: INTERIOR MINISTER VISITS TO GAGE PROGRESS, 
PASF CONTINUE VILLAGE PATROLS 
 
REF: JERUSALEM 817 
 
¶1.  (U) PA Minister of the Interior (MoI) Abd al-Raziq 
al-Yahya met with Governor Qaddura Musa and PA security 
forces (PASF) commanders in Jenin on May 13 to measure the 
security operation's progress.  Al-Yahya criticized IDF 
incursions into Jenin but said the PA will overcome all 
obstacles and impose law and order and improve the economic 
situation. 
 
PA Patrols Southern Villages 
----------------------------- 
 
¶2.  (SBU) According to Jenin NSF commander BG Sulayman Imran, 
PASF conducted presence patrols and searched for wanted 
militants and criminals in the following villages 
south/southwest of Jenin: Maythalun, Siris, al-Judayda, Sir, 
Sa Nur (the Area A village, not the dismantled Israeli 
settlement) Silt al-Dahir (Area B), Attara, Jaba' (Area B), 
Bizzariya and Fandaqumiya (Area B). 
 
 
PA Coordinating for Operation SE of Jenin 
----------------------------------------- 
 
¶3.  (SBU) BG Imran told local press that the PA will expand 
operations near Tubas and al-Fara'a Refugee Camp in coming 
days.  He said the PA had not received notice from the IDF 
that it would limit operations in the Jenin area, according 
to terms outlined by Tony Blair's announcement, and said the 
issue of security responsibility is still being discussed by 
the PA and GOI.
