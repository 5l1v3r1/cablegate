SUBJECT: GOI PRESSED TO PUBLICLY SUPPORT NEW SPECIAL 
REPRESENTATIVE OF THE UN SECGEN FOR AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 25020 
 
¶1. (SBU) Acting PolCouns delivered reftel demarche to MEA 
Undersecretary (United Nations Political) Vishwesh Negi on 
March 12, urging the GOI to publicly support the new 
Special Representative of the UN Secretary General for 
Afghanistan, Kai Eide.  Negi noted our request and said 
that he would discuss it internally, but made not 
commitment. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) COMMENT: Post expects the GOI will offer public 
support for Eide, and will report separately if/when such a 
statement is made.
