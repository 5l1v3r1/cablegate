SUBJECT: NETHERLANDS: DUTCH SUPPORT NEW UN SPECIAL 
REPRESENTATIVE FOR AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 25020 
 
Poloffs raised reftel points with Dutch officials on March 
13, noting our full support for Ambassador Eide's appointment 
as the UN Special Representative heading the United Nations 
Assistance Mission in Afghanistan (UNAMA).  MFA Task Force 
Uruzgan Coordinator Pieter Jan Kleiweg told polmiloff March 
14 that the Dutch completely support Amb. Eide's appointment, 
including especially his role as the leading authority in the 
international community for implementing the London Compact. 
The Dutch look forward to working with Amb. Eide in 
Afghanistan, Kleiweg added.
