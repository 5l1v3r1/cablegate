SUBJECT: SAUDI ARABIA SENDING SHOURA DELEGATION TO ARAB 
INTER-PARLIAMENTARY UNION MEETING IN ERBIL 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 18226 
     ¶B. RIYADH 346 
 
¶1.  Post delivered reftel demarche via diplomatic note on 
February 27.  The Shoura Council replied on behalf of the MFA 
to PolOff on March 3.  According to Mohamed Dibaji, a 
consultant to the Shoura Council, the Assistant Head of the 
Shoura Council will lead a delegation to the Arab 
Inter-Parliamentary Union (AIPU) Meeting in Erbil.  He will 
be accompanied by two general members of the Shoura Council 
and two members of the Shoura Council who are also permanent 
members of the AIPU.
