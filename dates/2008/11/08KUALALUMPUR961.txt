SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: DESIGNATION OF IRANIAN 
ENTITIES UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: STATE 114408 
 
(SBU)  Poloff conveyed reftel demarche points related to U.S. 
designation under E.O. 13382 of the Export Development Bank 
of Iran and three other Iranian entities, to Mr. Shazyril 
Zahiran, Principal Assistant Secretary in the Malaysian 
Foreign Ministry's Disarmament and Non-Proliferation 
Division, on October 29.  Econoff also conveyed demarche 
points on October 29 to contacts at the Financial 
Intelligence Unit in Bank Negara, which routinely 
disseminates the information to financial institutions 
operating in Malaysia.  No response to the demarche has been 
received from either party as of COB November 3.  Post will 
continue to follow up.
