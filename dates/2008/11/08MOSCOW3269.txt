SUBJECT: RUSSIA RESPONSE: NO KNOWLEDGE OF RIO-PLUS-20 CONFERENCE 
PROPOSAL 
 
REF: STATE 116436 
 
Per reftel, Post informed Aleksandr Pankin, Deputy Director of the 
MFA's Department for International Organizations, of Brazil and 
South Korea's expressed intentions to host a "Rio 20" sustainable 
development summit.  Pankin said he knew nothing of such a proposal 
and could not comment on it.  Likewise, Vladimir Zakharov, director 
of the nongovernmental organization Center for Russian Environmental 
Policy and Chairman of the Commission on Environmental Policy of the 
Public Chamber under the President of the Russian Federation, said 
he had heard nothing about the proposal.  However, Zakharov said he 
would ask his contacts in the presidential administration at his 
next opportunity.
