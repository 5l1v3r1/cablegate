SUBJECT: TURKEY: DTP CONGRATULATIONS TO PRESIDENT-ELECT 
OBAMA 
 
¶1.  (U)  The following congratulatory message was received 
for the President-elect from Co-President of the Democratic 
Society Party (DTP). 
 
Begin text: 
 
We would like to present our hearty congratulations on your 
election as the President of the United States of America. 
We hope that, your conciliatory and liberalistic messages are 
a new era's beginning. 
 
In this regard, Democratic Society Party expects democratic 
and peaceful solutions for Middle East's issues with support 
of the United States.  Accordingly, Kurdish Issue which 
problem of particular urgency and comprises Turkey, Iran, 
Iraq and Syria, in a pivotal role for Middle East's 
stability.  Our Party is ready to share sincerely our 
accumulations and visions for bring to lights the democratic 
solutions in the Middle East with your government 
 
We would like to take this opportunity to explain our best 
wishes for Your health and happiness and that of Your family. 
 
Faithfully yours 
 
Ahmet Turk                                        Emine Ayna 
Co-President of Democratic Society Party 
Co-President of Democratic Society Party 
 
End text.
