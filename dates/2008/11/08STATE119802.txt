¶1.  After careful consideration of your assessment and 
recommendations (REFTEL), I am terminating the AD and 
returning post to limited-accompanied status for working adult 
dependants effective November 10, 2008.  Evacuees may return 
to post effective day after the termination date. 
 
¶2.  Action addressees should submit an after-action report 
including lessons learned from the evacuation within fifteen 
days.  This report should be slugged for NEA, M, S/ES-O/CMS, 
CMT, and FLO. 
 
¶3.  Administrative and accounting information regarding return 
travel of evacuees will be sent septel. 
 
¶4.  Minimize considered.
