Econoff delivered reftel demarche and background information 
to Karolina Zalach, Chief of Section in the Ministry of 
Infrastructure's Department of Maritime Security.  Zalach 
said her Ministry would be very interested in looking into 
participation in the Global Maritime Partnership initiative. 
However, Zalach added, the broad scope of the GMP initiative 
would require her to consult with other ministries and other 
offices within the Ministry of Infrastructure.  She said she 
would contact the embassy after she had the opportunity to 
consult with other ministries and offices.
