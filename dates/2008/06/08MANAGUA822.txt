NICARAGUA CONCURRENCE 
 
REF: COMSOCSOUTH (DTG 131719Z JUN 08) 
 
¶1.  (U) This message responds to request in reftel A for 
Embassy concurrence on planned Joint Combined Exchange 
Training (JCET) SNU2009JCET603W, 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Per request in para 2 of reftel, Embassy Managua 
concurs with the proposed JCET for FY09.  We provide the 
following concurrence: 
 
-- Ambassador Paul A. Trivelli, U.S. Ambassador to Nicaragua 
-- LTC Micheal Regan, Commander, U.S. Military Group Nicaragua 
-- Maj Gen Julio Cesar Aviles Castillo, Chief of Staff, 
Nicaraguan Army 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  Post confirms that a Leahy Human Rights Unit 
Vetting for the Nicaraguan Army's Special Operations Command 
(Comando de Operaciones Especiales or "COE") has been 
approved and is currently valid.  Post requested Leahy 
Vetting for the COE via Managua 150 (DTG 072254Z FEB 08) and 
received affirmative response from Department of State via 
SecState 19181 (DTG 261735Z FEB 08).  Per that response, 
Embassy Managua verifies that, as of February 25, 2008, the 
Department of State possesses no credible information of 
gross violations of human rights by the Special Operations 
Command (COE) of the Nicaraguan military.  Per Leahy SOP, 
this vetting for the COE is valid through February 24, 2009.
