¶2. (SBU) On June 19, poloff discussed ref A points with Riado 
Sembiring, Head of Peacekeeping Affairs in the Directorate of 
International Security and Disarmament Affairs at the 
Indonesian Department of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU).  Sembiring 
was receptive to U.S. concerns regarding the need to support 
some concrete action against sexual violence in conflict 
situations.  Sembiring said he would forward the U.S. points 
to the other units coordinating Indonesia's position on the 
follow-up to UNSCR 1325 and would ensure they reached the 
Indonesian UN delegation in time for the June 19 thematic 
debate on sexual violence in armed conflict situations. 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  Foreign Minister Wirajuda is in Kampala for the 
Foreign Ministers Conference of the Organization of the 
Islamic Conference (OIC); PermRep Natalegawa will represent 
Indonesia in the debate.
