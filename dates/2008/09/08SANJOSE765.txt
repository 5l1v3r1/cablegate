SUBJECT: COSTA RICA REQUESTS SUPPORT OF CANDIDATE FOR 
INTER-AMERICAN CHILDREN'S INSTITUTE 
 
¶1. On September 9, we received a diplomatic note from the MFA 
requesting support of Mario Viquez Jimenez, the GOCR's 
candidate for the Inter-American Children's Institute, during 
elections to be held October 15-16. Relevant text follows. 
 
¶2. BEGIN unofficial translation of diplomatic note: 
 
"The MFA has the honor to inform the Embassy that Costa Rica 
has decided to present Mario Viquez Jimenez as candidate for 
President of the Executive Board of the Inter-American 
Children's Institute for 2008-2010, in elections to be held 
October 15-16, 2008, in Ottawa, Canada, during the 
Institute's 83rd Meeting...The GOCR seeks the support of the 
U.S. for this candidacy." 
 
END TEXT. 
 
¶3. Please advise of any response that Post should transmit to 
the MFA.
