SUBJECT: FINLAND: AVIAN AND PANDEMIC INFLUENZA 
MINISTERIAL CONFERENCE DEMARCHE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 92335 
 
¶1. Econ Assistant delivered reftel points to Dr Merja 
Saarinen, Ministry of Social Affairs' Ministerial 
Counselor for Health/medical Affairs, primary POC for 
Avian Influenza, and chairwoman of a pandemic 
preparation working group at the Finnish Ministry of 
Social Affairs and Health. Points were also delivered 
to Ms Taina Aaltonen, Director, Animal Health and 
Welfare, Department of Food and Veterinary Control, 
Finnish Food Safety Authority, EVIRA. Ms Saarinen 
responded that she will head a three-person delegation 
to the sixth International Ministerial Conference on 
Avian and Pandemic Influenza in Sharm el-Sheikh, Egypt, 
October 25-26. The other members are: Ms Outi 
Kuivasniemi, Ministerial Adviser, Ministry of Social 
Affairs and Health, and Ms Gisela Blumenthal, 
Counselor, Ministry for Foreign Affairs. 
 
¶2. In response to reftel regarding announcement of 
additional pledges of assistance, Saarinen expressed 
strong support for fighting avian and pandemic 
influenza. She noted however that due to Finland's 
tight budget situation, new pledges are highly 
unlikely.
