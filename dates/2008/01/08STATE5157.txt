SUBJECT: RESPONSE TO REQUEST FOR HUMAN RIGHTS VETTING FOR 
MAJOR ROTEM ELGAD 
 
REF: TEL AVIV 000020 
 
 ¶1.  The Department of State has reviewed its files and finds 
that as of January 16, 2008, it possesses no credible 
information of gross violations of human rights by the 
individual listed below. 
 
 
LAST NAME: Elgad 
FIRST NAME: Rotem Rafael 
GENDER: Male 
DOB: 30 October 1975 
RANK/SERVICE: Major, Israel Defense Intelligence, Deputy 
Intelligence Officer for IDF West Bank Division 
 
¶2.  Post is permitted to provide the following statement to 
authorized parties: Embassy Tel Aviv verifies that the 
Department of State possesses no credible information of 
gross violations of human rights by the Israeli Defense 
Officer identified in paragraph 1 above, as of this date. 
Post may cite this cable in response. 
 
¶3.  If in the future Post becomes aware of possible human 
rights violation by the individual listed above, Post should 
inform the Department and not proceed with the training.
