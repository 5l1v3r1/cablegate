¶1. (S) The Department of Enterprise, Trade and Employment's 
Export Licensing Unit has denied Mac Aviation Group licensing 
for imports/exports into and out of Ireland, Desk Officer 
Michael Keaveney, Disarmament and Non-Proliferation, 
Department of Foreign Affairs, informed POLOFF on January 3. 
Keaveney added that Mac Aviation Group was a "known entity" 
and that Irish authorities are continuing their investigation 
into possible violations of Irish law or international 
non-proliferation agreements.  Post will continue to monitor 
this investigation.
