¶1.  The U.S. Immigration and Customs Enforcement (ICE) Attach, 
Paris, France requests the assistance of the Regional Security 
Office, Algiers, Algeria in obtaining information on Mohamed Farid 
SI LARBI.  SI LARBI is a citizen and national of Algeria who was 
granted Refugee status and allowed to enter the United States on

involvement and/or connections to terrorist groups and terrorist

polygraph concerning this new information.  The results of the exam 
concluded that SI LARBI was deceptive during his responses.
