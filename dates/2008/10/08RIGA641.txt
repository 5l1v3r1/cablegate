SUBJECT: LATVIA TO PLEDGE AT GEORGIA DONORS' CONFERENCE 
 
Ref: State 109212 
 
PolEcon Off spoke with Liga Andersone, Head of the MFA's Eastern 
Europe and Central Asia Division, and Andersone confirmed that 
Latvia's FM will attend the Ministerial Donors' Conference for 
Georgia in Brussels on October 22.  She said that the MFA's proposal 
is to have Latvia pledge 500,000 Euros, paid over three years, but 
that this proposal is still awaiting approval by the Cabinet of 
Ministers.
