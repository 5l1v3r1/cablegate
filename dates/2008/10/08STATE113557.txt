¶1.  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
pre-license check for the export of lithium batteries to 
Canada.  Post is requested to complete this Blue Lantern 
check within 30 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern 
check will affect pending and future licenses involving 
parties to this license. 
 
¶3.  Reason for request: the Foreign Consignees, Pulse Inteco 
and Semicom Lexis, have a limited licensing history with our 
office. 
 
¶4.  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, Canada 
Department of National Defense, Electronics, Simulators and 
Defense Systems, and verify its request for the exact 
quantity and specific type of battery listed below.  Please 
also confirm the intended use of the commodity, and the 
involvement of both Foreign Consignees in this transaction. 
END ACTION.
