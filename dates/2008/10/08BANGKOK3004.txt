SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: IMPLEMENTING UN SECURITY 
COUNCIL RESOLUTIONS ON IRAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 105173 
 
¶1. (U) Pursuant to reftel action request, Embassy Bangkok 
provided the Thai Ministry of Foreign Affairs Department of 
International Organizations Peace, Security and Disarmament 
Division and Department of South Asian, Middle East and 
African Affairs, Iran Desk copies of the United Nations 
Security Council (UNSC) Resolution 1835 and associated 
non-paper.  Officials of both departments advised that they 
had received copies of the resolution from their 
representatives in New York and expressed appreciation for 
the non-paper.  Neither department had any substantive 
comments or questions on the resolution or non-paper. 
 
¶2. (U) As directed, we stressed the importance of 
implementing all UNSC resolutions adopted on Iran and the 
importance of Iran abiding by its UNSC obligations, IAEA 
Board of Governors' requirements, and the provisions of the 
Treaty on the Nonproliferation of Nuclear Weapons without 
further delay.
