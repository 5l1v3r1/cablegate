SUBJECT: CANADA WILL DONATE TO UNRWA'S RECONSTRUCTION AND 
RELIEF APPEALS IN LEBANON 
 
REF: STATE 106010 
 
(SBU) On October 8 Econoff delivered reftel points to Kathryn 
Porter, Senior Policy Advisor, Middle East Peace Process, 
Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade. 
Porter advised that the GOC is totally in line with USG goals 
with regard to the need for funding for Nahr al-Bared refugee 
camp.  Porter said that as soon as internal deliberations are 
complete, the GOC will announce financial support for UNWRA's 
emergency appeal and long term goals.  She explained that the 
GOC is engaged broadly on Palestinian issues and promotes 
regional stability in Palestine and Lebanon in part by 
providing capacity building assistance to the Lebanese 
Palestinian Dialogue Committee.
