SUBJECT: IRELAND WILL NOT ATTEND IRAN'S NOVEMBER 30 NUCLEAR 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 112229 
 
On October 31, Poloff spoke with Nicholas Twist, Deputy 
Director of Disarmament and Non-Proliferation at the 
Department of Foreign Affairs.  Twist stated that Ireland had 
not yet received an invitation to this conference and that 
they would not participate.  He asserted that the EU is 
closely aligned on this issue and that no member state wishes 
to send the wrong message by participating.
