¶2.  (SBU) The Brazilian Ministry of External Relations (MRE) has 
informed Post that the official delegation to the Washington 
International Renewable Energy Conference (WIREC 2008) is as 
follows: 
 
Head of Delegation 
-- Antonio Patriota, Brazilian Ambassador to the United States 
 
Delegation 
-- Senator Jose Tenorio, President of the Senate Biofuels 
Sub-Commission 
-- Jose Sergio Gabrielli, President of Petrobras 
-- Manoel Bertone, Secretary for Production and Agro-energy, 
Ministry of Agriculture, Livestock, Farming and Supply (MAPA) 
-- Alexandre Strapasson, Director of the Department of Sugarcane and 
Agro-energy, MAPA 
-- Joao Alziro Herz Jornada, President of INMETRO (National 
Institute of Metrology, Standardization and Industrial Quality), 
Ministry of Development, Industry and Foreign Trade 
-- Ricardo Dornelles, Director of the Department of Renewable Fuels, 
Ministry of Mines and Energy 
-- Jose Miguez, Executive Secretary of the Inter-ministerial 
Commission of Climate Change, Ministry of Science and Technology 
-- Isaias de Carvalho Macedo, Interdisciplinary Nucleus of Energy 
Planning, University of Campinas 
-- Minister Andre Correa do Lago, Director of the Department of 
Energy, Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MRE) 
-- Ministro Carlos Henrique Moojen, Brazilian Embassy in Washington, 
MRE 
-- Counselor Marcus Arbizu, Organization of American States - Brazil 
Mission 
-- First Secretary Claudia Vieira dos Santos, Head of the Department 
of New and Renewable Energy Resources, MRE 
-- First Secretary Emerson Kloss, Brazilian Embassy in Washington, 
MRE 
-- First Secretary Marcelo Martinez, Department of New and Renewable 
Energy Resources, MRE 
-- Dr. Kepler Euclides Filho, the Executive-Director of Brazilian 
Agricultural Research Corporation (EMBRAPA) 
 
¶3.  (SBU) The President of the Brazilian Agricultural Research 
Corporation (EMBRAPA) Silvio Crestana, who had initially indicated 
his intent to attend WIREC (REFTEL), now will not be able to attend. 
 Dr. Kepler Euclides Filho, the Executive-Director of EMBRAPA, will 
replace him at the conference and on the Bioenergy Feedstocks Panel 
scheduled for 4:00 - 6:00pm on Wednesday, March 5, 2008.
