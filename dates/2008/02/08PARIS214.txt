SUBJECT: FRANCE SUPPORTS PRIVATE SECTOR OUTREACH AND THE 
GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT NUCLEAR TERRORISM 
 
REF: A. STATE 5399 
 
     ¶B. 2007 STATE 156602 
 
¶1. (SBU) Embassy poloff delivered reftel demarche together 
with Russian Federation terrorism expert Artem Studennikov on 
February 6 to Thomas Guibert, French MFA Strategic Affairs 
desk officer for the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear 
Terrorism.  Guibert expressed France's support in principle 
for enhancing the involvement of the private sector in the 
Global Initiative and agreed to work with the GOF interagency 
to identify France-based companies that are best positioned 
to be active and positive contributors to the Initiative. 
Guibert agreed to advise us periodically on the GOF's 
progress. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Guibert also noted that France would be submitting 
comments late this week/early next week on the Detection 
Architecture Model Guidelines Document prepared for the March 
31- April 1 expert working group meeting in Washington.  The 
GOF looks forward to participating in the meeting, and plans 
to send a delegation of five to six people.
