(SBU) We delivered reftel letter from USTR Schwab to Minister 
of Trade and Industry Rachid Rachid on February 26 and 
demarche to Dina Mohammed, Trade in Services Manager for the 
Ministry.  Mohammed said the GOE welcomes the USG request for 
bilateral negotiations on trade in services in Geneva and 
noted that Walid el-Nozahay, head of the ministry's WTO 
department, is already in Geneva.  She said that she was 
uncertain of the GOE's plans for negotiations but that she 
would seek a formal response to the request.
