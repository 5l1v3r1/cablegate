SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON WTO SERVICES NEGOTIATIONS 
 
REF: STATE 18373 
 
¶1. (SBU) Trade Affairs MinCons delivered reftel demarche points and 
letter regarding WTO services negotiations to the Ministry of 
Commerce (MOFCOM), Department of WTO Affairs Director General Zhang 
Xiangchen on February 25. Per separate instructions from USTR, MC 
Trade Affairs also previewed telephone call planned by Ambassador 
Schwab to MOFCOM Minister Chen DeMing on this subject. 
 
¶2. (SBU) DG Zhang thanked the Embassy for the letter and promised to 
have it translated and promptly delivered to Minister Chen and to 
report to him on the substance of the demarche and the upcoming call 
by Ambassador Schwab.   He said he would coordinate so that Minister 
Chen is available for the call. 
 
¶3. (SBU) DG Zhang also said that China would send a delegation to 
Geneva in March, including for services meetings , and believes that 
services should be an integral part of the undertaking of the Doha 
Round negotiations.  He noted that China has "offensive" interests 
as well in services.
