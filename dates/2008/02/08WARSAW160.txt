SUBJECT: POLAND TO ATTEND UN.GIFT CONFERENCE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 9008 
 
On February 4, PolOff met with Artur Gebal, Attache in the 
MFA,s Americas Department to deliver reftel demarche 
regarding the upcoming UN.GIFT conference in Vienna.  Gebal 
indicated that Poland shares the U.S. views on this 
conference and on its results.  He informed us that Piotr 
Mierecki, Deputy Director of Migration Policy Department in 
the Ministry of Interior will lead the Polish delegation to 
the conference.  Mierecki will also be joined by Dominika 
Kreuz, First Secretary from the Polish Mission to the UN in 
Geneva.
