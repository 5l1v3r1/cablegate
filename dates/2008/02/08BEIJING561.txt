To: The Embassy of the United States of America: 
 
 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the People's 
Republic of China presents its compliments to the Embassy 
of the United States of America, and has the honor to 
inform the latter of the following regarding diplomatic 
note 0232 by the Embassy on February 12, 2008: 
 
 The Ministry of Foreign Affairs received the note on 
February 14, 2008. According to Article 7 of the Protocol 
on the Amendment of the Civil Air Services Agreement 
between the People's Republic of China and the United 
States of America signed on July 9, 2007 in Seattle, the 
Protocol is effective as of February 14, 2008. 
 
With Highest Considerations. 
 
Beijing, February 15, 2008. 
 
End text of note.
