¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment end-use check on license number 05-050011090 
for the export of defense hardware to Spain.  Post is 
requested to complete this Blue Lantern inquiry within 45 
days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern inquiry will affect 
pending and future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: Diversion concerns; to confirm that 
commodity was actually received by the end-user. 
 
¶4. Action Madrid:   Department requests that Post make 
inquiries to identify and verify the role of all foreign 
parties involved in this transaction, confirm the bona fides 
of EADS CASA as a responsible recipient of United States 
Munitions List (USML) items, and remind them that the 
unauthorized re-export or retransfer of USML items is 
strictly prohibited.  Department further requests that Post 
determine whether or not the Service of Maritime Rescue and 
Safety (SASEMAR), Spanish Ministry of Defense placed an order 
for and received the below-listed items for the purpose 
stated on the application. 
 
End Action Request.
