SUBJECT: BRASILIA COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST - DR. SUZANNE WESTMAN, 
DR. ARISTIDES BARBOSA AND MRS. ERIN JOHNSON 
 
 
¶1. Embassy Brasilia requests country clearance for Dr. Suzanne 
Westman, Dr. Aristides Barbosa and Mrs. Erin Johnson for August 03, 
¶2006. 
 
¶2. The purpose of this visit is to view access to care and HIV 
testing services for high risk migratory groups along the corridor 
between Guyana and Brazil; to conduct a joint (CDC GAP Guyana - CDC 
GAP Brazil)preliminary field visit to the area. Informal meetings 
would be held with Regional Health authorities to get an 
understanding of their view of the need for outreach and provision 
of services. The CDC GAP Guyana Office would travel with a letter of 
introduction from the Guyana Minister of Health to Guyana Regional 
Medical authorities in the border area. Similarly the CDC GAP Brazil 
Office would have a letter of introduction from the Brazilian 
Regional Medical authorities. No formal interview of high risk 
populations or patient data would be collected at this first level 
of proposal development. A CDC vehicle from Georgetown would meet 
the Braslia team in Boa Vista, Brazil for the drive back to Lethem, 
Guyana. 
 
¶3. If concurrence is granted, Dr. Suzanne Westman and party would be 
picked up by the CDC Guyana team in Boa Vista and propose to drive 
from Boa Vista to Lethem in CDC Guyana official vehicle DPL 5-28, 
Thursday, August 03, 2006 and return to Boa Vista on Friday, August 
4, 2006 with return from Boa Vista to Braslia on Saturday, August 
5, 2006.
