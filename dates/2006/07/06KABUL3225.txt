SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE GRANTED TO ERIC PELOFSKY 
 
¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Eric Pelofsky, Attorney-Adviser, 
Office of the Legal Adviser for Political Military Affairs, 
and grants country clearance for his visit to Afghanistan on 
July 20-24, 2006, for consultations.  Control Officer is 
Idris Rahimi, Political Officer, email address: 
RahimiIA@state.gov, telephone 011-937-010-8160 or cell number 
011-937-020-1563.  All travelers should bring USG ID for 
identification purposes and to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2. Embassy Kabul will provide transportation from the airport 
and lodging. 
 
¶3. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
