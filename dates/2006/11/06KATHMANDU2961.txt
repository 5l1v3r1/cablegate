On November 3, Emboff delivered reftel talking points to 
Narayan Dev Pant, Under Secretary in the United Nations, 
International Organizations, and International Law Section of 
the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, highlighting the need for 
Nepal to disassociate itself from the NAM sponsorship of 
these resolutions.  Pant agreed with talking points, and 
promised to take them up to his superiors as soon as possible.
