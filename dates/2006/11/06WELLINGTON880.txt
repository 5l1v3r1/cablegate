SUBJECT:  NEW ZEALAND RESPONSE TO U.S. DIPLOMATIC NOTE FOR ITU RADIO 
REGULATIONS BOARD CANDIDATE 
 
REF:  State 168693 
 
¶1.  Embassy delivered reftel diplomatic notes to the New Zealand 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT) and a copy to the New 
Zealand Ministry of Economic Development. 
 
¶2.  Post received the following diplomatic note from MFAT in 
response. 
 
¶3.  Begin text of diplomatic note, dated 1 November, 2006: 
 
"The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade presents its compliments 
to the Embassy of the United States of America and has the honour to 
refer to the Embassy's Note No. 62-06 of 12 October 2006 informing 
 
the Ministry that the United States has nominated Julie Napier 
Zoller for election to the International Telecommunications Union's 
(ITU) Radio Regulations Board at the elections to take place at the 
ITU Plenipotentiary Conference which will be held from 6-24 November 
2006 in Antalya, Turkey. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade wishes to assure the 
Embassy of the United States of America that New Zealand will give 
careful consideration to this candidature. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade takes this opportunity to 
renew to the Embassy of the United States of America the assurances 
of its highest consideration." 
 
McCormick
