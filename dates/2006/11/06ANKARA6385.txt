(SBU) We delivered ref a letter from U/S Joseph to MFA Deputy 
U/S for Security Affairs Refat Akgunay on November 7.  We 
followed up November 9 with MFA Arms Control official Sirhan 
Yigit, who informed us that the GOT fully supports UNSCR 
1718, welcomes U.S. views, and will carefully consider the 
points in U/S Joseph's letter in preparing its implementation 
strategy for UNSCR 1718.
