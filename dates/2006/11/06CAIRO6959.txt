SUBJECT: BMENA: EGYPTIAN FOREIGN MINISTER TO HEAD GOE 
DELEGATION TO FORUM FOR THE FUTURE MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 189502 
 
(U) MFA CABINET OFFICER ASHRAF SWEILAM TOLD POLOFF ON 
NOVEMBER 22 THAT EGYPTIAN FOREING MINISTER AHMED ABOUL GHEIT 
WOULD LEAD THE EGYPTIAN DELEGATION TO THE THIRD BMENA FORUM 
FOR THE FUTURE MEETING TO BE HELD AT THE DEAD SEA IN JORDAN 
FROM NOVEMBER 30 TO DECEMBER 1.  ABOUL GHEIT WILL BE 
ACCOMPANIED BY HIS ASSISTANT MINISTER FOR REGIONAL 
INITIATIVES, AMBASSADOR MOHAMED SHAABAN, HIS MIDDLE EAST 
PEACE PROCESS ADVISOR AND CABINET STAFF MEMBER EZZEDINE 
SHUKRY, MFA HUMAN RIGHTS OFFICE DIRECTOR IHAB GAMALEDIN, AND 
SWEILAM HIMSELF.
