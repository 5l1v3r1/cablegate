SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST FOR JARED CAPLAN TO 
TRAVEL TO DAMASCUS 
 
 
¶1.  Embassy Kuwait requests country clearance for consular 
officer Jared S Caplan to travel to Embassy Damascus to 
provide TDY consular assistance for the period November 1 - 
November 17, 2006. CA/EX has provided Caplan with TDY travel 
orders for his travel from Washington, DC to Damascus. 
 
¶2.  Official travel schedule is as follows: 
 
Wednesday, November 1 depart Kuwait City, Kuwait at 1615 via 
RB342, arriving in Damascus at 1715. 
 
Friday, November 17 depart Damascus, Kuwait at 1215 via 
RB341, arriving in Kuwait at 1515 
 
¶3.  Caplan understands that an embassy expeditor will meet 
and assist him on arrival and will take him to TDY housing. 
He further understands that motorpool assistance will only be 
provided for the first morning, Thursday, November 2. 
 
¶4.  Caplan has TS clearance and will carry positive US 
Department of State identification. 
 
¶5.  US Embassy Kuwait thanks US Embassy Damascus for its 
assistance
