SUBJECT: NICARAGUA SUPPORTS HELMS-BURTON RESOLUTION AND THE 
SANTO DOMINGO DECLARATION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 87933 
 
Classified By: Ambassador Paul A. Trivelli. Reasons 1.4 (B,D). 
 
(C) Nicaraguan Foreign Minister Norman Caldera informed the 
DCM on June 1 that the Nicaraguan government fully supports 
the U.S. position on the Helms-Burton Resolution and the 
Santo Domingo Declaration and believes the text should not be 
altered.  Caldera will be attending the OAS General Assembly 
in Santo Domingo and promised that he "would see too it that 
we stick together on this."
