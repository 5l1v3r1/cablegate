SUBJECT: GOE RESPONDS TO DEMARCHE ON ICANN MANAGEMENT OF 
THE DNS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 89981 
 
On June 4, Econoff delivered reftel demarche inviting GOE 
input on the transition of the Internet's Domain Name and 
Addressing System (DNS) management to ICANN, and explaining 
the means for communicating such input.  The Director of 
International Relations for the Ministry of Communication and 
Information Technology had no immediate reaction, but said 
that she is "sure the GOE will have input on this important 
issue."
