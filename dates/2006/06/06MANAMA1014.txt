¶1. (SBU) Per reftel request, Post was notified in April 2006 
of a potentially qualifying dispute by a collection agency 
operating on behalf of Claimant A.  Post is unaware of any 
other U.S. person-GoB dispute or expropriation claims.  Post 
has no previous disputes to update.

owed USD 19 thousand by the Bahrain Air Force and that 
attempts to engage the Bahrain Air Force have been 
unsuccessful.  Post has requested, but has thus far not 
received, details regarding the claimant and claim, including 
information that would confirm that the claimant would meet 
the definition of a U.S. person.
