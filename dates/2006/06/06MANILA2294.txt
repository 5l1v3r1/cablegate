SUBJECT: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR THE IRAQI GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: STATE 81632 
 
¶1.  (SBU) In a meeting on May 30 with Assistant Secretary of 
Foreign Affairs for African and Middle East Affairs Oscar 
Valenzuela, poloff urged the GRP to issue a statement of 
support for the new Iraqi political leadership and 
congratulate them on forming a unified government (reftel). 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Valenzuela noted that the GRP had made, and would 
continue to make, public statements supporting the Iraqi 
government.  He added that the GRP intended to maintain a 
diplomatic presence in Iraq, even though the current Charge 
d'Affaires was forced to reside in Amman for safety reasons. 
Valenzuela indicated that the GRP hoped him to return to 
Baghdad as soon as conditions permitted.
