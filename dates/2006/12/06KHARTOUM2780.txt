SUBJECT:  COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST FOR POLOFF NATHAN HOLT 
 
 
¶1. (SBU) Nathan Holt, Embassy Khartoum Political Officer, requests 
country clearance to visit Embassy Nairobi for consultations with 
KUSLO on December 5.  The purpose of the travel is to hold 
discussions with KUSLO, Kenya Department of Defense (KDOD) 
personnel, and representatives of the Sudan People's Liberation 
Movement/Army (SPLM/A) over potential training and other assistance 
to the SPLM/A in Southern Sudan.  We understand that KDOD will 
provide the venue for some meetings, but that others may occur at 
the Embassy.  Mr. Holt has a valid Top Secret clearance. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Mr. Holt expects to arrive in Nairobi on Monday, December 
4, via Kenya Airways arriving from Entebbe, Uganda at 16:20.  He 
expects to depart Nairobi on December 6, en route to Juba, Sudan. 
He has not made final arrangements for the departure flight, but 
will do so before traveling to Kenya. 
 
¶3. (U) Mr. Holt does not require airport pick-up. 
 
¶4. (U) Mr. Holt would appreciate assistance in making hotel 
reservations.  Please make reservations at either the Serena or the 
Norfolk hotel in Nairobi for the nights of December 5 and 6, as long 
as single room rates for those hotels are within per diem. 
 
¶5. (U) Thanks for assistance.
