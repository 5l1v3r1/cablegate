SUBJECT:  Darfur:  UN Advisors on the Ground 
 
Ref:  a) Khartoum 02917, b) Khartoum 02918, b) Khartoum 02904, c) 
 
Khartoum 02908 
 
¶1.  On the morning of December 28, UN Chief Political Advisor Bashua 
Abeyedun spoke to Pol/Econ Counselor fromEl Fasher, Darfur, and 
advised that 36 UN police advisors and military staff officers had 
arrived.  This represents the first group of UN advisors to be 
dispatched toward the planned total of 105 provided for in Phase I, 
the "light package," of the UN/African Union peacekeeping effort in 
Darfur. 
 
¶2.  Abeyedun noted that UN Deputy Special Representative Manuel Da 
Silva had presented the delegation to AU Force Commander Major 
General Aprezi in a formal ceremony the same morning.
