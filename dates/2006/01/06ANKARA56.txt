¶1. (U) This cable constitutes the monthly Visas Viper report 
for Embassy Ankara and Consulates Istanbul and Adana. 
Consulates submit Visas Viper reports to Ankara for 
inclusion in the Embassy's Visa Viper working group report. 
 
¶2. (U) Visas Viper meetings were held by Embassy Ankara on 
03 January, by Consulate General Istanbul on 30 December, 
and by Consulate Adana on 29 December, to review on-going 
Visas Viper program activities throughout U.S. Mission 
Turkey.  Representatives of CONS, DEA, ECON, FBI, OSI, PMA, 
POL, RAO and RSO participated. A total of 212 additional 
names were reported (REFTEL). 
 
¶3. (U) The Visas Viper Officer for Ankara, Turkey is Sharon 
Gordon, who can be reached by e-mail at GordonSE@state.gov.
