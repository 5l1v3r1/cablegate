Drawing from points conveyed reftel, poloff urged the GOM to 
press actively for the full implementation of UNSCR 1701 
during a September 13 meeting with Abdel Fattah Kadiri, 
Director of UN and International Organizations Affairs at the

political and material support" for the Seniora government

that a strong central government was key to restoring 
stability and peace to Lebanon.
