SUBJECT: KAZAKHSTAN: INVITATION TO IRAQ CONFERENCE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 155973 
 
¶1. (U) Pol-Econ Chief conveyed reftel demarche and 
invitation letter addressed to FM Tokayev to Daniyar 
Sarekenov of the MFA's Middle East division on September 22. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Sarekenov noted that in the absence of FM Tokayev, 
currently in New York for UNGA, he would forward the letter 
to Deputy FM Abdrakhmanov for immediate consideration.  He 
indicated that the MFA would coordinate its response with 
the MOD, but did not speculate as to whether Kazakhstan 
would ultimately send a civilian representative.
