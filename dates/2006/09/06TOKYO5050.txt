SUBJECT: ADDITIONAL QUESTIONS FROM JAPAN REGARDING GNEP 
STATEMENT OF PRINCIPLES 
 
REF: SECSTATE 139918 
 
¶1. (SBU) THIS IS AN ACTION MESSAGE. 
 
¶2. (SBU) On September 5, science officer spoke to MOFA,s 
International Nuclear Energy Cooperation Division Deputy 
Director Takayuki Kitagawa regarding GNEP,s Statement of 
Principles (Reftel).  Science officer provided additional 
background to Kitagawa on the Statement of Principles 
concept. After the exchange, Kitagawa raised a series of 
questions that Japan would like Washington to address before 
the Japanese side responds. 
 
¶3. (SBU) The questions are as follows: 
 
Background: 
Is this statement legally binding? 
 
Does the U.S. alone issue this statement or are you thinking 
in a form of a joint statement? 
 
Who is going to receive this statement? 
 
Why does the U.S. want to formulate this statement at this 
time of the year? 
 
What would be the meaning or significance of this statement 
issuing at this time of the year? 
 
Statement: 
Which states are GNEP Partners? The six countries you listed 
before      or something else? 
 
The fourth bullet beginning with Establish supply arrangement 
among       nations.  The word --arrangement-- means treaty 
or measure? 
 
The fourth para beginning with --We are committed to comply 
strictly    with our commitments-- Please clarify what these 
commitments means.
