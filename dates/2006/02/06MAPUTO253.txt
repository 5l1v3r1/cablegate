SUBJECT: MOZAMBIQUE: DEMARCHE RESPONSE - STATEMENTS OF 
CONDEMNATION OF THE AL-ASKARIYA SHRINE BOMBING 
 
REF: STATE 28802 
 
On February 23, poloff delivered reftel demarche to Faizal 
Cassam, Directorate for Africa and the Middle East, Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs.  He gave no substantive comment at the 
time; however, in a follow-up meeting with poloff on February 
27, Cassam stated that although the Government of Mozambique 
condemned this and all acts of terrorism, the GRM had no 
plans to release a public statement denouncing the incident. 
Cassam explained that, since the GRM did not have an official 
government spokesperson, the GRM only rarely issued 
statements.
