SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR MEETS WITH NEW MINISTER OF AGRICULTURE 
 
 Â¶1. On February 26, Ambassador and USAID Director met with 
the newly appointed Minister of Agriculture and Irrigation, 
Jalal Ibrahim Fukairah.  Ambassador and Minister Fukairah 
discussed ROYG-USG cooperation on encouraging growth and 
development in the agricultural sector.  Fukairah hoped that 
USG assistance for the agricultural sector could be expanded. 
 Fukairah welcomed USAID support for improving monitoring and 
control measures to combat the spread of Avian Flu into 
Yemen.  He stated that prevention and control of Avian Flu 
would be his top priority because of the potential danger to 
public health and the possible economic damage an outbreak 
would cause.
