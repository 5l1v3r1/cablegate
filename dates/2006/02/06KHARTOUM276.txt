SUBJECT: BANK OF SOUTHERN SUDAN BEGINS OPERATIONS 
 
 
Â¶1. (U) The establishment of the Bank of Southern Sudan (BOSS) 
was made official by a public announcement dated February 4. 
The BOSS will provide the services of a central bank in 
Southern Sudan, including the regulation of the banking 
system and issuance of banking licenses.  Under the CPA, the 
banking system of Northern Sudan will continue to operate 
under Sharia law, while a conventional banking system will be 
established in Southern Sudan. 
 
2.(U) Full text of the public announcement follows: 
 
(Begin text) 
 
It is brought to the notice of the public, as the 
Comprehensive Peace Agreement (CPA) requires, we are glad to 
announce to the public that, on the first of February 2006 
the Bank of Southern Sudan (BOSS) is established and started 
operations with a staff of 45 persons (28 from SPLM caders 
(sic) 17 who were already working in the Central Bank of 
Sudan, Juba Branch). 
 
The BOSS will soon start the processes of granting licenses 
to commercial banks (domestic and foreign) which will be 
interested to operate in the Southern Sudan using 
conventional banking system as stipulated in the CPA. 
 
Signed 
Elijah Malok Aleng 
Deputy Governor, Central Bank of Sudan 
Head of Bank of Southern Sudan 
 
(End text)
