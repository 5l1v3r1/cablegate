SUBJECT: NEW ZEALAND WILL NOT ATTEND APEC SEMINAR ON 
PANDEMIC EXERCISES 
 
REF: SECSTATE 111495 
 
¶1. (SBU) On July 10, Poloff delivered reftel invitation to 
Llewellyn Roberts, Pandemic Planning Advisor, Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT), and on July 19 to MFAT 
representatives with the Americas Division and the United 
Nations, Human Rights and Commonwealth Division, which is the 
division responsible for issues related to multilateral fora. 
 On July 19, Poloff also delivered the invitation to Dr. 
Andrea Forde, Senior Public Health Advisor, Ministry of 
Health (MOH) and to Marlene Castle, Scientific Analyst, 
External Assessments Bureau, Department of Prime Minister and 
Cabinet (DPMC). 
 
¶2. (U) On July 27, Jo Horrocks; Project Manager; National 
Emergency Planning, Risk and Assurance; Ministry of Health 
responded that GNZ will not be sending anyone to attend the 
APEC Seminar because of time and budget constraints. 
 
¶3. (SBU) MOH will represent GNZ at Australia's Exercise 
Cumpston in October, Horrocks said.
