SUBJECT: SPAIN ACCEPTS BURMA'S PRESENCE AT ASEM 
 
REF: STATE 124139 
 
(SBU)   Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Deputy European 
Correspondent Victoria Ortega Gutierrez on July 31.  Ortega 
stated that Spain supports the EU compromise position that 
Burma is a member of ASEAN and is entitled to attend the ASEM 
summit at the Finn's invitation.  Ortega noted the many EU 
nations, Spain included, view the summit as an opportunity to 
raise human rights concerns with Burma.
