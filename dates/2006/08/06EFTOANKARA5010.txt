¶1. (SBU) Summary: On Wednesday, August 30, 2006 at 
approximately 1310 hours local an explosion occurred in the 
tourist port city of Mersin injuring one person.  End summary. 
 
¶2. (SBU) At approximately 1310 hours local, an explosion 
occurred in the shopping area of downtown Mersin.  TNP 
contacts report that the device (NFI) was placed in a garbage 
container in the shopping area.  One turkish woman was 
injured, but they are not considered life threatening. 
 
¶3. (SBU) There has been no claim of responsibility for this 
bombing although the KGK-affiliated Taybazen Azadiya 
Kurdistan (TAK - Turkish Freedom Hawks) has been linked to 
similar attacks in the past. 
 
¶4. (SBU) Per Reftel B, an update on the Antalya bombing:  TAK 
has claimed responsibility for the bombing.
