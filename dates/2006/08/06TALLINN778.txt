SUBJECT: ESTONIA: DONOR ASSISTANT TO LEBANON 
 
REF: STATE 135734 
 
(U) On August 25 Poloff delivered reftel points to 
Arti Hilpus, MFA Director for Security and Arms 
Control.  In addition to the $41,000 already donated, 
Hilpus said that the GOE will contribute an additional 
$25,550 in support of the World Food Program at the 
Donors Conference in Stockholm.  Hilpus also mentioned 
that the GOE intends to participate in the future 
reconstruction process in Lebanon, although no 
specific plans have been decided.
