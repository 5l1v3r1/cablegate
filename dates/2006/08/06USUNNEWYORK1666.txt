SUBJECT: RWANDA TRIBUNAL (ICTR) EXTENSION OF TENURE OF AD 
LITEM JUDGE BOSSA 
 
REF: SECSTATE 142341 
 
¶1.  The Security Council on August 29 adopted (15-0-0) 
Resolution 1705 (2006) extending Judge Bossa's term on the 
International Criminal Tribunal for Rwanda until June 24, 
¶2007. 
 
¶2.  Following the Council's action the General Assembly 
decided to endorse the extension of Bossa's term.
