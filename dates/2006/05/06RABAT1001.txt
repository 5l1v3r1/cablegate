SUBJECT: MOROCCO: TRIPARTITE DEMARCHE REQUEST ON THE UN 
DECLARATION ON THE RIGHTS OF INDIGENOUS PEOPLES 
 
REF: STATE 78740 
 
¶1. (SBU) Polcouns met May 24 with MFA Director of 
International Organizations and UN Affairs Nasser Bourita to 
discuss reftel demarche request on the UN declaration on the 
rights of indigenous peoples.  There is no Embassy of New 
Zealand or Australia in Morocco.  Polcouns left behind 
non-papers with Bourita.  Bourita had no substantive comment, 
and per reftel, post will follow up with Bourita once the MFA 
has had the opportunity to review the material. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Comment:  The GOM will review the draft declaration 
with the Western Sahara in mind.  It will scrutinize the 
draft text for any impact it might have on Morocco's claim to 
the disputed territory and on the rights of the people who 
live there.
