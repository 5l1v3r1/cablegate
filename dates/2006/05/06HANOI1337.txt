SUBJECT:  IRAN: Demarche Delivered to Vietnam 
 
REF: STATE 87682 
 
The Prime Minister and Foreign Minister were occupied at the 
National Assembly session June 1, so DCM Boardman delivered 
reftel demarche to Deputy FM Le Van Bang.  VFM Bang said the 
GVN is studying Secretary Rice's proposal on Iran and is 
considering the appropriate public response.  Vietnam is 
against proliferation of weapons of mass destruction and in 
favor of the peaceful use of nuclear energy in general, and 
wants to see diplomacy used to resolve international 
conflicts, VFM Bang said.
