SUBJECT: OMAN ESCHEWS DAMASCUS BOYCOTT MEET 
 
REF: MUSCAT 745 
 
¶1. (U) As reported in regional and international media, 
including by the Syrian Arab News Agency, Oman was not/not 
represented in a four-day conference of Israel Boycott 
Liaison Officers that began May 15 in Damascus. 
 
¶2. (SBU) A senior contact in the Foreign Ministry reiterated 
to P/E Chief May 16 that Omani officials have long ago been 
instructed to reject invitations for any meetings that 
support economic boycotts, including the Arab League boycott 
of Israel.  He underscored that Oman will continue to adhere 
steadfastly to this policy.
