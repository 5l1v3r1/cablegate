(C) Nicaraguan Foreign Minister Norman Caldera informed the 
Ambassador on May 5 that the Nicaraguan government supports 
the creation of the new Red Crystal emblem to enable Israel's 
Magen David Adom (MDA) to join the Red Cross/Red Crescent 
Movement and will so vote in the June meeting.
