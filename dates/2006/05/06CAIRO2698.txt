SUBJECT: EGYPT TO ATTEND D-8 SUMMIT IN BALI;  EGYPT TO ACT 
AS MODERATING INFLUENCE 
 
REF: STATE 72603 
 
Assistant Minister for American Affairs, Ambassador Salama 
Shaker, told Political MinCouns May 9 that Egypt would 
participate in the Bali Summit of "D-8" countries, and that 
Egypt would act as a moderating influence regarding issues of 
concern to Washington.  Shaker welcomed the U.S. views on key 
regional issues and said she would ensure the reftel talking 
points (left as a non-paper) were shared with Egypt's 
delegation.
