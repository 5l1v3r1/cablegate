SUBJECT: IRAQ:  ARAB LEAGUE INVITES USG PARTICIPATION IN 
JUNE NATIONAL ACCORD CONFERENCE 
 
 
¶1.  (U) Post received a note verbale (text below) from the 
Arab League May 17, inviting U.S. participation for the 
opening session of the "Iraqi National Accord Conference" 
slated to be convened June 21, 2006 in Baghdad.  The League 
asks that the Embassy provide it with the name(s) of those 
representatives who will attend the session.  A copy of the 
note verbale has been faxed to NEA/ELA.  League staff 
informed us May 16 that Secretary General Moussa and senior 
Iraqi government officials recently agreed on dates for the 
Baghdad conference (including a preparatory meeting June 20, 
and the conference itself June 21-22).  Arab League Iraq 
envoy, Mohktar Lamani was currently in Baghdad preparing for 
the gathering. 
 
¶2.  (U) Begin text of Arab League note: 
 
The League of Arab States presents its compliments to the 
Embassy of the United States of America in Cairo and has the 
honor to extend an invitation to the U.S. Department of State 
to attend the opening session of the Iraqi National Accord 
Conference due to take place in Baghdad on 21 June 2006. 
 
The League of Arab States hopes to receive the name(s) of the 
representative(s) to this conference in the earliest possible 
opportunity. 
 
Complimentary closing.
