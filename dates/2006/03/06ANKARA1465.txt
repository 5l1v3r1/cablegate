SUBJECT: TURKEY: UN OCHA DONOR CONFERENCE ISTANBUL 
 
REF: STATE 40777 
 
¶1.  With respect to the UN OCHA Donor Support Non- 
Traditional Donors Conference co-chaired by the GOT and 
USAID and scheduled for April 27-28 in Istanbul at the 
Hotel Dedeman, Embassy POC is David Young (90-312-455 
5555 X2256; YoungDK@state.gov) and Consulate PA POC is 
Katharina Gollner-Sweet (90-212-335 9000 x9145; 
SweetKP@state.gov). 
 
¶2.  Got MFA Officer Volkan Oskiper (90-312-292-2851; 
voskiper@mfa.gov.tr) made contact with Embassy and shared 
the draft agenda.  The goal of the conference is to 
increase humanitarian funding partnerships with non- 
traditional (non-OECD) donors.  Per Reftel USAID will 
provide a fund-site to cover conference expenditures.  In 
consultation with USAID, The Turkish Emergency Management 
Agency (YAY) has made initial research and arrangements 
through Aktuel Tours travel agency in Istanbul.
