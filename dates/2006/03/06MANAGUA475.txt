SUBJECT: NO NICARAGUAN STATEMENT AS YET ON THE AL-ASKARIYA 
SHRINE BOMBING 
 
REF: STATE 28802 
 
(SBU) On February 23, poloff delivered the talking points in 
reftel to Miriam Fonseca, Director of the Foreign Ministry's 
Asia, Africa, Middle East and Oceania section.  Fonseca 
replied that the ministry would study the USG request, but 
noted that any Nicaraguan statement on the bombing would have 
to be approved by Foreign Minister Norman Caldera, who has 
been out of the country for most of the last two weeks.  Post 
will continue to follow up with the Foreign Ministry and will 
report any substantive developments.
