SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR FEBRUARY 2006 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
 
¶1.  (U) Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General 
Casablanca joint Visas Viper Committee met on March 6, 2006. 
The meeting was chaired by the DCM and attended by the CG 
and representatives from CONS, RAO, RSO, POL, ECON, DAO, 
RAO, PAS, and LEGATT. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) The Committee agreed that LEGATT will seek 
additional biodata information from the GOM regarding an 
individual recently reported in the local press as having 
been convicted on terrorism related charges.  The Committee 
has no further names to submit for the February reporting 
period.  GREENE
