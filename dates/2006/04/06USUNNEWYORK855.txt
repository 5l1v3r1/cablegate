SUBJECT: UN/IRAQ: UNSC PRESS STATEMENT ON IRAQI GOVERNMENT 
FORMATION 
 
 
¶1.  On April 25, UN Security Council (UNSC) President, 
Chinese PR Wang read out a press statement agreed by the UNSC 
regarding the formation of an Iraqi government.  UKUN had 
drafted the statement in consultation with the U.S. 
 
¶2.  Text as Follows.  Begin text. 
 
The members of the Security Council welcome the election of 
the Presidency Council, Speaker and Deputy Speakers of the 
Council of Representatives as well as the nomination of Prime 
Minister al-Maliki. 
 
The members express the hope that the constitutionally 
elected government of Iraq can now rapidly be formed.  They 
stress the importance of that government being as broad 
based, representative and effective as possible. 
 
End text.
