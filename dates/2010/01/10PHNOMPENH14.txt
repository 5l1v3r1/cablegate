¶4.  U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, 
as of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above-mentioned individuals. 
 
¶5.  The individuals will attend the fourth session of the 
"Criminal Investigation Training in Trafficking Cases" Course, 
February 1-12, 2010 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia.  The course is 
sponsored by the United States Department of State, with instruction 
provided by the Cambodian National Police and experts from the 
Southeast Asia Investigations into Social and Humanitarian 
Activities (SISHA) organization. 
 
¶6.  Action request: Please verify whether the Department possesses 
any credible information that the individuals named above have been 
implicated in gross violations of human rights.
