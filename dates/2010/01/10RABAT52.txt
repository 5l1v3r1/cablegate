SUBJECT: MOROCCO TO ASSOCIATE ITSELF WITH COPENHAGEN ACCORD 
 
REF: STATE 003080 
 
(SBU) The Ambassador delivered reftel demarche to Minister of 
Energy, Mines, Water and the Environment Amina Benkhadra on 
January 26, emphasizing our hope that Morocco would take 
action to associate itself with the Copenhagen Accord 
demarche before January 31.  Later on January 26, Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs and Cooperation Chief of Sustainable 
Development Faical Souissi told EconOff that the Minister of 
Foreign Affairs had decided to send an official notification 
to the UNFCCC Secretariat to confirm Morocco's decision to 
associate itself with the Accord.  That letter, Souissi 
indicated, was still being composed, but should be sent 
before January 31.
