SUBJECT: TFHA01: Demarche Request: U.S. Aid to Haiti 
 
REF: 10 STATE 6918; 10 CARACAS 51; 10 CARACAS 57 
 
¶1.        (SBU)  On January 27, Polcouns delivered ref a demarche 
and non-paper on U.S. assistance to Haiti to the Venezuelan Foreign 
Ministry's Director of North American Affairs, Yaneth Arocha. 
Polcouns also provided a copy of our January 26 diplomatic note 
strongly protesting the inclusion on Venezuelan government websites 
of allegations that the United States caused the Haitian earthquake 
through the testing of a purported new earthquake weapon (refs b 
and c). 
 
 
 
¶2.       (SBU)  Arocha said the Foreign Ministry would review the 
non-paper and diplomatic note and provide an official response.
