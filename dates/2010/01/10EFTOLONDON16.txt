SUBJECT: UK TO SEND EMBASSY REPRESENTATIVE TO GICNT EPG MEETING REF: SECSTATE 130750 (SBU)

 Poloff delivered reftel demarche and proposed amendments to the GICNT Terms of Reference (TOR) to Sharon McDonald, CBRN Terrorism Desk Officer in the Counter-Proliferation Department of the Foreign and Commonwealth Office (FCO), on January 4. McDonald told Poloff that the UK would be sending a representative from its Embassy in Budapest to this month's Exercise Planning Group (EPG) meeting and said that the FCO would send along any feedback regarding the proposed amendments to the TOR with its Embassy representative for discussion at the EPG meeting.
