¶1.  (SBU)  On February 24, AIT passed reftel non-paper to 
Wang Henyi, Director of the Legal and Planning Division at 
Taiwan's Financial Supervisory Commission (FSC).  We are also 
providing a courtesy copy of the non-paper to the Ministry of 
Economic Affair's Bureau of Foreign Trade. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Mr. Wang indicated that as per Taiwan's usual 
practice, the FSC will circulate a notice to direct Taiwan 
banking institutions and relevant agencies to end any 
activities, if any, with the designated entities and 
individuals.  The FSC will also inform the financial services 
industry associations and request that they follow up with 
their members to ensure compliance with the FSC directive.
