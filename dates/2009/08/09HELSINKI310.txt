 ¶1. (U) PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Heidi Marttila, 
Finnish MFA Unit for Asia and Oceania on August 18.  Marttila 
noted that while Government of Finland (GoF) does not comment 
or issue press releases unilaterally on issues covered 
through the EU, the Finnish Foreign Minister (Stubb) is well 
versed on Burma and Aung San Suu Kyi's conviction and if 
asked in interviews would reiterate the EU position on behalf 
of Finland. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Marttila said the organization Independent Diplomat 
has approached the GoF for funding to assist the National 
Coalition Government of the Union of Burma (NCGUB) in 
implementation of its opposition strategy for the democratic 
elections.  She said that the GoF is likely to fund the 
request and that the project has support from former 
President Ahtisaari and other former diplomats.
