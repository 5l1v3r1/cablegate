 ¶1. (U) DCM delivered reftel demarche August 13 to MFA 
Director for Bilateral Affairs Dhafer Al-Umran.  Al-Umran was 
already well briefed on this year's BMENA schedule and was 
enthusiastic about the Forum planned for Marrakech November 
2-3.  Al-Umran confirmed he would attend the senior 
officials' meeting in Rome October 23, and said Bahrain would 
be represented at the three thematic workshops. 
 
¶2.  (U) Al-Umran said FM Shaikh Khalid bin Ahmad Al Khalifah 
hoped to attend the Marrakech Forum, but would not make 
scheduling commitments until after his return August 16 from 
vacation.
