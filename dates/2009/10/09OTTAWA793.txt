SUBJECT: CANADA TO SUPPORT US CANDIDACY IN IMO ELECTION 
 
REF: STATE 98012 
 
(SBU)  In response to the demarche requested in reftel, the 
Government of Canada expressed in a diplomatic note that it 
will support the candidacy of the US for re-election in the 
IMO Council under Category A, with the "understanding" that 
the US will support Canada's candidacy for re-election to the 
IMO Council under Category B.  NOTE: In their diplomatic note 
the Government of Canada sought written confirmation of this 
agreement despite its official's earlier verbal 
acknowledgment that Canada knows the US does not disclose its 
votes before or after elections. END NOTE.
