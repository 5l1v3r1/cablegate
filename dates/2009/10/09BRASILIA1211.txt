REF:  A) STATE 98012 B) EMAIL JWHITTLESEY/JLONG 10/01/09 
 
(U)  THIS CABLE IS SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED AND NOT FOR INTERNET 
DISTRIBUTION. 
 
¶1.  (SBU) On September 25 Science Officer delivered the demarche per 
REFTEL A requesting Brazil's support the U.S. candidacy at the 
International Maritime Organization (IMO) to Second Secretary Andre 
Tenorio Mourao of the Division of Oceans, Antarctica, and Outer 
Space at the Ministry of External Relations.  Mourao was pleased to 
hear of the U.S. candidacy, but would not say whether Brazil would 
support it. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Mourao also noted Brazil's candidacy for a Category B 
council seat and asked for the United States' support. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) Mourao indicated that the Ministry of External Relations 
would be interested in an exchange of votes/support, however per 
REFTEL B Post informed Mourao that the U.S. Delegation does not 
reveal its votes in advance or after the election.
