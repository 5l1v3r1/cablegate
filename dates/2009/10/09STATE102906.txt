REF: 2006 BLUE LANTERN GUIDEBOOK 
 
¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on licenses 050106069 and 050119782 for 
the export of International Traffic in Arms (ITAR)-controlled 
F-16 and C-130 aircraft parts to Indonesia.  Post is 
requested to complete this Blue Lantern inquiry within 90 
days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern inquiry will affect 
pending and future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: to confirm receipt of aircraft parts 
by Indonesian Air Force; commodities with risk of diversion. 
 
¶4. ACTION: The Department requests that Post make inquiries 
with appropriate officials to determine if the Indonesian Air 
Force received the aircraft parts listed below and that the 
articles are being used for the purpose stated on the 
licenses.  Post should confirm with the Indonesian Air Force 
its relationship with foreign consignee KFS Aviation. 
 
END ACTION.
