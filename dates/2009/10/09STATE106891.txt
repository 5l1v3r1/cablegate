SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN PRE-LICENSE END USE CHECK ON 
APPLICATION 050185190 
 
¶1.  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
pre-license check for the export of gyroscopes to Indonesia 
via Singapore.  Post is requested to complete this Blue 
Lantern check within 90 days.  Lack of response to a Blue 
Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3.  Reason for request: sensitive commodity with potential 
for diversion.  A check on intermediary parties involved in 
the transaction. 
 
¶4.  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, Indonesia 
Ministry of Defense, and verify its request for the exact 
quantity and type of the gyroscopes listed below.  Also 
confirm intended use of the commodity.  Lastly, make 
inquiries with the MOD about the Foreign Consignee involved 
in this transaction, Staraero Pte Ltd.  Does the MOD consider 
Staraero to be a reputable company?  END ACTION.
