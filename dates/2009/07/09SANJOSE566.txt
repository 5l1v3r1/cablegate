SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED - OPPOSITION TO THE ITSO 
ARBITRATION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 68520 
 
¶1. (U) On July 6 we delivered reftel to Linyi Baidal, Deputy 
Director of External Policy in the Costa Rican MFA.  Baidal 
thanked us for the information, and confirmed that Costa Rica 
would be sending a small delegation to the meetings in Rome 
on July 8-9, headed by Vice-Minister of Telecommunications 
Hania Vega. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Baidal went on to state that as the matter is 
technical in nature, they would work with the Minister and 
her staff to develop Costa Rica's position on the potential 
arbitration.  Baidal confirmed that Vega had already left for 
Italy, however stated that she would pass on the information 
we presented to the Costa Rican Embassy in Rome, to transmit 
to Vega and her staff.
