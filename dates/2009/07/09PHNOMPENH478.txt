conducted local human rights vetting for the following individual 
selected for the GPOI Regional Training UN Support Operations Course 
(UNSOC) in Mongolia, August 3-12, 2009.

DPOB: March 10, 1960; Phnom Penh 
POSITION: Chief of Foreign Aid Office 
RANK: Colonel 
DEPT/UNIT: Department of International Relations, Ministry of 
National Defense

of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above individual.

Operations Course (UNSOC) in Mongolia, August 3-12, 2009.

any credible information that the individual named above has been
