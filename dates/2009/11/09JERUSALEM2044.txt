¶5. COMMENT: PA security services arrested five Palestine Liberation 
Jihad (PIJ) members in the Jenin area in the northern West Bank on 
or about October 28, 2009.  According to PA security services, the 
arrests came after an investigation of an active PIJ cell in the 
northern West Bank areas.  The group was said to be making pipe 
bombs and preparing to destabilize the security situation in the 
Jenin area. One of those arrested was named as Sharif Tahayna, who 
was subsequently released on October 30, 2009. Palestine Liberation 
Jihad is a designated Foreign Terrorist Organization.
