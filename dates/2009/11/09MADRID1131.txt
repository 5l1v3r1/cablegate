(U) Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Deputy European 
Correspondent Pablo Nuno November 20.  Nuno said that the GOS 
fully supports Macedonia's European Union aspirations, 
understanding that this is important for regional stability 
in the Balkans.  He agreed to pass on the USG request for 
partners' support for the UN-led process and Greek and 
Macedonian bilateral discussions to colleagues in Madrid and 
Brussels.
