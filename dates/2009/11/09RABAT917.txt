SUBJECT: MOROCCO RESPONDS TO USG PROPOSALS PURSUANT TO 
RESOLUTION 1822 
 
REF: STATE 109494 
 
¶1.  (SBU) EconOff delivered names of interest (reftel) to the 
Deputy President of the Financial Intelligence Unit (FIU) 
Abdelhamid Afi on November 13.  Afi reiterated the 
seriousness with which Morocco treats questions about 
terrorism finance, and undertook to share the names with the 
President of the FIU and the Central Bank.  The FIU will 
follow-up with the Central Bank for further input, he added, 
and will share with the Embassy any relevant information that 
they may be able to develop. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Regarding reftel para 13, Post will report any 
reaction from Moroccan authorities concerning the possible 
de-listing of Abdelghani Mzoudi if and when received.
