¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in 
conducting a post-shipment check on license 050147620 for 
the export of International Traffic in Arms 
(ITAR)-controlled turret harnesses and logic laser 
harnesses to Colombia.  Post is requested to complete this 
Blue Lantern inquiry within 45 days.  Lack of response to 
a Blue Lantern inquiry will affect pending and future 
licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: To verify the bona fides of Rafael 
Advanced Defense Systems Ltd. (Rafael ADS) in the 
transaction; to ensure all parties to the transaction have 
been identified. 
 
¶4. ACTION TEL AVIV: The Department requests that Post make 
inquiries to determine if foreign consignee Rafael ADS 
both received the harnesses and exported them to end-user 
the Colombian Air Force. 
 
ACTION BOGOTA: The Department requests that Post make 
inquiries with appropriate officials to determine if the 
Colombian Air Force received the harnesses listed below 
and that the articles are being used for the purpose 
stated on the license.  Post should confirm with the 
Colombian Air Force its relationship with foreign 
consignee Rafael ADS. 
 
END ACTION.
