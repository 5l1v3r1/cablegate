ICSC 
---- 
 
¶1. (SBU) The PRC agrees to support the candidacy of Ms. 
Lucretia Myers for election to the UN International Civil 
Service Commission (ICSC), MFA International Organizations 
and Conferences Department UN Division Officer Liu Bing 
reported to PolOff November 4.  In affirming PRC support for 
Myers' candidacy, Liu referenced the P5 "gentlemen's 
agreement" under which P5 members generally support each 
other's candidacies to UN bodies. 
 
ECOSOC 
------ 
 
¶2. (SBU) Liu requested U.S. support for PRC re-election to 
ECOSOC in 2011 and for PRC membership in ECOSOC's NGO 
Committee.
