SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING RESPONSE (KYIV 1883) 
 
REF: KYIV 1883 
 
 ¶1. (U) This cable responds to the action request in reftel 
requesting human rights vetting for the candidate nominated 
to attend english language training at Lackland Air Force 
Base, Texas and Manpower Systems Analysis course in Monterey, 
Califoria on 11/11/09 - 03/23/12. 
 
¶2. (U) The Department of State has vetted the 
individual listed in reftel and possesses no credible 
derogatory information as of 11/04/2009.  Post is 
authorized to include the following individual in 
USG-funded training: 
 
NAME:  KRYVINOS, OLEKSII VALERIYOVYCH 
DOB:  12/17/73, POB:  KYIV, UKRAINE 
POSITION: STAFF OFFICER, FOREIGN TRAININS CENTER, PERSONNEL 
POLICY 
DIRECTORATE, MINISTRY OF DEFENSE. 
 
¶3. (U) Post is permitted to provide the following 
statement to authorized parties: Embassy verifies that the 
Department of State possesses no credible information of 
gross violations of human rights by the individual(s) 
identified above, as of 11/04/2009. Post may cite this 
cable in response. 
 
¶4. (U) Note that if Post subsequently becomes aware of 
possible human rights violations by the individual(s) 
listed above, Post should inform the Department and not 
proceed with training.  Post should refer to the Leahy 
Vetting guidance manual for additional assistance.
