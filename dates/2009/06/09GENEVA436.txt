SUBJECT: JCIC-XXXIV:  (U) OPENING OF THE THIRTY-FOURTH 
SESSION OF THE START TREATY'S JOINT COMPLIANCE AND 
INSPECTION COMMISSION, JUNE 8, 2009 
 
¶1.  (U) This is JCIC-XXXIV-001. 
 
¶2.  (U) The Thirty-fourth Session of the START Treaty's Joint 
Compliance and Inspection Commission (JCIC) opened on June 8, 
¶2009.  Addressees are requested to route cable traffic on all 
START-related issues, as well as Moscow Treaty implementation 
issues, to U.S. Mission Geneva.  The slug line should be 
shortened to read "Geneva for JCIC."  In addition, the 
initial tag on incoming cables should be "JCIC." 
 
¶3.  (U) Taylor sends.
