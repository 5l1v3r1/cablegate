SUBJECT: IAEA REPORTS ON IRAN AND SYRIA DEMARCHE

(SBU) Per reftel, PolOff delivered the demarche to Dr. Kojo 
Assan, Director, International Organizations and Multilateral 
Bureau at the Ghana MFA. Assan stated that Ghana supports the 
peaceful use of nuclear energy, not nuclear weapons, and that 
the GOG believes in nuclear non-proliferation. He said that 
he will send to the Foreign Minister the US position urging 
GOG to make a statement demanding the two countries comply 
with their international obligations. Post will advise if 
additional information is received.
