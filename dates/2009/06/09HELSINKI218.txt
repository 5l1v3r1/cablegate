 (SBU) On June 4, PolOff delivered U.S. Diplomatic Note for 
the Protocol to the Extradition Treaty and the Treaty on 
Certain Aspects of Mutual Legal Assistance in Criminal 
Matters between the United States of America and the Republic 
of Finland to Paivi Kaukoranta, Director and Anna Esko, First 
Secretary, MFA Unit for EU and Treaty Law.  Esko noted that 
Italy and Greece are pending action for the EU to complete 
the agreements; however, she expected results in the near 
future.  A formal note from Finland in response to the 
delivery of the documents will be forthcoming.
