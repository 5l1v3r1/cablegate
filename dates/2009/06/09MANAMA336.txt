(SBU) MFA International Organizations Director Fouad Darwish 
told poloff on June 3 that it was his understanding that 
Bahrain had already submitted its ballot in favor of Kosovo's 
World Bank membership.  He added that he would call 
Washington at the opening of business to ensure that the 
ballot had been cast ahead of today's deadline.
