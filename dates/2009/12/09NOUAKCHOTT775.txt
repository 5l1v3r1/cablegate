SUBJECT: THE PRESIDENT'S ANNOUNCEMENT ON THE WAY FORWARD IN 
AFGHANISTAN AND PAKISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 122731 
 
 ¶1. (SBU) Ambassador drew on Ref talking points to demarche 
Foreign Minister Naha Mint Mouknass on December 1, 2009. 
Ambassador noted further that Mauritania was a country that 
could fully appreciate the importance of countering extremism 
while fostering economic development. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Minister Mouknass listened attentively and assured 
Ambassador that she would transmit our message to President 
Aziz right away and expressed her appreciation for an 
approach in advance of President Obama's announcement. 
Ambassador noted that as an active member of the OIC and the 
Arab League and a country that concerned itself with 
developments in the region, Mauritania's understanding and 
support were important to the U.S.
