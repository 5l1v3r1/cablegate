¶1.  (U) Post nominated the following ten individuals for the 
Presidential Entrepreneurship Summit:

Tripoli and Northern Lebanon (BIAT); 
- Mr. Nicolas Rouhana, Business Development & Communication 
Director, Berytech Technological Pole; 
- Mr. Kamal Mouzawak, a USAID Synergos Institute Arab World Social

- Mr. Youssef Fares, Owner of an olive oil company;

Foundation;

Association for the Development of Rural Capacities (ADR); 
- Ms. Nada Tarraf, founding member and Treasurer of the Lebanese 
League for Women in Business (LLWB; 
- Ms. Tania Helou, General Manager of Saradar Foundation.
