SUBJECT: FINLAND STILL CONSIDERING PLEDGE FOR PAKISTAN 
DONORS CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 24367 
 
Charge Michael Butler delivered reftel demarche on the 
Pakistan donors conference to the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs' Director General for the Americas and Asia, Elina 
Kalkku on March 19,2009.  CDA urged the Government of Finland 
(GOF) to send a minister-level representative to the 
conference, and to make a robust pledge to help stabilize 
Pakistan's economy.   Kalkku noted that Finland would attend 
the conference, but would not likely send a Minister-level 
representative.  Additionally, the GOF has not yet reached a 
decision on the size or form of its contribution.
