SUBJECT: AUSTRALIAN RESPONSE: URGING IRAN TO RELEASE 
DETAINED AMERICAN, MS SABERI 
 
REF: STATE 30046 
 
Classified By: Political Counselor, James F. Cole, for reasons 1.4 (b) 
and (d) 
 
(S/NF) March 31, Poloff approached Justin Whyatt, Director of 
the Middle East Section, Australia's Department of Foreign 
Affairs and Trade, to request the GOA urge the Iranian 
government through the resident Iranian Embassy and/or the 
Australian Embassy in Tehran to release Roxana Saberi as a 
humanitarian gesture.  Mr. Whyatt, acknowledging the urgency 
of the matter, provided an assurance that Australia would 
pursue the matter through its Mission in Tehran or by means 
of the Iranian Mission in Canberra. The text of the 
Australian message to the Iranians and their response will be 
provided septel.
