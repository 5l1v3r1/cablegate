SUBJECT: COSTA RICA: VENEZUELAN DIP NOTE SEEKS SUPPORT FOR 
OAS-IJC CANDIDATE CASTILLO 
 
¶1. On March 23, we received a circular diplomatic note from 
the Venezuelan Embassy in Costa Rica announcing that Freddy 
Castillo Castellanos would seek re-election to the OAS' 
Inter-American Juridical Committee for 2010-2014.  Elections 
will be held at the 39th OAS General Assembly in San Pedro 
Sula, Honduras, June 1-3, 2009. 
 
¶2. The note was addressed to all missions accredited to Costa 
Rica.  It expressed particular hope that Venezuela could 
count on GRULAC members for their support.  Post will email 
Castillo's attached resume to the Department.
