SUBJECT: AUSTRIA WILL ISSUE UNSC STATEMENT SUPPORTING 
AMISOM, PLEDGES FINANCIAL SUPPORT THROUGH EU 
 
REF: STATE 24665 
 
¶1. Poloff delivered reftel demarche March 20 to Peter Huber, 
MFA Head of International Organizations.  Huber replied that 
the GOA would make a statement in support of AMISOM at the 
March 20 UNSC meeting on Somalia, as well as endorse the EU 
statement on the issue. 
 
¶2. Huber said the GOA supports Somalia through the EU, which 
has so far committed 40 million euros for AMISOM and is open, 
"in principle," to contributing through the African Peace 
Facility.  He added that the EU also contributes to the UNDP 
Rule of Law and Security program, and has committed 4 million 
euros to support Somalia's transitional government.  On 
general development, the GOA supports Somalia through the 
EU's 2008-2013 development program for Somalia, which 
projects to spend 215 million euros in priority sectors such 
as good governance, security, education, and economic 
development.  Poloff will report further developments via 
septel.
