SUBJECT: AFGHANISTAN CONFERENCE: BRUNEI LIKELY TO ATTEND

Â¶1. (SBU) After repeated discussions with DCM, Government of 
Brunei (GoB) officials confirmed that Brunei is planning on 
attending the March 31 "International Conference on 
Afghanistan" in the Netherlands.  Ms. Janeh Sukaimi, Deputy 
Director, Politics II, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade 
(MFAT) told DCM on March 29th, that GoB representatives will 
likely attend the conference. The GoB plans to send Brunei's 
High Commissioner to the United Kingdom as their lead 
representative.  Initially, MFAT was not fully aware of all 
the conference details.  DCM delivered Reftel talking points 
to Sukaimi and other GoB officials.  While confirming GoB's 
likely attendance, Sukaimi did not provide any further 
information on the extent of Brunei's participation at the 
conference.
