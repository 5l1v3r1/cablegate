(SBU) Further to information relayed Ref A, Japanese 
Economic Counselor Toru Maeda advised us on March 27 that 
the Japanese Embassy in Jakarta had received instructions 
from Tokyo to deliver an invitation from the Japanese 
Foreign Minister and World Bank President Zoellick to 
the government of Indonesia to attend the upcoming 
Pakistan Donors Conference in Tokyo.  Maeda said the 
Japanese Embassy was delivering the invitation to 
the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on March 27.
