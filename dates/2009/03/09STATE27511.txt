¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) 
requests Post's assistance in conducting a post shipment end 
use check 
on license 050033008 for the export of defense hardware to 
Israel. 
Post is requested to complete this Blue Lantern within 45 
days. 
Lack of response to a Blue Lantern inquiry will affect 
pending and future 
licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request:  To confirm bona fides of transaction. 
 
¶4. Action:  Department requests that Post make inquiries to 
verify that 
the Government of Israel, Ministry of Defense received the 
listed United 
States Munitions List (USML) items for the purpose stated on 
the license. 
 
End Action Request.
