SUBJECT: TUNISIA OPPOSES DE-LISTING BAAZAOUI 
 
REF: A. ROME 12 
     ¶B. 08 STATE 125548 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  The GOT informed the Embassy on January 8 that it 
opposes the removal of Mondher Ben Mohsen Ben Ali Baazaoui 
from 1267 sanctions via the UN Focal Point.  No further 
details were provided by the GOT.  Post initially contacted 
the GOT in December in response to Ref B, regarding 
Baazaoui's petition to be removed from the list.
