SUBJECT: FINLAND: MADAGASCAR RETURN TO DEMOCRACY 
 
REF: STATE 35809 
 
(SBU) PolOff delivered objectives to Hanna Vaananen, Finnish 
MFA Regional Manager, Department for Africa and the Middle 
East on April 15.  Vaananen said that a troika mission from 
the EU is expected to visit Madagascar.  Vaananen also said 
that Finland has NGO networks in Madagascar but doesn't 
bilaterally fund the government.  She will pass the 
objectives to Finland's delegation to the EU.
