SUBJECT: UN HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL ELECTION DEMARCHE 
DELIVERED TO GOVERNMENT OF ISRAEL 
 
REF: STATE 36629 
 
Embassy Tel Aviv delivered reftel demarche to the GOI on 
April 16.  MFA International Organizations and Human Rights 
Department Director Simona Halperin received the demarche, 
and gave assurances that the GOI would support the U.S. 
candidacy.  Halperin also noted previously stated Israeli 
concerns about the Human Rights Council and delivered a non 
paper summarizing those concerns.  Post emailed the non paper 
to DRL/MLGA and NEA/IPA.
