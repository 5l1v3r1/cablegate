SUBJECT: SWEDEN WILL PARTICIPATE IN GICNT 2009 PLENARY 
MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 35549 
 
Deputy Director at the Department of Disarmament and 
Non-Profliferation, Asa Gustafsson, told post that Sweden 
intends to participate in the GICNT 2009 Plenary meeting, but 
does not plan to make any presentations at the meeting. 
Gustafsson said Sweden would send one diplomat from Embassy 
Hague and two as of yet unnamed experts from the Swedish MFA. 
 Sweden had no comments on the proposed agenda.  Post will 
report any additional information septel.
