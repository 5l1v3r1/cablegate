SUBJECT: PARTICIPATION IN THE MAY 13-14 ISLAMABAD REGIONAL 
ECONOMIC COOPERATION CONFERENCE ON AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: A. A) STATE 39779 
     ¶B. B) GARRAMONE 5/7 E-MAIL TO DEUTSCH AND MICALLER 
 
¶1. (U) USEU Poloff and Econoff delivered REF A points to 
Czech Government in its capacity as European Union (EU) 
Presidency, EU Council Secretariat, and European Commission 
officials.  Czech Vice Minister for Foreign Affairs Tomas 
Pojar will represent the Czech Presidency.  EU Special 
Representative for Afghanistan and Pakistan Ettore Segui will 
attend for Council Secretariat.  Representing the Commission 
will be DG RELEX Director for Asia James Moran and Paul 
Turner, DG RELEX Desk Officer for Afghanistan, along with 
staff from the EC delegations in Kabul and Islamabad. 
 
¶2.  (U) Commission interlocutor and DG RELEX Head of Unit 
David Tirr had some concerns with REF A points.  Tirr 
suggested the points did not entirely mirror the agreed upon 
objectives and recommendations covered at the Experts Meeting 
in held in Brussels in March.  USEU has forwarded those 
concerns (REF B) directly to Bob Deutsch and Manuel Micaller 
in SCA/FO and SCA/RA for consideration.
