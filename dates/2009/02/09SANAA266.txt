SUBJECT: YEMEN: CARTAGENA PROTOCOL DEMARCHE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 11910 
 
Yemen will not the attend the Cartagena Protocol Group of 
Friends meeting in Mexico City, according to Abdul-Hakim 
Rajih, Coordinator for the Cartagena Protocol in the Ministry 
of Water and the Enviroment.  No one among Post contacts in 
the Ministries of Agriculture, Trade and Industry, and Water 
and the Enviroment had heard of the Mexico City meeting. 
Comoff delivered reftel points to Mr. Rajih, who was familiar 
with the USG's position, on February 11.
