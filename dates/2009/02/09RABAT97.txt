SUBJECT: MOROCCO POCS FOR NONPROLIFERATION ISSUES 
 
REF: STATE 06970 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Ministry of Foreign Affairs Head of United Nations 
Division Azzedine Farhane is the primary Government of 
Morocco (GOM) point of contact (POC) for Nonproliferation 
Treaty (NPT) related issues.  Poloff Jimmy Mauldin will 
remain Post's primary POC on NPT issues until June 2009. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Poloff contacted Farhane by telephone February 2 
to discuss reftel message related to the upcoming May 2009 
NPT Third Preparatory Committee meeting in New York and is 
preparing a non-paper of NPT related questions for Farhane to 
address.  Poloff will follow up with Farhane next week to 
receive GOM response to the proposed questions and will 
report via Front Channel cable before the February 20 
deadline.
