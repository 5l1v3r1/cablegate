2.(SBU) The boy, the first AI victim in Suez, got sick February 2, was hospitalized February 3, received Tamiflu the same day, and is in good condition. MOHP's Central Laboratories and NAMRU-3 have confirmed the boy was struck by the H5N1 virus.
