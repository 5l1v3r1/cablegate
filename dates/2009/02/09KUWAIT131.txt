SUBJECT: GOK INQUIRY INTO STATUS OF MLAT NEGOTIATIONS 
BETWEEN US/KUWAIT 
 
REF: 08 STATE 42487 
 
 ¶1.  (U)  This is an action request.  See paragraph 3. 
 
 ¶2.  (U)  On February 3, 2009 Embassy Kuwait received an 
official inquiry from Kuwait's Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
Legal Department regarding the status of it's draft Mutual 
Legal Assistance Treaty agreement between the US and Kuwait. 
The MLAT was prepared by the GOK in August 2008, subsequent 
to meetings held in Kuwait with Department of Justice (DOJ) 
Attache Jeffrey Cole (Cairo) and Econoff. 
 
 ¶3.  (U) Action Request:  Please provide post with 
information on the status of the MLAT we can pass to the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs in response to its inquiry.
