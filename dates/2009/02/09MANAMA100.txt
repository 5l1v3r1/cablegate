SUBJECT: BAHRAIN'S FM TO ATTEND MARCH 2 CONFERENCE IN SHARM 
 
REF: STATE 14575 
 
¶1.  (SBU) CDA discussed reftel with MFA U/S Abdulaziz bin 
Mubarak Al Khalifa February 18.  Shaikh Abdulaziz said 
Foreign Minister Khalid bin Ahmad Al Khalifa would attend the 
March 2 Sharm Al-Shaikh conference on Gaza relief.  The FM 
would announce new Bahraini assistance still to be 
determined, and also highlight Bahraini public and private 
assistance efforts already underway.  These latter included 
treatment in Bahraini hospitals for injured Gazans, and the 
repair of a school and a hospital in Gaza. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Shaikh Abdulaziz confirmed that the GOB was 
requiring Bahraini donors to channel their contributions 
through the national relief committee chaired by the King's 
son Shaikh Nasser.  Bahrain was working to ensure assistance 
did not flow through Hamas.
