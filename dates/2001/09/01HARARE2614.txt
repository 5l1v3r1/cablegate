SUBJECT: ZIMBABWE'S ARMED FORCES RECRUITMENT AGES 
 
REF: STATE 149572 
 
¶1.  PER REFTEL REQUEST, POST PROVIDES THE FOLLOWING 
INFORMATION ON THE RECRUITMENT PRACTICES AND AGE 
RESTRICTIONS OF ZIMBABWE'S MILITARY FORCES. 
 
-- THE MANDATORY MINIMUM, VOLUNTARY RECRUITMENT AGE FOR 
MILITARY SERVICE IS 18.  (ALL RECRUITMENT INTO THE MILITARY 
IS VOLUNTARY AND THERE IS NO DRAFT OR CONSCRIPTION.  GIVEN A 
FORMAL UNEMPLOYMENT RATE EXCEEDING 60 PERCENT, THERE IS NO 
DEARTH OF CANDIDATES WHEN APPLICATIONS FOR INTAKE ARE 
ACCEPTED, RATHER, VERY LONG QUEUES ARE THE NORM.) 
 
-- AGE VERIFICATION OF RECRUITMENT CANDIDATES IS VIA BIRTH 
CERTIFICATES AND THE NATIONAL IDENTITY CARD. 
 
-- POST IS NOT AWARE OF ANY CREDIBLE REPORTS OF THE 
EXISTENCE OF MINORS OR THOSE UNDER THE AGE OF 18 SERVING IN 
THE MILITARY IN ZIMBABWE 
 
-- VOLUNTEERS UNDER THE AGE OF 18 ARE NOT ACCEPTED INTO 
MILITARY FORCES.
