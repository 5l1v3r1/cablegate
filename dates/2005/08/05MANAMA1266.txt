SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  FOOD AID AND WTO

(U) Pol/Econ chief August 31 delivered reftel points and 
non-paper concerning food aid and the WTO to MFA Director for 
International Organization Affairs Ghassan Shaikho.  Shaikho 
listened carefully to the points and responded that the UN 
World Food Program could possibly play a role in coordinating 
the activities of the WTO and FAO on food aid, if necessary. 
He pledged to pass the non-paper to relevant GOB ministries.
