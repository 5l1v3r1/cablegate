SUBJECT: AUSTRIA ON IRAQ DRAFT CONSTITUTION -- VIENNA 
CONFERENCE AN OPPORTUNITY FOR SUPPORT 
 
REF: STATE 158420 
 
¶1.  EconPolCouns discussed reftel points with Ralph Scheide, 
the Austrian MFA's assistant secretary level director of 
Middle East and African affairs.  Scheide said Austria shared 
the sentiments the British Presidency had issued on behalf of 
the EU.  Sheide added that he had provided input to Foreign 
Minister Plassnik for a statement of support. 
 
¶2.  Scheide noted that Austria's planned conference on a 
"Dialogue of Civilizations" will be an opportunity to 
highlight the themes of support for Iraq's political process. 
 The conference, which will be in Vienna November 14-16, will 
include Iraqi President Talabani and Afghanistan President 
Karzai, among others, Scheide said. 
van Voorst
