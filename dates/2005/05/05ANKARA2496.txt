¶1.  Subsequent to release of reftel b, Embassy received a 
response from Turkish authorities verifying that the Turkish 
Armed Forces is developing the UHF Jamming Program described 
in reftel A, case number 05-957032.  On April 20, 2005, the 
MFA sent a Diplomatic Note confirming that Turkish Armed 
forces ordered one Interad Limited Model 5001 Disconnect 
Antenna (220-1200 MHz) from Interad Limited, located in 
Melfa, Virginia under the reftel license number.  The Note 
also stated that the purpose of the UHF jamming program is to 
protect Turkish military forces against IEDs (improvised 
explosive devices).
