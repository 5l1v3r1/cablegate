SUBJECT: UNESCO'S MAN AND BIOSPHERE PROGRAM, SITE 
NOMINATION PROCEDURES 
 
Reftel: State 60390 
 
As Requested, Science and Technology Officer met with Dr. 
Natajaran Ishwaran (Director of Ecological and Earth 
Sciences) to ask that the nomination of the Reserva de 
Biosfera del Bosque Comunitario del Pueblo (Puerto Rico) be 
put on hold and not be submitted to the Advisory Committee 
for Biosphere Reserves for consideration until proper 
procedures for vetting nominations have been established. 
Ishwaran agreed that UNESCO would take no action on this 
nomination until contacted by the US Mission to UNESCO.
