SUBJECT: GOA STRONGLY SUPPORTS THE IRAQI TRANSITIONAL 
GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 79107 
 
¶1.  (U) On May 2, Poloff delivered reftel demarche to 
Australian Department of Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) 
Iraq Task Force Executive Officer Bernadette Siely.  Siely 
noted that Foreign Minister Alexander Downer had issued a 
press release on May 1 welcoming the formation of the new 
Iraqi government and committing Australia to help the Iraqis 
"forge ahead" with their democratic transition.  (FM Downer 
had also issued an April 7 press release congratulating the 
newly-elected Iraqi President and Deputy Presidents.)  Siely 
told us that Prime Minister John Howard, FM Downer, and Trade 
Minister Mark Vaile would write to their counterparts in the 
Iraqi government to congratulate them on their 
selection/election.  Siely also said that GOA Defense 
Minister Robert Hill would write to his counterpart once one 
was chosen. 
 
¶2.  (U) Baghdad minimize considered.
