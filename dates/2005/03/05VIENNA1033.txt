SUBJECT: AUSTRIAN PARTICIPATION AT OSLO DONORS' CONFERENCE 
ON SUDAN AND IN UN PEACEKEEPING MISSION 
 
REF: STATE 56724 
 
¶1.  (SBU) On March 30, we delivered reftel talking points to 
Africa Director Caroline Gudenus in the MFA's Middle East and 
Africa Bureau.  Gudenus called back after hours to say that 
Under-Secretary for Development Assistance (USAID 
Director-equivalent) Irene Freudenschussreichl would attend 
the April 11-12 Donors' Conference on Sudan in Oslo. 
 
¶2.  (U) On March 25, Foreign Minister Ursula Plassnik 
publicly welcomed the creation of a UN Peacekeeping Mission 
to Sudan (UNMIS), and announced that Austria would contribute 
up to five military personnel to UNMIS.
