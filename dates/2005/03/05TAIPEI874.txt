SUBJECT: TAIWAN: POTENTIAL FISHERIES MEETING AND DELIVERY 
OF ICCAT TALKING POINTS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 29244 
 
¶1. Per reftel, AIT ESTOFF provided the talking points on the 
International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic 
Tuna (ICCAT) to Ministry of Foreign Affairs Deputy Director 
General of North American Affairs Kelly Hsieh on March 3. 
MOFA thanked AIT for following up on its request. 
 
¶2. AIT also informed Taiwan's Fisheries Agency Administrator 
Shieh Dah-wen that AIT would like to hold discussions on 
fisheries issues in Washington early this spring prior to the 
ICCAT intersessional meeting.  AIT ESTOFF noted that April 5 
and 6 are currently the dates under consideration. 
Administrator Shieh is looking into the dates.  If the dates 
under consideration have changed, please inform AIT ESTOFF. 
AIT will forward Administrator Shieh's reply as soon as it is 
received.
