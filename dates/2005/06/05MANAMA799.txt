SUBJECT: GATHERING SUPPORT ON UZBEKISTAN: BAHRAIN 
 
REF: STATE 100623 
 
(U) Ambassador, during a June 1 meeting with Minister of 
State for Foreign Affairs Mohammed Abdul Ghaffar, delivered 
reftel demarche seeking support from Muslim countries for an 
independent assessment of events in Andijon, Uzbekistan. 
Expressing concern about the recent events in Uzbekistan, 
Abdul Ghaffar said he would review our points.
