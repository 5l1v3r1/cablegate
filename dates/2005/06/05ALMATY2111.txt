SUBJECT:  KAZAKHSTAN: DEMARCHE ON DELIVERABLES FOR THE US-EU 
INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON IRAQ DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 103222 
 
¶1. (SBU) On June 3, CDA Asquino delivered the demarche 
requested in reftel to Foreign Minister Tokayev, urging the GOK 
to attend the US-EU International Conference on Iraq to be held 
in Brussels on June 22.  Tokayev said he planned to attend the 
event if other countries were also being represented at the 
ministerial level. 
 
¶2. (SBU) CDA also urged the GOK to establish full diplomatic 
relations with Iraq. Tokayev was receptive to the request, but 
made no commitment. 
 
¶3. (U) Dushanbe minimize considered.
