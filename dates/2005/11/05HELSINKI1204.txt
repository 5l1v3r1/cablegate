SUBJECT: FINLAND CONSIDERING SUPPORT FOR OAS COLOMBIA 
MISSION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 207705 
 
(U)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Hannele Tikanvaara, 
counsellor in the MFA's Unit for Latin America, on Nov. 15. 
Tikanvaara said that Finland will likely provide assistance 
to the OAS Mission in Colombia.  The Finnish Embassy in 
Washington is discussing the type of assistance--  financial 
or in-kind--  with the OAS.  The GoF is already preparing to 
include the necessary appropriations in a supplemental 
development assistance bill for the final quarter of 2005 or 
first quarter of 2006.
