SUBJECT: EGYPT DONATES MI-171 PARTS TO PAKISTAN 
 
REF: A. STATE 200915 
 
     ¶B. CAIRO 8424 
     ¶C. CAIRO 8496 
 
CENTCOM delivered Egypt's donation of helicopter parts (Refs 
B and C) for Pakistan's MI-171s to Islamabad on November 13. 
Egypt's donation included part or all of 15 of the 23 items 
that Pakistan said it needed for its helicopters to continue 
with the earthquake relief operation.  Local Egyptian press 
gave the donation favorable coverage.
