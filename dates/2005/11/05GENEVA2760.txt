SUBJECT: JCIC-XXVII:  CLOSE-OUT OF PART TWO OF JCIC-XXVII 
 
¶1.  This is JCIC-XXVII-056. 
 
¶2.  This is the end of operations for the second part of 
JCIC-XXVII.  The next meeting of the JCIC will be conducted 
at a date yet to be determined.  Washington and other 
addresses are requested to continue to include U.S. Mission 
Geneva, slugged "Geneva for JCIC" on all cables concerning 
START implementation and other strategic arms issues.  The 
initial tag on incoming cables should be "JCIC."
