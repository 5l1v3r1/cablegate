SUBJECT: G-8 BMENA:  EGYPT TO BE REPRESENTED AT RABAT 
DEMOCRACY MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 171708 
 
Sensitive but unclassified.  Please handle accordingly. 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche to MFA Cabinet staff 
member Ashraf Swellam September 11, urging the GOE to send 
senior representation to the October 1-3 G-8 BMENA Democracy 
Assistance Dialogue in Rabat.  Swelam promised to share the 
USG message with the Minister and confirmed that the GOE 
would be represented by Ambassador Mohamed Shaaban, Advisor 
to the Minister for Regional Initiatives.  Our colleagues at 
the UK Embassy shared their understanding that FM Aboul Gheit 
would be traveling elsewhere at that time.  Shaaban was the 
GOE point person for the aborted March 2 G-8/Arab League 
meeting that was to have taken place in Cairo.
