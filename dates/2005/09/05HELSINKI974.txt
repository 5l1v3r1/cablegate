SUBJECT: TFUS01: ACCEPTANCE OF FINNISH ASSISTANCE OFFER FOR 
HURRICANE KATRINA RELIEF 
 
REF: STATE 167044 
 
¶1. Per reftel instructions, Embassy Helsinki has conveyed to 
the GOF the USG's acceptance of part of Finland's offer for 
assistance, to include 9,000 sheets, 200 small tarps, 200 
first aid kits, 120 cooler cases, 1,000 pillow cases, and 
3,500 thermosheets.
