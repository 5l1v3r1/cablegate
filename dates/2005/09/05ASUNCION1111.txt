SUBJECT: PARAGUAY ON PRESENTATION OF DRAFT IRAQI 
CONSTITUTION 
 
REF: STATE 158420 
 
In a 9/1 meeting with Acting Foreign Minister Emilio Gimenez, 
the Ambassador drew on reftel talking points to ask that the 
GOP make a public show of political support and 
congratulations to the Iraqi people.  Gimenez said that he 
would consult with Foreign Minister Rachid upon her return, 
and that the GOP would look for an appropriate forum in which 
to make a formal declaration.
