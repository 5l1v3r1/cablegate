SUBJECT: IMI: INVITATION FOR BIDS - BID NO. EGS/05-14 FOR 
THE PROCUREMENT OF HEAVY EQUIPMENT IN YEMEN 
 
 
¶1. The Groundwater & Soil Conservation Project of the 
Ministry of Agriculture and Irrigation is inviting 
international companies for the procurement of the following 
heavy equipments: 
 
Bulldozers (220-240 HP) quantity 2 nos 
Wheel loaders (217-220 HP) quantity 2 nos 
Motor Graders with laser leveling equipment (220-240 HP) QTY 
2 nos 
 
¶2. A complete set of bidding documents in English may be 
purchased by interested bidders on the submission of a 
written application to the address below and upon payment of 
a non refundable fee of USD 150 or in a freely convertible 
currency.  The method of payment will be cashier's check. 
Prospective bidders can receive the bidding documents by 
courier upon payment of extra charge of USD 35. 
 
¶3. Bids must be delivered to the address below at or before 
13:00 hrs on NOV 24, 2005.  Late bids will be rejected. 
Bids will be opened physically at 13:00 hrs, NOV 24, 2005 in 
the presence of the bidders' representatives who choose to 
attend in person. 
 
¶4. All bids shall be accompanied by a bid security of not 
less than 2.5% of the bid price valid for 120 days from bid 
opening date. 
 
¶5. Interested eligible bidders may obtain further 
information from: 
 
Groundwater and Soil Conservation Project 
Director of Project Coordination Unit
