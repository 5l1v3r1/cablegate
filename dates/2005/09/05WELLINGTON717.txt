SUBJECT: NOMINATION REQUEST FOR VOLUNTARY VISITOR PROGRAM 
FOR VICE CHANCELLOR DR.STUART MCCUTCHEON (AND DELEGATION) OF 
AUCKLAND UNIVERSITY, AUCKLAND, NEW ZEALAND 
 
¶1. POST REQUESTS A SEVEN-DAY VOLUNTARY VISITOR PROGRAM TO 
ASSIST VICE CHANCELLOR STUART MCCUTCHEON OF AUCKLAND 
UNIVERSITY (PLUS A FOUR-MEMBER DELEGATION) FOR MEETINGS WITH 
LEADING UNIVERSITY RESEARCH INSTITUTIONS AND POLICY AND 
FUNDING AGENCIES. 
 
¶2. POST REQUESTS A PROGRAM OF MEETINGS AND ADVICE FOR THE 
ITINERARY BASED ON VISIORS RECOMMENDATIONS AND TIME/DISTANCE 
REALITIES. WE ALSO REQUEST ADVICE ON ACCOMMODATIONS BOOKINGS 
AND DOMESTIC TRAVEL BOOKINGS. 
 
¶3. VICE CHANCELLOR STUART MCCUTCHEON HEADS NEW ZEALAND'S 
LEADING RESEARCH UNIVERSITY.  THIS REQUEST RESULTS FROM 
DISCUSSIONS WITH AMERICAN CONSUL-GENERAL SIRIA LOPEZ IN 
AUCKLAND, (FORMER DIRECTOR OF PUBLIC DIPLOMACY FOR EAP) 
PETER KOVACH, AND PAO ROY GLOVER. 
 
¶4. PROGRAM DATES REQUESTED. 
THE DELEGATION HAS 10 DAYS IN THE UNITED STATES BEGINNING 13 
NOVEMBER 2005. THE PROGRAM REQUESTED (PENDING ADVICE FROM 
PROGRAMMERS ON THE BEST ITINERARY) IS INITIALLY LISTED AS:
