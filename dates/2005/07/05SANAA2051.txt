SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON TERRORISM FINANCE: MOVEMENT 
FOR ISLAMIC REFORM IN ARABIA DESIGNATION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 132400 
 
Econoff delivered reftel demarche July 26 to Ibrahim 
al-Nahari, Director General of External Relations at the 
Ministry of Finance, on the designation of the Movement for 
Islamic Reform in Arabia by the UN 1267 Committees.  Nahari 
promised to share the information with the ROYG's 
inter-ministerial Terrorist Finance Committee, and to update 
Post about progress in locating and freezing assets belonging 
to the organization.
