SUBJECT: PANAMANIAN V/MFA ON SECURING OAS ACTION TO 
INVESTIGATE TRAFFIC IN MANPADS 
 
REF: STATE 26516 
 
(SBU) POL Counselor delivered reftel demarche on securing 
Nicaraguan assent for OAS action to investigate traffic in 
MANPADS to Vice Foreign Minister Ricardo Duran on February 
17, pointing out the danger that illegal Nicaraguan arms 
flows pose for Panamanian and regional economies, security, 
investment, civil aviation, and tourism.  Vice Minister Duran 
acknowledged the risks and said he would discuss the matter 
with Foreign Minister Samuel Lewis.
