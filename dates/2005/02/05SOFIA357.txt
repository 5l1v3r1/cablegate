SUBJECT: BULGARIA FORMALLY CONFIRMS FIVE TROOPS WILL 
JOIN NATO TRAINING MISSION IN IRAQ 
 
REF: (A) USNATO 0054 (B) SOFIA 267 
 
(SBU) The Bulgarian government formally confirmed on 24 
February that it will send five trainers to take part 
in the NATO Training Mission - Iraq (NTM-I). Deputy 
Minister of Defense Ilko Dimitrov confirmed earlier to 
Post that the trainers could leave for Baghdad as soon 
as required by NATO.
