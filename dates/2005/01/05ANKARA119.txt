¶1.  (U) This is an action request.  See paragraph 3. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) We delivered reftel points January 7 to Turkish 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Middle East Department Head 
Botsali.  Botsali said Turkey strongly supports the IMIE.  He 
asked for specific suggestions about the type of help IMIE 
seeks from Turkey; how IMIE will work with the UN and OSCE; 
and the U.S. perspective on how IMIE is linked to UNSC 1546. 
We referred him to Elections Canada on the first two 
inquiries. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) Action request:  We request that the Department 
provide us the U.S. prespective on how IMIE is linked to UNSC 
¶1546.  End Action Request.
