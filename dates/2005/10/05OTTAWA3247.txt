SUBJECT: NMFS CHIEF HOGARTH SEEKING ICCAT CHAIRMANSHIP 
 
REF: SECSTATE 199093 
 
ESTOFF shared talking points in reftel with Sylvie Lapointe, 
International Fisheries Advisor, Atlantic Affairs Division, 
Department of Fisheries and Oceans.  Lapointe indicated that 
Canada will support Dr. Hogarth's nomination as ICCAT 
Commission Chairman.  She added that the GOC is still unsure 
whether any Canadian officials will be seeking leadership 
positions in ICCAT at the upcoming meeting in Seville.
