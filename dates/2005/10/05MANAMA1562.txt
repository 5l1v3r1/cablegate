SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  APPEAL FOR CONTRIBUTIONS TO PAKISTAN 
EARTHQUAKE RELIEF AND REHABILITATION 
 
REF: STATE 195483 
 
Sensitive but unclassified; please protect accordingly.  Not 
for Internet distribution. 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Pol/Econ chief October 23 delivered reftel demarche 
concerning contributions to Pakistan earthquake relief and 
rehabilitation to MFA Assistant Under Secretary for 
Coordination and Follow Up Shaikh Abdul Aziz bin Mubarak Al 
Khalifa.  Shaikh Abdul Aziz was grateful for information on 
the Geneva donors conference on October 26 and said he would 
inform the Foreign Minister of the substance of the demarche. 
 
¶2.  (U) The Bahraini press has provided heavy coverage of the 
U.S. Navy airlifting some $3 million in supplies for 
earthquake relief in Pakistan.  The aid consists of tents, 
beds, heaters, blankets, bed sheets, generators, medical aid, 
and food.  The package was put together following GOB talks 
with Pakistani Embassy officials on what was needed most.
