REMEMBRANCE RESOLUTION 
 
REF: STATE 188927 
 
¶1.  EconPolCouns delivered reftel points on October 20 to 
Helmut Boeck, the Austrian Foreign Ministry's assistant 
secretary level head of International Organizations affairs. 
 
SIPDIS 
 
¶2.  Boeck confirmed that Austria is a cosponsor of the 
Israeli Holocaust Remembrance Resolution.  He said the 
Austrian mission to the UN in New York foresaw no problems on 
the issue, but he undertook to contact the Austrian mission 
to heighten its awareness of the need to seek more cosponsors 
and to remain vigilent against "killer" amendments. 
van Voorst
