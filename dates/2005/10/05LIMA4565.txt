SUBJECT: ISRAEL AT UNGA - I: HOLOCAUST REMEMBRANCE 
RESOLUTION (PERU) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 188927 
 
Polcouns delivered the demarche on the Holocaust Remembrance 
Resolution to the Foreign Ministry's Director of Multilateral 
Affairs, Julio Garro, and Director of UN Affairs, Miguel 
Barreto, on 10/24.  Garro said that Peru strongly supports 
the Holocaust Remembrance Resolution, expects it to pass by 
consensus, and added that Peru's Permrep in New York is 
actively coordinating with his Israeli counterpart on this 
issue.
