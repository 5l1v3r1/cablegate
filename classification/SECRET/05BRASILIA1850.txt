SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: HOTEL NASCO PRENOTIFICATION 
 
REF: STATE 127800 
 
Classified By: ECONOMIC COUNSELOR BRUCE WILLIAMSON FOR REASONS 
1.4 b,d 
 
(S)  Emboff delivered reftel prenotification of USG intent to 
amend its designation of Nasco business Residence Center 
(List 17) to add the name of Hotel Nasco to Luiza Lopez da 
Silva of the Foreign Ministry's office for the Combat of 
Illicit International Activities and to Jaime Freitas of 
Brazil's Financial Intelligence Unit (COAF).  Freitas 
undertook to search COAF's databases and share the list with 
Central Bank and intelligence agency (ABIN), for them to 
conduct similar searches.  Lopes da Silva likewise promised 
to follow up.
