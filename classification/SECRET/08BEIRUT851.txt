SUBJECT: COM CONCURS WITH SECTION 1206 FY 08 TRANCHE FOUR 
PROPOSAL 
 
Classified By: CDA MICHELE J. SISON; REASON: SECTION 1.4(B) 
 
¶1. (S) CHIEF OF MISSION FULLY SUPPORTS THE FY 08 SECTION 1206 
(TRANCHE FOUR,4) PROPOSALS AS SUBMITTED BY USCENTCOM. THIS 
PROPOSAL ADDRESSES THE LEBANESE ARMED FORCES' (LAF'S) 
CAPABILITY TO CONDUCT MILITARY OPERATIONS IN URBAN TERRAIN 
(MOUT). IT REINFORCES THE MISSION'S STRATEGIC GOALS OF 
COUNTERTERRORISM COOPERATION AND MILITARY-TO-MILITARY 
ENGAGEMENT WITH LEBANON. 
 
¶2. (S) THE URBAN SOLDIERS' EQUIPMENT PACKAGE USEP) COMPONENT 
WILL PROVIDE MATERIAL SUPPORT FOR LEBANON'S EFFORTS TO 
COUNTER TERRORIST RESOURCES AND PERSONNEL IN URBAN AREAS 
WHERE U.S. AND OTHER INTELLIGENCE INDICATE THE THREAT 
RESIDES.  THE URBAN SOLDERS' EQUIPMENT PACKAGE (USEP) 
COMPONENT ENHANCES A GROWING LEBANESE CAPACITY THAT WAS 
PROVEN SO CRUCIAL IN THE MAY-SEPTEMBER 2007 COUNTERTERRORISM 
OPERATIONS IN THE NAHR AL-BAHRID CAMP.
