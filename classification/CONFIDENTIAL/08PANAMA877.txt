¶1.  (C)  The Ambassador phoned Acting Foreign Minister 
Ricardo Duran November 18 to reinforce the demarche from 
reftel A, which had already been delievered to working level 
MFA contacts as reported in reftel B. PolOff subsequently 
contacted Director General for International Organizations 
Delia Villamonte to further explain the demarche, and 
reinforce the message. Acting FM Duran called the Ambassador 
on November 19 to report that he had contacted the Panamanian 
mission to the UN, and delivered instructions to oppose the 
no-action motions, and to support the underlaying resolutions 
in Iran, Burma and DPRK. He reported that Panamanian 
Ambassador to the UN Ricardo Arias understood his 
instructions, and would carry them out.
