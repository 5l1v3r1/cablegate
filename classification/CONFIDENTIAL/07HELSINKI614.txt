Classified By: POLCHIEF GREGORY THOME FOR REASONS 1.4 (B) AND (D) 
 
(C) PolChief delivered subject demarche to Anu Ryter, 
Office Director of the MFA's Unit for Civilian Crisis 
Management August 10.  Ryter welcomed the new US 
counternarcotics strategy in Afghanistan, noting the 
significant impact that drug money has had on the Taliban's 
ability to regroup and raise funds.  She did question the 
concept of aerial spraying, but appeared satisfied with our 
reassurances that no spraying would be undertaken without 
GOA consent.
