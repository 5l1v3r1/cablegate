SUBJECT: TENTATIVE SETTLEMENT AT DRUMMOND 

REF: BOGOTA 5384 

Classified By: CDA MILTON DRUCKER FOR REASONS 1(B) AND (D) 

¶1. (C) Early June 14, Drummond reached a tentative agreement 
with leaders of the miners' union to end the 23 day-long 
strike.  Drummond's President of Coal Operations, Mike Tracy 
of Drummond, said the agreement would be put to a vote of the 
union rank and file members later in the day. 

¶2. (C) According to Drummond, the agreement included a wage 
increase and an increase in the indemnity payments for 
workers that leave the company.  The agreement does not 
include provisions to change the company's ability to fire a 
worker without "just cause" (reftel).  Tracy added he would 
briefEmboffs before returning to the US .
