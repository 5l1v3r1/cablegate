¶1. (C) On November 15, PolCouns delivered ref A demarche on 
countering Belarus' anti-US resolutions at the UNGA and 
soliciting GOU support for the Third Committee Resolution on 
Belarus, sponsored by the US. Demarche was delivered to the 
MFA's Acting Director of Multilateral Affairs Beatriz Nunez. 
Nunez indicated that the GOU had not moved from its position 
last discussed at the MFA on November 8 (ref B) -- which was 
to consider all four resolutions. Polcouns has a meeting 
scheduled with Nunez and other MFA officials on November 17 
to further discuss this demarche and other issues. Embassy 
will report to the Department any substantive developments as 
appropriate, septel.
