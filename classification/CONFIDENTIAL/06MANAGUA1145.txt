SUBJECT: NICARAGUA: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR IRAQI 
GOVERNMENT FORMATION COMPLETION 
 
REF: STATE 81632 
 
Classified By: Ambassador Paul Trivelli for reason 1.4 (b) 
 
(C) Ambassador spoke with Nicaraguan Foreign Minister Norman 
Caldera on May 26 to convey points in reftel.  Caldera 
explained that, although he will not make any public 
statements, the GON plans to re-establish diplomatic 
relations with Iraq.  He hopes to see the Iraqi Ambassadors 
to the U.S. and United Nations in late June to negotiate and 
finalize the agreement.  Caldera added that, if feasible, he 
will visit Baghdad after his summer trip to Saudi Arabia to 
exchange notes on the re-establishment of relations.
