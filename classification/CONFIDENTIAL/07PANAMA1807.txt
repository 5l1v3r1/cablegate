SUBJECT: PANAMA:  DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON U.S. VIEWS ON IAEA 
DIRECTOR GENERAL'S REPORT ON IRAN AND NEXT STEPS 
 
REF: STATE 157039 
 
Classified By: Classified by Ambassador William A. Eaton.  Reason:  1.4 
 (d). 
 
(C) "We share your point of view," Panamanian MFA Senior 
Advisor for UN Security Council Affairs Nils Castro informed 
POLCOUNS on November 20, in response to reftel demarche 
delivered by POLCOUNS on November 16.  Additionally, on 
September 14 during a periodic breakfast, Ambassador and 
POLCOUNS noted to First VP and FM Samuel Lewis that the issue 
of Iran would be returning to the UNSC soon and that the U.S. 
would be counting on Panama's support.  Lewis, aware of the 
issue, said that Panama would work to support the U.S. 
efforts in the Council.
