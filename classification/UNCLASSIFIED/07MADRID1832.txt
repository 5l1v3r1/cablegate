 Post delivered reftel demarche on the split-currency 
assessment proposal to Barbara Trincado, Chief of the 
International Relations Directorate at the Ministry of 
Infrastructure and Public Works, on September 17.  She had no 
substantive comment.  Matters related to civil aviation and 
ICAO are handled by this Ministry in coordination with the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs' Directorate of Multilateral 
Economic Relations.  Manuel Bautista Perez, Director General 
of Civil Aviation at the Ministry of Infrastructure and 
Public Works, is currently representing Spain at the ICAO 
session.
