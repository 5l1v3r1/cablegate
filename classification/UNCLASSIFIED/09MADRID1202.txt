SUBJECT: SPAIN REVIEWING DRAFT MOU ON LIFE, SECURITY AND 
MOBILITY SUPPORT IN AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 129187 
 
 The Spanish MFA confirms that they have been reviewing the 
draft MOU on life, security and mobility support in 
Afghanistan for the last couple weeks.  While they don't have 
a firm timeline for finishing consideration, they hope to 
conclude the process quickly in the new year.  Post 
emphasized the importance of building the capacity of Afghan 
institutions to withstand and diminish the threat posed by 
extremism.
