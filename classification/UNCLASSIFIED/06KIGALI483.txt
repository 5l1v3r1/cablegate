LETTER 
 
REF: A. STATE 72729 
 
     ¶B. STATE 75656 
 
¶1.  Per ref A, post delivered on May 9 a letter of 
invitation from Secretary Rice to Minister of Commerce 
Protais Mitali and Minister of Finance James Musoni for 
Rwanda to participate in the fifth U.S.-Sub-Saharan Africa 
Trade and Economic Cooperation Forum (AGOA Forum) in 
Washington, D.C. June 6-7, 2006. 
 
¶2.  The Government of Rwanda has not yet finalized its 
official delegation list.  Per ref B, post will provide a 
detailed list of all GOR delegation members, with the 
requested passport and biographical data, once the list has 
been finalized.
