SUBJECT: TURKEY WILL SUPPORT UNEP UNIVERSAL MEMBERSHIP 
 
 
REF: SECSTATE 60914 
 
 
Asli Oral, Head of the MFA's Environment Department, told us 
that Turkey decided to support the proposal for universal 
membership to the UNEP Governing Council and asked its 
embassy in Nairobi to pass this information to UNEP 
officials.  She added that Turkey will not send a delegation 
to the UNEP Governing Council meeting in Jeju, Korea, March 
29-31. 
EDELMAN
