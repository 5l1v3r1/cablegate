SUBJECT: COALITION CONFERENCE: NAVY CHIEF WILL REPRESENT 
DOMINICAN REPUBLIC 
 
REF: STATE 54923 
 
¶1. (U) Vice Admiral Ivan Pena Castillo, Chief of Naval 
Operations, has accepted reftel invitation to represent the 
Dominican Republic at the Iraq Coalition Conference that will 
be held in Tampa, Florida, April 30-May 2, 2007.  Vice 
Admiral Pena is in contact with Embassy Defense Attache 
Office (DAO), and DAO will communicate with CENTCOM regarding 
the details of Pena's travel.
