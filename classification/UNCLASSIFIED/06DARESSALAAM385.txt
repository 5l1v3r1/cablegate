Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Mawazo Paul Kaducha, 
head 
of U.N. Systems at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' 
Department of Multilateral Cooperation, on February 27. 
Kaducha did not have an update on the Government of 
Tanzania's progress toward enacting draft legislation for a 
national authority and national plan required under the 
Chemical Weapons Convention.  Poloff reminded Kaducha of 
the 
target dates of May 2006 and December 2006, respectively, 
and 
Kaducha said he would consult with colleagues regarding the 
status of such legislation.
