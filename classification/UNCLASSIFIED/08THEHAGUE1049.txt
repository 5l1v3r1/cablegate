REF: STATE 133199 
 
Senior Policy Advisor Bob Aal in the Crisis Management, Peace 
Support Operations and Military and Civil Cooperation 
Division confirmed the Dutch plan to attend the inaugural 
meeting of the Contact Group on Somalia Piracy (CGSP) 
proposed for January 13 and 14 in New York.  Pol-Mil Officer 
delivered points from reftel on December 23.  Aal reiterated 
Dutch support for the CGSP concept and appreciation for the 
invitation for the Dutch to be a charter member of the group. 
 He was not able to confirm the level of representation at 
the meeting and will provide inputs on the agenda after 
review and discussion within the Ministry.
