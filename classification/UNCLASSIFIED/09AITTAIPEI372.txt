SUBJECT: PSV: NATIONAL TAIWAN UNIVERSITY 
 
REF: USDOC 0370 (APPLICATION NUMBER: D400514) 
 
¶1. ON MARCH 20, 2009, COMMERCIAL OFFICER ERIC CROWLEY AND FN CINDY 
CHANG VISITED THE NATIONAL TAIWAN UNIVERSITY GRADUATE INSTITUTE OF 
ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING (GIEE) AT THE ADDRESS CITED IN PSV REQUEST 
CABLE TO CONDUCT A POST-SHIPMENT VERIFICATION.  DURING THE VISIT 
POST MET WITH DR. SHEN-LUAN LIU, GIEE PROFESSOR; MR. KUN-HUNG TSAI, 
A PH.D. STUDENT OF PROFESSOR LIU; AND MS. APRIL WANG, SALES ENGINEER 
OF THE ANRITSU COMPANY (IMPORTER OF THE CONTROLLED ITEMS). 
 
¶2. THE NATIONAL TAIWAN UNIVERSITY ESTABLISHED THE GRADUATE INSTITUTE 
OF ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING (GIEE) IN 2001.  THE GIEE CONSISTS OF 
THREE RESEARCH GROUPS: INTEGRATED CIRCUITS AND SYSTEMS (ICS), 
NANO-ELECTRONICS (NE), AND ELECTRONIC DESIGN AUTOMATION (EDA).  THE 
RESEARCH ACTIVITIES IN GIEE FOCUS ON RESEARCH AND DEVELOPMENT OF 
TECHNOLOGIES USED IN THE GLOBAL ELECTRONICS INDUSTRY. 
 
¶3. PH.D. STUDENT TSAI GAVE A DETAILED AND HIGHLY TECHNICAL 
EXPLANATION OF THE TYPE OF RESEARCH THEY WERE CONDUCTING AND WHY 
GIEE REQUIRED THE CONTROLLED TECHNOLOGY IN QUESTION TO COMPLETE 
THEIR RESEARCH.  THE RESEARCH INVOLVES WIDEBAND DATA TRANSMISSION 
AND COMMUNICATIONS SYSTEMS THAT REQUIRE PHASE-LOCKED LOOPS (PLLS) TO 
GENERATE STABLE REFERENCE CLOCKS. 
 
¶4. THE GRADUATE INSTITUTE OF ELECTRONICS ENGINEERING WAS OPEN AND 
COOPERATIVE AND DR. LIU SHOWED US THEIR LABORTORY FACILITIES.  AIT 
WAS ALLOWED TO ENTER THE TESTING CENTER WHERE THE CONTROLLED ITEMS 
IN QUESTION, INCLUDING THE 110-170GHZ HARMONIC MIXER AND SIPLEXER 
ANALYZER, ARE LOCATED. 
 
¶5. DR. LIU SHOWED US THE GIEE'S ORIGINAL EXPORT LICENSE APPLICATION 
AND CONFIRMED THAT THE CONTROLLED ITEMS ARE FOR THEIR OWN USE IN THE 
GIEE'S LABORATORIES. 
 
¶6. AIT FOUND NO EVIDENCE TO INDICATE THAT THE CONTROLLED PRODUCTS 
WERE USED FOR PURPOSES OTHER THAN THOSE PROVIDED IN THE STATED END 
USE.
