Ambassador met August 29 with Minister of Foreign Affairs 
Abdelwaheb Abdallah who confirmed he will lead Tunisia's 
delegation to UNGA.  He plans to depart September 23 or 24 
and stay in New York for a week.  He would welcome a meeting 
with A/S David Welch.  He expressed hope there will be an 
Arab Maghreb Union (AMU) Foreign Ministers' meeting with the 
Under Secretary for Political Affairs as happened last year. 
He said the Tunisian speech before the General Assembly is 
scheduled for September 29.
