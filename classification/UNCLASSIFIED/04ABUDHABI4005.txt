SUBJECT: OAS: PANAMA TO SUPPORT U.S. CANDIDATE FOR 
INTER-AMERICAN COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS, ANNOUNCES 
SUPPORT FOR ADMINISTRATIVE TRIBUNAL CANDIDATE 
 
REF: A. STATE 75344 
 
     ¶B. STATE 99038 
 
¶1. (SBU) Ambassador Watt called Foreign Minister Samuel Lewis 
on Friday, May 27, to encourage support for the U.S. 
candidate for Inter-American Commission on Human Rights Paolo 
¶G. Carozza.  The Foreign Minister informed Ambassador Watt on 
Tuesday, May 31, that the GOP would support Carozza. 
 
¶2. (U) The MFA's Office of International Organizations and 
Commissions delivered a diplomatic note to post on May 20 
indicating support for OAS Administrative Tribunal candidate 
Andre Surena in the elections that will take place during the 
OAS General Assembly June 5-7.
