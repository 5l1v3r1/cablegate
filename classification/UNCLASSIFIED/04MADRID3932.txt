SUBJECT: STRATEGY TARGETING ORGANIZED PIRACY (STOP) 
PROVIDED TO GOS 
 
REF: STATE 214134 
 
 Embassy provided reftel talking points and USTR fact sheet 
on STOP to relevant GOS officials on 10/7/04.  We provided 
the information to the Ministry of Industry, Tourism and 
Commerce; Ministry of Culture; Ministry of Foreign Affairs; 
Ministry of Justice; Ministry of Finance (Customs 
Department); and the Patent and Trademark Office.  Our 
Commerce interlocutor said he would comment on the 
initiative.  Embassy will also share the STOP information 
with relevant private sector contacts. We will, among other 
things, ask private sector IP representatives to what extent 
the GOS posture on STOP should influence Special 301 
considerations.
