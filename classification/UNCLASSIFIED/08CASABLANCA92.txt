SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR APRIL 2008 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met May 5, 2008.  The meeting was 
chaired by the DCM and was attended by CG, CONS, RAO, LEGATT, DHS, 
DAO, RSO, POL, ECON and PD. 
 
¶2. Discussion centered on two persons arrested along with one of the 
escaped terrorists who was recaptured recently. 
 
¶3. Legatt will confer with Moroccan law enforcement counterparts to 
obtain name and date of birth information for submission to Visas 
Viper Committee. 
 
¶4. The Mission's Visas Viper Coordinator is Consular Chief Miguel 
Ordonez.
