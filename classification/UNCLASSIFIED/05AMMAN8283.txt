SUBJECT: JORDAN: HOLOCAUST REMEMBRANCE DEMARCHE DELIVERED

(SBU)  Polcouns delivered reftel demarche October 19 to Jamal 
Al-Shamayleh, acting chief of the Foreign Ministry's 
International Organizations and Foreign Relations Department. 
 Shamayleh stated that if a vote took place on the 
resolution, Jordan would vote in favor.  He commented that 
some Arab League members were working to alter the wording of 
the draft, but that Jordan would not join in these efforts.
