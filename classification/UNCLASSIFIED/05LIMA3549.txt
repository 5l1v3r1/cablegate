¶1. (U) President Toledo swore in his new cabinet of ministers 
on 8/16.  Pedro Pablo Kuczynski, previously Minister of 
Economy and Finance, became the Prime Minister. Six new 
cabinet members were approved: Oscar Maurtua Romana as 
Minister of Foreign Relations, Marciano Rengifo Ruiz as 
Minister of Defense, Romulo Pizarro Tomassi as Minister of 
Interior, Alejandro Tudela Chopitea as Minister of Justice, 
Fernando Zavala Lombardi as Minister of Finance, and 
Rudesindo Vega as Minister of Housing.  Nine Ministers from 
the previous cabinet remained in their positions.  Ref A 
provided bios for Kuczynski, Maurtua, and Rengifo.  Bios for 
the remaining new ministers will be provided Septel. 
 
¶2. (U) A complete list of Cabinet Ministers follows: 
 
-- Prime Minister: Pedro Pablo Kuczynski 
 
-- Minister of Agriculture: Manuel Manrique (re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Commerce: Alfredo Ferrero 
 
-- Minister of Defense: Marciano Rengifo Ruiz 
 
-- Minister of Education: Javier Sota Nadal (re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Economy and Finance: Fernando Zavala Lombardi 
 
-- Minister of Energy and Mines: Glodomiro Sanchez 
(re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Foreign Relations: Oscar Maurtua Romana 
 
-- Minister of Health: Pilar Mazzeti (re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Housing: Rudesindo Vega 
 
-- Minister of Interior: Romulo Pizarro Tomassi 
 
-- Minister of Justice: Alejandro Tudela Chopitea 
 
-- Minister of Labor: Juan Sheput (re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Production: David Lemor (re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Transportation and Communication: Jose Ortiz 
(re-appointed) 
 
-- Minister of Women and Social Development: Ana Maria Romero 
(re-appointed)
