SUBJECT:  LATVIAN OFFICIALS TO ATTEND PRT CONFERENCE IN PRAGUE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 03595 
 
¶1.  Latvian Ambassador to the Czech Republic Argita Daudze will 
attend the January 25-27 PRT conference in Prague.  According to MFA 
officials, she will be joined by DCM Edgars Bondars and MFA 
Afghanistan Desk Officer Ilze Strasere.  Ministry of Defense 
officials reported earlier that Jevgenijs Pavlovics, Deputy Head of 
the MOD International Operations Policy Section, Crisis Management 
Department will also participate in the conference. 
 
¶2.  In addition to GOL officials, Latvian Radio and Television's 
Brussels correspondent Ina Strazdina will also attend in the 
conference.
