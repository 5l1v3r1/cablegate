rights vetting process: at this time the Embassy verified

rights violations by Major Tawfik Nagi Kaid Al-Bawah of the 
Yemen Coast Guard (YCG).

information is provided:

attend the Ninth Host Nation Coordinators Conference at 
Combined Joint Task Force - Horn of Africa Headquarters in 
Djbouti 30 May - 8 June.

Hood. 
 
¶C.  Current threat assessment: High.

vetting requirements for Major Tawfik Nagi Kaid Al-Bawah.
