SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: SWEDES SUPPORT TURKEY'S EU 
ACCESSION 
 
REF: STATE 79278 
 
On July 29, poloff delivered reftel demarche to Tove 
Skagerwall, Desk Officer at the MFA.  Skagerwall said Sweden 
is a strong supporter of Turkey's accession to the EU.  In a 
press statement on July 30, Foreign Minister Carl Bildt 
positively reacted to the decision by the Turkish 
Constitutional Court not to ban the AK Party. 
 
Begin Excerpted Text: 
"The decision opens up for further reforms, ensuring Turkey's 
further progress in line with European norms of democratic 
governance, human rights and the rule of law.  The Council of 
Europe stands ready to assist in this process." 
 
End Excerpted Text.
