SUBJECT: CYPRUS LIKELY TO FOLLOW EU ON UNGA "REGISTER OF 
DAMAGES" RESOLUTION 
 
REF: STATE 193698 
 
On December 4, PolOff delived reftel demarche points to MFA 
Multilateral Affairs Deputy Director Marios Kountourides and 
Middle East and North African Affairs Officer Dionyssis 
Dionyssiou.  The latter reported back to PolOff on December 8 
that Nicosia still needed to consult with its UN PermRep 
prior to the December 15 (per latest info) Emergency Special 
Session on Register of Damages.  However, Dionyssiou made 
clear that Cyprus "would be interested in the positions of 
other EU member states" and that it would work toward a 
"common (EU) reaction."  Post will report septel any 
additional substantive response.
