SUBJECT: PRESIDENT KARZAI ACCEPTS THE PRESIDENT'S 
INVITATION TO UNGA DEMOCRACY EVENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 149099 
 
 (U)  The Ambassador used his meeting with President Karzai 
on September 9 to extend the invitation (Ref) from President 
Bush to the September 19 roundtable event on democracy at 
UNGA.  Palace spokesman Ershad Ahmadi confirmed to us on 
September 14 that President Karzai will attend the event. 
The Afghan Mission in NY and the Afghan Embassy in Washington 
have been instructed to add it to Karzai's schedule.  The 
Palace relayed the request from President Karzai for 
information beyond that provided in Ref regarding the event. 
They asked specifically about format, agenda, and the 
participation list -- if at all possible before September 16.
