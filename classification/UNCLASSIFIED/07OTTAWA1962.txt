SUBJECT: GOVERNMENT OF CANADA SHARES USG'S IPR CONCERNS 
REGARDING WHO IGWG 
 
REF: STATE 144926 
 
(U) On October 23 Tanuja Garde, Director, Intellectual 
Property and Innovation, USTR, and Econoff delivered points 
in reftel to Julie Boisvert, Senior Trade Policy Analyst, 
Intellectual Property, Information and Technology Trade 
Division, Department of Foreign Affairs and International 
Trade.  Boisvert confirmed that Canada will be sending trade 
officials to the November IGWG meeting in Geneva.  She 
expressed agreement with the U.S. position regarding IPR and 
suggested that the U.S. and Canada should coordinate in 
advance with like-minded countries, such as Australia, as 
much as possible.
