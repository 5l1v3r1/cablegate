  AUTHORITIES.  ZUBIMENDI IZAGA WAS DETAINED ON JAN. 28,

  VIOLENCE RELATED TO TERRORISM. SPANISH INVESTIGATORS 
  BELIEVE SHE WAS PRESENTLY AN ACTIVE MEMBER OF ETA. 
  PREVIOUSLY, SHE WAS A HIGH RANKING MEMBER OF THE YOUTH 
  BASQUE RADICAL ORGANIZATION "HAIKA" / "SEGI" OUTLAWED 
  BY COURT ORDERS IN 2001 AND 2002 RESPECTIVELY.
