SUBJECT: FINLAND:  BELARUS RESOLUTION AT THE UNCHR 
 
REF: SECSTATE 41971 
 
(U)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to the MFA's Global 
Affairs Bureau on March 11.  Janina Hasenson, counselor in 
the Unit for Human Rights and Acting UNCHR Coordinator, told 
Poloff that her understanding was that the EU would 
co-sponsor the Belarus resolution.  Finland would support 
this EU common position.  She said that she was unaware of 
what changes (if any) the EU might suggest to the draft 
resolution, but that she did not believe Finland would 
recommend any changes to the draft.
