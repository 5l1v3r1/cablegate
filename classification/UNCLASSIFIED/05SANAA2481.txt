SUBJECT: IMI: THIRD MAJOR OIL BID IN YEMEN 
 
¶1. The Ministry of Oil and Minerals of the Republic of Yemen 
is inviting international oil companies to bid for the 
exploration and production opportunities on 14 open oil 
blocks. 
 
¶2. The contract model to be used is a Production Sharing 
Agreement (PSA). 
 
¶3. All interested oil companies must submit, with their 
request for the exploration and production in any of the 14 
blocks, the following documents: 
 
      ¶A. Company profile 
      ¶B. Technical and financial reports for the last two 
years 
      ¶C. Latest audit report 
 
¶4. For further information about the blocks, please click on 
http://www.pepa.com.ye/thirdbid.htm 
 
¶5. The deadline for submitting applications is January 04, 
¶2006. 
 
¶6. Interested eligible bidders may obtain further information 
by contacting:
