SUBJECT:  PORTUGAL H1N1 UPDATE - NOV 13 
 
REF: LISBON 558 
      LISBON 528 
      LISBON 509 
 
¶1. Between November 2 and November 8 Portugal diagnosed 14,111 new 
cases with flu symptoms.  One hundred twenty-one patients remain 
hospitalized, including seventeen in intensive care.  Portugal has 
seven confirmed H1N1 fatalities to date. 
 
¶2. The three latest fatalities include a 10 year-old boy and two men 
in their fifties.  All three had previous health problems that were 
aggravated by H1N1. 
 
¶3. The Health Ministry confirmed the arrival of 32,000 additional 
doses of vaccine last week.  Some health centers began vaccinating 
other priority groups, including chronically ill patients with 
asthma, obesity, immunodeficiency, and respiratory disease, as well 
as additional health care professionals.
