¶1. This is an action request.  Please see paragraph 3.

forwards the names and biodata of twenty-nine (29) Afghan nationals 
selected to participate in INL's Corrections System Support Program 
(CSSP).  When there are individuals with one name, Post has made a 
good faith effort to ensure that these individuals actually only 
have one name.

human rights violations on the below-named individuals. Post 
requests Department's review of these candidates and response by 
June 13, 2008. This is the fourth of ten cables.
