SUBJECT: NON-PAPER ON IRISL TRANSMITTED TO GOI 
 
REF: STATE 94723 
 
¶1. On September 8, Econoff passed non-paper transmitted 
reftel to a representative of Udi Levi, Director of the 
National Security Council's (NSC) Bureau of Counterterrorism 
Finance.  Econoff's GOI contact promised to transmit the 
non-paper via secure fax to Ariel Shafransky, Deputy Director 
for Counterterrorism at the MFA. 
 
¶2. Econoff informed GOI that the USG was in the process of 
passing the information to other friendly countries that 
might have the ability to act upon it.  Udi Levi's 
representative expressed appreciation for the information 
itself and especially for the USG's initiative to share such 
information with other countries.  He was supportive of 
taking strong measures against any of the malfeasant 
activities cited in the paper.
