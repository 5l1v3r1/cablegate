SUBJECT: MOROCCO ASKS FOR SUPPORT IN ITU 
 
REF: RABAT 0224 
 
¶1.  On June 21 post received diplomatic note number 2443 
dated June 21, 2006, requesting USG support for Morocco's 
candidacy for re-election to the Council of the International 
Telecommunications Union (ITU) during the elections that will 
take place during the ITU Plenipotentiary Conference in 
Turkey, November 6-24, 2006. 
 
¶2.  Post will fax a copy of the diplomatic note with informal 
embassy translation to NEA/MAG. 
 
¶3.  Note that in Feb. 2006 Morocco requested U.S. support for 
the candidacy of Ms. Najat Rochdi for the post of ITU 
Director of the Office of Telecommunications Development (Ref 
A).
