SUBJECT: GOI ISSUES TENDER FOR CONSTRUCTION IN ELKANA 
SETTLEMENT 
 
 
Â¶1.  (U) The Israel Lands Authority on Monday, June 12, issued 
a tender for construction of single-family homes on 53 plots 
in the religious neighborhood of the Elkana settlement near 
Qalqilya.  (Note:  Press incorrectly reported 54 units.  End 
note.)  According to the tender, the plots range in size from 
344 to 540 square meters, The minimum price is NIS 49,000, 
approximately $11,000, and the expenditure for development 
ranges between NIS 256,000 and NIS 328,000, or 
$57,000-73,000, depending on the size of the plots.  Both 
plots of land can be leased for 98 years, with an option to 
extend for an additional 98 years.  Buyers can receive 
informational booklets beginning June 26, and must bid by 
July 31, 2006.
