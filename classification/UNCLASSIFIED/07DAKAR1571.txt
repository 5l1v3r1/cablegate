(SBU) On July 27, poloff delivered reftel demarche to Mame 
Baba Cisse, Director for International Affairs at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Cisse said that as yet no 
decision had been made but he is sure, "Insallah" that 
President and Mrs. Wade, Foreign Minister and Mrs. Gadio and 
himself will attend.  President Wade has attended every 
General Assembly meeting since his election in 2000 and Cisse 
informed us that he has also been invited by UN 
Secretary-General Ban to attend a September 23 conference on
