SUBJECT: CZECH MFA PROMISES FOREIGN MINISTER WILL ATTEND

     ¶B. 06 PRAGUE 1548 
 
¶1. MFA Security Policy Director Veronika Smigolova confirmed 
to Poloff on January 5 that the Czech Republic will be 
represented by a Foreign Minister at the January 26 NATO 
ministerial proposed by Secretary Rice. Smigolova noted that, 
despite a possible change of Foreign Minister as part of 
ongoing government formation negotiations (Ref B), whoever 
holds the job on January 26th will participate in Brussels.
