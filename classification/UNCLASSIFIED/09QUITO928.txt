SUBJECT: Ecuador's Agriculture Minister to Attend UNGA Food Security 
Event; Plans to Make Presentation 
 
REF: 09 SECSTATE 92894 
 
¶1.  This cable is in response to action request in reftel. 
 
¶2.  On September 14, reftel invitation to the UNGA Food Security 
event and draft statement were delivered to Ecuador's Minister of 
Agriculture, Livestock, and Fisheries Ramon Leonardo Espinel with a 
copy to Ambassador Lourdes Puma, Director General of Multilateral 
Affairs at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA). 
 
¶3.  Minister Espinel plans to attend the September 26 Food Security 
meeting, accompanied by Denise Jeaneth Romero, Institutional 
Coordinator for the Ministry of Agriculture.  According to the MFA, 
there is a possibility that Foreign Minister Fander Falconi will 
also attend.  The expectation is that this will be the subject of 
internal deliberations this week. 
 
¶4.  During the September 26 meeting, Minister Espinel would like to 
make a 10-minute presentation on the Ministry of Agriculture's 
actions regarding rural development, in particular, on how the 
Ministry is working with small producers under a constitutional 
framework of food sovereignty.
