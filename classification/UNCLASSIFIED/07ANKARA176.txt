SUBJECT: EXBS: TURKISH PARLIAMENT RATIFIES NP COOPERATION 
AND ASSISTANCE AGREEMENT 
 
REF: A. 05 STATE 87781 
     ¶B. 05 ANKARA 3358 
 
¶1.  The Turkish Grand National Assembly approved the 
"Agreement Between the Government of the Republic of Turkey 
and the Government of the United States of America Regarding 
Cooperation to Facilitate the Provision of Assistance for 
Non-Proliferation Purposes" following debate on January 24, 
¶2007.  The final ratification vote was 231 to 40, with the 
opposition Republican People's Party (CHP) voting against. 
 
¶2.   We understand the Agreement is awaiting the signature of 
President Sezer, after which it will be published in the 
Official Gazette.  The Agreement will take effect following 
an exchange of diplomatic notes.
