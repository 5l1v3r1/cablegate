SUBJECT: ROK RESPONSE TO IRANIAN CRITICAL EQUIPMENT NEEDS 
ACTION REQUEST 
 
REF: A. A. STATE 052030 
     ¶B. B. STATE 021770 
     ¶C. C. STATE 039585 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel information to Kim Jae-woo, 
First Secretary for Disarmament and Nonproliferation 
Division, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MOFAT) on 28 
May.  Kim relayed continued support for U.S. and UN efforts 
to curtail Iranian illicit procurement 
activities.  He undertook to convey the specific items of 
concern in reftel to the ROK Ministry of Knowledge and 
Economy (former Ministry of Commerce, Industry and Energy) 
and said the ROKG would continue to share any relevant 
information on Iranian procurement efforts with the USG, 
Nuclear Suppliers Group, and IAEA.  Kim further stated that 
he and several officials from the Disarmament and 
Nonproliferation Division recently attended a UN information 
exchange meeting in Berlin where he received 14 briefings on 
Iranian nuclear activities.
