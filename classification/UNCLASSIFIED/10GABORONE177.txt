SUBJECT: GOB RECEIVES DEMARCHE ON IRGC DESIGNATIONS 
 
REF: STATE 15554 
 
¶1.  This is a response to an action request contained in 
State 15554. 
 
¶2.  PolOff met with the Assistant Director of the Department 
of Europe and Americas in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
International Cooperation, Lebogang Diteko on February 25. 
PolOff passed information contained in reftel and discussed 
the designation of four entities and one individual related 
to Iran,s Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps. 
 
¶3.  The assistant director indicated she would read the 
nonpaper and pass the information to appropriate people 
within the Ministry, likely in the Multilateral Affairs 
Division.  Diteko did not say anything else of substance but 
said she would follow up with any questions or comments that 
the Ministry may have. 
 
¶4.  Post will continue to monitor these events and will 
convey any other information received SEPTEL.
