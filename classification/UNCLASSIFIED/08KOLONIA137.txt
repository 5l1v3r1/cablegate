SUBJECT: DESIGNATION OF ADDITIONAL IRANIAN ENTITIES AND INDIVIDUALS 
UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: STATE 088759 
 
Charge d'Affaires met with Department of Foreign Affairs DAS 
Suzanne Lowe on August 26, 2008, presented Non-Paper and 
discussed designation of additional Iranian entities and 
individuals under E.O. 13382.  DAS Lowe said that the names 
would be passed to the appropriate Finance officials and banking 
authorities to determine if any assets connected to these 
entities or individuals can be located in the FSM requiring 
action under UNSCRs 1747 and 1803.
