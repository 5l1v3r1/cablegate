SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON ADDITIONAL FISCAL RESTRAINT 
ON ILO BUDGET 
 
REF: STATE 94764 
 
Poloff delivered reftel points on the ILO budget for 
2006-2007 to MFA International Organizations action officer 
Emmanuelle Delmer May 24.  Delmer responded May 27 that the 
points had been passed to the French mission in Geneva in 
time for discussions on this issue beginning June 1.  Delmer 
indicated that the French mission in Geneva was ready to work 
with the U.S. mission on the ILO budget.
