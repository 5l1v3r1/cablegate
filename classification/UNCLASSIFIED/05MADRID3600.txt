¶2.   SPANISH DAILY PRESS REPORTED OCT. 7, 2005, THAT 
  FRENCH AUTHORITIES EXTRADITED THE ALLEGED ETA MEMBER 
  ANTXON SASIAIN RODRIGUEZ TO SPAIN ON OCT. 6, 2005.  THE 
  SPANISH NATIONAL COURT HAD ISSUED AN INTERNATIONAL 
  WARRANT FOR HIS ARREST IN 1999, FOR HIS PARTICIPATION 
  WITH ETA'S "COMMANDO ARABA" IN NUMEROUS TERRORIST 
  ATTACKS SINCE 1996. SASIAIN WAS ARRESTED IN FRANCE IN 
  NOV. 2001, AND SENTENCED TO SERVE FIVE YEARS FOR 
  TERRORISM AND POSSESSION OF ARMS.
