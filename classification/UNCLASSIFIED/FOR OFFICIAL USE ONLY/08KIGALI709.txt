SUBJECT: RWANDA SPECIAL SELF HELP PROGRAM: OBLIGATION 
STATUS REPORT FY 2008 
 
¶1. (U) Summary: On September 26, 2008 CDA signed six grant 
agreements totaling $40,000 for the Ambassador's Self Help 
Program for FY 2008.  Post has fully obligated all of the 
Ambassador's Special Self-Help Program funds in FY 2008. 
 
¶2.  (U) Project(s) summary and status as follows: 
 
¶A.  Project Number: SHF-8-696-01 
    Project Name: Bakery 
    Grantee: Rebero Orphan Center 
    Beneficiaries: 72 
    U.S. Contribution: $7,231 
    Date Obligated: September 26, 2008 
    Description: Funds will be used to purchase a new dough 
    mixer and kitchen supplies to expand the center's 
    bakery. 
 
¶B.  Project Number: SHF-8-696-02 
    Project Name:  Sewing workshop 
    Grantee:  Cooperative des Techniciens en Couture et 
    Tricotage (CTCTG) 
    Beneficiaries: 18 
    U.S. Contribution: $8,924 
    Date Obligated:  September 26, 2008 
    Description:  Funds will be used to buy new sewing 
    machines for a sewing workshop cooperative located in 
    Byumba North Rwanda. 
 
¶C.  Project Number: SHF-8-696-03 
    Project Name:  Bicycle repair project 
    Grantee:  DUTI Cooperative 
    Beneficiaries: 220 
    U.S. Contribution: $2,868 
    Date Obligated:  September 26, 2008 
    Description: Funds will be used to set-up a training 
    center for bicycle repair in Kayonza district eastern 
    Rwanda. 
 
¶D.  Project Number:  SHF-8-696-04 
    Project Name:  Handicraft workshop 
    Grantee:  Cooperative Rwandaise des Art (CORWAR) 
    Beneficiaries: 90 
    U.S. Contribution: $6,803 
    Date Obligated:  September 26, 2008 
    Description: Funds will used to purchase equipment and 
    supplies for a handicraft workshop in Kigali. 
 
¶E.  Project Number:  SHF-8-696-05 
    Project Name:  Cheese 
    Grantee:  Urugero Association 
    Beneficiaries: 10 plus numerous local dairy farmers 
    U.S. Contribution: $4,914 
    Date Obligated:  September 26, 2008 
    Description: Funds will be used to purchase aluminum 
    molds, refrigerator and other supplies for making 
    cheese. 
 
¶F.  Project Number: SHF-8-696-06 
    Project Name:  Flour mill 
    Grantee:  Association des Femmes Abahuje 
    Beneficiaries: 22 plus approximately 1,000 members of 
    the local community that will use the mill. 
    U.S. Contribution: $9,260 
    Date Obligated:  September 26, 2008 
    Description:  Funds will be used to purchase a 
    community grain mill to mill sorghum and cassava.
