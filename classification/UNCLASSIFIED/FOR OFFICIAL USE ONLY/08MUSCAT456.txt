SUBJECT: POINTS ON P5+1 REFRESHED PACKAGE FOR IRAN 
DELIVERED TO OMAN 
 
REF: STATE 63960 
 
(SBU) The Ambassador informed Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA) Secretary General Sayyid Badr al-Busaidi on June 15 of 
delivery of the updated P5 plus 1 package to Iran by EU High 
Representative Javier Solana.  The following day, Pol-Econ 
Chief delivered reftel talking points to Humaid al-Ma'ani, 
Officer Director for the MFA Secretary General.  Ma'ani 
provided no substantive response, but asked that the U.S. 
continue to keep Oman apprised on P5 plus 1 negotiations with 
Iran.
