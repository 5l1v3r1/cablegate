SUBJECT: HONDURAS RECEIVES TALKING POINTS REGARDING REVIEW 
OF U.S. POLICY IN IRAQ 
 
REF: SECSTATE 18283 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche on the official review 
and changes in USG Iraq policy to Jose Lorenzana, Director of 
Multilateral Affairs at the Honduran Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, on March 10.  Lorenzana thanked us for the 
information and requested details on the 2011 USG-GOI agreed 
military pullout.  He then brought up the inevitability of 
change, and revealed that under the new Minister of Foreign 
Affairs, Patricia Rodas, day to day operations at his own MFA 
have also changed substantially.  Lorenzana did not provide 
details on the aforementioned changes.
