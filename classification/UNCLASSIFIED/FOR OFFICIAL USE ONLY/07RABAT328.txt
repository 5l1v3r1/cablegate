SUBJECT: ROYAL AIR MAROC SERVICE TO CONAKRY MONDAY, 19 FEB 
 
REF: SECSTATE 17835 
 
(SBU) Royal Air Maroc (RAM) Director of African Network 
reports RAM will not (not) operate any service to Conakry on 
either 17 or 18 February.  RAM intends to operate B757 
service to Conakry on Monday, 19 Feb; Wednesday, 21 Feb; and 
Friday, 23 Feb.  Each flight will originate from Casablanca, 
stop in Dakar, and arrive in Conakry at 15:00 local time. 
Planned departure from Conakry for Dakar is 16:00, and then 
follow-on to Casablanca.  Arrival in Casablanca is scheduled 
for 19:30.  RAM is providing coordination details to the 
Moroccan Embassy in Conakry, tele 224-30-41-36-86.
