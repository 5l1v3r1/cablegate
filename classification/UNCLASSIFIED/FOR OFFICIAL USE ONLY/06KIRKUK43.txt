SUBJECT: (U) PEACEFUL DEMONSTRATIONS IN KIRKUK AND KURDISH REGION IN 
REACTION TO SAMARRA GOLDEN DOME BOMBING 
 
 
SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED; PROTECT ACCORDINGLY 
 
Three peaceful demonstrations took place in the city of Kirkuk 
in reaction to the incident in Samara. 
 
February 22:  two protests took place at separate mosques: the 
first included some 125 Sadr supporters; the second was a larger 
gathering of approximately 350 people. 
 
February 23: 4000 individuals assembled in Southern Kirkuk.  No 
further information at this time. 
 
We have had no reports thus far of demonstrations in Erbil or 
Sulaymaniyah. 
 
All protests thus far have been peaceful and no violence has 
been reported as of 1345 Iraq time Thursday.
