SUBJECT: FINLAND PARTICIPATES IN EU WIREC PLEDGE 
 
REF: STATE 26870 
 
¶1. (SBU) According to WIREC participant Henri Backman, 
Advisor to the Minister at the Finnish Ministry of Employment 
and the Economy, Finland is participating in the EU's pledge 
to obtain 20 percent of its energy needs from renewable 
energy by 2020.  Finland is not planning to make a separate 
WIREC pledge. 
 
¶2. (U) The Finnish delegation appreciated being invited to 
WIREC and the opportunity to have Minister Mauri Pekkarinen 
participate in the panel discussion on the role of forestry 
in renewable energy production.  Pekkarinen also met with 
Energy Secretary Bodman and he toured the National Renewable 
Energy Laboratory in Colorado.
