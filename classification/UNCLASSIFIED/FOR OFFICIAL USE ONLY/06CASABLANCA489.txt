SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR APRIL 2006 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
 
¶1. (U) Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General 
Casablanca joint Visas Viper Committee met on May 8, 2006. 
The meeting was chaired by the DCM and attended by the CG 
and representatives from CONS, RAO, RSO, POL, ECON, PAS, DAO 
and LEGATT.
