SUBJECT: TUNISIAN UNGA PARTICIPATION NOT DECIDED 
 
REF: STATE 101170 
 
¶1. (SBU) On July 23, Post sent a diplomatic note requesting 
reftel information on the Tunisian delegation to the 62nd 
UNGA meetings.  On July 25, Ambassador followed up with MFA 
Chief of Staff Hatem Atallah.  Atallah said that he had seen 
Post's diplomatic note, but that the GOT had not yet decided 
who will attend UNGA. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Comment: The GOT usually decides and announces who 
will attend only days before UNGA.  Post will continue to 
press for a response and report any additional information 
septel.  End Comment.
