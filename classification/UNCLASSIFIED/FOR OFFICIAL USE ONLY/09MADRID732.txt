¶1.  Poloff submitted reftel request to Minister 
Moratinos'International Affairs Advisor Victoria Ortega on July 16. 
As of today, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs does not yet have 
definite itineraries for UNGA participants. The following 
information, however, has been confirmed. 
 
¶A. Attendance of Head of Government 
 
- Jos Luis Rodriguez Zapatero, President, will attend the UNGA 
meetings sometime between September 23 and 30 
 
- No definite itinerary available yet. 
 
- Spouse will not travel to New York. 
 
- Interpretation from English to Spanish will be required 
 
¶B. Attendance of the Foreign Minister 
 
- Miguel Angel Moratinos, Minister of Foreign Affairs and 
Cooperation, will attend the General Assembly. 
 
- No definite itinerary available yet. 
 
- Spouse will not travel to New York. 
 
- No interpretation is needed.
