REF: SECSTATE 40909 
 
(SBU)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to MFA United Nations 
and Multilateral Affairs Division Deputy Director Satya 
Rodrigo on April 3.  Rodrigo stated that Sri Lanka has no 
relations with Iran's Defense Industries Organization (DIO) 
under that or any other name listed in the nonpaper.  Rodrigo 
forwarded the nonpaper to GSL's Financial Intelligence Unit 
at the Central Bank so that it could flag any potential 
transactions with DIO.  Embassy will report any further 
developments.
