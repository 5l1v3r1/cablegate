¶2.  (SBU) Per reftel message, poloff on November 21 engaged 
officials at the Department of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU) 
Directorate for International Security and Disarmament 
Affairs, regarding the UNGA resolution on compliance with 
nonproliferation commitments.  Poloff thanked them for 
Indonesia's support for the resolution in the First Committee 
and urged the GOI to vote in favor of the resolution in the 
UNGA plenary.  DEPLU interlocutors confirmed that Indonesia 
planned to vote in favor of the resolution in the plenary 
session.
