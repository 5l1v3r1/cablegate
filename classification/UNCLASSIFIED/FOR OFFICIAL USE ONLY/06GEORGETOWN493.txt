SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR NEW 
IRAQI GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 81632 
 
¶1. (SBU) PolOff delivered demarche per reftel May 24 to 
Ambassador Elizabeth Harper, Director General (and number 
two) at Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Harper does not believe 
the GoG has had any communication or interaction with the new 
Iraqi government.  Although providing assistance is out of 
the question for Guyana, she agreed to pass on the message 
seeking a public statement of support for the new Iraqi 
political leadership. 
 
¶2. (U) Post will continue to monitor and report on any 
developments in the GoG's attitude towards Iraq.
