SUBJECT: PRIME MINISTER HARPER TO LEAD AMERICAS SUMMIT 
DELEGATION 
 
REF: STATE 34666 
 
¶1. (SBU)  Department of Foreign Affairs and International 
Trade (DFAIT) Summit of the Americas Coordinator Paul 
Williams on April 9 confirmed that Prime Minister Stephen 
Harper will lead Canada's delegation, accompanied by Foreign 
Minister Lawrence Cannon.  He expects the delegation also to 
include: 
 
-- OAS Ambassador (and National Summit Coordinator) Graeme 
Clark 
-- DFAIT Assistant Deputy Minister for Latin America and the 
Caribbean Alexandra Bugailiskis 
-- DFAIT Director General for Latin America and the Caribbean 
James Lambert 
-- DFAIT Director for South America and Inter-American 
Affairs Heidi Kutz 
-- Canadian High Commissioner to Trinidad and Tobago Karen 
MacDonald 
-- DFAIT Summit Coordinator Paul Williams 
 
¶2. (SBU) Several analysts from the Privy Council Office and 
the Prime Minister's Office will likely join the delegation, 
making the total size of the official party between 10 and 15 
delegates. 
 
¶3. (U) Canadian point of contact for arranging bilateral 
meetings in Port of Spain is Canadian High Commission officer 
Matthew Loken, according to Williams.
