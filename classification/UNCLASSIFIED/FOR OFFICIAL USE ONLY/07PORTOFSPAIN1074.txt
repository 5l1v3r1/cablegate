(SBU) Pol Chief spoke with  Susan Gordon, MFA Director of 
Multilateral Affairs and presented the points in reftel.  Ms. 
Gordon thanked Pol Chief for the demarche and stated that the 
request to either vote against or abstain on the UNGA 
resolution had been duly noted.  She then reminded Pol Chief 
of the Government of Trinidad and Tobago,s previous support 
for the resolution, broadly implying that it would remain the 
same.
