SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO AUSTRIA: CRITICAL EQUIPMENT NEEDS FOR 
IRAN'S NEW IR-2 CENTRIFUGE DESIGN 
 
REF: STATE 52030 
 
(SBU) On May 19, post conveyed reftel non-paper to Alexander 
Benedict, Deputy Head of the MFA's Arms Control, Disarmament, 
and Non-Proliferation Department, and to Helmut Krehlik, Head 
of the Ministry of Economic's (MoE) Import and Export Control 
Division.  EconUnit Chief followed up with Krehlik on May 20. 
 Krehlik welcomed the information regarding Iran's equipment 
needs for its IR-2 centrifuge design.  Krehlik said that the 
GoA remained highly sensitive to applications to export 
equipment to Iran, particularly in light of the UNSCR 
sanctions against Iran.
