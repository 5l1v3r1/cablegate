(SBU) Gershon Kedar, the acting director of the MFA's 
Department for UN Political Affairs, confirmed GOI attendance 
at the UNGA as reported in reftel (A) with one change.  FM

Washington and remain through September 19; they will arrive 
the second time on September 25 from Tel Aviv and remain 
through September 27.
