SUBJECT: ACTION REQUEST: USG COMMENTS ON PRC DRAFT GOVERNMENT 
PROCURMENT LAW IMPLEMENTING REGULATIONS 
 
¶1. (SBU) China's State Council Legislative Affairs Office 
published draft implementing regulations for the Government 
Procurement Law on January 11, 2010.  The draft regulations are 
available in Chinese at the following website: 
http://www.gov.cn/gzdt/2010-01/11/content_150 7655.htm.  Comments 
are due February 5, 2010.  Post is preparing a translation of 
the draft and will forward it to the interagency as soon as 
available. 
¶2. (SBU) ACTION REQUEST:  Interested agencies are requested 
to coordinate consolidated USG comments on the draft regulations 
and provide them to State EAP/CM Econoff Julie Hutto 
(huttojn@state.gov) by February 1, 2010 for onward transmission 
to post in time to allow translation and official submission to 
the PRC by the February 5, 2010 deadline.
