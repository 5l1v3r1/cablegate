SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: ZIMBABWE TALKS 
 
Department for AF/S (Greg Garland and Elizabeth Pelletreau) 
 
REF: State 79617 
 
¶1.  (SBU) DCM verbally delivered demarche per reftel July 25 to 
Ignace Gata, Vice Minister of Foreign Affairs as Foreign Minister 
Antipas Mbusa Nyamwisi was unavailable.  Gata requested a meeting 
July 29 with DCM to discuss the Zimbabwe in detail.  Poloff attended 
as notetaker.  Gata advisor Jean Pierre Masala was also present. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Gata noted the good relations between the DRC and Zimbabwe 
and labeled the recent discussions between the ZANU-PF and the MDC 
as a positive development.  He said the DRC will raise U.S. concerns 
as provided in talking points at the next Southern Africa 
Development Community (SADC) meeting in South Africa in August.
