SUBJECT: TURKEY SUPPORTED KOSOVO'S WORLD BANK MEMBERSHIP 
 
REF: A. ANKARA 629 
     ¶B. ANKARA 582 
     ¶C. ANKARA 543 
 
¶1.  Per reftels, the Government of Turkey voted in support of 
Kosovo's application to become a member of the World Bank and 
International Monetary Fund.  According to Memduh Akcay, 
Treasury's Director General for International Economic 
Relations, Turkey sent in its vote on May 27, 2009.  In 
addition, Reftel B outlines Treasury Minister Mehmet Simsek's 
efforts to garner support from additional countries.
