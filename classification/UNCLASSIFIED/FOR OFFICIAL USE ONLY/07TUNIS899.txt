¶1. (U) THIS CABLE CONTAINS AN ACTION REQUEST -- PLEASE SEE 
PARA 3. 
 
¶2. (SBU) On July 6, Ambassador delivered reftel talking 
points requesting Tunisian support, in the form of police 
and/or military observers, for the Darfur Hybrid Operations 
to MFA Chief of Staff Hatem Atallah.  Atallah asked what 
countries besides Egypt have agreed to support the Hybrid 
Operation.  He also asked what type of "equipment or 
training" would be available if Tunisia was able to offer 
support in Darfur. 
 
¶3. (U) Post requests Washington guidance on which countries 
plan to contribute forces and how the USG might support those 
countries that do.
