SUBJECT: GOE AFFIRMS SUPPORT FOR SHIPMENTS OF COMMERCIAL DOD CARGO 
THROUGH ESTONIAASTANA, AMEMBASSY 
 
On 25 August, Poloff and DATT delivered reftel demarche to Evelin 
Loman, NATO Desk Officer at the MFA.  Loman affirmed GOE support 
for non-lethal, commercial shipments bound for Afghanistan to 
transit via Estonia.  She also informed that the MFA has provided a 
letter of support to one commercial shipper in case questions arise 
when coordinating with other Estonian government entities.
