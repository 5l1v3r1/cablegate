SUBJECT: Update on PKK Violence (June 8-15, 2009) 
 
REF: ANKARA 829 and previous 
 
(U) Sensitive but unclassified - please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶2. (U) During the week of June 8-15, 2009, one security official was 
killed in a clash with the PKK in Hakkari Province on June 13.  Five 
security officials were injured, including four in an IED explosion 
in Hakkari Province on June 12, and one in a clash with the PKK in 
Hakkari Province on June 13.  The total number of security force 
casualties for the year through June 15, was 31 killed and 41 
injured. 
 
¶3. (U) During the same period, one PKKer was killed in a clash in 
Hakkari Province on June 13.  Three PKKers surrendered in Sirnak 
Province on June 10.  According to official TGS website figures, 
through the second week of June, the number of PKK militants killed 
this year was 26, the number of those captured was 25, and the 
number of those who surrendered was 57. 
 
¶4. (U) During the reporting period no civilian died but one was 
injured due to a PKK IED that exploded Hakkari Province on June 12. 
The total number of civilian casualties for the year through the end 
of the second week of June was five killed and ten wounded.
