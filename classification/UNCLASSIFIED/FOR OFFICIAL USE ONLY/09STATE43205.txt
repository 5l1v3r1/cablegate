SUBJECT: U.S. START TREATY ITEMIZED COST LISTS 
 
¶1.  This is an action request for Embassy Astana (DTRO-A), 
Embassy Kyiv (DTRO-K), and Embassy Moscow (DTRO-M/ACID). 
Please see paragraph 2. 
 
¶2.  In accordance with Annex 14 to the START Treaty 
Inspection Protocol, the Defense Threat Reduction Agency 
(DTRA) has prepared and forwarded to embassies (via express 
mail and unclassified e-mail) START Treaty itemized cost 
lists for the period January 1, 2009, through March 31, 2009. 
 Upon receipt of these lists, embassies are requested to 
deliver the lists to host nation government officials not 
later than April 30, 2009.  Embassies are further requested 
to confirm, via cable to Washington and unclassified e-mail 
to DTRA/OSAS (robert.habashi@dtra.mil and 
philip.smith@dtra.mil) the delivery of the cost lists, the 
name and office of the official to whom they were delivered, 
the date of delivery, and any comment or reaction provided at 
that time. 
 
¶3.  This U.S. START Treaty itemized cost list submission is 
being submitted directly to the Belarusian Government via a 
service message transmitted by the U.S. Nuclear Risk 
Reduction Center.
