SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  INTERNATIONAL MARITIME ORGANIZATION'S 
MARITIME SAFETY COMMITTEE MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 183434 
 
Sensitive but unclassified; please protect accordingly.  Not 
for Internet distribution. 
 
(SBU) Pol/Econ Chief November 14 delivered reftel demarche 
concerning the International Maritime Organization's (IMO) 
November 29 meeting of the Maritime Safety Committee to MFA 
Director for International Organization Affairs Ghassan 
Shaikho.  Shaikho confessed that he was not familiar with the 
issues to be discussed at the meeting but promised to share 
the demarche information with the Bahraini Ports Authority, 
which takes the lead on IMO issues.
