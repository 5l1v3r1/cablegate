SUBJECT: UNSC PRESS STATEMENT ON IRAQI PROVINCIAL ELECTIONS 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is an action message.  Mission is requested to 
seek adoption of a UNSC press statement welcoming the 
provincial elections scheduled to take place in Iraq on 
Saturday, January 31.  USUN should introduce the text at para 
2 as the basis for the press statement, drawing on talking 
points at para 3 as justification, and advising the 
Department if there are requests from other members for any 
substantive changes.  USUN should introduce the text on 
Monday, February 2 after obtaining final confirmation from 
the Department that events in Iraq over the weekend have not 
required a modification of this approach. 
 
¶2. (U) Begin text of draft press statement: 
 
&The members of the Security Council welcome the holding of 
provincial elections in Iraq on January 31.  The members look 
forward to the announcement of Iraq,s Independent High 
Electoral Commission (IHEC) certifying the election results, 
and express their appreciation to both the Commission and the 
United Nations Assistance Mission for Iraq (UNAMI) for their 
dedicated work in preparing for these elections. 
 
&The members of the Security Council commend and 
congratulate the people of Iraq for demonstrating their 
commitment to a peaceful and democratic political process.8 
 
End draft text. 
 
¶3. (U) Begin Talking Points: 
 
-- The provincial elections mark a significant milestone in 
Iraq,s democratic development, with all Iraqis choosing to 
participate in the political process. 
 
-- The United States, along with others, has worked with the 
United Nations and Iraq,s Independent High Electoral 
Commission to provide technical assistance in support of 
these provincial elections.  It is important that the 
international community continue to support peaceful, 
credible and transparent elections in Iraq. 
CLINTON
