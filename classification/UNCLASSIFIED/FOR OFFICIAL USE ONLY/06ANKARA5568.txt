¶1. (U) Accompanied by a Russian emboff, PolMilCouns provided 
reftel key documents to Foreign Ministry International 
Security Affairs Director General Ambassador Tomur Bayer on

circulate them among relevant offices, and provide initial 
comments at the earliest opportunity. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Bayer said that Turkey is willing to host the next 
Global Initiative meeting, but would prefer to do so in 
January rather than December for budgetary reasons.
