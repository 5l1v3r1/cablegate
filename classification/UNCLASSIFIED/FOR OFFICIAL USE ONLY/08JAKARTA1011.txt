SUBJECT: Indonesia - Delivery of BEP MOU Response 
 
REF: A) State 53344 B) State 28996 
 
1.(SBU) On May 22, we delivered the U.S. response to the Indonesian 
Bioengagement Program Memorandum of Understanding (MOU) April 
counter draft and accompanying  cover letter to Desra Percaya, 
Director for International Security and Disarmament, Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs per instructions in reftel A. 
 
2.(SBU) Percaya confirmed that Indonesia considers the document to 
be legally binding, regardless of whether the word "will" or "shall" 
is used. Percaya also confirmed that the Government of Indonesia has 
authority under its law to grant the tax exemptions called for in 
the agreement. 
 
3.(SBU) Percaya noted that he needs time to inform other Indonesian 
agencies and will request a quick turnaround, with the hopes for a 
MOU signing in June.
