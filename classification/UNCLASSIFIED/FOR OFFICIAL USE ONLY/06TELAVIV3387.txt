SUBJECT: ISRAEL TO SUPPORT USG CANDIDATE FOR INTERNATIONAL 
LAW COMMISSION 
 
REF: STATE 137429 
 
(SBU) Poloff presented reftel note and points to Eli Ben-Tur, 
the director of the MFA's International Organizations Office, 
on August 24.  Ben-Tur replied that the GOI has not yet 
decided on its voting slate for the International Law 
Commission, but emphasized that Israel will support Michael 
Matheson.  He added that the USG should "rest assured" that 
Israel will vote for the U.S. candidate.
