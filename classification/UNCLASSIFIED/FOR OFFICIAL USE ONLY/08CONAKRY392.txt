SUBJECT: GUINEA - DEMARCHE ON DESIGNATION OF ADDITIONAL 
IRANIAN ENTITIES DELIVERED 
 
REF: SECSTATE 76596 
 
¶1.  On July 22, Econoff met with Karakomba Camara, Secretary 
General of the Minister of Finance, to deliver reftel 
demarche regarding the designation of additional Iranian 
entities under E.O. 13382.  Post provided Camara with 
Department nonpaper, and will provide a French language 
translation as resources allow. 
 
¶2.  In response, Mr. Camara expressed his thanks for the 
information and stated Guinea's continued support for the 
U.S. in the fight against terrorism.  Any further substantive 
comments or reactions will be reported septel.
