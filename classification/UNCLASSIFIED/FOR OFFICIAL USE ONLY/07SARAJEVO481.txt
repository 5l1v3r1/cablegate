SUBJECT: BOSNIA SUPPORTS EFFORTS IN UNSCR 1737 
IMPLEMENTATION 
 
REF: STATE 24344 
 
(SBU) We presented points per reftel, informing of USG steps 
under UNSCR 1737,  to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs Sector 
for Multilateral Affairs' Section Heads Edin Sehic and Sanja 
Kenig-Corovic.  Sehic and Kenig-Corovic thanked us for the 
information, and expressed the Bosnian government's continued 
full support for any efforts arising from UNSCR 1737.  They 
both noted that while Bosnia had significantly less 
capabilities than the United States in regards to UNSCR 1737 
implementation, it would take take steps within its abilities.
