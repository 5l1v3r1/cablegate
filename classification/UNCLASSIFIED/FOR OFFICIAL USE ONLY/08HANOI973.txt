SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON EO 13382 DESIGNATIONS 
 
REF: STATE 88759 
 
(SBU) On August 20, Poloff met with MFA IO Section Chief Do Hung 
Viet to notify the GVN, per reftel, of the additional U.S. 
designations and to urge Vietnam to exercise its obligations under 
UN Security Council Resolutions 1747 and 1803 by freezing the assets 
of these entities or of any persons/entities acting on their behalf. 
 Viet took note of the U.S. position and assured poloff that the 
non-paper would be studied carefully.  He emphasized the Vietnam 
takes seriously its responsibilities under the relevant UNSC 
resolutions and has always made timely reports to the 1737 
Committee.  He asked that the United States keep Vietnam informed of 
any further developments.
