SUBJECT: KAZAKHSTAN: REACTION TO IRAN DEMARCHE 
 
REF: STATE 119436 
 
¶1. (U) Pol-Econ Chief conveyed reftel demarche to Daniyar 
Sarekenov of the MFA's Middle East division on July 27. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Sarekenov said that Kazakhstan was very concerned 
with the developing situation in Iran and hoped for a 
constructive response from the GOI to the P5+1 proposal. 
The GOK places great emphasis on the need for all 
signatories to abide by the provisions of the NPT, while at 
the same time supporting Iran's right to the peaceful use of 
nuclear energy.  Kazakhstan strongly favors a diplomatic 
solution.
