SUBJECT: ARGENTINA: DEMARCHE ON DESIGNATION OF IRAN'S 
DEFENSE INDUSTRIES ORGANIZATION UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: SECSTATE 40909 
 
(SBU) POLOFF delivered reftel background/nonpaper April 4 to 
Minister Carlos Hernandez of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA) Directorate of International Security and Nuclear and 
Space Issues (DIGAN).  Hernandez stated that the GOA would 
include Iran's Defense Industries Organization in the list 
for the GOA Commission for Export Controls.  In addition, 
DIGAN would circulate the information in the 
background/nonpaper to the Secretary of Finance and the legal 
section of the MFA to determine who else would need to take 
action.  Finally, the GOA would review the register to check 
for transactions with Iran's Defense Industries Organization 
and contact the Embassy in the event of further developments.
