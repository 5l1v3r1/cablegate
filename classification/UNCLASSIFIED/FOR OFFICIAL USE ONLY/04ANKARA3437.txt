(SBU) ECONOFF TRANSMITTED REFTEL DEMARCHE SEEKING 
SUPPORT FOR PROPOSED NONPROLIFERATION AMENDMENTS TO 
THE UN CONVENTION ON THE SUPPRESSION OF UNLAWFUL 
ACTS AGAINST THE SAFETY OF MARITIME NAVIGATION (THE 
SUA CONVENTION) NEGOTIATED AT THE INTERNATIONAL 
MARITIME ORGANIZATION (IMO) TO GUCAN BALIK, OF THE 
MFA MARITIME AFFAIRS DEPARTMENT.  BALIK TOLD US 
THAT PAKISTAN HAS LOBBIED THE GOT AS PART OF ITS 
EFFORTS TO IMPEDE PROGRESS ON THIS ISSUE.  HE 
STRESSED THAT TURKEY STRONGLY SUPPORTS USG EFFORTS 
ON THIS INITIATIVE, AND DOES NOT BELIEVE INDIA AND 
PAKISTAN WILL SUCCEED IN PREVENTING FURTHER WORK ON 
THE SUA AMENDMENTS.  EDELMAN
