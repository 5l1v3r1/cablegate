SUBJECT: PANAMA POSITION ON G-4 PROPOSAL 
 
REF: STATE 126870 
 
(SBU)  On July 12, POL Counselor delivered reftel talking 
points to Acting Foreign Minister Ricardo Duran, and PolOff 
delivered reftel points to MFA International Organizations 
(IO) Deputy Director Nubia Lezcano.  On July 13, Lezcano told 
PolOff that the GOP position on UN Security Council reform 
was "similar" to that of the U.S.  She said that the GOP 
believes that UN reform needed to be considered as an 
"integral" package without focusing solely on reform of the 
Security Council.  Lezcano passed talking points to IO 
Director Javier Bonagas, who had no further position to share 
on the current G-4 proposal.  Embassy will report any updates 
to the Department.
