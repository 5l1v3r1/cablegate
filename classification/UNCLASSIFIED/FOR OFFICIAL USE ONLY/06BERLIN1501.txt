SUBJECT: PSI:  GERMAN ATTENDANCE AT HIGH-LEVEL POLITICAL 
MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 84640 
 
(SBU) On June 2, per reftel, poloff discussed German 
attendance at the June 23 High-Level Political Meeting (HLPM) 
of the Proliferation Security Initiative (PSI) with Thomas 
Muetzelburg, MFA Action Officer for PSI affairs.  Muetzelburg 
said that he expects Ambassador Ruediger Luedeking, Deputy 
Federal Commissioner for Arms Control and Disarmament 
Affairs, will lead the German delegation.  He further noted 
that Federal Commissioner Friedrich Groening has a schedule 
conflict that precludes his attendance.
