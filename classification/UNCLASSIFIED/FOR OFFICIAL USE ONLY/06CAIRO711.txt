Sensitive but Unclassified.  Please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) Econoff delivered reftel demarche and points to 
Samir Abd El Maboud, Head of the Egyptian Civil Aviation 
Authority (ECAA).  Vice Chairman of the ECAA Gen. Shawky El 
Azzab was also present at the meeting.  Maboud was thankful 
for the points outlining the U.S. position and for the Voss 
biography. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Maboud indicated that Egypt had not yet decided on 
its vote and was not necessarily tied to the Arab League 
endorsement of Taieb Cherif.  El Azzab added that Egypt must 
take into account its African, not just Arab, role in the 
organization.  Maboud and El Azzab both noted that they knew 
William Voss personally. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Post will also demarche MFA contacts and report any 
developments regarding the Egyptian position.
