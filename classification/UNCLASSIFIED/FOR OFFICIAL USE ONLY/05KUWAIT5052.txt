¶1. (SBU) On December 6, Econcouns, accompanied by Econoff, 
delivered reftels B-D demarche points (verbally and through 
non-papers) to Khaled Al-Mughamis, Director of the MFA's 
Follow-up and Coordination Department responsible for 
terrorism finance and anti-money laundering issues. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Without promising any specific GOK action, 
Al-Mughamis thanked Emboffs for the information and offered 
to share the background papers and information with relevant 
GOK agencies, including the Central Bank and the ministerial 
committee on anti-money laundering.
