SUBJECT: TERRORISM FINANCE: AL-AKHTAR TRUST INTERNATIONAL 
 
REF: A) STATE 169212 
 
¶1. (SBU) EmbOff delivered reftel designation of Al-Akhtar 
Trust International to Luisa Lopes da Silva of the Foreign 
Ministry's office for the Combat of Illicit International 
Activities and to Jaime Freitas of Brazil's Financial 
Intelligence Unit (COAF).  Freitas undertook to search 
COAF's databases and share the list with Central Bank and 
the Brazilian intelligence agency (ABIN), for them to 
conduct similar searches.  Lopes da Silva will also follow 
up. 
 
¶2. (U) We will report septel should we receive any 
substantive response.
