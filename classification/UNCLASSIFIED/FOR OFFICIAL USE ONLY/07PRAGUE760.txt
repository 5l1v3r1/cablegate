SUBJECT: INFORMATION ON U.S.-RUSSIA MISSILE DEFENSE 
DISCUSSIONS DELIVERED 
 
REF: A. STATE 87909 
     ¶B. WHITEHOUSE 6211656 
 
¶1. (SBU) A/DCM delivered information on suggested U.S.-Russia 
expert-level missile defense consultations to Deputy Minister 
of Foreign Affairs Tomas Pojar and the Czech 
inter-ministerial missile defense working group June 25. 
A/DCM informed Pojar the consultations should take place in 
the next weeks and that the U.S. will keep the Czechs 
informed as discussions progress. Pojar expressed his 
appreciation for the information and reiterated Czech desire 
to be kept up to date on evolving U.S.-Russian talks 
regarding the Azeri radar.
