(SBU) On November 25, Poloff delivered reftel points to Luc 
Morin, Second Secretary Multilateral Directorate - Political, 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Morin told Poloff that Mauritus 
has been monitoring the Iran situation and will take the U.S. 
concerns into account.  He indicated that the Iran situation 
is difficult for Mauritius to follow given the multiple 
changes in Iran's position; however, he also stated that 
Mauritius hopes a resolution can be found through the IAEA.
