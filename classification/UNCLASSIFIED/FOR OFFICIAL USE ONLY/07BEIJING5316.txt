SUBJECT: INTERIM REPLY: FOREIGN MINISTER TO LEAD CHINA'S 
DELEGATION TO 62ND UN GENERAL ASSEMBLY 
 
REF: A. STATE 101170 
     ¶B. STATE 104172 
 
(U) Sensitive, but unclassified.  Please handle accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) MFA Department of International Organizations Deputy 
Director Yao Shaojun told PolOff informally on August 14 that 
FM Yang Jiechi will almost certainly lead China's delegation 
to the upcoming UN General Assembly (UNGA) session.  No other 
Chinese senior officials will attend.  However, because the 
United States and China are still working out arrangements 
for Yang's trip to the United States, including a possible 
visit to Washington, the MFA cannot yet make a formal reply 
or yet provie formally arrival and departure dates. 
 
¶2.  Using reftel's prescribed format: 
 
¶A. Head of State: No plans to attend.

¶C. Foreign Minister: 
--Name and Title: YANG Jiechi, Minister of Foreign Affairs of 
the People's Republic of China 
--Arrival and Departure: TBD 
--Spouse to accompany? No. 
--Interpretation: FM Yang speaks fluent English but Yao says 
"as a matter of standard practice" Yang will use 
interpretation in multilateral meetings. 
¶D. Other VIPs: No plans to attend 
¶E. Other MFA Officials: TBD.
