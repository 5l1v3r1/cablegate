¶1.  (U)  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  (U)  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
pre-license check for the export of Claymore fragmentation 
weapons, detonation cord, and safety fuse to Georgia.  Post 
is requested to complete this Blue Lantern check within 30 
days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern check will affect 
pending and future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  Reason for request: transactions involving the 
Israeli company, M. Paz Logistics, are undergoing review and 
confirmation checks. 
 
¶4.  (U)  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, the 
Georgia Ministry of Defense.  Request the Georgia MOD to 
provide complete copies of the acquisition contracts it 
signed for the two commodity transactions described below. 
Please forward contract copies to PM/DTCC.  END ACTION.
