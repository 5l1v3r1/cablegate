SUBJECT: MOROCCO ACCEPTS U.S. INVITATION FOR INFORMAL 
MEETING AMONG CTBT SUPPORTERS 
 
REF: STATE 97170 
 
(SBU) On September 23rd, Poloff passed the reftel objectives, 
including the U.S. invitation for Morocco to participate in 
an informal meeting with Comprehensive Nuclear Test-Ban 
Treaty (CTBT) supporters on the margins of the Article XIV 
Conference, to Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) 
Head of Security and Disarmament Office Bouchaib Eloumni. 
Eloumni accepted the invitation on behalf of the Government 
of Morocco (GOM), adding that either MFA Secretary General 
Amrani or MFA Chief of Staff Nasser Bourita would likely 
represent the GOM at the meeting.
