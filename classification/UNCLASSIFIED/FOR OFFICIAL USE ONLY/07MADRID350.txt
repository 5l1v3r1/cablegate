SUBJECT: SPAIN WITH EU ON SOMALIA PEACEKEEPING 
 
REF: STATE 17083 
 
(SBU)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Spanish 
Subdirector General for Sub-Saharan Africa Antonio 
Sanchez-Benedito Gaspar on February 16.  Sanchez-Benedito 
told Poloff that Spain is following the EU position on 
Somalia and approved of the 15 million euro reserve created 
at the January GAERC to support the AU.  However, he said 
Spain is not likely to contribute directly at this time.
