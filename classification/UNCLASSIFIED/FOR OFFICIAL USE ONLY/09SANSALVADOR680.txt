SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON RUSSIA SUMMIT, PLUS KOSOVO 
 
REF: STATE 71326 
 
¶1.  PolCouns delivered reftel nonpaper July 16 regarding the 
results of the U.S.)Russia Presidential Summit to MFA 
Director General of Foreign Policy Werner Romero.  He said 
that he would review the information and consult with the 
MFA's European desk officer. 
 
¶2. When asked if the GOES had given further consideration to 
recognizing Kosovo, Romero noted that they were aware that 
the Dominican Republic and Jordan had recently made public 
announcements recognizing Kosovo. He said that ministry 
staffers had prepared a report to update the Foreign Minister 
regarding the recent announcements, but that the GOES was 
waiting on a decision by the International Court of Justice 
before making a decision.
