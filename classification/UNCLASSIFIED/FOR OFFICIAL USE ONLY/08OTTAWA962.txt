SUBJECT: CANADA REQUESTS INFORMATION ON ECOSOC AND HUMAN 
RIGHTS FOUNDATION 
 
REF: STATE 70801 
 
¶1. (SBU) According to Department of Foreign Affairs and 
International Trade Human Rights, Gender Equality, Health, 
and Population Division Senior Policy Officer Cynthia Taylor, 
Canada is still considering its support for the Human Rights 
Foundation's application for UN Economic and Social Council 
(ECOSOC) consultative status (reftel).  Taylor stated that 
Canada does not, however, want to ask the ECOSOC Committee of 
NGOs to review its decision if it has already conducted a 
thorough review of the NGO. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Action requests: 1). please advise whether the 
Committee of NGOs has already conducted an in-depth review of 
the Human Rights Foundation; and,  2). please advise date on 
which the ECOSOC plans to review this issue.
