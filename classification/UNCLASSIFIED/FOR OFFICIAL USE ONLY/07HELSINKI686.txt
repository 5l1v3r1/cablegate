SUBJECT: FINNISH RESPONSE: IAEA GENERAL CONFERENCE/MIDDLE 
EAST: URGING SUPPORT FOR CONSENSUS PACKAGE ON MIDDLE EAST 
RESOLUTION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 119672 
 
(SBU) PolChief delivered subject demarche to Tarja 
Pesamaa, A/Director of the Finnish MFA's Unit for Arms 
Control, Non-Proliferation and Disarmament Sept. 6.  Noting 
that Finland supported a "no action" outcome when this 
issue came up during last year's IAEA GC, Pesamaa said that 
Finland's goal was to avoid having "purely political" issues 
(such as an Israel-specific agenda item) derail or distract 
from the GC's substantive business, and that the GOF could 
be counted on to work with serious partners to support any 
reasonable resolution to achieve that goal.
