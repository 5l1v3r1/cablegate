Â¶1.  (SBU)  In a meeting November 7 with PolOff concerning the 
designation of the Export Development Bank of Iran (EDBI) and 
its affiliates under E.O. 13382 with Director General of 
International Organizations in the Ministry of External 
Relations Ambassador Adolphe Nahayo.  Ambassador Nahayo, said 
that Burundi will respect the designation of the Export 
Development Bank of Iran (EDBI) and its affiliates under E.O. 
13382; stressed that Burundi does not currently have ties 
with and would not pursue associations with Iranian financial 
institutions, and confirmed that Burundi has no associations 
with the financial entities in Venezuela.
