SUBJECT: BAHRAIN SUPPORTS U.S. CANDIDACY FOR UN 
INTERNATIONAL CIVIL SERVICE COMMISSION 
 
REF: STATE 55499 
 
(SBU) A/DCM discussed the candidacy of Lucretia Myers for the 
UN International Civil Service Commission with MFA 
International Organizations Director Fouad Darwish on June 3. 
 Darwish stated that the U.S. candidate could "count on 
Bahrain's support," and instructed an aide to contact the 
Bahraini mission in New York with the same message.
