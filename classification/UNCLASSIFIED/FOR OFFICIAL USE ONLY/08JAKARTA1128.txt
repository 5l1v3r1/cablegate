¶2. (SBU) Foreign Minister Hassan Wirajuda cannot attend the 
June 19 UNSC thematic debate on Women, Peace and Security, 
according to Fikry Cassidy, Deputy Director for International 
Security and Disarmament Affairs at the Department of Foreign 
Affairs (DEPLU).  Cassidy told poloff on June 10 that FM 
Wirajuda had already committed to participate in the June 
18-20 meeting of the Council of Foreign Ministers of the 
Organization of the Islamic Conference (OIC) in Kampala, 
Uganda.  FM Wirajuda had planned to write directly to 
Secretary Rice concerning this matter.  Indonesia's PermRep 
to the UN, Ambassador Marty Natalegawa, will represent 
Indonesia at the thematic debate.
