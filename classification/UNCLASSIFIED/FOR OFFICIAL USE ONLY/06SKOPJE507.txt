SUBJECT: MACEDONIA: SUPPORT FOR IRAQI GOVERNMENT FORMATION 
 
REF: STATE 81632 
 
(SBU) P/E Chief delivered reftel points on support for the 
newly formed Iraqi government to MFA State Counselor Milan 
Spirodonovski on May 23.  The GOM often endorses EU foreign 
policy statements or declarations, and Spirodonovski said he 
would contact Macedonia's Ambassador in Brussels to check 
whether Macedonia already was included as a signatory to the 
May 22 EU statement on the new Iraqi government.  If not, he 
said, his office would submit for the Foreign Minister's 
approval a public statement (modeled on the EU declaration) 
supporting the newly-formed Iraqi government.  Post will 
report septel if Macedonia does issue such a statement.
