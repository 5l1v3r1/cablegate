SUBJECT: SOUTH AFRICA: CONSTRUCTIVE OUTCOME FROM THE 
GOLDSTONE REPORT 
 
REF: STATE 98567 
 
Delivering reftel points in an October 1 meeting with 
Department of International Relations and Cooperation Acting 
U.S. Director Godfrey Mulaudzi, Political Counselor expressed 
an understanding of Justice Goldstone's stature 
internationally and, more particularly, within South Africa. 
Pol Counselor called on the SAG officials to look at the 
report critically and, in the spirit of renewed dialogue 
between our two governments under new administrations, to 
share with us views on the strengths and weaknesses of the 
report.  Mulaudzi assured us that our views would be given 
full consideration.
