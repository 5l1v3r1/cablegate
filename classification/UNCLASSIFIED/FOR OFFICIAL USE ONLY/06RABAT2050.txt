SUBJECT: MESSAGE TO THE ARAB LEAGUE REGARDING UPCOMING 
CENTRAL BOYCOTT OFFICE MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 180280 
 
¶1.  This cable is sensitive but unclassified.  Please treat 
accordingly. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Hassan Hami, 
Chief of Division of Arab and Islamic Organizations at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs on November 3.  Hami, spoke 
unofficially because Mohammed Azeroual, the Director General 
of multilateral Relations and Global Cooperation was not at 
the meeting.  He, however, assured Poloff that if a Moroccan 
attended the meeting, the person would not be a high-level 
member of the Moroccan embassy in Syria.  Emboffs will meet 
with Azeroual after the Moroccan Monday holiday.
