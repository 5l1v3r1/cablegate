SUBJECT:  Sudan MFA Requires Approval of FSN Dismissals 
 
REF:  Khartoum 02542 
 
¶1. (SBU) The Ministry of Foreign Affairs has sent another diplomatic 
note placing restrictions on the activities of diplomatic missions. 
This diplomatic note states that every diplomatic mission, 
consulate, and international and regional organization must provide 
the Foreign Ministry with copies of work contracts signed with local 
employees.  It also requires that the missions get approval from the 
MFA through a note verbale before dismissing any employee. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The Embassy will canvass other diplomatic missions to 
determine how they intend to respond to this new directive prior to 
responding to this Diplomatic Note. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The diplomatic note reads as follows: 
 
(Begin text) 
 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
Directorate General of Protocol 
 
Date: 10/4/2006 (Note:  Date is incorrect.  End note.) 
No. W KH M/13/1/ (Note:  This is the same number as the Diplomatic 
Note in reftel.  End note.) 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of the Sudan 
presents its compliments to all Diplomatic Missions, Honorary 
Consulates and International and Regional Organizations accredited 
to Khartoum and in seeking to regulate the local employees' labor 
with these circles and to avoid many molestations and judicial 
follow ups, the Ministry has the honor to request all the Diplomatic 
Missions, Consulates and International and Regional Organizations 
accredited to the Sudan to provide copies of all the temporary and 
permanent work contracts signed with the local employees.  The 
Ministry urges that in the event of dismissal of any of the 
employees, a previous approval shall be obtained before the 
dismissal through a Verbal Note to be sent to the Ministry. 
 
The Ministry requests all Diplomatic Missions, Honorary Consulates, 
and International and Regional Organizations accredited to the Sudan 
to provide copies of the requested contracts soonest. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Sudan avails 
itself of the opportunity to renew all Diplomatic Missions, 
Consulates and International and Regional Organizations the 
assurances of its highest consideration. 
 
TO: ALL DIPLOMATIC MISSIONS, CONSULATES AND INTERNATIONAL AND 
REGIONAL ORGANIZATIONS ACCREDITED TO THE REPUBLIC OF THE SUDAN - 
KHARTOUM 
 
(End text)
