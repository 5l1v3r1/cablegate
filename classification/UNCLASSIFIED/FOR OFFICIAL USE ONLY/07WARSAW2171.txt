¶2. (SBU)  On October 29, post delivered reftel points to 
Mieczyslaw Kuzinski and Marian Siemakowicz of the Security 
Policy Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA), 
and to Elzbieta Frankow-Jaskiewicz of the Department of 
Financial Information of the Ministry of Finance (MoF). 
While appreciative of the information, neither the MFA nor 
the MoF had any substantive reaction to the Iranian 
Designations or FATF Guidance. 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  The MFA and MoF both confirmed that Poland 
implements designations according to the EU and UN sanction 
lists.  Both ministries said that Poland will continue to be 
supportive of the U.S. designations when/if they are 
submitted to the UN Sanctions Committee.
