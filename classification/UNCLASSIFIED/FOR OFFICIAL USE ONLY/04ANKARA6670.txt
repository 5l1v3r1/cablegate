Sensitive but unclassified--please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) PolMilCouns discussed reftel demarche Nov. 29 with 
Avni Botsali, MFA Head of Iraq Desk.  PolMilOff followed up 
Dec. 1 with Korkut Gungen, Head of the OSCE Section.  Gungen 
responded that Turkey supports an OSCE election observer 
mission for Iraq.  Gungen said that the GOT believes OSCE has 
the "necessary experience and expertise" to carry out such a 
mission. 
 
¶2. (U) Baghdad minimize considered.
