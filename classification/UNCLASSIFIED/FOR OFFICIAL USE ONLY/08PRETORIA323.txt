SUBJECT: DEFUELING THE DRC RESEARCH REACTOR 
 
REF: SECSTATE 015626 
 
(SBU) Per reftel instruction, on 15 February DepPolCouns 
informed DFA Deputy Director:  Nuclear Disarmament and 
Non-Proliferation Michiel Combrink of USG acceptance of the 
DRC invitation and USUNVIE's intent to organize a meeting of 
representatives of concerned governments and IAEA officials 
in Vienna the week of February 25th.  Combrink pledged to 
pass the information on to South African IAEA Governor Abdul 
Minty, who is currently on official travel.  Combrink expects 
that Minty will wish to attend the meeting and anticipates he 
will work directly with the South African and U.S. Missions 
in Vienna on scheduling.
