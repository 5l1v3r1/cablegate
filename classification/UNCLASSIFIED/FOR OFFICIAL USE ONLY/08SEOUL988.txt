SUBJECT: SEOUL RESPONSE: PARTICIPATION IN INT'L COMPACT 
WITH IRAQ MINISTERIAL 
 
REF: STATE 43799 
 
(SBU) Per reftel request, Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
Trade's (MOFAT) Development Cooperation Division Acting 
Director Kyun Jong-ho recently confirmed that First Vice 
Foreign Minister Kwon Jong-rak will head the ROKG 
delegation to the ICI Ministerial in Stockholm.  The ROKG 
delegation will primarily be in "a listening mode" at the 
conference, hoping to learn more about progess achieved to 
date by the ICI Secretariat and other parties in order to 
refine its own aid programs funded via the ROKG's 
$200-million pledge made at Sharm el-Sheikh.
