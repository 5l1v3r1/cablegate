¶2.  (SBU)  On December 22 Deputy EconCouns and econoff took 
advantage of a call on other business to deliver reftel 
demarche to MOFAT Middle East Division Director Sung-kyu Kwak 
and Second Secretary Sung-whan Lee.  Kwak and Lee listened 
attentively, thanked us for the notification, and noted that 
the ROKG adheres to its obligations as a UN member state. 
Econoff delivered the demarche to First Secretary Sang Soo 
Lee in the Disarmament and Nonproliferation Division of MOFAT 
on December 23.
