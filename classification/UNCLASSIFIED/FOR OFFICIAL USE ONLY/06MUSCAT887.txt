SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON NAM STATEMENT CONCERNING IRAN NUCLEAR

REF: A. SECSTATE 89325 
     ¶B. MUSCAT 868

¶1. (SBU) The Embassy delivered ref A points on June 3 to the 
Foreign Ministry expressing the USG's serious disappointment 
with the Non-Aligned Movement statement in support of Iran's 
nuclear program at the Ministerial meeting in Kuala Lumpur. 
P/E Chief previewed the USG position on June 1 with the 
deputy head of the MFA Under Secretary's office, seeking an 
explanation of Oman's position.  The Omani diplomat replied 
that he had received no read-out from the Under Secretary's 
party (Oman's representative at the NAM Ministerial), which 
does not return to Muscat until June 6. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The Embassy notes the active and positive engagement 
of Oman's Minister Responsible for Foreign Affairs (refs B-C) 
in encouraging Iran's acceptance of the USG's May 31 
conditional offer to join in direct talks with Iran.  The 
Embassy will report any further response.
