¶1. (U) This is an action request.  Please see para. 4. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post received a diplomatic note from the Turkish MFA 
dated Feb. 2, 2006 informing us that the GOT's legal 
procedures for entry into force of the CFE Host Country 
Agreement for Turkey have been completed. Text of diplomatic 
note follows in para 2. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Begin text of diplomatic note: 
 
USGY-I-663 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey 
presents its compliments to the Embassy of the United States 
of America and with reference to its Note No: USGY-I-6782 
dated August 3, 2005 and the Embassy's Note No: 1587 dated 
October 4, 2005, has the honor to inform the Embassy that the 
necessary legal procedures for entry into force of the 
Agreement for the Turkish Government have been completed. 
The Ministry awaits the notification of the Embassy 
concerning the completion of the legal procedures of the 
United States of America, which will complete all the 
necessary procedures for the entry into force of the 
Agreement for both Parties. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Turkey 
avails itself of this opportunity to renew to the Embassy of 
the United States of America the assurances of its highest 
consideration. 
 
Ankara 2 February 2006 
 
End text 
 
¶4. (SBU) Action Request: Post requests the Department's 
guidance on whether we can reply to the MFA by a diplomatic 
note which confirms that the Agreement is now in effect in 
accord with U.S. law.
