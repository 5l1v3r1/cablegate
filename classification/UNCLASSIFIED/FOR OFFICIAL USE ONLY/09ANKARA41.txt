SUBJECT: Update on PKK Violence (January 1-7, 2009) 
 
REF: ANKARA 0007 and previous 
 
(U) Sensitive but unclassified - please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶2. (U) During the January 1-7, 2009, reporting period, no security 
official was killed or injured.  The total number of security force 
casualties for the year through January 7, 2009, was zero killed and 
zero injured. 
 
¶3. (U) During the same period, no PKK terrorist was killed in 
clashes.  One PKKer was captured in Diyarbakir on January 5. 
According to official TGS website figures, through the first week of 
January, the number of PKK militants killed this year was zero, the 
number of those captured was one, and the number of those who 
surrendered was zero. 
 
¶4. (U) During the reporting period no civilian was killed or injured 
in PKK-linked violence.  The total number of civilian casualties for 
the year through the end of the first week of January, was zero 
killed and zero wounded.
