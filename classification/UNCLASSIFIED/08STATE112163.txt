SUBJECT: SINGAPORE: PERMISSION GRANTED FOR AMBASSADOR 
HERBOLD TO BE ABSENT FROM POST 
 
REF: SINGAPORE: 1125 
 
¶1. Department grants permission for Ambassador Patricia L. 
Herbold to be absent from post October 30 - November 1, 2008 
and November 5 - 14, 2008 for personal leave in Seattle, 
Washington; and for official travel to St. Louis, Missouri 
from November 2 - 4, 2008 to attend the F-15SG Roll-out 
Ceremony of Boeing Company on November 3, 2008. 
 
¶2. In Ambassador Herbold's absence, DCM Daniel L. Shields 
will serve as the Charge d'Affaires ad interim.
