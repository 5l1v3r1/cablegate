¶1.  This is an action message.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2.  The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests post assistance in conducting a 
pre-license check for the export of a propellant grain test 
system to Egypt via South Korea.  Post is requested to 
complete this Blue Lantern check within 30 days.  Lack of 
response to a Blue Lantern check will affect pending and 
future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3.  Reason for request: the Foreign End User, Abu Zaabal 
Company For Specialty Chemicals, has a limited licensing 
history with our office. 
 
¶4.  ACTION: Please contact the Foreign End User, Abu Zaabal 
Company For Specialty Chemicals, and verify its request for 
the commodity item listed below.  Please also confirm 
intended use as explained in the Statement of Purpose.  END 
ACTION.

STATEMENT OF PURPOSE: 
 
Sub-contractor company Hanwha Corporation together with main 
contractor company Samsung Techwin is providing a 155 mm 52 
caliber howitzer establishment program for three years 
production line at Abu Zaabal Company for Specialty Chemicals 
in Cairo.  The Strand Burner System will be used for testing 
composite propellant grains at Abu Zaabal Company in relation 
to the production of 155 mm ammunition projectiles.  Hanwha 
is procuring the Strand Burner System and shipping it 
directly to Abu Zaabal Company as part of a package program 
for the 155 mm 52 caliber howitzer project.
