PolChief delivered subject demarche to Laura 
Kansikas-Debraise, Office Director 
for the MFA Unit on Arms Control and Disarmament. 
Kansikas-Debraise said that 
the GOF fully agrees with our points; it fully supports an 
early start to FMCT 
negotiations and rejects any "package proposals," linked 
negotiation or creation 
of subsidiary bodies.  FMCT negotiations are a very high 
priority for the GOF, 
she added; as for the mandate, the Finns support the raising 
of any relevant 
issue -- such as verification -- even if it is not explicitly 
mentioned in the 
mandate.
