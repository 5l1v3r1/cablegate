¶1.  We delivered reftel points to the Indonesian Ministry of 
Trade and Ministry of Foreign Affairs on July 13.  Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU) Deputy Director of Commodity and 
Natural Resources Musthafa Taufik Abdulatif said that he 
would be willing to discuss progress on the bilateral MOU on 
illegal logging on the margins of the upcoming ASEAN Senior 
Economic Officials Meeting (SEOM) in Singapore with Mr. 
Katz.
