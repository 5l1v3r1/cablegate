¶2.   DAILY "EL PAIS" OF OCT. 18, 2005 REPORTED THE 
  DETENTION IN PARIS OF TWO SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF THE 
  SPANISH TERRORIST ORGANIZATION "GRAPO", MILITARY ARM OF 
  THE RECONSTITUTED SPANISH COMMUNIST PARTY (PCER).  THE 
  ARREST IS THE RESULT OF A COMBINED ON-GOING SPANISH, 
  FRENCH AND ITALIAN POLICE INVESTIGATION ON TERRORIST 
  GROUPS.  ACCORDING TO INVESTIGATORS, THE INDIVIDUALS 
  MAINTAINED CONTACTS WITH OTHER KNOWN EUROPEAN TERRORIST 
  GROUP MEMBERS IN AN ATTEMPT TO CREATE A PAN-EUROPEAN 
  LEFTIST TERRORIST GROUP. THE INVESTIGATION IS ALSO 
  RELATED TO ITALIAN ANARCHIST GROUPS THAT SENT LETTER 
  BOMBS TO SOME EMBASSIES AND EUROPEAN COMPANIES. NO ARMS 
  OR EXPLOSIVES WERE FOUND IN THE SEARCH OF THE SAFE 
  HOUSE OF THE INDIVIDUALS.
