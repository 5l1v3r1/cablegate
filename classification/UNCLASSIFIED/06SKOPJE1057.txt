SUBJECT: MACEDONIA WILL FOLLOW EU POSITION ON CUBA EMBARGO 
RESOLUTION 
 
REF: STATE 182099 
 
Poloff delivered reftel points on the Cuba embargo resolution 
to MFA State Counselor Svetlana Geleva on November 3.  Geleva 
said the GOM will follow the joint EU position on the annual 
Cuba embargo resolution at the UN General Assembly, as it has 
done in previous years, and will vote for the resolution.
