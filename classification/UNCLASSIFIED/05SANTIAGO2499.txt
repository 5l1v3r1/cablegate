SUBJECT: SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR DECEMBER 15 IRAQI 
ELECTION 
 
REF: STATE 220728 
 
¶1. The Ambassador called on Foreign Ministry Director General 
(the MFA's number 3) Carlos Portales on December 12, in part 
to deliver reftel demarche regarding the December 15 
elections in Iraq.  The Ambassador briefed Portales on the 
significance of the elections and emphasized that it was 
important that successful democracies like Chile show their 
support for the Iraqi people by issuing positive statements 
prior to and after the December 15 elections.  Portales 
listened attentively and said he would study the request. 
 
¶2. Poloff separately delivered reftel points to Foreign 
Ministry Director for Middle East Affairs Luis Palma.  Poloff 
briefed Palma on the Ambassador's meeting with Portales and 
reiterated the request that Chile issue positive statements 
about the elections. 
 
¶3. Post will follow up with the Foreign Ministry after the 
December 15 election.
