ZAMAN / OZGUR GUNDEM / EVRENSEL:        Inspectors of the Ministry 
of Internal Affairs have completed their investigation of 
support extended by 56 DTP (Democratic Society Party) mayors to 
the pro-PKK Roj TV in late December in 2005.  The inspectors' 
report, which designated Roj TV as the broadcasting instrument 
of the PKK/Kongra-Gel, will be included in the file opened by 
the Diyarbakir Prosecutor's Office for its ongoing probe 
concerning the issue.  It was highly probable that an 
investigation will be started by the Diyarbakir Prosecutor's 
Office following this report, reported Ozgur Gundem daily.  56 
mayors announced that they would not back away from their 
support. 
 
ZAMAN / HURRIYET:       During his visit to the U.S., National 
Security Council Secretary General Yigit Alpogan, sought U.S. 
support for the fight against PKK/Kongra-Gel.  Reportedly, 
Turkey, disturbed about the PKK presence in northern Iraq, made 
a call to U.S. officials to seek help in arresting top PKK 
officials in the northern Iraq.  Hurriyet reported that Alpogan 
found no positive response to his call from the U.S. side.
