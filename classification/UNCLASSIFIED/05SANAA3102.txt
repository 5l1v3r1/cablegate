SUBJECT: PRESIDENT SALEH REQUESTS ACCESS TO BETHESDA NAVAL 
MEDICAL CENTER 
 
 
President Saleh recently requested post's assistance to 
schedule a standard physical exam at the Bethesda Naval 
Medical Center during his upcoming visit to Washington.  The 
Republic of Yemen Government will pay all related costs. 
Post requests Department assistance in arranging an 
appointment on November 11.  Yemen is a key partner in the 
GWOT and allowing Saleh access to Bethsda will contribute 
positively to our bilateral relationship. 
Krajeski
