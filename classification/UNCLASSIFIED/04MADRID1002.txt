SUBJECT: SPAIN:HUMANITARIAN AID FOR HAITI 
 
REF: SECSTATE 52611 
 
¶1. We received Spain's response to reftel from Felipe Bragado 
de las Heras, Chief of Staff to the Secretary General of the 
Spanish Agency for International Development.  Spain has 
donated EUR 300,000 (USD 366,000 at an exchange rate of EUR 
1.0=USD 1.21) to the United Nations Flash Appeal for Haiti 
through the World Food Program. 
 
¶2. Spain is currently going through a political transition 
between governments.  We will ascertain if additional aid is 
envisioned when we meet with the new government's personnel. 
 
¶3. Minimize considered. 
ARGYROS
