SUBJECT: ACTIVISTS FOR DEMOCRACY OUTREACH 
 
REF: A. STATE 44903 
     ¶B. 07 STATE 108942 
 
¶1.  Mission contacted human rights groups to inquire about 
dissidents from un-free nations who may be living in exile or 
seeking asylum in Indonesia, per reftels.  Human rights 
contacts confirmed that there are no such dissidents living 
in exile or seeking asylum in Indonesia. 
 
¶2.  Mission will continue to look into this matter.  We 
continue to work actively to promote human rights and human 
dignity through Mission programs and meetings with activists 
for democracy.
