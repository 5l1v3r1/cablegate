¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on license 050106146 for the export of 
International Traffic in Arms (ITAR)-controlled cables for 
C-295 aircraft to Spain. Post is requested to complete this 
Blue Lantern inquiry within 45 days.  Lack of response to a 
Blue Lantern inquiry will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: Unusual routing (through Indonesia); 
no documentation from end-user Spanish Air Force. 
 
¶4. ACTION: The Department requests that Post make appropriate 
inquiries to determine if the Spanish Air Force  received the 
cables listed below and that the articles are being used for 
the purpose stated on the license. 
 
END ACTION.
