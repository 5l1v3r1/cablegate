SUBJECT: ACTING MINISTER OF DEFENSE REQUESTS MEDICAL EVACUATION 
AND TREATMENT OF SALVADORAN MILITARY OFFICER AT U.S. MILITARY 
TREATMENT FACILITY 
 
 
¶1. On 19 Oct, Salvadoran Vice Minister of Defense, General Abrego, 
the acting Minister of Defense, requested U.S. military 
support in transporting a critically injured Salvadoran Air Force 
officer, Colonel Juan Hector Reyes, to a U.S. military medical 
facility as soon as possible. Colonel Reyes was injured in a 
Salvadoran Air Force helicopter crash on 12 Sep and is currently 
hospitalized in the Salvadoran military hospital. The attending 
physician characterizes the injuries as life threatening and 
provided the following diagnosis: multiple traumas,second and third 
degree burns on 20 percent of the body surface, renal failure and 
sepsis throughout his body. 
 
¶2. I strongly recommend U.S. government concurrence with this request
