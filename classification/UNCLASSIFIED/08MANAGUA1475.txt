SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: IMPLEMENTATION OF AMENDED 
LACEY ACT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 126654 
 
¶1.  (U) On December 10, Econoff and USDA/APHIS Rep delivered 
reftel demarche to Martin Agenor, Deputy Director of the 
Phytosanitary Department in the Nicaraguan Ministry of 
Agriculture and Forestry (MAGFOR). MAGFOR represents the GON 
on matters related to the Lacey Act and the export of plants 
and plant products.  Agenor reacted positively to the 
demarche and said that the Lacey Act amendments would benefit 
Nicaragua in the long run.  Agenor promised to distribute our 
points to appropriate agencies within the Government of 
Nicaragua. 
 
¶2.  (U) Per reftel, FCS and FAS representatives at post have 
been provided demarche points to share with their contacts as 
appropriate.
