On November 16, Post discussed ref A points with Michael 
Keaveney, Department of Foreign Affairs (DFA) Third Secretary

to attend the ROEG meeting in Hamburg due to personnel 
constraints, but has made arrangements with the German 
Embassy in Dublin to obtain all documents that come out of 
the ROEG event.
