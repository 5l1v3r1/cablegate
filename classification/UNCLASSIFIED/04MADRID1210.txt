     ¶B. MADRID 1061 
 
¶1.  Spain's MFA informed us that Spain will be represented to 
the April 14 UN Special Session in observance of World Health 
Day by Spain's Ambassador to the United Nations as well as by 
Susana Estevez, Head of International Relations, Ministry of 
Interior. 
 
¶2.  In a separate communication, the Director General of 
Traffic, Ministry of Interior agrees that Working Party 1 of 
the Economic Commission for Europe has the technical 
knowledge and experience needed to serve as the proposed UN 
focal point.
