SUBJECT: BOSNIA: HAMAS AND FATAH AGREEMENT DEMARCHE 
DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 16453 
 
On February 12 Post delivered REFTEL demarche to MFA 
Assistant Minister for Multilateral Affairs Nedzad 
Hadzimusic.  Hadzimusic said BiH fully supports USG efforts 
to help the Israeli and Palestinian people live in an 
atmosphere of mutual recognition and peaceful co-habitation. 
He said Bosnia would support the USG taking an even more 
active role in the area, as USG involvement was essential to 
achieving this vision.
