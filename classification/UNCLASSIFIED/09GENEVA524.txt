                         PRESS RELEASE 
 
       On the Third Round of U.S.-Russian Talks in Geneva 
           to Work Out a New, Comprehensive Agreement 
                  to Replace the START Treaty 
 
     The third round of U.S.-Russian talks on strategic 
offensive arms was held from June 22-24 in Geneva. 
 
     The delegations focused their attention on preparing a 
report to the Presidents on progress made at the talks, for 
the July summit in Moscow.  Discussion also continued on the 
basic parameters of the future agreement. 
 
     The sides agreed to continue negotiations. 
 
End text.
