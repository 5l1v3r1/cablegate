SUBJECT: ARGENTINA ON SUDAN MISSION 
 
REF: STATE 43008 
 
¶1. POLOFF delivered reftel points March 23 to Minister Jorge 
Luis Gomez in the MFA's Office of International Organizations. 
 
¶2. Gomez said that the had spoken with Argentina's UN Mission 
regarding our draft resolution on the rehatting of the 
African Union Mission in Sudan.  He said that Argentina had 
no problems with the text in general and said the GOA would 
support an extension of AMIS' mandate as well as the call to 
"prepare the modalities for transition."
