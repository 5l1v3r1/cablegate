SUBJECT: NEW ZEALAND'S POSITION ON U.S. CANDIDACY FOR IMO 
COUNCIL 
 
REF: STATE 207080 
 
Post delivered reftel demarche November 14 to the New Zealand 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT).  Kristina Kirk, 
of MFAT's United Nations and Commonwealth Division, reported 
that New Zealand will support the U.S. candidacy for 
re-election to the International Maritime Organization 
Council in Category A.  Kirk added that New Zealand would 
stand for election to the Council in Category C in 2007 and 
would appreciate the U.S. government's consideration and 
support.
