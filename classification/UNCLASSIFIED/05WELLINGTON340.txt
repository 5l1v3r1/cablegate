SUBJECT: NEW ZEALAND: FUNDING AFGHANISTAN'S 
PARLIAMENTARY ELECTIONS 
 
REF: STATE 61560 
 
Post delivered reftel demarche April 6 to the New Zealand 
Agency for International Development (NZAID).  On April 28, 
Jane Coster, development program manager at NZAID, reported 
that the New Zealand government had approved NZ $1 million 
(US $723,300) in new funding for the Afghan National 
Assembly and Provincial Council elections scheduled for 
September 18.
