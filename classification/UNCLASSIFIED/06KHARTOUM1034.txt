SUBJECT:  Palestinian Foreign Minister to Visit Sudan 
¶1.  Palestinian Foreign Minister Mahmud al-Zahhar is 
slated to visit Khartoum from May 2 - 3, following his 
visit to Yemen and other countries in the region. 
¶2.  Aside from bilateral political meetings, expected to 
 
include President Bashir and Foreign Minister Lam Akol, 
his visit seeks to boost commercial relations.
