SUBJECT: NO RESPONSE YET ON DEMARCHE: WHO RECOMMENDATIONS 
TO THE UN COMMISSION ON NARCOTIC DRUGS (CND) TO CHANGE

REF: SECSTATE 195365 
 
 ¶1.  Poloff delivered reftel points December 8 to Jaroslaw 
Strejczek, officer in the MFA UN Department.  Strejczek 
responded that this was the first he had heard of the issue 
and would confer with other offices within the GOP, including 
the Ministry of Interior's (MOI) Narcotics Department. 
 
¶2. On December 19 Poloff followed up with Strejczek, who 
stated that he had no response from the other departments 
within the GOP.  Due to the holiday period, he expects no 
further information until after the new year.  Poloff will 
update when new information is available.
