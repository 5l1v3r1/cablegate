SUBJECT: KUDOS FOR ASSISTANCE TO FREE GAZA AMCITS 
 
¶1.  Jim:  The Bureaus of Consular Affairs and Near Eastern 
Affairs want to thank you and your staff for the exceptional 
assistance to "Free Gaza" American citizens Cynthia McKinney, 
Katherine Sheetz, and Adam Shapiro Warshaw. 
 
¶2.  We want to acknowledge the hard work of DCM Moreno, CG 
Andy Parker, Conoffs Elisa Greene, Wendy Vincent, Jay 
Sorensen, Daniel Mattern, Econoff Abram Paley, Assistant 
Naval Attache Frank Schlereth, and Cons LES Eve Zuckerman. 
 
¶3.  Post's efforts during the last week have demonstrated 
your commitment to the Department's highest priority: the 
safety and welfare of American citizens abroad, demonstrating 
the highest standards of consular and diplomatic service.  In 
working with the Department and other missions to assist the 
three Americans, you also epitomized the Consular Leadership 
Tenet "Build Great Teams."  Thank you for your continued 
service to Americans in distress overseas.   Warm regards, 
Janice and Jeffrey.
