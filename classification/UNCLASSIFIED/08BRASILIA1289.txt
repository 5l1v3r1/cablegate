SUBJECT: BRAZIL: DIPLOMATIC NOTE ON APPLICATION OF IAEA SAFEGUARDS 
FOR SHIPMENT OF IRRADIATED RESEARCH REACTOR FUEL 
 
REF:  A. STATE 89032, B. E-MAIL DELABARRE-DRISCOLL 09/12/2008 
 
Per REFTEL A (as clarified by REF B), on September 24, 2008, Post 
delivered diplomatic note number 387 (dated September 22, 2008) to 
the Brazilian Ministry of External Relations' (MRE) Division of 
Disarmament and Sensitive Technologies (DDS) with respect to 
arrangements that proposed to apply International Atomic Energy 
Agency (IAEA) safeguards to irradiated research reactor fuel of U.S. 
origin to be returned to the U.S. Department of Energy Savannah 
River Facility for disposition.  The note requested the MRE to 
confirm that the proposed arrangements are satisfactory.  Post will 
advise Washington on the response from the MRE.
