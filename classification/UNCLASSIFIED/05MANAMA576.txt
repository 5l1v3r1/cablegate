SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  DEMARCHE ON EU PARTICIPATION AT UNESCO 
NEGOTIATIONS ON CULTURAL DIVERSITY 
 
REF: STATE 67937 
 
Pol/Econ chief delivered reftel demarche April 18 on EU 
participation at UNESCO negotiations on cultural diversity to 
MFA Director for International Organization Affairs Ghassan 
Shaikho.  Shaikho said he would consult with the Ministry of 
Industry and Commerce on this issue and get back to us, but 
confessed he could not understand why the EU would want to 
introduce a trade issue into UNESCO negotiations.
