SUBJECT: GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT NUCLEAR TERRORISM: 
MOROCCAN DELEGATION FOR ANKARA 
 
REF: A. 06 RABAT 02299 
     ¶B. 06 STATE 198296 
 
¶1. (U)  Further to reftel A, Post informally received on 
February 8 details regarding Morocco's delegation to the 
Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism meeting 
scheduled in Ankara, Turkey on February 12-13, 2007.  Morocco 
will send a nine-member group consisting of the following 
individuals: 
 
-- Omar HILALE, Secretary General, Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, Head of Delegation 
 
-- Itimad SOUFI, Director of Safety and Security, National 
Center for Energy, Science and Nuclear Technology 
 
-- Abdellaziz HAJJANI, Nuclear Safety and Enforcement 
Officer, Ministry of Energy and Mines 
 
-- Nouredine MOUJAHID, Ministry of Interior 
 
-- Mohammed MONCIF, Head of Border Health Control Department, 
Casablanca Airport 
 
-- Nizar NASR, Customs and Tax Administration 
 
-- Driss Maskani FILALI, Advisor 
 
-- Hicham TOUIL, Directorate-General for National Security 
 
-- Asmae DERKAOUI, First Secretary, Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs. 
 
¶2.  (U) Nasser Bourita, Director of United Nations and 
International Organizations at the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, informed Embassy that Morocco would be making some 
presentations on the principles solicited by the USG, but did 
not provide precise details.
