SUBJECT: US PARTICIPATION IN UN HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL AND 
POSTURE TOWARD DURBAN REVIEW CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 18282 
 
¶1.  Pol Couns delivered reftel points to MFA Deputy Director 
General for International Organizations (IO A/S equivalent) 
Eviatar Manor February 27.  Manor expressed appreciation for 
the U.S. decision not to continue to participate in the 
Durban Review Conference based on the current draft outcome 
text. 
 
¶2.  Regarding our decision to participate as an active 
observer in the upcoming Human Rights Council session, Manor 
said Israel's recommendation is that the U.S. wait to 
announce a decision to run for a seat on the Council until it 
is clear that the Council is reforming.
