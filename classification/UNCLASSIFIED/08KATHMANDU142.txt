SUBJECT: TERRORISM FINANCE: DOMESTIC DESIGNATION OF FOUR 
INDIVIDUALS AND ONE ENTITY UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER 13438 
DELIVERED TO NEPAL 
 
REF: STATE 3749 
 
On February 1, Emboffs delivered per reftel identifying 
information on four individuals and an entity designated 
under Executive Order 13438 for threatening the peace and 
stability of Iraq and the Government of Iraq to the Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs (MFA).  Rudra Nepal, Under Secretary for 
UN and International Organizations at the MFA, said he would 
give the information to the Ministry of Home Affairs and also 
to Nepal Rastra Bank, the central bank of Nepal.
