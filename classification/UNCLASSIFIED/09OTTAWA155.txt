¶1.  (U) In response to reftels, Department of Foreign Affairs 
and International Trade (DFAIT) Deputy Director for 
Non-proliferation Karen Garner confirmed to pol/miloff on 
March 2 that PermRep in Geneva Marius Grinius will lead the 
Canadian delegation to the May 2009 Non-proliferation Treaty 
(NPT) PrepCom.  Garner also noted that Grinius will head 
Canada's delegation to the Centre for Non-proliferation 
Studies' Workshop on the NPT in Annecy, France on March 13-14.
