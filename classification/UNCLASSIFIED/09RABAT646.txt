SUBJECT: MOROCCO WILLING TO HOST CITES WORKSHOP 
 
REF: STATE 61744 
 
In response to reftel request, Moustapha Rezzouk, Head of 
Service of the Americas Division of the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, delivered a diplomatic note to EconOff on July 24 
confirming Morocco's willingness to host the proposed 
regional workshop for Convention on the International Trade 
in Endangered Species (CITES) Scientific Authorities.  The 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs invited the Embassy to coordinate 
the details of the workshop directly with the High Commission 
for Water and Forests.
