Â¶1.  In response to the diplomatic notes relayed to German 
government as instructed in reftel, post recently received 
two Notes Verbal, both dated October 16.  The first Note 
Verbal confirmed German support for the U.S. candidacy to the 
International Telecommunication Union (ITU) Council in the 
upcoming election at its Plenipotentiary Conference in 
Antalya on November 6-24, 2006.  The second note stated the 
German government's support for the candidacy of Julie Napier 
Zoller to the Radio Regulations Board of the ITU.  This note 
reiterated the request of the German government for U.S. 
support of the candidate of Matthias Kurth, currently head of 
the German Federal Regulatory Agency, to the post of General 
Secretary of the ITU.
