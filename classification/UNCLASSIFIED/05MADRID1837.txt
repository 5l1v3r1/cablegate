¶2.   DAILY SPANISH PRESS REPORTED ON MAY 12, 2005, THAT 
  THE SPANISH POLICE ARRESTED TWO ALLEGED MEMBERS OF 
  ETA'S FINANCIAL NETWORK.  THE DETAINEES HAD MADE 
  TRANSACTIONS AMOUNTING TO 50 THOUSAND DOLLARS TO ETA 
  ACTIVISTS, FELIX MANZANOS MARTINEZ AND ENRIQUE IZTUETA 
  BARANDICA RESIDING IN CAPE VERDE.  ACCORDING TO POLICE, 
  THE TRANSACTIONS WERE MADE AS PART OF ETA'S ECONOMIC 
  SUPPORT TO TERRORIST MEMBERS RESIDING IN CAPE VERDE AND 
  VENEZUELA.  DURING THE ARREST THE POLICE ALSO SEIZED 
  DIVERSE DOCUMENTATION AND COMPUTER DISCS, WHICH ARE 
  BEING ANALYZED.
