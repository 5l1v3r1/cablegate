SUBJECT: VIETNAMESE RESPONSE TO DESIGNATION OF IRAN'S DEFENSE 
INDUSTRIES ORGANIZATION 
 
 
REF: STATE 040909 
 
Per Reftel, on April 10 Post delivered to Vietnamese officials the 
talking points and background information regarding the addition of 
Iran's Defense Industries Organization (DIO) to the U.S. E.O. 13382 
assets freeze list for protecting the U.S. financial system from 
financial activities that support DIO's ongoing proliferation 
efforts.  Post will advise of any response.
