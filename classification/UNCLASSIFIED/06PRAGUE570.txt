SUBJECT: CZECH REPUBLIC WELCOMES FORMATION OF IRAQI 
GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: STATE 81632 
 
(SBU) On May 24, Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Czech 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Iraq Desk Officer Sylvia 
Sulaimanova, asking for a GOCR statement of support for the 
newly formed Iraqi government. Sulaimanova said that her 
government fully supports the new government. The MFA issued 
a public statement congratulating the Iraqis in English and 
Czech on the MFA website (www.mzv.cz). She said the MFA will 
discuss an exchange of diplomatic visits after the upcoming 
June 2-3 Czech elections.
