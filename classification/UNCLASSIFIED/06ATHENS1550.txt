BIOGRAPHY 
 
Professor Dr. Kalliopi Koufa is a Professor of Law and the 
Director of the Institute of International Public Law and 
International Relations at the University of Thessaloniki, 
Greece. She also serves as a Member of the United Nations 
Subcommission on the Promotion and Protection of Human Rights 
and its Special Rapporteur on Human Rights and Terrorism. 
Prof. Koufa teaches the newly-added course on Human Rights 
and Terrorism in the academic year 2002-2003.
