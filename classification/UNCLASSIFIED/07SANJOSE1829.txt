¶1. PolOff hand delivered a copy of the 2007 Costa Rica 
Report on International Religious Freedom (IRF) to 
Alejandro Solano Ortiz, Deputy Director of Foreign Policy 
of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) and to Viviana 
Tinoco, Human Rights Director of the Multilateral Policy 
Section of the MFA, per reftel.  Both of them expressed 
their appreciation for the report. 
 
¶2. Embassy has translated and posted on its web site the 
translated Spanish text of the report and a link to the 
English text, per reftel.  There was no local media or 
immediate public reaction to the report.
