SUBJECT: ITALIAN GOVERNMENT RESPONSE TO PREVENTING IRANIAN 
PROLIFERATION THROUGH USE OF A FALSE END USE WATCHLIST 
 
REF: STATE 164758 
 
¶1.  (U) Sensitive but unclassified.  Not for Internet 
distribution. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Econoff delivered reftel demarche and nonpaper on 
the possible use of false end users in Iranian proliferation 
efforts to Roberto Liotto, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
Office Director responsible for Nuclear Suppliers' Group 
(NSG) issues, on December 12.  In a December 14 conversation, 
Liotto noted that a similar list was circulated by U.S. 
experts at a recent NSG consultative group meeting.  Liotto 
has passed reftel list of items which can be used in the 
petrochemical and nuclear sectors to Italian licensing 
officials, and has asked them to use the list in making their 
export licensing decisions.
