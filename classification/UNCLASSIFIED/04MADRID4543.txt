¶1.  Per REFTEL, AmEmbassy Madrid takes this opportunity to 
certify that according to 12 FAH-1, H-024, the Mission 
Emergency Action Plan has received it's annual review.  In 
fact, the RSO office maintains an on-going update program 
review of the EAP and when appropriate, forwards any relevent 
updated information to DS/SPP/EP.  In addition, RSO is 
preparing a two-hour Mission-wide EAP mini-seminar, scheduled 
to be given early 2005.

taken will be published on-line at

registration for the Tripwire contact at post is allowed.
