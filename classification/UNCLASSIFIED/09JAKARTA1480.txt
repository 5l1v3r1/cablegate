SUBJECT:  RESULTS of BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: PRE-LICENSE END-USE CHECK 
ON LICENSE 050183340 AND 050183339 
 
REF: STATE 83460 
 
¶1. Post contacted the Foreign End User, the Indonesian Air Force 
(IAF), regarding licenses 050183340 and 050183339.  IAF Colonel 
Amrullah Asnawi confirmed the two 2009 procurements for the parts 
listed in reftel.  Asnawi said that the F-16 parts (case 050183340) 
were ordered by the IAF Avionics Department, and the C-130 parts 
(case 050183339) were ordered by the IAF Aeronautical Dept, both for 
the purposes listed on the licenses.
