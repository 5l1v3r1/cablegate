SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR DECEMBER 2009 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met January 4, 2010.  The meeting was 
chaired by DCM with Ambassador, CG, CONS, RAO, LEGATT, DHS, DAO, 
RSO, POL, ECON, USAID and PD in attendance.  Discussion centered 
around new requirements in the Visas Viper program and issues 
concerning visa revocation policies and procedures. 
 
¶2. There are no submissions for the December 2009 reporting period. 
 
¶3. The Mission's Visas Viper Coordinator is Consular Chief Miguel 
Ordonez.
