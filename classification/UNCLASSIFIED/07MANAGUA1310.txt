SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR DELIVERS DEMARCHE ON UNSCR 1747 
IMPLEMENTATION -- GON NONCOMMITAL 
 
REF: STATE 67462 
 
(U) Ambassador delivered demarche points on UNSC Resolution 
1747 to Nicaraguan Foreign Minister Samuel Santos and 
Americas Office Director Orlando Gomez on May 21.  They will 
review our position and consider it.  We emphasized that all 
governments are expected to report on the matter to the UNSC 
by May 24.  Gomez explained that Ambassador Magda Enriquez, 
the Director of Multilateral Affairs office and who just 
returned from a week in New York, will follow up on the 
issue.  Enriquez may be tagged to serve as Nicaragua's 
ambassador to the United Nations.
