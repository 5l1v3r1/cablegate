SUBJECT: CIVAIR BRAZIL: MASTER TOP LINHAS AEREAS DESIGNATED AS CARGO 
CARRIER UNDER AVIATION BILAT 
 
¶1. Post has received a diplomatic note from the Ministry of External 
Relations designating Brazilian cargo carrier Master Top Linhas 
Aereas as a cargo carrier to operate charter cargo flights under the 
terms of the U.S.-Brazil bilateral aviation agreement. 
 
¶2. Begin text of note: 
 
The Ministry of External Relations of the Federative Republic of 
Brazil presents its compliments to the Embassy of the United States 
of America and has the honor to inform the Embassy, as requested by 
the National Agency of Civil Aviation (ANAC), of the designation of 
the company Master Top Linhas Aereas, Ltda. - MTA to operate 
non-regular cargo flights, pursuant to Article 3 and Annex II of the 
Agreement on Air Transport between the Government of the Federative 
Republic of Brazil and the United States of America, as amended. 
 
Brasilia, August 3, 2006. 
 
End text of note.
