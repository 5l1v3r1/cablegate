¶1. The following is the mission's FY10 slate of 21 candidates 
for multi-regional (MRP) and regional projects (RP), 
including one nomination for the Edward R. Murrow Program for 
Journalists. Nominees have Country Team approval and Front 
Office clearance. 
 
¶2. Nominees by program: 
 
-Combating Trafficking in Persons (MRP): Mr. Yahya AFFARA, 
principal chief inspector, Department of Asylum and 
Migration, Lebanese General Security 
 
-International Crime Issues (MRP): Maj. Charles SAADE, chief 
of Gemmayze police station 
 
-Protection of Intellectual Property Rights (MRP): Capt. 
Khaled YOUSSEF, IT specialist and analyst, Internal Security 
Forces 
 
-U.S. Trade Policy (MRP): Ms. Zouha SAKR, chief of party, 
World Trade Organization, and lawyer 
 
-Women and Entrepreneurship (MRP): Ms. Odile RIACHI, creative 
director, Saatchi & Saatchi 
 
-State and Local Governments (MRP): Ms. Hala Jane MOUGHANIE, 
project manager, Technical Office for Lebanese Cities 
 
-U.S. Foreign Policy (RP): Maj. Joseph MOUSALLEM, chief of 
public relations, Internal Security Forces 
 
-Volunteerism: Together We Can (MRP): Ms. Angelique BEAINO, 
volunteer manager and recruiter, Francophone Games, and 
member of Lebanese Volunteers Association 
 
-U.S. Judicial System (MRP): Mr. Chadi EL HAJAL, lawyer, 
legal consultant and professor 
 
-Combating International Crime (MRP): Col. Monzer EL AYOUBI, 
Gendarmerie commander in South Lebanon 
 
-U.S. Foreign Policy: Decision Making Process (MRP): Mr. 
Salim HAMADE, foreign affairs officer in Lebanese Democratic 
Party and advisor to Minister of Youth and Sport 
 
-Small Business Development (MRP): Mr. Nidal NAHRA, founder 
of Next Cone Ltd. 
 
-Cultural Heritage Preservation (MRP): Mr. Antonine LAHOUD, 
assistant professor, School of Architecture, Lebanese 
American University 
 
-TV Broadcast Journalism (MRP): Mr. Elie AHWACH, anchor, 
Future Television, and freelance producer 
 
-Emerging Markets and Global Economy (MRP): Mr. Youssef 
SADEK, deputy secretary-general, Beirut Stock Exchange 
 
-Agricultural Trade and Food Security (MRP): Mr. Khalil AKL, 
head of agricultural services in the Bekaa, Ministry of 
Agriculture 
 
-Promoting Tolerance through Arts (MRP): Mr. Michel 
ELEFTERIADES, co-owner of Elefteriades Productions and Elef 
Records 
 
-Young Leaders: Political and Civic Engagement (RP): Ms. 
Crystel EL CHAYEB, head of public relations, General 
Secretariat of Catholic Schools in Lebanon 
 
-Accountability in Government and Business (MRP): head of 
legal studies, Ministry of Finance 
 
-U.S. Foreign Policy and Human Rights (MRP): Ms. Elisabeth 
ZAKHARIA, director of Human Rights Institute, Beirut Bar 
Association 
 
-Murrow Program for Journalists (French): Ms. Dima SADEK, 
news anchor and talk show presenter, OTV 
 
¶3. Post will submit individual nominations for the
