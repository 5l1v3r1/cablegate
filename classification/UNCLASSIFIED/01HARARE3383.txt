SUBJECT: PRIVATE WEEKLY CRITICIZED CHANGES TO ELECTORAL ACT 
 
 
¶1.   THE PRIVATELY OWNED WEEKLY "THE STANDARD" DEDICATED 
   ITS NOVEMBER 11 EDITORIAL TO THE GOVERNMENT'S PROPOSED 
   CHANGES TO ZIMBABWE'S ELECTORAL ACT.   UNDER HEADLINE 
   "MASSIVE FRAUD IN THE MAKING," THE PAPER COMMENTS: 
 
¶2.   "AMENDMENTS APPROVED THIS WEEK TO THE ELECTORAL ACT BY 
   THE GOVERNMENT. . .REPRESENT ONE OF THE MOST ELABORATE 
   FRAUD SCHEMES EVER DEVISED IN RECENT HISTORY. . .  THE 
   LATEST AMENDMENTS TO THE ELECTORAL ACT BY THE 
   GOVERNMENT. . .ARE THE MOST SHAMEFUL, DISHONEST, CRUDE, 
   AND DESPERATE ATTEMPTS TO DISENFRANCHISE GROUPS OF 
   PEOPLE SUSPECTED OF HAVING OPPOSITION SYMPATHIES AT 
   HEART. . .  AGAINST THIS PRESENT BACKGROUND, NEXT YEAR'S 
   PRESIDENTIAL ELECTION IS SET TO BE A MASSIVE 
   SCANDAL. . ."
