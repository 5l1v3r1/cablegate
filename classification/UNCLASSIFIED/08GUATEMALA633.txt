For the May 21 Day of Solidarity with the Cuban People, 
Embassy Guatemala will place an op-ed in the major Guatemalan 
daily "Prensa Libre".  As a follow-up, the Political and 
Economic Counselor, drawing on his experience as PAO in 
Havana, will host a press roundtable to discuss the current 
conditions in Cuba, the efforts of Cuban dissidents, and the 
realities faced by political prisoners.  He will also appear 
on a local radio talk show to discuss these issues.  Summary 
cables will be sent as requested in ref A.
