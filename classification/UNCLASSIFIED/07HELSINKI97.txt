SUBJECT: UN MIDDLE RING SECURITY IN IRAQ - FINLAND WILL 
CONTRIBUTE TO DISTINCT ENTITY TRUST FUND 
 
REF: STATE 17022 
 
PolChief discussed subject request with Anssi Leino, 
Counselor in the Finnish MFA's Unit for the Middle East, and 
urged Finland to consider matching or exceeding its 
2004-2005 contribution of USD 1.35 million to the Distinct 
Entity Trust Fund.  Leino could not specify now the exact 
amount of Finland's contribution, which is still the subject 
of interagency discussion.  However, he confirmed that 
Finland intends to make a contribution to the DETF this year 
and will inform us as soon as an exact amount is approved.
