SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON UNGA RESOLUTIONS WITH ANTI-ISRAEL BIAS 
DELIVERED TO AZERBAIJAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 159386 
 
Poloff delivered reftel demarche on UNGA resolutions with an 
anti-Israel bias to Azerbaijani Security Affairs Department 
Deputy Director Yusuf Mammadaliyev on November 28. 
Mammadaliyev said Azerbaijan's voting on Israel-related UNGA 
issues is often complicated as a result of the 
Nagorno-Karabakh conflict and Azerbaijan,s position in the 
Organization of Islamic Conference, but that that the GOAJ 
would take the U.S. views on board.
