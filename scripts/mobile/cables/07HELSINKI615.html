(C) PolChief delivered subject demarche to A/Director for Arms Control and Disarmament Nina Nordstrom August 10. Finnish officials fully understand the technical errors and intentional misrepresentations inherent in Moscow's public arguments against the placing of missile defense assets in Poland and the Czech republic. The GOF has not taken a public stance on the missile defense issue, aside from welcoming US efforts to dialogue with Moscow. Nordstrom said she did not expect any of this to change, but reiterated the GOF's appreciation for our willingness to share with partners the substance of our talks with Russia. WARE
