Karen C. Reider for reasons 1.4 (b) and (d). (C) Poloff delivered reftel A on July 31 to Czech MFA Asia Department's Head of the Far East and Pacific Unit Ivana Grollova. Grollova said the EU decision to permit a lower level Burmese official to attend ASEM was reached on the basis of thoughtful discussions among EU ministers (ref B). Therefore, she believes this consensus decision is not likely to be reversed. The Czechs supported the decision on the condition that the EU use the Burmese Foreign Minister's attendance at ASEM as an opportunity to have substantive discussions on human rights violations in Burma. Grollova said the Czechs are also pressing for an EU-sponsored resolution on Burmese human rights abuses at UNGA. CABANISS
