------------------ POLITICAL VIOLENCE ------------------ ¶1. (SBU) DEMONSTRATIONS ¶A. YES ¶B. YES ¶C. YES ¶D. ALCAN POSTS PREVIOUSLY REPORTED THE AVERAGE NUMBER OF DEMONSTRATORS TO BE APPROXIMATELY 50. HOWEVER, A STEADY INCREASE IN THE NUMBER AND FREQUENCY OF DEMONSTRATIONS DID OCCUR THROUGHOUT CANADA. DURING AND AFTER THE IRAQI WAR/CONFLICT CG'S TORONTO AND MONTREAL, DUE TO THE ETHNIC MAKE-UP OF THOSE CITIES, EXPERIENCED DEMONSTRATIONS WITH NUMBERS IN THE THOUSANDS. OBVIOUSLY, THIS INCREASE WAS A DIRECT RESULT TO GROWING ANTI-WAR ACTIVISTS AND U.S. POLICY IN THE MIDDLE-EAST AND PERSIAN GULF IN ADDITION TO THE ETHNIC MAKEUP, POLITICAL ENVIRONMENT, AND LARGE STUDENT POPULATION WHERE STUDENTS TEND TO BE SOMEWHAT LEFT-LEANING. ANTI-WAR DEMONSTRATIONS AND RALLIES DID OCCUR IN OTHER LARGE CANADIAN CITIES SUCH AS OTTAWA, VANCOUVER, CALGARY AND HALIFAX AND WERE CONSTANT DURING AND IMMEDIATELY FOLLOWING THE IRAQI WAR. ¶E. MILITARY ACTIONS AND U.S. FOREIGN POLICY IN GENERAL ¶F. PEACEFUL ¶G. THE USG DID HAVE TO PAY ABOUT CAD 13,000 LAST SPRING TO THE BUILDING MANAGEMENT CORPORATION TO REPAIR DAMAGE TO THE BUILDING HOUSING THE CONSULATE IN MONTREAL AS A RESULT OF A LARGE ANTI-WAR PROTESTS -- MOSTLY FOR THE REMOVAL OF RED PAINT THROWN REPEATEDLY ON THE EXTERIOR WALLS, REMOVAL OF BLACK MARKER FROM OUR GREAT SEAL, AND OTHER NON-STRUCTURAL, SUPERFICIAL DAMAGE. A MONTREAL POLICE OFFICER DID SUSTAIN SOME MINOR INJURIES AFTER HE WAS STRUCK WITH A THROWN BRICK. ¶H. NO ¶I. YES, ANTI-WAR ¶J. YES ¶K. NUMBERS OF ANTI-WAR DEMONSTRATORS STEADILY INCREASED. ¶L. ALMOST EVERY KNOWN ISLAMIC EXTREMIST GROUP HAS EITHER A PRESENCE OR SYMPATHIZERS IN CANADA SUCH AS THE HIZBALLAH; AL-ITIHAD; ALGERIAN GIA; THE LTTE; EGYPTIAN ISLAMIC JIHAD AND AL-QAEDI. SIKH EXTREMISTS ARE ALSO PRESENT AND ARE UNDER SCRUTINY BY CANADIAN SECURITY SERVICES AS A RESULT OF THEIR INVOLVEMENT IN THE 1985 AIR INDIA BOMBING. ALCAN POSTS HAVE SEEN AN INCREASE IN THE NUMBER OF VISA VIPER ENTRIES. ¶B. THE ORGANIZATIONS AND SYMPATHIZERS ARE PRIMARILY ASSESSED AS PROPAGANDA, FINANCIAL, AND/OR SUPPORT CELLS. MOST RECENTLY, FINANCIAL SUPPORT FRONTS FOR HAMAS. OPERATIONAL ACTIVITIES HAVE BEEN PLANNED IN CANADA IN RECENT YEARS SUCH AS WELL DOCUMENTED EL-MAATI PROBE. ANOTHER NOTABLE EXAMPLE WAS THE DECEMBER 1999 ARREST OF CONVICTED TERRORIST AHMED RESSAM AND THE SUBSEQUENT ARREST OF A NUMBER OF HIS ACCOMPLICES. RESSAM WAS A MONTREAL BASED TERRORIST APPREHENDED AT THE BRITISH COLUMBIA/WASHINGTON STATE BORDER AS HE WAS ENROUTE TO CONDUCT AN ATTACK IN THE UNITED STATES. INVESTIGATIONS INDICATE CANADA-BASED EXTREMISTS HAVE TIES TO IDENTIFIED OR SUSPECT TERRORISTS OVERSEAS. THE NATURE AND EXTENT OF THE CANADA-BASED PERSONS INVOLVEMENT WITH THESE TERRORISTS IS OFTEN UNCLEAR BUT SHOULD GIVE CAUSE FOR CONCERN. ¶C. NO ¶D. (S/NF) YES, THERE ARE EXTREMISTS WHO ARE IN SYMPATHY WITH THE ISLAMIC FUNDAMENTALIST ORGANIZATIONS INCLUDING AL-QAEDA. SEVERAL ARRESTS BY CANADIAN SERVICES WERE MADE OVER THE LAST YEAR OF SUSPECTED AL-QAEDA MEMBERS. NGO'S HAVE ALSO BEEN DETERMINED TO BE IN SUPPORT OF SUSPECTED TERRORIST FUND RAISING ACTIVITIES. THERE ARE SIKH EXTREMISTS IN SYMPATHY WITH THE SIKH ORGANIZATION(S). THE LARGEST OF THOSE COMMUNITIES, WE BELIEVE, ARE NOT FULLY KNOWN TO SECURITY SERVICES, WHO CONTINUE TO WORK THE TARGETS IN QUESTION TO ACCESS AND RE-ASSESS THAT QUESTION. FOR EXAMPLE, THE GLOBAL RELIEF FOUNDATION (GRF) AND THE BENEVOLENCE INTERNATIONAL FUND (BIF), BOTH LISTED AS HAVING SUPPORTED TERRORISM BY THE U.S. HAVE BEEN ACTIVE IN CANADA. THE TAMIL LTTE IS ALSO VERY ACTIVE IN RAISING MILLIONS OF DOLLARS THROUGH A VARIETY OF FRONT GROUPS. ¶E. YES ¶F.
