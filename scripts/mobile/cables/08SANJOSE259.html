REF: STATE 30472

¶1. (S) On April 1 we notified Carlos Cordero Madrigal, Director of Disarmament and Terrorism Section of the MFA of the USG decision to designate Al-Shabaab as a Foreign Terrorist Organization (FTO) and as a Specially Designated Global Terrorist. We provided Cordero with the identifiers for Al-Shabaab and made him aware of the consequences of these designations, per reftel. ¶2. (S) Cordero informed us that the GOCR condemns these terrorist organizations, in accordance with the GOCR policy on human rights. BRENNAN
