(C) Deborah Panckhust, officer in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade's (MFAT) Disarmament Division, says that MFAT lawyers have now approved the U.S. draft text for the Zheleznogorsk MOU. (C) Panckhurst said that GNZ is mindful that they must spend the proposed funds by June 30, the end of New Zealand's fiscal year. She is hopeful the agreement can be approved and signed in time.
