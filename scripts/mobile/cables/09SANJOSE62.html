SUBJECT: TERRORISM FINANCE: PRE-NOTIFICATION OF DESIGNATION 

OF FOUR AL-QAIDA ASSOCIATES DETAINED OR FORMERLY DETAINED 

IN IRAN 

 

REF: STATE 4313

(S) On January 26, we notified Carlos Cordero of the MFA's Department of Disarmament, Terrorism and Organized Crime of USG plans to submit the names of four individuals (listed reftel) to the UNSC 1267 Sanctions Committee for inclusion on its consolidated list of individuals subject to UNSC-mandated sanctions (reftel). Cordero appreciated the pre-notification but offered no substantive response. Cordero explained, per GOCR established procedures regarding UNSC designations, that the MFA would notify Costa Rica's banking oversight agencies, Costa Rica's intelligence service (DIS), and coordinate with Interpol.
