FINNISH FIRM (S) 

 

REF: A. STATE 10937 

     ¶B. SCOTT - MAYES E-MAIL 27 FEB 09

¶1. (U) This is an action request. Embassy Helsinki, please see paragraph 3. ¶2. (S) Background: In February 2009, the U.S. provided Finnish officials with information that Chinese company Shanghai Technical By-Products International Corp. Co. (STBPI) was working to procure an ultrasonic cleaning unit produced by the Finnish company Finnsonic. STBPI and the Iranian intermediary Doostan were seeking this equipment on behalf of Ardalan Machineries Company, which is a procurement cover for Shahid Hemmat Industrial Group (SHIG - Iran's liquid-fueled ballistic missile organization) (REF A). We have not received a response from the GOF since we raised this issue (REF B). We therefore want to follow-up with Finnish authorities and request an update on the status of Finland's investigation into this case at the earliest possible time. ¶3. (S) Action Request: Request Embassy Helsinki follow-up with appropriate host government authorities to request an update on the GOF's investigation of this case and report response. ¶4. (U) Washington POC is ISN/MTR Mike Kennedy (Phone: 202- 647-3176). Please slug any reporting on this issue for ISN/MTR and EUR/PRA. CLINTON
