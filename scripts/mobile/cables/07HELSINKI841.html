SUBJECT: FINLAND: SANCTIONS ON BURMA 

 

REF: STATE 155019

(C) Polchief delivered reftel demarche Nov. 19 to Theresa Zitting, Counselor for Burma in the Finnish MFA's Unit for Asia. Zitting welcomed our sharing information on US sanctions against the Burmese junta and agreed that the US and the EU can intensify the impact of the sanctions on the regime if they are coordinated and complement one another. She welcomed the US Congress's consideration of new sanctions pertaining to gems and timber, along the lines of existing EU sanctions. She also expressed the hope that Congress would consider widening those sanctions to precious metals as well. HYATT
