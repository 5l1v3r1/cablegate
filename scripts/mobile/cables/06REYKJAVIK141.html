SUBJECT: ICELAND TO SUPPORT GUATEMALAN UNSC BID 

 

REF: SECSTATE 20173

(C) Icelandic MFA Head of International Institutions Division Nikulas Hannigan affirmed to poloff April 24 that Iceland will support Guatemala over Venezuela for the Group of Latin American Countries' seat in the UN Security Council for 2007-2008. Hannigan noted that Reykjavik is not announcing its choice publicly. van Voorst
