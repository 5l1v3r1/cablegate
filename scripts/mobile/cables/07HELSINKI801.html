SUBJECT: FINLAND: COMBATING PKK TERRORISM IN EUROPE 

 

REF: STATE 148531

¶1. (C) PolChief delivered subject demarche to Finnish MFA Counterterrorism Coordinator Pekka Oinonen Oct. 29. Oinonen welcomed our views as a follow-up to recent PKK-related demarches and said that Finland )- as a longtime friend of Turkey )- was following developments with great concern. He welcomed US efforts to work with the Turkish and Iraqi governments to urge restraint and non-military efforts to defuse the PKK threat. ¶2. (S) Oinonen noted that there are approximately 3500 Kurds in Finland, roughly half from Iran and half from Iraq. There are very few Turkish Kurds in Finland, and little evidence to suggest that the PKK is engaged in extensive fundraising here. However, Oinonen said that there have been some press and other reports from Ankara accusing a London-based NGO, the Kurdistan Human Rights Organization, of seeking to raise funds in the Nordic region. The GOF has not found proof of this type of activity here but is watching. WARE
