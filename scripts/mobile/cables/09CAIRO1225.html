JONES APPAREL: EGYPT'S QIZS ARE GOOD FOR BUSINESS1.(SBU) KEY POINTS -- Compliance Officers from Jones Apparel Group told us that labor conditions in the factories of their Egyptian suppliers are generally good. -- Jones Apparel has not seen evidence of worker mistreatment in QIZ factories. -- As a result of the QIZ and proximity to U.S. markets, Egypt continues to gain market share over its Southeast Asian rivals. -- There are some signs of a slow recovery in apparel demand.
