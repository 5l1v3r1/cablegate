SUBJECT: HONDURAS LIKES THE IDEA OF A LATIN AMERICAN 
CANDIDATE FOR UNESCO DIRECTOR-GENERAL POST 
 
REF: SECSTATE 130914 
 
POLOFF delivered reftel demarche on the election for the 2009 
UNESCO Director General position to the Director of 
Multilateral Affairs at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Jose 
Lorenzana, on December 16.  Lorenzana noted that Honduras had 
been approached by Oman, Egypt and Morocco regarding each 
countries' own candidate for the position.  After hearing 
that the United States would like to see a Latin American 
candidate, Lorenzana excitedly began to think of possible 
Honduran candidates.  POLOFF will follow up with MFA and 
report any subsequent GOH decision.
