SUBJ: HUMAN RIGHTS VETTING FOR CAMBODIAN NOMINEES FOR ILEA 
"PROTECTION DETAIL BRIEFING" COURSE IN BANGKOK 
 
REF:  STATE 46501 
 
¶1. This is an action cable.  See Paragraph Six. 
 
¶2. Reftel is guidance for screening nominees for U.S.-sponsored 
training programs. 
 
¶3. To comply with the Leahy amendment relative to U.S. sponsored 
security assistance training, the U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has 
conducted local human rights vetting for the following individuals 
selected for the Protection Detail Briefing course from August 3-7, 
2009 in Bangkok, Thailand. 
 
NOMINEES: 
 
NAME: Nget, Thea (Male) 
DPOB: May 24, 1966; Kampot 
RANK/POSITION: Lt. Col, Unit Commander 
DEPT/UNIT: Ministry of Interior, Protection Police Department 
 
NAME: Suy, Sokhom (Male) 
DPOB: Feb. 5, 1963; Phnom Penh 
RANK/POSITION: Major, Deputy Unit Commander 
DEPT/UNIT: Ministry of Interior, Protection Police Department 
 
ALTERNATE: 
 
NAME: Hong, Ly (Male) 
DPOB: October 10, 1966; Takeo 
RANK/POSITION: Major, Deputy Unit Commander 
DEPT/UNIT: Ministry of Interior, Protection Police Department 
 
¶4.  U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, 
as of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above-mentioned individuals. 
 
¶5. The individuals will attend the Protection Detail Briefing course 
from August 3-7, 2009, in Bangkok, Thailand.  The Protection Detail 
Briefing will consist of a cadre of U.S. Secret Service instructors 
with a curriculum designed to further the participants' knowledge of 
organizing a protective visit and the preventive measures that 
should be taken prior to the arrival of a Protectee. 
 
¶6. Action request: Please verify whether the Department possesses 
any credible information that the individuals named above have been 
implicated in gross violations of human rights.  Post requests a 
Leahy vetting response from the Department by June 26, 2009.
