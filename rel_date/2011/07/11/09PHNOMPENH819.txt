¶4.  U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, 
as of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above-mentioned individuals. 
 
¶5. The individuals will attend the "Criminal Investigation Training 
in Trafficking Cases" Course, November 16-27, 2009 in Phnom Penh, 
Cambodia.  The course is sponsored by the United States Department 
of State, with instruction provided by experts from The Asia 
Foundation and the Southeast Asia Investigations into Social and 
Humanitarian Activities (SISHA) organization. 
 
¶6. Action request: Please verify whether the Department possesses 
any credible information that the individuals named above have been 
implicated in gross violations of human rights.  Post requests a 
Leahy vetting response from the Department by November 13, 2009.
