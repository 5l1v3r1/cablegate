SUBJECT: BRASILIA REQUESTS COPIES OF AGREEMENTS SIGNED AT 
U.S.-BRAZIL SCIENCE AND TECHNOLOGY COOPERATION MEETING 
 
REF:  06 STATE 190806 
 
¶1.  This is an action message.  See point 3. 
 
¶2.  Reftel notes that at the July 21, 2006, U.S. - Brazil S&T 
Commission meeting held in Washington, the United States and Brazil 
signed renewals of the Implementing Arrangement for Cooperation on 
Environmental Scientific Research in the Large Scale 
Biosphere-Atmosphere Experiment in Amazonia (LBA) between NASA and 
the National Institute for Amazonian Research (INPA), and the 
Biological Determinants of Forest Fragment Project (BDFFP) under the 
Technical/Scientific and Financial Cooperation Agreement between the 
Smithsonian Institution and the Brazilian Ministry of Science and 
Technology (MCT). 
 
¶3.  Post has not received copies of these agreements.  Posts 
requests that the Department forward clearly legible, signed copies 
of these agreements to the attention of: Patricia Norman, U.S. 
Embassy Brasilia. 
 
¶4.  Please reply by cable giving the date sent.
