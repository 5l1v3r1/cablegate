¶4. The individuals listed above currently serve with the 911 
Airborne Brigade; however, none of the listed individuals were 
members of the 911 Airborne Brigade prior to 31 December 1997. 
Furthermore, this friendship exercise will not provide any direct 
USG training, but will make use of U.S. aircraft.  U.S. Embassy 
Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, as of this date, 
it possesses no credible information of gross violations of human 
rights by the above named individuals. 
 
¶5. Individuals will participate in the Static Line and Free Fall 
Friendship Jumps, February 21, 2010 in Phnom Penh, Cambodia. 
 
¶6. Action request: Please verify whether the Department possesses 
any credible information that the individuals named above have been 
implicated in gross violations of human rights.
