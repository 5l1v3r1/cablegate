Director of the Office of International Organizations in the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Tuy Ri stated that Cambodia 
would continue its full support (Ref B) for the resolution on 
compliance in the UNGA Plenary.  He added that Cambodia stood 
against the proliferation of arms and supported a significant 
number of the non-proliferation, arms limitation and 
disarmament agreements and commitments.
