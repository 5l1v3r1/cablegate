SUBJECT:  CLEARANCE GRANTED TO R/V KA'IMIMOANA, JUNE 1 - AUGUST 30,

REF:  A) 05 WELLINGTON 932; B) 05 STATE 218122 
 
¶1.  Embassy is in receipt of a letter from the New Zealand Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT) office of the Administrator of 
Tokelau granting clearance for the research vessel Ka'Imimoana to 
enter the waters of Tokelau from June 1 to August 30, 2006. 
 
¶2.  The text of the letter is as follows. 
 
Begin text: 
 
I refer to your letter of 5 December 2005 requesting a vessel 
clearance for Tokelau waters for the 'Ka'Imimoana' during the period 
1 June 2006 - 30 August 2006. 
 
Tokelau has considered the request and accepted it.  Consent is 
granted on the condition that New Zealand scientific representatives 
and Tokelau officials have access to all data and samples collected, 
and that research information is submitted after the conclusion of 
the research voyage. 
 
The vessel should also inform the Fisheries Communication Centre for 
the New Zealand Ministry of Fisheries of the position and times it 
enters and exits Tokelau's EEZ. 
 
End text. 
 
¶3.  A copy of the letter will be faxed to OES/OA, with the original 
to follow via pouch under registration number 9239455.
