SUBJECT: FRENCH RESPONSE TO UPCOMING PRESIDENTIAL 
ANNOUNCEMENT ON THE "GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT NUCLEAR 
TERRORISM" 
 
REF: STATE 110160 
 
¶1. (SBU) Ambassador Stapleton, accompanied by Russian 
Ambassador Alexander Avdeev, delivered reftel demarche to 
French Foreign Minister Douste-Blazy.  The Minister gave a 
private preliminary statement of support in principle, noting 
France's own concerns about nuclear terrorism.  France, 
Douste-Blazy said, was already strongly supporting efforts 
underway at the UN to protect against nuclear terrorism.  The 
Russian Ambassador left a non-paper with the Minister 
(translated reftel talking points) who promised a speedy 
reply.  Post is following up with appropriate MFA officials 
on public statements of support.
