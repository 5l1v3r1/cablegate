SUBJECT: BERMUDA OUTREACH ON ESTA COMPLIANCE 
 
REF: STATE 005558 
 
¶1.      Post has worked in conjunction with U.S. Customs and 
Border Protection (CBP) Preclearance officers to inform 
Bermudians and third country nationals resident in Bermuda about 
mandatory compliance with ESTA provisions.  Post issued a press 
release on January 21, 2010 and recently updated its website to 
include current information about ESTA compliance. On January 6, 
2010, Consul Rosholt and CBP Supervisory Officer Eric Sappol 
spoke at a meeting of the local Rotary Club where Officer Sappol 
responded to questions about ESTA. 
 
 
 
¶2.      The Consulate plans additional outreach, including 
attempts to place a monthly outreach column in local newspapers.
