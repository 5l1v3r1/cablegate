Dear Mr. Mikulak 
 
Thank you for your letter of August 10, 2009 (sic) referring 
to discussions between Canada and the United States to 
establish principles and arrangement governing the conduct of 
challenge inspections under the Chemical Weapons Convention 
(CWC). 
 
I have the honour to confirm that the principles and 
procedures outlined in your letter under reference are 
acceptable to the Canadian National Authority for the 
Chemical Weapons Convention. 
 
Yours sincerely, 
 
Louis-Philippe Sylvestre 
National Coordinator
