Post delivered reftel demarche December 18 to the New 
Zealand Agency for International Development (NZAID).  Jane 
Coster, development program manager at NZAID, reported 
January 12 that the New Zealand government had approved NZ 
$15 million (US $10.4 million) in assistance to Afghanistan 
over the next three years -- or NZ $5 million (US $3.47 
million) a year -- beginning in July 2006.  The New Zealand 
government is expected to announce that assistance at the 
Afghanistan Compact in London on January 31-February 1.
