SUBJECT: PACIFIC SALMON TREATY AMENDMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 75706 
 
Per reftel, Embassy sent diplomatic note and attachments 
regarding the Pacific Salmon Treaty Amendment on May 26, 2005 
to Foreign Affairs Canada.  According to a representative of 
Foreign Affairs' Oceans Law Division, the GOC response will 
require an "Order in Council," or cabinet-level approval. 
The process normally takes up to six weeks, but can be 
expedited when necessary.
