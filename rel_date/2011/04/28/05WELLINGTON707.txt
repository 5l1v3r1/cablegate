Pol-Econ Counselor discussed reftel seminar with Deborah 
Panckhurst, Deputy Director of the Disarmament Division in 
the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade.  Panckhurst said 
that unfortunately no GNZ official is available to attend the 
seminar.  Panckhurst stressed this does not mean that New 
Zealand is not interested in the meeting or its outcomes. 
GNZ officials would be very interested in seeing papers and 
related materials from the seminar.
