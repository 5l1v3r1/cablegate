SUBJECT: FINLAND SUPPORTS BUERGENTHAL ICJ RE-ELECTION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 199724 
 
(U) Poloff delivered reftel demarche and diplomatic note to 
Marja Lehto, Director of International Legal Affairs at the 
MFA.  Lehto said that Finland appreciates Judge Buergenthal's 
long record of excellent service to the international 
community and strongly supports his re-election to the ICJ. 
She added that Finland would have joined other countries in 
nominating Buergenthal, but that Finland also plans to 
nominate an ICC justice this year and did not want to 
nominate two candidates in so short a time period.
