SUBJECT: FINLAND - TERRORISM FINANCE: DESIGNATION OF 
ABOUSOFIAN ABDELRAZIK 
 
REF: STATE 119988 
 
¶1. (SBU) Econoff delivered reftel demarche on designation of 
Abousofian Abdelrazik to Juha Rainne, MFA Legal Officer for 
Terrorist Financing.  Rainne appreciated the notification and 
stressed that inclusion on the UN 1267 Committee consolidated 
list requires that EU countries (per EU regulations) act 
immediately to implement UN Security Council-mandated 
sanctions. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Rainne will follow up with designated GOF agencies 
to ensure that the proper government registries are checked 
for any information regarding Abousofian Abdelrazik or his 
aliases.
