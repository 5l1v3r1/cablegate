¶1. (C) PolOff spoke with Juha Ottman, Director, Unit for 
Western Balkans on February 21 regarding reftels.  On 
February 18 GoF declared its intention to recognize Kosovo 
independence, with formal recognition expected on February 
¶29.  GoF's process requires that the cabinet approve the 
recognition then present it to the president via meetings 
held only on Fridays.  The first possible occasion for such 
approval is after the president's return on February 29. 
Ottman stated the item is on the agenda.
