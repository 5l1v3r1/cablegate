SUBJECT: PANAMA TO SIGN FTA WITH CANADA; SIGNING AT SOTA 
 
Classified By: DCM David Gilmour for reasons 1.4 (b) and (d) 
 
¶1.  (C) Panama will conclude negotiations on a free trade 
treaty with Canada this week, Minister of Commerce and 
Industry Gisela Alvarez de Porras told the Ambassador on 
March 17.  The GOP hopes to sign the treaty with Canada 
during the Summit of the Americas, continued Porras. 
 
¶2.  (U) Panama currently has bilateral free trade agreements 
in place with El Salvador, Costa Rica, Taiwan, Chile and 
Singapore. The GOP has also concluded negotiations on free 
trade agreements with Honduras and Guatemala, which are 
awaiting ratification by their respective legislatures. 
Also, the GOP is currently negotiating free trade agreements 
with Nicaragua and the Caribbean Community (CARICOM).  Panama 
cooperates with the Secretariat of Central American 
Integration (SIECA) as a non-voting member in order to 
participate in SIECA's free trade negotiations with the 
European Union.
