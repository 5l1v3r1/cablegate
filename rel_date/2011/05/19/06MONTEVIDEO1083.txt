(C) On November 6, PolCouns delivered reftel demarche to 
Acting Director for Multilateral Affairs Beatriz Nunez. Nunez 
accepted the talking points attentively, but clearly stated 
that Uruguay would continue its past pattern of voting in 
favor of Cuba. She added that Uruguay voted in concert with 
other Mercosur countries, but offered no further substantive 
comment.
