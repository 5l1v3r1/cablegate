SUBJECT: JAPAN'S MOFA PUNTS NICARAGUAN ELECTION-RELATED 
DRAFT STATEMENT TO MANAGUA 
 
REF: STATE 114848 
 
Classified By: Acting Political Minister Counselor Carol Reynolds. 
Reason: 1.4 (b)(d). 
 
(C) Embassy Tokyo Political Officer conveyed reftel message 
and draft to MOFA Mexico and Central America Division 
Director Setsuo Ohmori on July 19.  Ohmori responded on July 
25 that his division had studied that draft and had no 
particular views on the matter.  He believed it was most 
appropriate for representatives of the Japanese embassy in 
Managua to work with the other donors on the issue and so had 
forwarded the draft to Managua for further consideration.
