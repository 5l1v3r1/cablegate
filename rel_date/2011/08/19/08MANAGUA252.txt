SUBJECT: NICARAGUA: DEMARCHE DELIVERED - MALDIVES DRAFT RESOLUTION RE CLIMATE CHANGE AND HUMAN RIGHTS

REF: STATE 19889 
 
(SBU) On March 3, Post delivered demarche points on the Maldives' 
draft resolution attempting to link climate change and human rights 
to Orlando Gomez, Director of the Americas and International 
Organization Offices, Mario Barquera Baltodano, Office of the 
Americas, and Gregorio Torrez, Office of International Policies, all 
with the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA).  At this time, the MFA 
has no response.  Post will follow-up and report when a formal 
Nicaraguan position is available.
