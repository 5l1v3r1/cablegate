SUBJECT: ROKG INFORMED OF WEB-BASED INFORMATION PORTAL 
PROJECT FOR GLOBAL INITIATIVE 
 
REF: STATE 91275 
 
(SBU) On July 2, poloff conveyed reftel information sheet 
regarding a Defense Threat Reduction Agency (DTRA)-hosted 
August 26-29 conference in Germany that will discuss the 
establishment of a web-based information portal to Kim 
Jae-woo, MOFAT's Deputy Director of the Nonproliferation and 
Disarmament Division.  Poloff noted that DTRA in June had 
announced its intent to establish the information portal at 
the third meeting of the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear 
Terrorism, and was now listing an August 1 registration 
deadline for the conference.  Kim expressed appreciation for 
the notification and undertook to pass the conference 
information to relevant authorities.
