SUBJECT: SPAIN SUPPORTS U.S. CANDIDATE FOR UN HUMAN RIGHTS 
COMMITTEE 
 
REF: STATE 130575 
 
Poloff delivered reftel demarche Spanish Sub-Director General 
for Human Rights Fernando Fernandez-Arias on August 10. 
Fernandez-Arias affirmed that Spain still intends to vote for 
Professor Ruth Wedgwood for the UNHRC.  He added that the 
Spanish delegation in New York controls this decision and 
suggested that the USG follow up at that level as necessary.
