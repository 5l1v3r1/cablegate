SUBJECT: INDIAN PARTICIPATION AT DEAD SEA CONFERENCE 
 
REF: A. STATE 127710 
     ¶B. NEW DELHI 5166 
     ¶C. STATE 124857 
 
¶1.  (U) Responding to points in Ref A, MEA Joint Secretary 
(Gulf) Sanjay Singh told PolMilOff on July 12 that he and the 
Indian Charge in Baghdad, Mr. S. Sharma, would attend the 
July 18-19 Dead Sea Conference on Iraq. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) He also confirmed that the GOI had disbursed the 
full amount of their $10 million pledge to the International 
Reconstruction Fund Facility for Iraq (IRFFI).  He did not 
know if IRFFI had used the balance of the Indian funding yet. 
 
 
¶3.  (SBU) Asked about future plans for assistance to Iraq, 
Singh stated that the GOI was reviewing the list of 
democratization projects (Ref C) and welcomed any information 
we could provide on small-scale electricity projects (Ref B).
