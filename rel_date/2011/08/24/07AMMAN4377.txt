SUBJECT: DEMARCHING JORDAN ON ARAB DONOR'S CONFERENCE ON 
DARFUR 
 
REF: STATE 148406 
 
¶1. (SBU) On October 28, PolCouns conveyed reftel points to 
Thamer al-Adwan, Director of the Foreign Minister's Private 
Office, noting U.S. support for greater Arab engagement on 
the Darfur issue, and urging that such contributions be in 
support of existing international efforts.  Adwan said that 
on issues such as those now facing Sudan, Jordan has 
preferred to work under a UN umbrella, observing that the GOJ 
will be contributing police to the international monitoring 
mission there. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Adwan said he expects that Abd al-Salam al-Abadi, 
Secretary-General of the Hashemite Charitable Organization,
