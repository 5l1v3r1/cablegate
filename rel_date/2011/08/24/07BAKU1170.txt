SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: ENCOURAGING CONTRIBUTIONS TO 
THE SPECIAL TRIBUNAL FOR LEBANON 
 
REF: STATE 129919 
 
¶1.  (SBU) A/DCM delivered reftel demarche to Deputy Foreign 
Minister Mahmud Mammadguliyev on September 21, noting Foreign 
Minister Mammadyarov's interest in the Lebanon crisis during 
the summer of 2006 and Azerbaijan's growing role as an 
international donor.  Mammadguliyev responded that 
international tribunal issues were handled by the MFA's Legal 
Department and promised to convey the information to Deputy 
Foreign Minister Khalaf Khalafov, who oversees legal issues. 
Mammadguliyev asked how Russia had voted on UNSC 1757 and 
also expressed surprise that the Tribunal's estimated annual 
operating costs were so high. 
 
¶2.  (U) We also briefed MFA Americas Desk staff on this issue 
in a September 21 telcon, asking that the information be 
conveyed to the MFA Legal Department.
