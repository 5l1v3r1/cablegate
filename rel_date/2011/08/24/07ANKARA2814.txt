SUBJECT: TURKEY: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON UNGA THIRD COMMITTEE 
RESOLUTIONS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 145641 
 
(U) We delivered reftel demarche on November 19th to MFA 
United Nations desk officer Ilknur Bademli.  She stated that 
Turkey was preparing to co-sponsor the resolutions on Burma 
and North Korea.  She did not have information regarding 
Belarus, and stated Turkey would abstain regarding Iran.  In 
addition, Turkey intends to support thematic resolutions on 
elections, religious intolerance, defamation of religion, and 
the EU death penalty.
