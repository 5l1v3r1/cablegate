SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: DESIGNATION OF FOUR JI 
INDIVIDUALS (LIST 77) 
 
REF: STATE 65201 
 
(U)  This cable is sensitive but unclassified, please 
protect accordingly. 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Econoff met April 28 with Moroccan Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs (MFA) official Mr Azouz Aboulkouroum 
to discuss terrorism finance-related issues and to 
deliver reftel demarche concerning the designation of 
four members of Jemaah Islamiyah. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Aboulkouroum was receptive to demarche and 
confirmed that information provided will be 
expeditiously distributed to the relevant government 
agencies.  He requested that USG provide more specific 
information, if possible, on actionable funds and/or 
financial assets present in Morocco.  Econoff explained 
that Post is unaware of any specific accounts at this 
time, but assured Aboulkouroum that he would be 
contacted should specific information become available.
