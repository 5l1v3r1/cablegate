SUBJECT: ARAB DONORS CONFERENCE ON DARFUR 
 
REF: STATE 148406 
 
(U) PolOffs delivered reftel points to MFA Director of 
International Organizations Fouad Darwish on October 30. 
Darwish believed Bahrain had sent a representative to the 
donors conference in Khartoum.  He agreed that the 
Transitional Darfur Regional Authority (TDRA), ICRC, IFRC, 
and other UN multilateral agencies were the appropriate 
conduit for humanitarian assistance in Darfur.
