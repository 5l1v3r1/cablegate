¶1.  (U) This is an Action Request--Please see paras 3-4. 
 
¶2.  (U) Per reftel, Embassy Jakarta will host the 2008 
Southeast Asia Reporting Officers Conference (SEAROC), 
October 8-9.  The conference will include policy-oriented 
panels and tradecraft discussions.  We encourage 
participation by economic and political officers from 
throughout Southeast Asia and beyond. 
 
¶3.  (U) ACTION REQUEST:  We ask that participants notify us 
as soon as possible--ideally by September 15-- of their plans 
to attend. 
 
¶4.  (U) ACTION REQUEST (Con'd):  We are currently developing 
a program of sessions that will address the key economic and 
political developments in the region as well as tradecraft 
issues.  We seek your input.  We need and welcome volunteers 
to give presentations.  Please send your ideas to the control 
officers listed below.
