SUBJECT: AMBASSADOR DELIVERS PRESIDENT BUSH - PM BROWN 
LETTER TO PRESIDENT GUL 
 
REF: A. ANKARA 1788 
     ¶B. WHITE HOUSE 261827Z SEP 08 
 
¶1. (SBU) Ambassador used the opportunity of an October 16 
meeting to present the President Bush -- PM Brown letter to 
Gurcan Turkoglu, Senior Foreign Affairs Advisor to President 
Abdullah Gul.  Turkoglu, who had not yet received a copy of 
the letter from the MFA, underscored the importance of food 
policy and trade for Turkey, and promised to bring the letter 
and the proposed Global Partnership for Agriculture and Food 
to President Gul,s attention.
