Â¶1. (SBU)  On July 25, PolOff informed Ms. Najwa al-Serri, 
First Secretary and the terrorist combating officer at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA), of the United Nations 
designations of Arif Qasmani, Mohammad Yahya Mujahid, and 
Fazeel-A-Tul Shaykh Abu Mohammed Ameen al-Peshawari, as well 
as the U.S. designation of Nasir Javaid, as terrorists. 
PolOff reiterated the ROYG's obligation to freeze all assets 
and to impose a travel ban and an arms embargo per reftel 
demarche.  Serri confirmed that all UN and U.S.-designated 
terrorists' identifying information would be passed to the 
Yemeni Central Bank.  Serri could not confirm that action 
would be taken on U.S.-designated terrorist Javaid.
