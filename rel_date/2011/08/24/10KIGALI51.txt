¶1. (SBU) President Kagame has accepted A/S Carson's 
invitation to meet at the AU summit (reftel).  Government of 
Rwanda is still working out details on the trip but has 
provided post the following information: 
 
Composition of delegation - President Paul Kagame, Foreign 
Minister Louise Mushikiwabo, officials from the Office of the 
Presidency including those who work on ICT issues, and MFA 
staff. 
 
Contact information in Addis / Arrival and departure times / 
Lodging information - all tbd. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post understands the Office of the Presidency has 
been in contact with USAU A/DCM Maybury.  Post will query 
again next week to ask for updates on the Rwandan 
delegation's itinerary, lodging and contact information.
