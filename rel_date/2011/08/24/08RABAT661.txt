SUBJECT: MOROCCAN MFA CONSIDERS U.S. POSITION ON NAM SUMMIT 
STATEMENT 
 
REF: STATE 74879 
 
(SBU) On 13 July, Poloffs spoke with Moroccan Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs (MFA) International Organizations Division, 
Chief of Non-Aligned Movement Nadia Talmi regarding the U.S. 
request for Morocco to influence the drafting of the NAM 
conference statement on Iran.  Talmi advised she would 
communicate the U.S. recommendations to her superiors and to 
the MFA team traveling to the NAM Summit in July.
