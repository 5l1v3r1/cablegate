(SBU) PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Oussama Khachab, 
Head of the Americas Desk at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 
 Khachab responded that the GOL is sending the Lebanese 
Ambassador to Senegal, Michel Haddad, to attend the subject 
meeting November 14-17 in Bamako.  He explained that the 
current political stalemate in Lebanon prevented the GOL from 
sending anyone at the ministerial level.
