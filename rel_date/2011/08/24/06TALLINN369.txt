SUBJECT: ESTONIAN RESPONSE TO IRANIAN REGIME'S TREATMENT 
OF RELIGIOUS MINORITIES 
 
REF: STATE 54484 
 
On April 12 Poloff delivered reftel points concerning 
Iran's treatment of religious minorities to Kadri Jogi, 
MFA Iran Desk Officer.  Estonia has a small Bahai 
community of 150 members and the GOE is concerned about 
the problems the Bahai and other religious minorities 
face in Iran.  At the April 11-12 GAERC meeting in 
Brussels Jogi said that Foreign Minister Urmas Paet 
strongly supported the EU's common opinion that Iran 
must pay more attention to human rights and religious 
freedom.
