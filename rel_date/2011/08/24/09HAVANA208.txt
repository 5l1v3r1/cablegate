¶1.  USINT Havana warmly welcomes and grants country clearance 
to the commissioners and staff of the U.S. Commission on 
International Religious Freedom.  Control officer for the 
visit will be Political-Economic Section Chief Jim Benson 
(e-mail: BensonJ@state.gov, Office phone: 53-7-836-4039, Cell 
phone: 53-5-286-7814).  Reservations have been made at the 
Hotel Nacional (daily rate $63.00, breakfast included).

However, USINT personnel are not permitted to enter the 
airport arrival area, so contact will be made curbside 
outside the baggage area.  USINT will provide local mobile 
phones as requested reftel.

requested reftel will be provided septel.
