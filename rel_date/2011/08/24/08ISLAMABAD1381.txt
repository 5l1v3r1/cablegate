SUBJECT: PAKISTAN SUPPORTS NEW UN SPECIAL REPRESENTATIVE 
FOR AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 25020 
 
(U) On March 27, Poloff spoke with Farhat Ayesha, Deputy 
Director for UN and International Organizations at the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs, to discuss Pakistan's support 
for the new Special Representative of the UN Secretary 
General for Afghanistan, Kai Eide.  Ayesha confirmed Pakistan 
does support Eide and informed Poloff the Pakistan delegation 
to the UN officially greeted him upon his appointment.
