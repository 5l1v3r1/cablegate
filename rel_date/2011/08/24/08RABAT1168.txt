(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel message on December 17 to 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Head of United Nations Division 
Azzedine Farhane on the Iran human rights resolution for the 
UNGA plenary.  Farhane reported that other countries have 
also asked Morocco to absent themselves again during the 
upcoming plenary but was unable to confirm Morocco's plan of 
action.  He promised to forward the U.S. request to his 
supervisors for their consideration.  (Comment: We have no 
reason to anticipate a change in their approach).
