Post delivered the Secretary's invitation letter for the UNGA 
meeting on Partnering for Food Security to Foreign Minister 
Moratinos' office on September 11.  The same day, Ministry 
Director General for Development Policy Planning and 
Evaluation Juan Lopez-Doriga told econcouns that either 
Minister Moratinos or Secretary of State for Foreign 
Assistance Soraya Rodriguez would represent Spain.  In 
response to separate expressions of hope from Lopez-Doriga 
and Presidency staff that both Minister Moratinos and 
Secretary Rodriguez could participate, econcouns confirmed 
that the invitation was for only one representative. 
Lopez-Doriga (who will attend the September 14-15 technical 
meeting in Washington) and our Presidency contact both 
welcomed the draft statement but made no substantive comments.
