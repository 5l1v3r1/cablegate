¶1. (U) Pol/C delivered reftel demarche regarding the USG 
candidate for the UN Audit Committee (IAAC) to Ramadansyah 
Hasan of the Directorate for International Security and UN

and David Walker's qualifications as a candidate.  Pol/C left 
behind a copy of Walker's c.v.

office would carefully review the U.S. request for support.
