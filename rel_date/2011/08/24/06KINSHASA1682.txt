¶1.  (SBU)  Embassy Kinshasa delivered non-paper and talking 
points regarding proposed UNGA resolution on the situation of 
human rights and children in Lebanon.

Deputy Director for International Organizations at the DRC 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs, said she understood the U.S. 
points but could not take a position on the issue until she 
had studied the matter further. 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  We will follow up with the Ministry and report any 
future response.
