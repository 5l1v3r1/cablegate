SUBJECT: WAY-AHEAD FOR CREATING THE UN PEACEBUILDING 
COMMISSION 
 
REF: A. STATE 130530 
     ¶B. PARIS 4527 
 
¶1. Poloff delivered reftel points July 15, together with 
draft UNGA, UNSC, and ECOSOC resolutions, to MFA IO UN Reform 
action officer Nicolas Kassianides. 
 
¶2. Kassianides thanked us for the information and said that 
the French translations would be particularly helpful.  We 
undertook to share the points in French as soon they were 
available.  In turn Kassianides said that the points would be 
shared with MFA IO Director Ripert and other concerned GoF 
officials.  NOTE: As reported ref B, GoF officials have 
expressed support for establishment of the Peacebuilding 
Commission, to be controlled by the UNSC.  END NOTE. 
STAPLETON
