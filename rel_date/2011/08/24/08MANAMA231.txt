SUBJECT: BAHRAIN: GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT NUCLEAR 
TERRORISM EXERCISE PLANNING GROUP MEETING LOGISTICS 
 
REF: STATE 34519 
 
¶1. Econoff conveyed reftel non-paper regarding the Global 
Initiative (GI) to Combat Nuclear Terrorism planning group 
meeting to MFA Legal Advisor Dr. Yousif Abdulkarim April 8. 
 
¶2. Dr. Abdulkarim reiterated the GOB's support for the GI, 
but said he had been unaware of the exercise planning group 
meeting.  He was unsure as to which two Bahraini officials, 
if any, would constitute Bahrain's delegation.  He promised 
to follow up with the Embassy when he had this information.
