SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE GRANTED FOR CODEL HAGEL 
 
REF: STATE 47580 
 
¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Senator Chuck Hagel, Chairman, 
Subcommittee on International Economic Policy, Staff members 
Lou Ann Linehan, Rexon Ryu, and Eric Rosenbach, and DOD Cdr. 
Steve Vahsen, and grants country clearance for their visit to 
Afghanistan on April 13-14, 2006, to conduct political, 
security and economic consultations with the government of 
Afghanistan.  Control Officer is Angus Simmons, Political 
Counselor, email addres: SimmonsAT@state.gov, telephone 
011-937-010-8128 or cell number 011-937-991-40555.  All 
travelers should bring USG ID for identification purposes and 
to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2. Embassy Kabul will provide transportation, security, 
escorts and lodging. 
 
¶3. Post requests confirmation of TS security clearance for 
Eric Rosenbach. 
 
¶4. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
