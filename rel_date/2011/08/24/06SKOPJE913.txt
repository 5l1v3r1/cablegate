SUBJECT: MACEDONIAN MFA STATE SECRETARY WILL PARTICIPATE IN 
CENTCOM CONFERENCE ON IRAQ 
 
REF: STATE 155973 
 
¶1. (U) P/E Chief delivered reftel invitation to participate 
in the October 3-5 CENTCOM-sponsored Iraq Coalition 
Conference in Warsaw to MFA State Secretary Igor Ilievski on 
September 21.  Ilievski confirmed on September 26 that he 
would attend the Conference, together with Brigadier General 
Zoran Dimov from the Ministry of Defense. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Ilievski is the third-ranking official in the MFA, 
following the Foreign Minister and Deputy FM.  A fluent 
English and Polish speaker, he is a graduate of Warsaw 
University (M.A., 2002) and the Warsaw Diplomatic Academy 
(2004). 
 
Name: Ilievski, Igor 
Title: State Secretary, Ministry of Foreign Affairs, Republic 
of Macedonia 
Contact Information: tel. 00-389-2-3110-333; email: 
igor.ilievski@mfa.gov.mk; address: Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, Dame Gruev 6, 1000 Skopje 
Security Clearance: NATO Confidential 
Travel Details: Will arrive Warsaw October 2, and depart the 
afternoon of October 5. Further travel details are not yet 
available.  The Macedonian Embassy in Warsaw will arrange his 
transportation from the airport to the hotel. 
 
¶3. (U) Ilievski plans to travel with MFA Junior Desk Officer 
Magdalena Vladeva, who will prepare additional bilateral 
meetings in Warsaw with Polish officials.
