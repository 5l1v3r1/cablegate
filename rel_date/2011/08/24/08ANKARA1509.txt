SUBJECT: TURKEY: DESIGNATION OF ADDITIONAL IRANIAN ENTITIES 
AND INDIVIDUALS DEMARCHE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 88759 
 
(U) We delivered reftel demarche to MFA Department Head for 
Nonproliferation and Disarmament Bora Kerimoglu on August 19. 
 Kerimoglu expressed appreciation for the information, 
reaffirmed the GOT's commitment to enforce all Iran-related 
UNSC resolutions, and pledged to share the information with 
relevant GOT agencies.  While he could not confirm whether 
any of the entities or individuals listed in reftel have been 
subject to GOT enforcement action, Kerimoglu said the GOT has 
been careful to adhere to the UNSC resolutions and ensure 
that appropriate action is taken against any individuals and 
entities listed in the annexes of UNSC resolutions found to 
be engaged in activities in Turkey.
