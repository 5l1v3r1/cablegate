Tanzania welcomes the offer of USG assistance regarding 
implementation of the Chemical Weapons Convention (CWC), 
Irene Mkwawa-Kasyanju, Senior Foreign Service Officer in the 
Legal Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA), 
told Poloff on August 17.  Kasyanju said the interim national 
authority met for the first time last week and that the focal 
point for the authority is the Ministry of Defense (MOD). 
Kasyanju suggested Poloff meet with representatives from MFA, 
MOD and the Ministry of Justice and offered to arrange such a 
meeting for the week of August 21.
