Sensitive but Unclassified.  Please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) Embassy Rabat has conveyed to senior levels of the 
GOM the U.S. invitation for Morocco to participate with 
President Bush in a roundtable discussion on democracy on the 
margins of UNGA, September 19 (ref B).  Morocco will be 
represented in the discussion by Prime Minister Jettou. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The Embassy has also conveyed the invitation for 
Morocco to participate in the September 20 Community of 
Democracies Ministerial (ref A), and the request for a 
bilateral meeting between U/S Burns and Foreign Minister 
Benaissa, within the parameters conveyed ref C.  Foreign 
Minister Benaissa is currently leading the Moroccan 
delegation to the NAM summit in Havana. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Additionally, the Embassy approached Zouheir 
Merchaoui, Chief of Staff to Arab Maghreb Union (AMU) 
Secretary-General Ben Yahia, with the request that U/S Burns

 Merchaoui undertook to pass the request to Ben Yahia, who is
