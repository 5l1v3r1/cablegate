SUBJECT: JORDAN AND THE INTERNATIONAL COMPACT WITH IRAQ

(SBU) Polcouns made reftel points April 11 at a meeting with 
Thamer Adwan, director of Foreign Minister Khatib's private 
office.  Adwan was not aware of the planned May 3 meeting on 
the International Compact with Iraq (ICI), but called in 
international organizations bureau director Ayman al-Ameri, 
who was.  Adwan and al-Ameri said the GOJ would wait until it 
had received the UN/Iraqi invitation to the meeting before 
deciding Jordan's level of representation.
