¶1.   POST WISHES TO SUBMIT THE FOLLOWING NAMES AND

¶2.   THE SPANISH DAILY NEWSPAPER "EL PAIS" ON SEPTEMBER 
  30, 2004, REPORTED THAT THE SPANISH NATIONAL POLICE 
  (SNP) ARRESTED FIVE SUSPECTED ETA MEMBERS IN VIZCAYA 
  AND LEON, SPAIN.  THE ARRESTED ARE ALLEGEDLY MEMBERS OF 
  THE ORGANIZATION LOGISTICS NETWORK.  THE RAIDS WERE 
  CONDUCTED AS A RESULT OF THE INVESTIGATION OF THE 
  DOCUMENTS SEIZED DURING THE ARREST OF IBON FERNANDEZ DE 
  IRADI, THE ALLEGED ETA LOGISTICS NETWORK LEADER, IN 
  ¶2002.  ACCORDING TO POLICE, THE FIVE DETAINED ARE PART 
  OF THE ORGANIZATION'S INFRASTRUCTURE THAT ASSISTED ETA 
  MEMBERS TO FLEE AND RE-ENTER SPAIN.  DURING THE RAIDS 
  POLICE SEIZED DOCUMENTS, COMPUTER DISCS, DIARIES, NOTE 
  BOOKS, ETC, THE CONTENTS OF WHICH ARE NOW BEING 
  ANALYZED.
