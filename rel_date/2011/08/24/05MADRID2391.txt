SUBJECT: SPAIN: RESPONSE TO WCO FRAMEWORK DEMARCHE 
 
REF: STATE 94927 
 
Econoff delivered reftel points on June 8 to Jose Maria 
Alvarez Gomez-Pallete, Subdirector General for International 
Relations, at the Spanish Customs Agency.  Alvarez advised 
Econoff that reftel points would be passed directly to the 
Spanish Customs Director General.  On June 21, Econoff 
followed up on reftel points with Alvarez.  Alvarez said that 
Spain would support the proposed WCO Framework and that the 
Spanish Customs Director General had replied directly to the 
US Customs Director General via letter.  Alvarez further said 
that he would provide Econoff with a copy of the letter 
confirming Spain's decision to support the WCO Framework.
