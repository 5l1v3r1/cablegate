SUBJECT:  DEMARCHE TO KAZAKHSTAN ON FORCE ADJUSTMENT 
 
REF: STATE 229319 
 
¶1. (SBU) Ambassador phoned Foreign Minister Tokayev late 
December 22 and told him that we would be making a force 
adjustment announcement, and that details would be provided 
to the Ministry the next day.  Poloff then delivered reftel 
demarche in full on December 23 to Aidar Aitashev, First 
Secretary, MFA Department of Asia and Africa.  Aitashev is 
 
SIPDIS 
the primary MFA working level contact on issues relating to 
Iraq and Afghanistan.  He expressed appreciation for the 
information, and noted that it would be passed to Minister 
Tokayev. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Immediately following Poloff's meeting with MFA, 
DATT delivered reftel points to Major General Almaz 
Abdulmanov, First Deputy to the Chairman of the Chiefs of 
Staff Committee and Chief of the Operations and Plans 
Department of the Joint Staff.  Abdulmanov is DATT's primary 
interlocutor on issues concerning the Kazakhstani contingent 
in Iraq.  Abdulmanov accepted the information, ordered it 
translated immediately, and said he would brief MINDEF 
Altynbayev later on December 23.
