SUBJECT: CANADA ON MDA:  EXPECT A YES VOTE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 220751 
 
Poloff delivered reftel to Catherine Godin, Deputy Director 
of the Humanitarian Affairs and Disasters Response Group at 
Foreign Affairs Canada, who has been in contact with Canada's 
delegation to the Geneva conference.  She said that Canada 
has worked hard to support the adoption of the Third Protocol 
both prior to and at the conference and that, if the ICRC 
plans to move forward with a vote on its adoption, Canada 
will support it.
