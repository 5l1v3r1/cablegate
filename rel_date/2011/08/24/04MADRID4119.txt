¶2.   SPANISH DAILY PRESS AND OTHER COMMUNICATION MEDIA 
  REPORTED ON OCT. 4, 2004 AND SUBSEQUENT DAYS THAT 
  FRENCH POLICE IN COORDINATION WITH THE SPANISH GUARDIA 
  CIVIL, AND UNDER ORDERS OF FRENCH ANTITERRORIST JUDGE 
  LAURENCE LE VERT, CONDUCTED A LARGE-SCALE OPERATION 
  AGAINST ETA INFRASTRUCTURES IN FRANCE IN WHICH MORE 
  THAN 150 POLICE MEMBERS PARTICIPATED. THE OPERATION IS 
  THE RESULT OF MORE THAN FOUR YEARS OF INVESTIGATION AND 
  HAS TAKEN PLACE IN SEVERAL LOCATIONS IN THE SOUTHWEST 
  OF FRANCE.  THE ALLEGED NUMBER-ONE POLITICAL LEADER OF 
  THE TERRORIST ORGANIZATION, SINCE 1993, MIGUEL ALBIZU 
  IRIARTE, ALIAS: "MIKEL ANTZA" WAS ARRESTED AS WELL AS 
  HIS SENTIMENTAL PARTNER SOLEDAD IPARRAGUIRRE GUENECHEA, 
  ALIAS: "ANBOTO" WHO HAS AN EXTENSIVE TERRORIST 
  CURRICULUM AND IS PRESENTLY CONSIDERED BY THE SPANISH 
  AUTHORITIES TO BE THE HIGHEST-RANKING WOMAN IN ETA. 
  POLICE BELIEVE SHE WAS NOW KNOWN AS BEING IN CHARGE OF 
  COLLECTING, MANAGING AND DISTRIBUTING FUNDS THAT THE 
  GROUP OBTAINS THROUGH KIDNAPPINGS AND EXTORTION OF 
  BUSINESS.  THE COUPLE WAS ARRESTED IN A HOUSE, WHICH 
  THEY USED SPORADICALLY IN THE CITY OF SALIES DE BEARN, 
  FRANCE.  IN ADDITION ANOTHER 19 INDIVIDUALS THAT THE 
  POLICE BELIEVE ARE PART OF ETA'S LOGISTICS NETWORK WERE 
  ARRESTED IN THE OPERATIONS. SOME OF THEM WERE LATER 
  RELEASED AFTER FIRST COURT PROCEEDINGS.  THE RAIDS TOOK 
  PLACE IN SEVEN TOWNS OF THE FRENCH ATLANTIC PYRINEES, 
  SAINT PIERRE D'IRUBE, BRISCOUS, AYHERRE, SALIES DE 
  BEARN, DOMEZAIN-BERRAUTE, URRUGNE AND HENDAYA.  IN 
  ADDITION A TRUCK DRIVER ALSO RELATED TO ETA'S LOGISTICS 
  GROUP WAS ALSO ARRESTED IN BURGOS, SPAIN.  THE POLICE 
  SEARCHED SEVERAL SAFE-HOUSES AND FOUND FIVE 
  ARSENALS/CACHES WHERE THEY SEIZED SEVERAL HUNDRED 
  KILOGRAMS OF DYNAMITE "TITADYNE" EXPLOSIVE, WHICH WAS 
  PREVIOUSLY STOLEN IN AN EXPLOSIVES FACTORY IN FRANCE, 
  AS WELL AS A BIG AMOUNT OF CHEMICALS, TO BE USED IN 
  CONNECTION WITH THE MANUFACTURING OF EXPLOSIVES, 
  SEVERAL DOZENS OF GUNS, INCLUDING 30 MACHINE GUNS AND 
  44 SUBMACHINE GUNS "UZI" AND "MAT", MORE THAN 200 
  PISTOLS, GRENADES, GRENADE LAUNCHERS, TWO "SAM" SURFACE 
  TO AIR MISSILES, AMUNITIONS, DETONATORS, TIMERS, 
  ABUNDANT DOCUMENTATION, COMPUTERS AND A LARGE AMOUNT OF 
  CURRENCY.  IN ONE OF THE HOUSES, THE ACTIVISTS HAD 
  CONSTRUCTED A FIRING RANGE.  PRESS REPORTS CONTINUE, 
  POLICE SOURCES SAID IT WOULD TAKE SEVERAL MONTHS TO 
  ANALYZE THE ENORMOUS AMOUNT OF DOCUMENTS AND 
  INFORMATION OBTAINED IN THE RAIDS. 
 
¶3.   SPANISH AND FRENCH INTERIOR MINISTRIES APPLAUDED 
  THE SUCCESSFUL OPERATION, RESULT OF THE CLOSER 
  COOPERATION BETWEEN THE FRENCH AND SPANISH ANTI- 
  TERRORISM UNITS. 
 
¶4.   ON THE FOLLOWING DAYS PRESS REPORTED THAT SOME OF 
  THE ARRESTED WERE RELEASED AND THE INVIDUALS BELOW WERE 
  IMPRISONED AFTER FIRST COURT PROCEEDINGS.
