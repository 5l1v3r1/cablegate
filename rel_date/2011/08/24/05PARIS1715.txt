SUBJECT:  FRANCE:  GOF ACCEPTS PROPOSED MARCH 29 MEETINGS ON NEW 
COMPUTER METRIC FOR WASSENAAR ARRANGEMENT 
 
REF: STATE 41476 
 
¶1.  EconOff raised reftel with MFA Wassenaar Arrangement expert 
Bruno Bisson on March 11 and followed-up with him on March 14 
and 15, when he confirmed that he and his GOF interagency 
colleagues accept the U.S. proposal to bring a small team of 
experts to Paris on March 29 for a presentation of our joint 
U.S.-Japan proposal followed by a discussion of the proposed new 
computer metric.  We have confirmed that the meeting will begin 
at 10:00am on March 29 in the Embassy library. 
 
¶2.  On March 29, Bisson will be accompanied by Anne Diaz of the 
Defense Ministry (MOD) along with perhaps one other person from 
the MOD that has yet to confirm attendance.  Bisson said that no 
one from the Industry Ministry with the relevant subject matter 
expertise was available, but he promised to share the points of 
our presentation with the appropriate people within the GOF 
before the April experts meeting.
