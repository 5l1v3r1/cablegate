 (SBU) In an August 18 meeting with Moroccan Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs Head of United Nations Division Redoune Houssaini, A/PolCouns 
and D/PolCouns discussed reftel invitation and delivered 
points/non-paper.  Also in attendance was Minister Counselor 
Alexandre Koudryachov of the Russian Embassy in Morocco, who was 
provided a copy as well.  Mr. Houssaini could not confirm immediately 
whether Morocco would participate, but stated that he would pass the 
information to the Ministry of Defense, Customs, and other 
appropriate offices.  Houssaini noted that the GOM may seek 
additional information, which we agreed to provide.
