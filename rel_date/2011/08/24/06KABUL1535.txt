¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Martha P. Amlin, program officer 
for Asia and Near East Assistance Office for Population, 
Refugees and Migration (PRM), and grants country clearance 
for her visit to Afghanistan on April 13, 2006, to meet with 
staff of UNHCR, NOGs and relevant country ministries. 
Control Officer is Gwen Pascoe, Political Officer, email 
address: PascoeGJ@state.gov, telephone 011-937-010-8156 or 
cell number 011-937-99-755-673.  All travelers should bring 
USG ID for identification purposes and to obtain an Embassy 
ID. 
 
¶2. Embassy Kabul will provide transportation, security, 
escorts and lodging.  Ms. Amlin will be met at airport by 
Refugee Assistant Naseer Ayanee. 
 
¶3. Embassy Kabul is arranging seats for Ms. Amlin in the 
UNHAS flight Istambul-Kabul-Istambul. 
 
¶4. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
