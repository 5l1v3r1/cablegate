(SBU) Political/Economic Chief delivered reftel's talking 
points to Ganesh Dhakal, Foreign Ministry Duty Officer on 
August 9.  The Political/Economic Chief stressed the 
importance to the U.S. Government of allowing the UN Security 
Council to take the lead on the issue of Lebanon rather than 
convening a special session of the Human Rights Council. 
Although this is a domestic holiday, Dhakal agreed to relay 
the message as soon as possible to the appropriate MFA 
official.
