¶1.   POST WISHES TO SUBMIT AND/OR UPDATE THE FOLLOWING 
  NAMES AND INFORMATION IN CLASS IN ACCORDANCE WITH VIPER

¶2.   SPANISH NEWS OF JUNE 15, 2005 REPORTED THAT THE 
  SPANISH POLICE CONDUCTED AN OPERATION IN DIFFERENT 
  LOCATIONS IN SPAIN, RESULTING IN THE ARREST OF 11 
  ALLEGED MEMBERS OF THE TERRORIST GROUP "ANSAR AL ISLAM" 
  LED BY AL ZARQAUI IN IRAQ AND LINKED TO AL-QAEDA. 
  ACCORDING TO THE SPANISH POLICE, THEY ARE PART OF A 
  NETWORK FOR THE FINANCING AND RECRUITMENT FOR JIHAD 
  (HOLY WAR). ALL ARRESTED ARE ACCUSED OF MEMBERSHIP 
  AND/OR COLLABORATION WITH A TERRORIST ORGANIZATION.
