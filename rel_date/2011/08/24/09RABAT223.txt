SUBJECT: MOROCCO WILL PARTICIPATE IN REGIONAL PROLIFERATION 
SECURITY INITIATIVE COUNTERPROLIFERATION WORKSHOP 
 
REF: STATE 19681 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloff and Legatt met with Moroccan Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs Head of Security and Disarmament Affairs 
Section Chief Bouchaib Eloumni on March 13 and encouraged 
Morocco to send up to six participants to the Regional 
Proliferation Security Initiative Counter-proliferation 
Workshop in Budapest, Hungary (July 14-16) (reftel).  Eloumni 
agreed to share the invitation with the appropriate 
Government of Morocco (GOM) civilian and military agencies, 
pledged the GOM's participation in the conference, and 
promised to provide details of participants by the May 18 
deadline. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Eloumni is the primary GOM point of contact (POC) 
for this conference.  Poloff Jimmy Mauldin and Legatt David 
Archey are the POCs for Post.
