SUBJECT: GLOBAL MARITIME PARTNERSHIPS 
 
REF: STATE 50488 
 
¶1. Pol/Econ Section Chief delivered talking points in reftel 
on June 5 to Bertin Tanoh Koffi, Deputy Director General of 
Maritime and Port Affairs at the Ministry of Transportation. 
 
¶2. Koffi stated that the Government of Cote d'Ivoire welcomed 
the Global Maritime Partnerships and would be interested in 
participating in this initiative.  He said Cote d'Ivoire has 
always been interested in cooperation on maritime issues with 
the United States.  Koffi noted that Cote d'Ivoire needs 
technical assistance, training, and equipment.
