SUBJECT: MALI: U.S. IRAQ POLICY TALKING POINTS DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 18283 
 
The Embassy delivered Reftel talking points to Amadou Toure, 
Deputy Director of Political Affairs at the Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs, on February 27.  As an initial reaction, 
Toure said Mali welcomed this news.  He stated that he would 
pass the information on to his superiors and would contact 
the Embassy with a response next week.  Toure also said he 
would pass the information to the Foreign Ministry's Public 
Affairs section to discuss the possibility of a press 
release.  We will report any subsequent information septel.
