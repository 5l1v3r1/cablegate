SUBJECT: MISSION DELIVERS UNDEF PROPOSAL 
 
REF: STATE 124036 
 
¶1. (SBU) Mission, including Congen Surabaya and Consulate 
Medan, delivered United Nations Democracy Fund (UNDEF) grant 
proposals to eligible organizations, per request in reftel. 
Mission submitted UNDEF grant proposals to nearly 50 
pro-democracy groups, human rights NGOs and government 
bodies, encouraging them to apply for funding.  We will flag 
for Department particularly noteworthy projects to support. 
 
¶2. (SBU) UNDEF funding would support Indonesia's 
transformation to a democracy as it gears up for national 
elections in 2009.  Indonesian organizations said they were 
very appreciative of this opportunity and intend to apply for 
the funding.
