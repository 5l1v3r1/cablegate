SUBJECT: INVITATION ACCEPTED: THE NEW DELHI NOVEMBER 18-19 
REGIONAL ECONOMIC COOPERATION CONFERENCE ON AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 173828 
 
(SBU) We have learned from the Indian Embassy and confirmed 
with MFA that DFM Alekseyev will lead the GOR delegation to 
the November 18-19 conference on Afghanistan. Yuriy Khokhlov, 
Head of MFA's Afghanistan Section, told us that Russia 
supports the Indian-Afghani initiative to organize a regional 
economic conference to promote Afghanistan's economic 
stabilization. He also confirmed that FM Lavrov will be in 
Hanoi November 17-19 for the APEC Summit.
