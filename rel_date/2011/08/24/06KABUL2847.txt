¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes John Gastright, Virginia Palmer, 
David Kilcullen, Daniel Green, and Meghan Bradley, and grants 
country clearance for their visit to Afghanistan on June 
25-29, 2006, to discuss security and counter-terrorism 
efforts.  Control Officer is Chris Hensman, Political 
Officer, email address: hensmancd@state.gov, telephone 
011-937-010-8173 or cell number 011-93-70-039-779.  All 
travelers should bring USG ID for identification purposes and 
to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2. Embassy Kabul will provide transportation, security, 
escorts and lodging. 
 
¶3. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
