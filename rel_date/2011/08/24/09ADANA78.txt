SUBJECT: ADANA EAC MEETS TO REVIEW NEO CONTINGENCY PLANS 
 
1.(sbu)  PRINCIPAL OFFICER CONVENED A CORE EAC ON WEDNESDAY 16 
dECEMBER TO REVIEW CONTINGENCY PLANNING FOR A NONCOMBATANT 
EVACUATION OPERATION (NEO).  PARTICIPANTS INCLUDED MEMBERS OF 
THE FOLLOWING OFFICES:  CONS/MGT/PSO and POL/ECON.  cONS/mgt/pso 
SUMMARIZED HER PARTICIPATION IN A NEO TABLETOP EXERCISE with the 
39th air wing AT INCIRLIK AIR BASE EARLIER THAT SAME DAY.  THE 
EAC REVIEWED the sections of the consulate's Emergency Action 
Plan (EAP) related to evacuations. 
 
2.(sbu)  aDANA EAC HIGHLIGHTS THE FOLLOWING POINTS RESULTING 
FROM DISCUSSIONS AT THE MEETING: 
 
¶A.      the FORCE SUPPORT SQUADRON AT INCIRLIK AIR BASE IS 
ASSISTING post to IDENTIFY an APPROPRIATE LOCATION FOR the 
DESIGNATED ALTERNATE COMMAND CENTER.  we WOULD SUPPORT an 
evacuation OUT OF the EVACUATION CONTROL CENTER, BUT ADDITIONAL 
SPACE WITH ADEQUATE COMPUTER/PHONE ACCESS is necessary. 
¶B.      incirlik is expecting delivery of a new NEO TRACKING 
SYSTEM.  post WILL ADDRESS WITH CA WHETHER THERE IS A METHOD TO 
SHARE DATA BETWEEN THIS SYSTEM AND THE CONSULAR TASK FORCE 
SOFTWARE. 
¶C.      EAC WILL REVIEW AND UPDATE EMERGENCY SUPPLIES FOR 
OPERATION OUT OF the ALTERNATE COMMAND CENTER, INCLUDING FOBS, 
LAPTOPS, PRINTERS AND SATELLITE PHONES.  we will also have KEY 
EMERGENCY ACTION DOCUMENTS AVAILABLE IN MULTIPLE FORMATS in case 
DOS INTRANET is inaccessible. 
¶D.      Post will hold a mini-cme, including an evacuation to 
alternate command center drill, in mid-january.
