SUBJECT: AFGHANISTAN-PAKISTAN WHITE PAPER DELIVERED 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 29482 
     ¶B. SECSTATE 28929 
 
¶1. (SBU) Charge delivered ref A and B paper and points to 
Peter Burian, Slovakia's Ambassador to the United States, 
during a meeting in Bratislava on March 27. Poloff delivered 
ref A paper and points to Lucia Marcisiakova, Afghanistan 
Coordinator in MFA's Security Policy Department on March 27; 
and by email to Major Peter Sliacky, GS J3 at the Slovak MoD 
on March 30. We have also passed copies to both President 
Gasparovic's and Prime Minister Fico's foreign policy 
advisors. 
 
¶2. (SBU) All interlocutors appreciated the direct 
communication and consultations that went into the 
Afghanistan Pakistan Strategic Review, but none offered any 
detailed comments. Marcisiakova included the White Paper in 
Foreign Minister Lajcak's briefing book ahead of the March 30 
conference on Afghanistan in the Hague. Marcisiakova said the 
results of the review were expected and appreciated.
