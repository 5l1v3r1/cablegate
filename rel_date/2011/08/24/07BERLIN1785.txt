¶3.  Individual Company Information 
 
Overall, companies did not see an advantage in supplying the data 
requested in a dispute that does not involve them, the country where 
they produce, or any other aspect of their strategic interests. 
Therefore, responses have been very limited, and none of the 
companies completed the questionnaire. 
 
Embassy Berlin and Consulates Hamburg, Munich and Frankfurt 
contacted companies in reftel regarding data on the product under 
investigation with the following results:
