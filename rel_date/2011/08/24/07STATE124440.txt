SUBJECT: (SBU) COUNTRY CLEARANCE REQUEST FOR DRL OFFICER 
KATHRYN LURIE 
 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Country clearance is requested for Kathryn Lurie, 
DRL/NESCA Foreign Affairs Officer to visit Azerbaijan, 
Turkey, United Arab Emirates (Dubai) and the United Kingdom 
during the period September 25-October 2.  The purpose of the 
trip is to meet with the Iran Watchers, NGOs, members of the 
Iranian Diaspora, and government officials on human rights 
and democracy issues in Iran. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Embassy assistance in setting up appointments would 
be greatly appreciated.  Department requests assistance with 
arranging lodging within per diem for the nights of September 
25-October 1. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) For Baku: Mrs. Lurie requests meetings with the 
Iran watcher, contacts knowledgeable about the Azeri minority 
in Iran and additional meetings as Embassy sees fit. 
 
¶4.  (SBU) For ConGen Istanbul: Mrs. Lurie requests meetings 
with the Iran watcher, and additional meetings as ConGen sees 
fit.   For Ankara, Mrs. Lurie request meetings with Conoffs 
on Iranian refugee processing and will attend additional 
meetings with Iran watcher. 
 
¶5.  (SBU) For RPO Dubai: Mrs. Lurie requests meetings with 
RPO Dubai staff. 
 
¶6.  (SBU) For London: Mrs. Lurie requests meetings with the 
Iran watcher, FCO, and additional meetings as Embassy sees 
fit. 
 
¶7.  (SBU) Mrs. Lurie,s diplomatic passport number is 
¶910040595. 
She holds a TS/SCI Clearance.
