SUBJECT: AUSTRIAN ATTENDANCE AT PSI HIGH-LEVEL POLITICAL 
MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 84640 
 
 EconPolOff discussed the June 23 PSI High Level Political 
Meeting (HLPM) with Dorothea Auer, Director of the MFA's 
Department for Disarmament, Arms Control and 
Non-Proliferation, on May 30, informing them of U/S Joseph 
and U/S Levy's participation and encouraging similarly 
high-level participation.  While the GoA welcomes the HLPM 
and supports PSI, Ms. Auer informed the Embassy on 7 June 
that the heavy workload of the EU Presidency, particularly in 
the June timeframe, made it impossible for the GoA to send 
high-level representation.  Ambassador Dr. Alfred Laengle, 
the Austrian chief of mission in Warsaw, will attend.
