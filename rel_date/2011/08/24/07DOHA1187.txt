SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: SHARING DRAFT IRAQ UNSCR 
TEXT/EXTENDING THE LIFE OF THE IAMB FOR IRAQ 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 167347 
     ¶B. SECSTATE 165490 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Poloff approached the MFA International 
Organizations, Conferences and Treaties Department December 
11 regarding Government of Qatar (GOQ) support for extending 
the mandates for the Multinational Force - Iraq (MNF-I), 
Development Fund for Iraq (DFI), and International Advisory, 
and Monitoring Board (IAMB) (reftels).  MFA Counselor 
Mohammed Al-Dohaimi responded that he would Circulate the 
U.S. position to MFA decision-makers and send copies to the 
Qatari UN mission in New York. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Al-Dohaimi provided no other substantive remarks, 
other than to underscore Qatar's support for helping Iraqis 
take advantage of the improved security environment, in line 
with previous statements by the Emir.
