SUBJECT: TURKEY - INTERNATIONAL PARTNERSHIP ON AVIAN 
INFLUENZA - SENIOR OFFICIALS MEETING PARTICIPATION 
 
REF: STATE 175585 
 
 In response to Post's transmission to GOT MFA of reftel 
information on the International Partnership on Avian and 
Pandemic Influenza Core Principles and Senior Official 
Meeting on October 6-7, MFA informed EconOff that GOT 
Ministry of Health General Health Services Department Deputy 
Director General Fehmi Aydinli and a representative from the 
Turkish Embassy in Washington will attend the meetings.  MFA 
told EconOff that the GOT agreed with the Core Principles, 
but had not yet received a formal response from the Ministry 
of Health.
