SUBJECT: GREEKS REMINDED ON FRIENDS OF IRAQ CONFERENCE 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 60393 
 
     ¶B. ATHENS 782 
 
¶1. Per ref B, Greece intends to send its ambassador in Abu 
Dhabi to the Friends of Iraq Conference.  On June 6, 
DepPolCouns informed MFA A6 Directorate for Middle East 
Affairs deputy head Giorgos Ayfantis that the conference 
organizers had not yet received a registration from the Greek 
participant, per ref A.  Ayfantis said he believed the Greek 
ambassador in Abu Dhabi had already registered but promised 
to get in touch with him immediately to confirm.
