On May 24, POLOFF delivered reftel demarche and talking 
points to Julio Peralta, Director for International 
Organizations within the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. 
Peralta responded that he was familiar with these elections 
and knew that the USG supported Barbados.  He noted that he 
was not in a position to give GOP stance at this time, but 
would provide talking points to his colleagues working on the 
issue within the MFA and report back.  Post will follow-up on 
the issue and forward the response when it is available.
