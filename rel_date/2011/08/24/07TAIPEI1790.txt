SUBJECT: TAIPEI RESPONDS TO APEC DEMARCHE ON USDOL APEC CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 106057 
 
(U) AIT delivered reftel demarche to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MOFA) APEC Task Force Acting Chief Daniel K.Y. Lin on August 7. 
Lin said Taiwan's Council of Labor Affairs (CLA) is responsible for 
replying to the invitation.  Due to budget constraints, Lin expects 
Taiwan will send one or two CLA officials to attend the meeting. 
MOFA was not able to provide information on the persons who will 
attend.
