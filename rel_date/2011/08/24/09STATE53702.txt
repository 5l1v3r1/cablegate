SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: POST-SHIPMENT END-USE CHECK 
ON LICENSE 050121834 
 
¶1. This is an Action Request. See paragraphs 2 - 4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on license 050121834 for the export of 
rifles to France.  Post is requested to complete this Blue 
Lantern check within 45 days. Lack of response to a Blue 
Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for Request: To confirm the quantity of rifles 
received by the end-user and to establish end-user's bona 
fides. 
 
¶4. Action Request:  Post is requested to confirm that the 
end-user received the rifles listed in para 5 and to verify 
the exact quantity of rifles received.  Post is also 
requested to establish the bona fides of end-user, Christian 
Chaulet.  The following points are included for guidance: 
 
(To Christian Chaulet) 
 
--How long has your company been in the business of importing 
and selling defense articles in France? What 
services and products are sold by your company? 
 
--Who are your regular customers and where do they reside? 
 
--How many rifles did you receive from Century Arms in this 
transaction? 
 
--Are you familiar with the regulations governing the import 
of U.S.-origin defense articles, including restrictions on 
re-transfer or re-export?  Did Century Arms inform you of 
these restrictions? 
 
In its reply, Post is encouraged to comment on any 
information pertinent to this transaction such as security of 
facilities, end-use, and concerns about the entities involved.
