SUBJECT: COTE D'IVOIRE NOMINATION FOR ATTENDEE OF USDA GLOBAL 
CONFERENCE ON AGRICULTURE-BASED BIOFUELS 
 
REF: USDA FAS 684655 
 
Embassy Abidjan recommends Mr. Souleymane Coulibaly to attend the 
USDA Biofuels Conference on August 20 - 27, 2007 in Minnesota.  Mr. 
Coulibaly is a chemical engineer and currently the Chairman of 
SENBCI, the wood exporter's union in Cote d'Ivoire. 
 
Mr. Coulibaly's focus is on agriculture and wood product 
conservation and processing.  He is the premier expert on the 
Development of Ethanol/Gel Fuel as Cooking Energy in the West 
African Economic Monetary Union.  He was invited by the African 
Union and the United Nations Industrial Development Organization to 
participate in the first high-level workshop on biofuels held in 
Africa, in Addis Ababa, on May 17-18, 2007.
