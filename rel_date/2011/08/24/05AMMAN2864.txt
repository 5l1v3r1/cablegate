SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON THE OSLO DONORS' CONFERENCE ON SUDAN 
DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 62086 
 
¶1. (U) Poloff delivered on April 7 reftel talking points to 
Walid Obeidat of the Foreign Minister's Private Office. 
Obeidat responded that the Government of Jordan fully 
supported the North-South Comprehensive Peace Agreement and 
that Jordan had sent two plane loads of humanitarian 
assistance to Darfur. Obeidat said he would forward the 
information on the Oslo Donors' Conference to the appropriate 
Jordanian government officials. 
 
¶2. (U) Khartoum minimize considered.
