SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST: AFGHAN NARCOTICS INTERDICTION 
PERSONNEL SELECTED FOR LEADERSHIP COURSE 
 
 
¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 2. 
 
¶2. In order to comply with Leahy vetting requirements, this cable 
forwards the names and biodata of 15 Afghan National Police officers 
working in the Narcotics Interdiction Unit.  This unit receives 
funding from the Department of State, Bureau of International 
Narcotics and Law Enforcement, in coordination with the U.S. Drug 
Enforcement Agency.  Post has reviewed the information available on 
these individuals and has found no derogatory information. These 
checks have included checks through DEA and FBI criminal databases. 
This vetting is being conducted so that that officers listed below 
can participate in a leadership course that will be taught in 
Ankara, Turkey, for drug unit commanders.  Request Department 
response by December 21 (10 working days).
