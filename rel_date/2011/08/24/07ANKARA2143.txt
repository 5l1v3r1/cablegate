SUBJECT: TURKEY: FEDERAL ASSISTANCE AWARD ACCOUNTING FOR 
VAN REFUGEE SHELTER 
 
REF: A. STATE 131139 
 
     ¶B. STATE 27113 
 
1.(U)  Per reftels U.S. Embassy Ankara dispersed U.S. Dollars 
19,962.00 to the Van Women's Shelter, in accordance with the 
Grant Agreement, dated September 9, 2005, between the USA and 
the Women's Counseling Center in Van, Turkey.  The funds were 
spent in their entirety by the Van Women's' Shelter. 
 
¶2. (U)  The award number was: TU-150-05-GR-056. 
The project title/description was: To provide a women's 
shelter for refugee and asylum-seeker women in Van, Van 
Women's Association, Van, Turkey. 
The cost of the grant was:  U.S. Dollars 19,962.00. 
The award period was: From 10-01-2005 through 09-30-2006. 
The Appropriation number was: 19X1143.6. 
Allotment: 3280. 
Date of Allotment: 7/13/05. 
 
¶3. (U) The funds were used to build a shelter in Van, Turkey 
for women refugee and asylum-seekers.
