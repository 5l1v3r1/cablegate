SUBJECT: SPAIN TO VOTE IN FAVOR OF ALL FOUR BIOTECH 
VARIETIES 
 
REF: SECSTATE 140452 
 
On 10/09/07, Agricultural Counselor delivered reftel demarche 
to the Ministry of Agriculture's Subdirector General for 
International Relations, Alicia Villaruiz.  EconOff delivered 
the demarche on 10/09/07 to Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
Economic Affairs Officer Pablo Montesino-Espartero and to 
Ministry of Industry, Tourism and Trade Subdirector General 
for EU Affairs, Inigo Febrel, on 10/09/07.  Villaruiz told 
the Agricultural Counselor on 10/10/07 that Spain would vote 
in favor of all four varieties as a result of a positive 
decision by Spain's Interministerial Committee on 
Biotechnology.
