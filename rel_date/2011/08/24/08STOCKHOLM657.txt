SUBJECT: SWEDISH RESPONSE: ASSISTANCE TO GEORGIA FOLLOWING 
CONFLICT WITH RUSSIA 
 
REF: STATE 100093 
 
On October 1, Katja Salsback, Desk Officer, Department for 
Assistance to Commonwealth of Independent States Countries, 
the Swedish Ministry for Foreign Affairs, told Deputy 
Economic Counselor that Sweden has provided some $13.9 
million to Georgia in bilateral assistance to Georgia in 
2008, in addition to the EU assistance which Sweden 
participates in.  Salsback commented that the level of 
assistance for reconstruction and economic support for 2009 
will remain at $13.9 million, but will then increase to $20.8 
million in 2010.  Sweden has also provided $4.2 million in 
crisis-related humanitarian assistance in 2008 to date.  In 
2009, Sweden will begin developing a new assistance strategy 
for reconstruction and economic support for Georgia. Post 
will report any additional information septel.
