SUBJECT: FATF/IRAN STATEMENT: DUTCH TO ADDRESS WITH BANKS 
 
REF: STATE 149648 
 
¶1.  Econoff shared on October 31 reftel points concerning 
FATF's public statement and guidance on Iran with Dutch 
officials Huib Mijnarends and Wendela Haringhuizen, both 
Senior Policy Officers in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' 
Terrorism and New Threats Unit, and Anne-Chris Visser, Senior 
Advisor in the Ministry of Finance's (MOF) Integrity Division. 
 
¶2.  Visser said the MOF had circulated FATF's guidance to the 
Dutch financial supervisors (Netherlands Central Bank and 
Authority for Financial Markets) and the Dutch Banker's 
Association directly following the October 10-12 FATF 
plenary.  Visser added that the MOF would discuss Iran's 
impact on the international financial system with the 
Banker's Association the week of November 19.  Post will 
follow up and report on any substantive information received 
or actions taken.
