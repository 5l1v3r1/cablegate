¶2.  (SBU) The Department of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU) has 
notified Mission via diplomatic note that Indonesia will vote 
in favor of USG candidate Ambassador Pierre-Richard Prosper's 
candidacy for the UN Committee on the Elimination of Racial 
Discrimination (CERD) in the January 17 election.  Per Ref A, 
Pol/C had previously urged the Indonesian government to 
support Ambassador Prosper's candidacy, underscoring the 
depth and range of his experience and other qualifications.
