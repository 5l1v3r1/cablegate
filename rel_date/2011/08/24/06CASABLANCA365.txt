SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR MARCH 2006 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1.  Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General 
Casablanca joint Visas Viper Committee met on April 3, 2006. 
The meeting was chaired by the DCM and attended by the CG 
and representatives from CONS, RAO, RSO, POL, ECON, PAS, and 
LEGATT.  DAO was unable to attend, but noted prior to the 
meeting that it had no names to submit. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) The Committee has no names to submit for the March 
reporting period. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) The Committee agreed to approach the GOM at the 
highest levels regarding post's outstanding request for 
biodata information on more than 40 people convicted of 
terrorism charges in early summer 2005.  GREENE
