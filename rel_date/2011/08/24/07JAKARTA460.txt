¶1. Following up on Ambassador's demarche with National 
Security Adviser Djalal on February 7 (ref c), we met with 
Ramadansyah Hasan of the Directorate for International 
Security and Disarmament in the Indonesian Department of 
Foreign Affairs (DEPLU) on February 12 to convey points and 
information in refs a and b toward implementing UNSCR 1737. 
Hasan thanked us for the information and said he would ask 
appropriate offices in DEPLU to review the resolution and 
relevant Indonesian law to determine Indonesia's obligations 
under UNSCR 1737.
