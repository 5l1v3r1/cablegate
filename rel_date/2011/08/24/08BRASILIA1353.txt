SUBJECT: BRAZIL: DEMARCHE DELIVERIES:  OFAC KINGPIN ACT DESIGNATIONS 
AND DESIGNATIONS UNDER EXECUTIVE ORDER 13438 
 
REFTEL:  A) STATE 101037 B) DEPTOFTREA 09291776 C) STATE 97316 
 
¶1. (SBU) On October 3, 2008 Econoff delivered ref A and B demarches 
to the Council for the Control of Financial Activities (COAF) at the 
Ministry of Finance.  COAF official Bernardo Mota took delivery of 
ref A document regarding the Department of the Treasury's (DOT) 
Office of Foreign Assets Control(OFAC) designation of eight FARC 
international commission members under the Foreign Narcotics Kingpin 
Designation Act (Kingpin Act); and Ref B document regarding DOT's 
designation of five individuals and two entities pursuant to 
Executive Order (E.O.) 13438 for threatening the peace and stability 
of Iraq and the Government of Iraq. 
 
¶2. (SBU) COAF official explained that COAF's standard operating 
procedure is to record the names of individuals and organizations in 
their databases, and perform a crosscheck to determine whether there 
are any links to the Brazilian financial system. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Regarding ref C designation of three senior Venezuelan 
officials under the Kingpin Act for materially assisting the FARC, 
COAF official remarked that a search of their records had yielded no 
information.
