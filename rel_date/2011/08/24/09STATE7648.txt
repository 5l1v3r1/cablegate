to date, it possesses no credible information of gross violations of 
human rights for the individuals identified below.  These individuals 
are candidates for participation in an ICITAP, Cyber Crimes Project 
with the North Sumatra Provincial Police, Criminal Investigations 
Division scheduled from February 13, 2009 through February 13, 2010. 
The approximate U.S. funding for this training is $7,000.
