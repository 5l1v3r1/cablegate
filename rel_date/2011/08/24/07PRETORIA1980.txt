SUBJECT: AGOA FORUM - SOUTH AFRICAN DELEGATION 
 
REF: STATE 58394 
 
 ¶1.  Post received on May 31 a provisional list of South 
African officials who will attend the AGOA Forum.  They 
include Minister of Trade and Industry Mandisi Mpahlwa, 
Deputy Director General for International Trade and Economic 
Development Xavier Carim (provided that the TIDCA 
negotiations take place as agreed upon at the meeting held on 
February 2, 2007), Director of Americas Victor Mashabela, 
Assistant Director of Americas Tshepang Malakwane, and 
Mudunwazi Baloyi.  The Department of Agriculture reports that 
it will send two officials, Deputy Director of International 
Trade Helieh Konstant and Assistant Director of International 
Trade Anthony Letsoalo. 
 
¶2.  Post is still trying to ascertain the composition of the 
NEPAD delegation and will provide this information upon 
receipt.
