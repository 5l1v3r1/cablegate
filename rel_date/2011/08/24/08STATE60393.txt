 ¶1. This is an action request, please see paragraph 2.  For 
Embassy Suva, please coordinate this action with the Kingdom 
of Tonga only. 
 
¶2. The June 17-19 Friends of Iraq conference in Abu Dhabi 
will commence in less than two weeks.  Action addressees' 
host governments have not yet registered for the conference 
or declined participation.  Action addressees are requested 
to contact appropriate host nation government to ascertain 
their participation in the conference and urge them to use 
the mechanisms in ref (a) to register, if they plan to 
attend.  In some cases a positive initial response was 
received by cable or e-mail but this has not been followed up 
with a complete registration.  If attending, immediate 
registration is required via the CLASSNET/SIPRNET conference 
website (ref a) or through host governments' Senior National 
Representatives at CENTCOM to ensure hotel availability.  To 
further encourage interest in the Economic and Governance 
discussions, ref (b) and the conference website provide 
agenda details and currently registered attendees, which 
Embassies may pass to prospective participants.  Negative 
responses are requested.
