SUBJECT: MOROCCO RECEIVES GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT 
NUCLEAR TERRORISM - MODEL GUIDELINES DOCUMENT FOR NUCLEAR 
DETECTION ARCHITECTURES 
 
REF: STATE 123059 
 
Poloff met with MFA Director of Security and Disarmament 
Affairs Bouchaib Eloumni on December 2 and delivered reftel 
message requesting comments no later than December 19, 2008. 
Eloumni reported that Morocco now has access to the Global 
Initiative Information Portal (GIIP) and that they will 
review the Model Guidelines Document for Nuclear Detection 
Architectures and submit any necessary comments by the 
deadline date.
