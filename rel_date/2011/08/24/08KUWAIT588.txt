SUBJECT: DEMARCHE:  KUWAIT 60-DAY REPORT TO UN ON UNSCR 
1803 A WORK IN PROGRESS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 48615 
 
On May 22, A/Polcouns delivered reftel points and text of USG 
60-Day Report to MFA Iran Desk Officer Rashed Al-Hejri. 
Al-Hejri confirmed that the MFA is still in the process of 
compiling information from various ministries in response to 
UNSCR 1803's reporting call, but added that these reports are 
likely to be sent in piecemeal fashion, as opposed to a 
collective report, directly to the Kuwaiti UN Mission in 
response to the various points raised in the resolution. 
Post will report further updates septel.
