SUBJECT: MOROCCO RESPONSE TO U.S. 60-DAY REPORT ON UNSCR 
1747 (IRAN SANCTIONS) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 73219 
 
(SBU)  During a June 1 meeting, econoff drew from reftel 
points to urge Moroccan implementation of UNSC-mandated 
sanctions against Iran.  Ahmed Faouzi, Division Chief for 
International Organizations at the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, affirmed Moroccan support for UNSCR 1747, but 
stopped short of specifying any particular sanctions Morocco 
has implemented.  Faouzi was thankful to receive a courtesy 
copy of the U.S. 60-day report and said he would forward the 
information to the appropriate offices within the GOM for 
review.  Faouzi emphasized the excellent cooperation the U.S. 
and Morocco enjoy on this subject and pledged to keep Post 
informed of any substantive developments.
