¶1.  Post received diplomatic note number 0518 dated February 
20, 2006, from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs accepting Ref 
A proposal for a blanket designation of all U.S. air carriers 
under the October 2001 Air Transport Agreement between the 
United States and Morocco. 
 
¶2.  Unofficial embassy translation of diplomatic note is 
below.  Original French-language version will be faxed to 
EB/TRA/AN attention Laurence Jones. 
 
BEGIN TEXT: 
 
"The Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation of the 
Kingdom of Morocco presents its compliments to the Embassy of 
the United States of America, and, with reference to its note 
number 369 dated December 20th, 2005 relative to the 
designation by the Government of the United States of all 
American airlines in accordance with Article 3 of the Air 
Transport Agreement signed on October 10, 2001, has the honor 
to inform the Embassy that the proposed designation does not 
raise any objections from the relevant Moroccan authorities, 
subject to the condition that the designated airlines have 
operating licenses provided by the authorities of each of the 
parties, and that these authorities maintain the effective 
regulatory control over their airline carriers."  END TEXT
