¶3. DAILY SPANISH PRESS "EL PAIS" REPORTED THAT THE 
SPANISH GOVERNMENT IS LOOKING INTO WHETHER TO CONVENE A 
INTER-MINISTERIAL COMMISSION ON ASYLUM AND POLITICAL 
REFUGEES TO INVESTIGATE AND REVISE THE REFUGEE STATUS 
OF THE EXILED LEADER OF THE "PARTIDO DEL PROGRESO DE 
GUINEA ECUATORIAL", SEVERO MOTO NSA, AFTER HE MADE A 
TRIP ON MARCH 19, 2005 TO CROATIA, WHERE HE REPORTEDLY 
MET WITH ANTE JERCIC, A WELL-KNOWN ARMS MAKER AND 
DEALER.  MOTO MADE SEVERAL DECLARATIONS TO CROATIAN 
PRESS MEDIA THAT THE SPANISH SECRET SERVICE WAS BEHIND 
"A PLOT TO LIQUIDATE HIM".  THE SPANISH GOVERNMENT IS 
AT PRESENT WAITING FOR A CROATIAN GOVERNMENT REPORT ON 
MOTO'S ACTIVITIES IN ZAGREB.  SEVERO MOTO WAS GRANTED 
POLITICAL REFUGEE STATUS IN SPAIN IN 1986.  SINCE THEN 
HE HAS BEEN ACCUSED OF BEING BEHIND SEVERAL COUP 
D'ETATS AGAINST PRESENT EQUATORIAL GUINEAN DICTATOR 
TEODORO OBIANG, WHO HAS BEEN FREQUENTLY ACCUSED OF 
HUMAN RIGHTS VIOLATIONS.
