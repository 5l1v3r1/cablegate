¶2.  (SBU) Poloff presented reftel invitation package for the 
September 10-12 ASEAN Regional Forum (ARF) Workshop on 
Stabilization and Reconstruction in Bangkok to Fenny 
Ferranita, ARF Desk Officer at the Department of Foreign 
Affairs (DEPLU), on August 13.  Poloff encouraged Indonesia 
to participate.  Ferranita responded that Indonesia would 
participate and hoped to send representatives from DEPLU, the 
Ministry of Defense and the National Disaster Coordinating 
Agency (BAKORNAS).  She promised to provide further details 
on the GOI delegation when it was finalized.
