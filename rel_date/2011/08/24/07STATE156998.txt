SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: PRE-LICENSE END-USE CHECK 
ON LICENSES 05-050072898 AND 05-050072956 
 
¶1. This is an Action Request. See paragraphs 2 - 4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a pre-license check on licenses 05-050072898 and 05-050072956 
for the export of firearms to Lebanon. Post is requested to 
complete this Blue Lantern check within 30 days. Lack of 
response to a Blue Lantern check will affect pending and 
future licenses involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for Request: To verify the stated end-use for the 
United States Munitions List (USML) firearms listed below. 
 
¶4. Action Request: Post should make inquiries with 
appropriate GOL interlocutors to verify that the pistols and 
rifles listed below are to be used exclusively by the 
Lebanese Republic National Defense Ministry. Post should also 
seek clarification on the stated end-use, and confirm that 
the end-user is familiar with the regulations governing the 
import of U.S.-origin defense articles, including 
restrictions on re-transfer or re-export. In its reply, Post 
may also comment on any other information pertinent to this 
transaction such as security of facilities and concerns about 
the parties involved.
