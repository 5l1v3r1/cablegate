SUBJECT:  Al-AZHAR VP WELCOMES COOPERATION WITH KABUL UNIVERSITY 
 
REF: KABUL 2998 
 
¶1. PAO met with Dr. Abdullah El-Hossieny Helal, Vice President for 
Educational and Students Affairs, Al-Azhar University, on October 8, 
to discuss possible cooperation and exchanges with Kabul University. 
 Dr. Helal said that Kabul University should send proposals to him. 
He added that Al-Azhar is happy to cooperate with Kabul, including 
sending its professors to Kabul and accepting students and 
professors from Kabul at Al Azhar.  He stressed, however, that 
Al-Azhar would need a formal protocol as they have with many other 
foreign universities throughout the Islamic world.
