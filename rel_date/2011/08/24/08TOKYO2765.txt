SUBJECT: DEMARCHE RESPONSE: CRITICAL EQUIPMENT NEEDS FOR 
IRAN'S NEW IR-2 CENTRIFUGE DESIGN 
 
REF: STATE 104182 
 
SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  On October 2, EconOff delivered points contained 
in ref to Ministry of Foreign Affairs Export Controls Deputy 
Director Shinichi Sato and Ministry of Economy, Trade and 
Industry Export Controls Deputy Director Atsushi Tanizawa. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Sato expressed appreciation for the non-paper. 
Tanizawa noted epoxy resin is readily available and requested 
additional details about the epoxy resin that presents a 
concern.  Tanizawa said METI Export Officials are in contact 
with executives from the companies listed in the demarche 
almost daily.  He said agreed to check with colleagues and 
the company representatives to see if there is any additional 
information that may be useful to the U.S. concerning this 
matter.
