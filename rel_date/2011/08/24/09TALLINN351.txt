SUBJECT: Estonia with U.S. on Iran 
 
REF: STATE 120288 
 
Pol Chief delivered reftel demarche November 25 to Juri Kahn, the 
MFA's Director for Africa, the Middle East and Latin America, 
Anneli Vares, Iran desk officer, and Kristi Torum, Senior Technical 
Export for Armaments and Strategic Goods Control.  Kahn said the 
GOE is in full agreement with the United States on Iran's nuclear 
program.  Kahn added that recently the Egyptian Ambassador came in 
to argue that, in the eyes of "the Arab world," Iran is in full 
compliance with its IAEA requirements.  Kahn did not accept the 
Egyptian position.
