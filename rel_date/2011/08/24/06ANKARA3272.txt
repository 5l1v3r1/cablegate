SUBJECT: TURKEY'S RESPONSE TO THE DEMARCHE FOR PROFESSOR 
RUTH WEDGWOOD 
 
REF: SECSTATE 85758 
 
(U) In response to our diplomatic note delivered on May 30 as 
instructed reftel, in a diplomatic note dated June 1, the 
Turkish MFA stated that Turkey will give "due consideration" 
to the candidature of Prof. Ruth Wedgewood for election to 
the United Nations Human Rights Committee (UNHCR).
