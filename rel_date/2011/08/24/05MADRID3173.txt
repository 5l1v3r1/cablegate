SUBJECT: SPAIN COUNTRY CLEARANCE FOR DOE A/SECRETARY JOHN 
SHAW AND DELEGATION 
 
REF: STATE 164683 
 
¶1.   Embassy welcomes and grants country clearance to 
Assistant Secretary John Shaw, Andrew Patterson and Steven 
Cary, U.S. Department of Energy, to travel to Madrid, Almeria 
and Barcelona, September 12-17, to visit nuclear facilities 
and meet with Government of Spain officials. 
 
¶2.  As per ref. cable, EconOff Ken Forder is control officer 
for this visit. He will meet and assist during this trip. 
 
¶3.  Hotel reservations in Madrid and Barcelona have been made 
as requested in ref. cable.
