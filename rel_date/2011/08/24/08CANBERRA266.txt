On March 19, Poloff delivered reftel conference invitation to 
Michael Kourtoff, Executive Officer Counter-Proliferation 
Section, DFAT.  Mr. Kourteff has already been in 
correspondence with Yvette Wong, Director, Office of Export 
Control Cooperation, ISN through the GOA Embassy in 
Washington and will remain the POC for the GOA for this 
conference.  Contact details will be reconfirmed via email. 
 
Mr. Kourteff indicated that the GOA is interested in 
exploring other cooperative export control efforts with the 
USG and Poloff is contacting Ms. Wong separately on this 
request.
