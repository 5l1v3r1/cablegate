SUBJECT: POLAND: AVIAN INFLUENZA UPDATE - JUNE 8, 2006 
 
REF: WARSAW 943 AND PREVIOUS 
 
On June 7, 2006, Dr. Krzysztof Jazdzewski, Chief Veterinary 
Officer of Poland announced that there are no more HPAI 
outbreak sites in Poland.  The veterinary officials closed 
the last quarantine zone in Warta near Lodz because there 
were no new cases of AI in this area for 30 days.
