SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: 64TH UN GENERAL ASSEMBLY: 
HIGH-LEVEL ATTENDANCE AND REQUESTS FOR MEETINGS 
 
REF: STATE 68590 
 
On August 3, PolOff requested information on Yemen's UN 
General Assembly attendance from Mr. Mohamed Abdul Qader, 
United Nations section official in the International 
Organizations Department at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
(MFA).  PolOff informed Qader of all pertinent dates and 
urged the host government to apply for visas as soon as 
possible per reftel demarche.  Qader stated that the 
delegation will not be selected until the end of August, but 
that the ROYG usually sends two to three officials headed by 
the Minister of Foreign Affairs.  Qader agreed to inform 
PolOff as soon as more details become available.
