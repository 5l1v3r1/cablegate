SUBJECT: MOROCCO GIVES CLEAR SUPPORT FOR UNSCR 1874 
 
REF: STATE 65483 
 
(SBU)  Poloffs met with Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
Head of United Nations Division Redouane Houssaini on June 26 
to discuss reftel message concerning United Nations Security 
Council Resolution (UNSCR) 1874 condemning North Korea's May 
25 claimed nuclear test and imposing new measures against the 
country.  Houssaini replied that as a member of the 
Proliferation Security Initiative and the Global Initiative 
to Combat Nuclear Terrorism, Morocco has a very clear stance 
on this issue and agrees that all member states should abide 
with the resolution.  Houssaini added that Morocco does not 
have diplomatic ties with North Korea and supported the UN 
Human Rights resolution against the rogue country.  He agreed 
to share the demarche information with all concerned GOM 
departments and agencies.
