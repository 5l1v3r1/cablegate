SUBJECT: TURKEY RESPONSE TO WMO DEMARCHE 
 
 
STATE 47359 
 
In response to our sharing reftel demarche and non-paper on 
preparing for the World Meteorological Organization (WMO) Congress 
in Geneva in May, the relevant MFA official gave assurance that the 
U.S. position would be helpful as Turkey forms its position and 
delegation, still in planning.  She also shared our non-paper with 
the Turkish State Meteorological Service.  Referring to her 
understanding that the U.S. welcomes the Swiss proposal on 
governance in the WMO, she noted that Turkey is a co-sponsor.
