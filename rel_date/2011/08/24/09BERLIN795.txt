SUBJECT: GERMANY H1N1 FLU UPDATE: 429 CONFIRMED CASES 
 
REF:  A) Berlin 783, B) Berlin 781 and previous. 
 
¶1. (SBU) SUMMARY: The net number of H1N1 infections in Germany rose 
by 12 cases to a total of 429 on July 1. END SUMMARY. 
 
¶2. (SBU) On July 1, the National Reference Center for Influenza at 
RKI announced in its press briefing thirteen new 
laboratory-confirmed cases of H1N1 for Germany, and subtracted one 
previously confirmed case.  This net increase of twelve cases brings 
the total to 429. One H1N1 infection previously confirmed in Hesse 
was rescinded.  New cases were distributed among the federal states 
as follows: Hesse (-1), North Rhine-Westphalia (4), Lower-Saxony 
(3), Bavaria (2), Thuringia (2), Saxony (1), and Baden-W|rttemberg 
(1). 
 
¶3. (SBU)  North Rhine-Westphalia remains the state showing the 
highest number of infections among all German states with 197 
confirmed cases, followed by Baden-Wuerttemberg (65) and Bavaria (59 
cases).  The Federal State of Saarland remains the only state 
without confirmed virus cases.  The number of confirmed virus 
infections resulting from domestic transmission increased by 5 
bringing the total up to 235.
