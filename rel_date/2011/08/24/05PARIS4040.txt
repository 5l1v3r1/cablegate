SUBJECT: FRENCH POLDIR UNABLE TO ATTEND P5 AND G-4 MEETING 
IN NEW YORK 
 
REF: STATE 107200 
 
¶1. Poloff contacted Arnaud Barthelemy, advisor to MFA 
Political Direct Stanislas de Laboulaye, June 9 to reiterate 
the invitation to meet with P5 and G-4 counterparts in New 
York next week. 
 
¶2. Barthelemy was aware of the proposed meeting, but 
regretted that because of prior commitments to travel to 
Luxembourg and the UK, Laboulaye wold not be able to go to 
New York.  He inquired whether MFA IO Director Jean-Maurice 
Ripert could attend in Laboulaye's place.  Department please 
advise.
