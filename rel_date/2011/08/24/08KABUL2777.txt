¶1. This is an action request.  Please see paragraph 3.

forwards the names and biodata of 14 Afghan nationals selected to 
participate in INL Personal Security Detail training for the 
Attorney General's Office on October 25, 2008.  When there are 
individuals with one name, Post has made a good faith effort to 
ensure that these individuals actually only have one name.

human rights violations on the below-named individuals. Post 
requests Department's review of these candidates and response by 
October 23, 2008.
