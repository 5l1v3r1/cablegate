SUBJECT: POLAND WILL SUPPORT PROSPER'S CANDIDACY FOR UN CERD 
 
REF: SECSTATE 157656 
 
 PolOff delivered the dipnote and reftel points to Grzegorz 
Kozuba of the MFA,s UN Department on December 7. Kozuba 
noted that Poland also received the information from its UN 
Mission in NY, and had made the decision to support the U.S. 
candidate, Pierre-Richard Prosper, for the United Nations 
Committee on the Elimination of Racial Discrimination.  He 
also stated that the MFA,s diplomatic note officially 
confirming Poland,s support for Mr. Prosper will be sent to 
Embassy Warsaw next week.  Once received, Post will pouch the 
original diplomatic note to Washington.
