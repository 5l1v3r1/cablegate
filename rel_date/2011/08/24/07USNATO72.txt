SUBJECT: USNATO NOMINATIONS FOR MARCH 26-27 CONFERENCE IN 
EUROPE ON MODERN INSURGENCY 
 
REF: STATE 12490 
 
¶1.  Per reftel, USNATO nominates the following individuals to 
participate in the March 26-27 European conference on 
insurgency, "A Comprehensive Approach to Modern Insurgency: 
Afghanistan and Beyond." 
 
a.)   Assistant Secretary General for Political Affairs and 
Security Policy Ambassador Martin Erdmann (note: German 
national) 
 
b.)   Assistant Secretary General for Operations Ambassador 
Adam Kobieracki (note: Polish national) 
 
c.)   International Military Staff Assistant Director for 
Operations Major General Ivan Fenton (note: Canadian 
national) 
 
d.)   SHAPE Assistant Chief of Staff for Plans and Policy 
(J5) Brigadier General Simon Porter (note: UK national) 
 
¶2.  Ambassador Nuland invited Secretary General de Hoop 
Scheffer to open the conference.  The SYG expressed interest, 
but noted he may not be able to travel during the proposed 
time frame.  He would be open to addressing the conference 
via DVC if travel proved impossible.
