SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR MARCH 2008 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met April 7, 2008.  The meeting was 
chaired by the DCM and was attended by CG, CONS, RAO, LEGATT, DAO, 
RSO, POL and PD. 
 
¶2. Discussion centered on two persons arrested on terrorism related 
charges in the Spanish enclave of Melilia named Ali Aarass and 
Mohammed El Bay. 
 
¶3. A/ALAT indicated that international arrest warrants were obtained 
from the Kingdom of Morocco, but that she would attempt to obtain 
further information on the two arrested.  A/ALAT has communicated 
with Legatt Madrid to confirm with Embassy Madrid that they would 
submit the names under their Visa Viper program. 
 
¶4. The Mission's Visas Viper Coordinator is Consular Chief Miguel 
Ordonez.
