SUBJECT: HUMAN RIGHTS VETTING FOR IMET TRAINING 
 
REF: KIGALI 651 
 
 ¶1.  The Department of State has reviewed its files and finds 
that as of July 17, 2007, it possesses no credible 
information of gross violations of human rights by the 
following Rwandan Defense Force (RDF) personnel scheduled to 
attend Military HIV/AIDS training: 
 
Name:           Maj. Marc Rugenintwaza 
DOB:            1 June 1964 
Training Date:  29 July 2007 
 
Name:           Maj. Innocent Segahinga 
DOB:            9 July 1965 
Training Date:  29 July 2007 
 
Name:           Capt. Paul Kabayiza 
DOB:            15 February 1973 
Training Date:  29 July 2007 
 
¶2.  Post is therefore authorized to forward the following 
verification message:  "Embassy verifies that as of July 19, 
2007, the Department of State possesses no credible 
information of gross violations of human rights by the 
Rwandan Defense Force personnel listed above.  This cable 
completes the joint human rights vetting approval process for 
the military personnel cited."  Note that if at any time 
Embassy becomes aware of any information regarding incidents 
which could be deemed to be credible information of a gross 
violation of human rights by any personnel involved in this 
training, Embassy should so inform the Department.
