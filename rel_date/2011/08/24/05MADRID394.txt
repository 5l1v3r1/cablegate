¶1.   THE VISAS VIPER COMMITTEE MET ON JAN. 5, 2005, AS PART 
  OF MADRID INTERAGENCY COUNTERTERRORISM WORKING GROUP AND WAS 
  CHAIRED BY THE DCM, ROBERT MANZANARES.  THE CHARGE EXPRESSED 
  HIS APPRECIATION FOR THE CONTINUING STRONG COOPERATION AND 
  COMMITMENT TO THE PROGRAM BY ALL AGENCIES AT POST. 
 
¶2.   DURING THE MONTH OF JANUARY 2005, MADRID SUBMITTED ONE 
  VISAS VIPER CABLE IDENTIFYING SIX INDIVIDUALS, SUSPECTED 
  MEMBERS OF ISLAMIST TERRORIST GROUPS LINKED TO AL-QAEDA. 
 
¶3.   CABLE REFERENCE NUMBER: MADRID 0050. 
 
¶4.   IN ADDITION, POST HAS SUBMITTED ONE TIPPIX MEMORANDUM 
  CONTAINING TWO PHOTOGRAPHS OF SUSPECTS.
