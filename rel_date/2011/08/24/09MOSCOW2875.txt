SUBJECT: RUSSIA: APPRECIATIVE OF U.S. SUPPORT SEEKING 
CO-SPONSORS FOR RUSSIAN RESOLUTION ON THE ANGARSK RESERVE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 120282 
 
SENSTIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED -- Please protect accordingly. 
 
(SBU) On November 23, ESToff discussed with MFA/DVBR Senior 
Counselor Aleksey Karpov U.S. efforts to enlist other IAEA 
Board members to join the United States in co-sponsoring the 
Russian resolution on the Angarsk LEU fuel reserve (reftel) 
at the next IAEA Board of Governors meeting.  Karpov 
expressed Russia's appreciation for U.S. cooperation and 
support of this important Russian initiative.
