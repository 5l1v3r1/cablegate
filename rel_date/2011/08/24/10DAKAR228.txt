SUBJECT: Possible Senegalese Additions to MINUSTAH 
 
REF: STATE 12558 
 
Senegal currently has 85 gendarmes deployed with MINUSTAH in Haiti, 
a force currently projected to rise to 140 by July 2010. 
Nonetheless, Gendarmerie Headquarters in Dakar has estimated that 
another 110 personnel could be made available within two to three 
months for a one-year deployment, contingent on unspecified 
training and equipment support from third parties.  Post will make 
available to Gendarmerie HQ information provided in Para 8, reftel; 
however, a demarche directly from UNDPKO/OMA may be the best avenue 
to obtain prompt results.
