SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: ADDRESSING UN FINANCIAL 
SHORTFALLS FOR ELECTION COSTS IN AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 57541 
 
¶1.  (SBU) On April 23, Econoff delivered reftel points to 
Mansour Alolaimi, First Secretary and Division Director for 
Afghanistan and Iran at the Ministry of ForQn Affairs. 
Alolaimi, appearing receptive but noncommittal, explained 
that only the Foreign Minister, with the approval of the 
Prime Minister, would be able to decide what amount, if any, 
the GOK would provide in support of the Afghan Independent 
Election commission. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Pointing to the GOK's original $30M grant 
assistance to Afghanistan pledged at the 2002 Tokyo 
conference, Alolaimi added that Kuwait was always ready and 
willing to assist its "Afghan brothers and sisters."  The USG 
request would be conveyed to the MFA's Undersecretary for 
subsequent review by the Foreign Minister with a reply 
expected in the coming days, he explained.  Post will 
follow-up with the MFA regarding this issue and will report 
accordingly.
