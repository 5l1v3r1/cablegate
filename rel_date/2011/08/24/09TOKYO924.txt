SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON 2009 GLOBAL INITIATIVE 
MEETING; MOFA PLAN TO HOST EVENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 35549 
 
¶1.  (U) This cable contains an action request.  Please see 
para 4. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Econoff, with Dutch First Secretary (Political) 
Onny Jalink and Russian Second Secretary (Political) Andrey 
Savinov, delivered the demarche contained in ref to Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs (MOFA) Deputy Director for International 
Nuclear Energy Cooperation Shoichi Nagayoshi April 20. 
Nagayoshi had no comments on the issue of speaking roles for 
GOJ officials at the 2009 Global Initiative (GI) Plenary 
Meeting, but said Japan likely will send a director general 
(A/S equivalent) for the first day's meetings, and a 
director-level official for the second day of working groups. 
 He requested information on who would attend from the U.S. 
 
¶3.  (SBU)  Regarding upcoming GI events, Nagayoshi noted 
Foreign Minister Nakasone's speech planned for April 27 will 
address disarmament, non-proliferation, and the peaceful use 
of nuclear energy.  The speech, Nagayoshi continued, will 
contain proposals for future actions, including a seminar or 
workshop that MOFA hopes can take place under the umbrella of 
the GI.  He provided no further specifics, but post will 
follow up with MOFA after Nakasone's speech. 
 
¶4.  (U)  Action request: Please provide any information that 
can be shared with MOFA regarding the composition of the U.S. 
delegation to the 2009 Plenary meeting.
