(SBU) Post delivered reftel demarche to government of 
Indonesia officials at the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
the Ministry of Finance.  Sri Endah Susilorini, Bank 
Indonesia Senior Economic Analyst, International Affairs 
Directorate, later confirmed that Indonesia has submitted its 
ballot form directly to the IMF today, April 30.  Indonesia 
abstained, in line with current government of Indonesia 
policy regarding recognition of Kosovo.  Post will continue 
to engage with the Ministry of Finance, which is responsible 
for submitting Indonesia's vote in the World Bank.
