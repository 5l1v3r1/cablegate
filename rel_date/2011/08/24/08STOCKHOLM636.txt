SUBJECT: SWEDISH RESPONSE: DESIGNATION OF ADDITIONAL 
IRANIAN ENTITIES AND INDIVIDUALS UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: STATE 88759 
 
On September 16, Asa Gustafsson, Deputy Director, Department 
for Disarmament and Non-Proliferation, the Swedish Ministry 
for Foreign Affairs, told Deputy Economic Counselor that she 
has passed reftel points to other relevant departments and to 
the Swedish Inspectorate of Strategic Products.  Gustafsson 
commented that the entities and individuals named in reftel 
have already been designated by the EU in Council Common 
Position 2007/246/CFSP (April 23, 2007) and Council Common 
Position 2008/479/CFSP (June 23, 2008), which are binding for 
all EU member states.
