SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE GRANTED TO STAFFDEL MCCARTHY 
 
REF: STATE 122247 
 
¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Ryan McCarthy, Professional Staff 
Member, House International Relations Committee and Bart 
Forsyth, Deputy Chief Counsel, House International Relations 
Committee, and grants country clearance for their visit to 
Afghanistan on August 7-8, 2006, to evaluate Counter 
Narcotics Training in Afghanistan and Counter Terrorist 
Training and discussions with Defense, Oil and Intelligence 
Ministries.  Control Officer is Idris Rahimi, Political 
Officer, email address: RahimiIA@state.gov, telephone 
011-937-010-8160 or cell number 011-937-020-1563.  All 
travelers should bring USG ID for identification purposes and 
to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2. Embassy Kabul will provide transportation, security, 
escorts and lodging. 
 
¶3. Post requests to advise travelers security clearance as 
soon as possible 
 
¶4. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
