SUBJECT: RESPONSE TO INVITATION FOR CONVENTIONAL ARMS AND 
REGIONAL SECURITY SEMINAR 
 
REF: STATE 19800 
 
On March 16, Security Cooperation Program Officer delivered 
reftel invitation to the Office of the Prime Minister. The 
Government of Burkina Faso informed the Embassy on 20 March 
of its intention to send two Participants to the seminar: 
Colonel Abdoulaye Barro (permanent secretary of the National 
Counter Light Weapons Proliferation Committee) and Captain 
Oula Coulibaly (Director of Equipment for the Third Military 
Region and member of the National Counter Light Weapons 
Proliferation Committee). Post is making travel arrangements 
in accordance with Department of State guidance.
