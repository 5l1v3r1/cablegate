SUBJECT: IRAQI ASSETS UPDATE 
 
 
On October 17, EconCouns met with Moroccan Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs Chief of Juridical Affairs and Treaties 
Azzouz Aboulkouroum regarding the recovery of Iraqi assets in 
Morocco.  In June and July Econoffs had provided Aboulkouroum 
with very specific account information for groups and 
individuals identified under UNSCR 1483.  Aboulkouroum 
confirmed that he had "relaunched" an interagency effort to 
resolve any outstanding Iraqi asset issues, sharing the 
information with, inter alia, the Ministries of Finance, 
Interior, and Justice, and the central bank.  He was awaiting 
final definitive reports from these Moroccan government 
entities before preparing a comprehensive response for the 
Embassy.
