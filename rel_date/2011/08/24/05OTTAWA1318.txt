SUBJECT: CANADA WILL VOICE SUPPORT FOR THE IRAQI 
TRANSITIONAL GOVERNMENT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 79107 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Ann Flanagan 
Whalen, Foreign Affairs Canada's Deputy Director for the 
Middle East and North Africa.  After reviewing the points, 
Whalen reiterated Canada's support for the Iraqi transition 
and said that the GOC planned to have Minister Pettigrew make 
favorable comments welcoming the creation of the Iraqi 
government at the earliest opportunity.
