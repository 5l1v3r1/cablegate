This is an action request. Mission has received from the MFA 
a note and accompanying documents, requesting U.S. support 
for its candidature of Prof. Sevil Atasoy for election to the 
International Narcotics Control Board for the term 2005-2010. 
Mission will pouch these materials to EUR/SE, and would 
appreciate the Department providing guidance on the U.S. 
position.
