SUBJECT:  CONSULAR SECTION ACTIVITIES -- October/November 2007 
 
¶1. (SBU) Summary: Despite the restrictive environment created by the 
ubiquitous Syrian security services, we continue to run an active 
outreach program.  Below is a list of events in October and 
November.  End Summary. 
 
¶2. (SBU) 
 
International Education week November 4-8: Consular Officers 
distributed student visa information during the PD-sponsored 
International Education week's open house to approximately 350 
students.  Our NIV Chief briefed a total of 90 students during three 
sessions on student visa requirements. 
 
Aleppo Outreach: ACS and IV Unit Chiefs provided over 50 services to 
our AmCit community in Aleppo on November 5th, saving those families 
the four-hour drive to Damascus. 
 
Just for us: The Consular Section celebrated Thanksgiving together 
with a double-turkey event at the Ambassador's residence on November 
20th.
