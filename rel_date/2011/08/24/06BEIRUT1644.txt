     ¶B. STATE 083239 
 
¶1.  (U)  On May 24 and May 25, Post delivered to the Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs reftel demarches concerning the May 27-30 
NAM ministerial.  The Director for the Americas Ghady 
al-Khoury received the material and promised to pass it to 
the responsible delegation members in preparation for the NAM 
meeting.  Al-Khoury advised us that Foreign Minister Fawzi 
Salloukh will head the Lebanese delegation to the NAM 
meeting.  The delegation will be leaving for the meeting on 
May 25. 
 
¶2.  (U)  Al-Khoury said that on any issue related to Iran's 
nuclear program, Lebanon is likely to follow the Arab 
consensus.
