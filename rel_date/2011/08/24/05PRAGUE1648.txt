SUBJECT: CZECHS WILL LIKELY SUPPORT ADDING A THIRD PROTOCOL 
(THE RED CRYSTAL) TO THE GENEVA CONVENTION 
 
REF: STATE 206948 
 
(U) Poloff delivered reftel talking points to the Czech MFA's 
International Law Department on Nov 16, 2005. The MFA 
informed us on Nov 21 that the MFA recommends support for the 
new protocol. FM Cyril Svoboda plans to present the 
recommendation to the government for approval on Nov 30.
