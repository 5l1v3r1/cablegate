(SBU)  Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) Director 
for International Organizations Azzedine Farhane contacted 
D/Polcouns today to inform him that Foreign Minister Taieb 
Fassi Fihri had officially approved the U.S. amendments to 
the draft resolution designed to promote the Global 
Initiative for Combating Nuclear Terrorism (refs A and B) 
without any changes.
