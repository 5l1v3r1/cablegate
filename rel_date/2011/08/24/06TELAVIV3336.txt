SUBJECT: SUMMER BUILDING:  GOI ISSUES TENDERS FOR 
CONSTRUCTION IN HAR HOMA AND MA'ALE ADUMIM 
 
REF: TEL AVIV 02271 
 
¶1.  (U) The Israel Lands Authority and the Ministry of 
Construction and Housing on Thursday, August 17, issued a 
tender in Globes for construction on ten plots in Har Homa 
settlement south of Jerusalem.  According to the tender, two 
plots are for a commercial area to include parking with a 
49-year lease, and an option to extend for an additional 49 
years.  The remaining eight plots are for 432 housing units, 
probably apartment buildings.  The plots range in size from 
2,228 square meters to 6,395 square meters, and will have 
30-88 living units each.  The minimum price is between NIS 
1,020,000 and NIS 2,074,800, approximately $233,000-474,000. 
The expenditure for development ranges between NIS 3,887,546 
and NIS 10,197,747, or $888,000-2,328,000, depending on the 
size of the plots.  Both plots of land can be leased for 98 
years, with an option to extend for an additional 98 years. 
The deadline for bids is October 29, 2006. 
 
¶2.  (U) The Israel Lands Authority on June 20, issued a 
tender in Yediot Aharanot for construction of 12 
single-family homes in the Mitzpe Navo religious neighborhood 
of Ma'ale Adumim settlement east of Jerusalem.  The original 
tender stated that bids were due on July 30, 2006, but the 
tender was later re-issued on July 25 with bids due on August 
27, 2006.  The plots range in size from 375 square meters to 
553 square meters.  The minimum price is between NIS 55,000 
and NIS 70,000, approximately $12,500-16,000.  The 
expenditure for development ranges between NIS 247,982 and 
NIS 365,690, or $56,000-83,111.  The land can be leased for 
98 years, with an option to extend for an additional 98 year. 
 
 
¶3.  (U) The tender for construction of 53 houses in Elkana 
settlement (reftel) has been delayed until September 10, 
¶2006.
