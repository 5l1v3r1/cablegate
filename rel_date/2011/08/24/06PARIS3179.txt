SUBJECT: MFA NEA-PDAS EQUIVALENT LIKELY TO REPRESENT FRANCE 
AT MOSCOW BMENA/FORUM PLANNING MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 76283 
 
MFA action officer for Middle East reform issues Jean-Marc 
Grosgurin confirmed to poloff May 12 that the GoF will likely 
send MFA PDAS-equivalent for North Africa/Middle East Affairs 
Gilles Bonnaud to attend the May 29-30 BMENA Forum for the 
Future planning meeting in Moscow.  Grosgurin described 
Bonnaud's participation as "99 percent confirmed" and 
affirmed that the GoF would be represented at a senior level 
at the May 29 planning dinner.  He also agreed that the May 
29 meeting would be an important chance to shape the agenda 
for the next Forum, and appreciated the update on U.S. 
participation.
