SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE GRANTED TO CODEL TURNER 
 
REF: STATE 123953 
 
¶1.  Embassy Kabul welcomes Rep. Mike Turner, Member of the 
House Government Reform Committee (HGOV); John Cuaderes, 
Staff Director, HGOV; Mike Wiehe, Professional Staff member, 
HGOV, and grants country clearance for their visit to 
Afghanistan on August 7-8, 2006, to visit military and 
civilian personnel as they fight the Global War on Terror. 
Control Officer is Marie Richards, Acting Political 
Counselor, email address: RichardsMJ@state.gov, telephone 
011-937-010-8159 or cell number 011-937-020-1901.  All 
travelers should bring USG ID for identification purposes and 
to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2. All support will be provided by U.S. Military. 
 
¶3. Post recommends all travelers to review information about 
traveling to Kabul on post's website: http://kabul.state.gov.
