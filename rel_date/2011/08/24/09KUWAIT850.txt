SUBJECT: RAISING PSI EXERCISE LEADING EDGE WITH GOK 
 
REF: STATE 84305 
 
Polcouns briefed MFA non-proliferation lead Jassem Al-Najem 
August 27 on the planned January 2010 Leading Edge NPT 
exercise to be conducted in the UAE (reftel).  Polcouns urged 
full Kuwaiti participation.  Al-Jassem expressed strong 
interest in the exercise and said he anticipated that Kuwait 
will be pleased to participate, but being new on the job said 
he would need to confirm this with his superiors and promised 
to get back to us asap.
