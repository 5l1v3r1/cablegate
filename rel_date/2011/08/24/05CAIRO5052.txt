SUBJECT: EGYPT AND ARAB LEAGUE REPRESENTATIVES TO ATTEND 
JULY 9 CEREMONY IN KHARTOUM 
 
REF: STATE 122850 
 
¶1.  Hesham Afifi of the Foreign Ministry's cabinet staff told 
poloff July 3 that Prime Minister Nazif would represent the 
GOE at the July 9 installation of the Government of National 
Unity. According to Afifi, PM Nazif is scheduled to be in 
Khartoum from 7-10 July. 
 
¶2.  Arab League Secretary General Amre Moussa told Senior USG 
Representative for Sudan Charles Snyder June 30 that he would 
attend the July 9 ceremony. 
 
¶3.  Khartoum minimize considered.
