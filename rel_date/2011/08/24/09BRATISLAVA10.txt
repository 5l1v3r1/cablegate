PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Tomas Hrbac, Deputy 
Director of the Slovak MFA's UN Department.  Hrbac said that 
the EU member states will have an internal consultation the 
morning of January 9 before the UNDP Executive Board meeting 
to discuss a proposal to delay the vote on Iran's Presidency. 
 Hrbac said Slovakia will not oppose the delay of the 
election.
