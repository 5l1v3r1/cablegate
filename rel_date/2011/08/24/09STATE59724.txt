¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment end-use check on license number 050096393 for 
the export of defense hardware and technical data to France. 
Post is requested to complete this Blue Lantern inquiry 
within 45 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern inquiry 
will affect pending and future licenses involving parties to 
this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request:  To confirm bona fides of Foreign 
Intermediate Consignee, Elexo, in the licensed transaction. 
 
¶4. Action: Department requests that Post make inquiries 
within the French Ministry of Defense to verify that it 
received the listed items and they are being used for the 
purpose stated on the license.  Also, confirm Foreign 
Intermediate Consignee Elexo's role in the authorized 
transaction.  Remind Elexo of the restrictions concerning the 
unauthorized retransfer or re-export of United States 
Munitions List (USML) items. 
 
End Action Request.
