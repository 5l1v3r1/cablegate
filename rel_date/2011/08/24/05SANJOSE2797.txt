RESOLUTION 
 
REF: A. SAN JOSE 2475 
 
     ¶B. STATE 220462 
 
¶1. (SBU) Political counselor delivered reftel B talking 
points to Jose Joaquin Chaverri, MFA Director General 
(number-three person in the ministry), on December 7. 
Polcouns noted U.S. disappointment with Costa Rica's votes on 
December 1, particularly in light of the promise we received 
that Costa Rica at a minimum would abstain (ref A). 
 
¶2. (SBU) Polcouns stressed the importance of the December 8 
vote and asked that Costa Rica vote no or at least abstain. 
Chaverri replied that Costa Rica would abstain.
