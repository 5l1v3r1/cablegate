SUBJECT: WHITE PAPER DELIVERED ON PAKISTAN/ AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 29482 
 
(SBU) Mission delivered reftel white paper on the 
Pakistan/Afghanistan strategic review to the Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs on March 30. Africa and Asia Director 
Augustin Rutikara received the white paper and promised a 
careful reading.  Rutikara said that, while Rwanda had 
embassies in Japan, China and India, and would soon open two 
more in Singapore and South Korea, the GOR concentrated the 
bulk of its efforts on regional security challenges.
