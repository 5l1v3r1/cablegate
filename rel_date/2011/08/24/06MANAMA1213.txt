SUBJECT: IRAQI PARLIAMENTARY DELEGATION TO VISIT BAHRAIN 
 
 
(U) The Bahrain press has reported, and Iraqi emboffs have 
confirmed, that a large Iraqi parliamentary delegation will 
visit Bahrain next week.  The delegation, consisting of 
approximately 20 parliamentarians and 10 support staff, is 
currently in Iran and will arrive in Bahrain on Saturday. 
Iran and Bahrain are the only stops on the trip.  In Bahrain, 
they are scheduled to meet with Bahraini parliamentarians and 
are expected to host a press event.   The delegation includes 
members of different parties, both Sunni and Shia, according 
to the Iraqi Embassy.
