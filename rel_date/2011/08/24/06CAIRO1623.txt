SUBJECT: GOE ASSURANCES FOR EXPORT OF NUCLEAR FUEL FOR

REF: STATE 011689 
 
¶1.  In response to reftel request for GOE assurances for the 
export of nuclear fuel for the ETTR-2 research reactor at Abu 
Zabal Nuclear Research Center, Post received the following 
letter on March 12 from Professor Aly Islam M. Aly, Chairman 
of the Egyptian Atomic Energy Authority (AEA). (Note: Letter 
received in English.  Spelling and punctuation unchanged. End 
note.)  Begin text: 
 
"The Egyptian Atomic Energy Authority presents its 
compliments to your Excellency. 
 
The Atomic Energy Authority of Egypt confirms that the 
Nuclear Material proposed to be exported to Egypt via 
Argentina comprises 8.4 Kg U-235 in 42.0 Kg Metallic Uranium 
enriched to less than 20 percent in U-235.  This material 
will be used in Argentina to produce (40) Kg of Uranium in 
the form U308 for further use to fabricate fuel elements by 
Egyptian Atomic Energy Authority for reload of the ETTR-2 
Research reactor at the Nuclear Research Centre located near 
Abou Zabal, Cairo.  The mentioned material upon receipt in 
Egypt will be subject to the terms and conditions of the 1981 
Agreement for Cooperation between Government of the United 
States of America and the Government of the Arab Republic of 
Egypt concerning Peaceful Uses of Nuclear Energy. 
 
The Egyptian Atomic Energy Authority avails itself of this 
opportunity to renew to you its highest consideration. 
 
Sincerely yours, 
Prof. Aly Islam M. Aly" 
 
End text.
