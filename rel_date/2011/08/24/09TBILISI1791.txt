SUBJECT: GEORGIA: DEMARCHE DELIVEVED: EXISTENCE OF A COVERT 
URANIUM ENRICHMENT FACILITY IN IRAN

(U)  On October 1, Post delivered reftel demarche describing 
the existence of a covert uranium enrichment facility near 
Qom, Iran, to Levan Khurtsidze, Georgian Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, Department of the Americas.  Khurtsidze expressed 
appreciation for the information and promised to move it 
forward to the appropriate people in the Georgian Government.
