SUBJECT: SWEDISH RESPONSE: CRITICAL EQUIPMENT NEEDS FOR 
IRAN'S NEW IR-2 CENTRIFUGE DESIGN (SBU) 
 
REF: STATE 52030 
 
(SBU) On June 3, Deputy Economic Counselor shared reftel 
points with Asa Gustafsson, Deputy Director, Department for 
Disarmament and Non-Proliferation, at the Swedish MFA. 
Gustafsson commented that she would discuss this information 
with other relevant authorities, such as the Swedish 
Inspectorate of Strategic Products (ISP).  She noted that her 
preliminary assessment is that Sweden exercises stringent 
control on the vast majority of these items.  However, she 
will request a more thorough analysis by the ISP to ensure 
that all items critical to Iran's new IR-2 centrifuge design 
are included.  Post will report any additional response 
septel.
