SUBJECT: BULGARIAN AMBASSADOR TO VIENNA WILL ATTEND VIENNA 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 60216 
 
PolOff delivered reftel demarche to the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs, Department Head for UN and Global Issues, Kamen 
Velichkov on June 13, and at that time, no decision had been 
made on the Bulgarian delegation or contribution to the 
Vienna Conference.  On June 16, Middle East and Africa 
Directorate Expert, Yuliya Pirozlieva told PolOff that the 
Foreign Minister will not be able to attend the conference 
and that the Bulgarian Ambassador to Vienna, Radi Naydenov, 
will attend.  Noting that Bulgaria did not have significant 
donor capacity and that past contributions had been in-kind, 
she said that no decision had been made about the Bulgarian 
contribution this time.  Pirozlieva said that Bulgaria shares 
the U.S. view on the importance of the conference.
