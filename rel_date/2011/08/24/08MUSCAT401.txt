SUBJECT: OMANI RESPONSE TO FRIENDS OF IRAQ CONFERENCE 
 
REF: A. STATE 56560 
     ¶B. STATE 50592 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Post delivered ref A invitation to the Friends of 
Iraq Conference on May 13 via diplomatic note.  The Omani 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) contacted the Embassy on 
May 24 to request a copy of the agenda for the conference. 
On May 28, post transmitted ref B information, including the 
proposed conference agenda, to the MFA under cover of another 
diplomatic note. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) During a June 1 meeting with Minister Responsible 
for Foreign Affairs Yusef bin Alawi, the Ambassador urged the 
Omani government to participate in the Friends of Iraq 
Conference.  Bin Alawi responded that he would "take a close 
look" at the invitation and agenda.  Post will follow up with 
MFA and Ministry of Defense officials to promote Omani 
attendance at the event.
