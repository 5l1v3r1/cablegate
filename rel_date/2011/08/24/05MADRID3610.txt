SUBJECT: SPAIN ON THE HOLOCAUST REMEMBRANCE RESOLUTION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 188927 
 
Poloff delivered reftel demarche to MFA Deputy Director 
General for the United Nations Juan Manuel Gonzalez de 
Linares. He reiterated the informaton in reftel, that Spain 
is already a co-sponsor of the Holocaust Remembrance 
Resolution, and he added that they had been in extensive 
contact with the Israeli Embassy in Madrid on the subject. He 
noted that the Spanish government has already set aside a 
national Holocaust remembrance holiday - January 27 - and is 
a strong supporter of the UN initiative.
