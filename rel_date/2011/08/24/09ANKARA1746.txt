SUBJECT: UPDATE ON PKK VIOLENCE (NOVEMBER 16-23, 2009) 
 
REF:  ANKARA 1629 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶2. (U) During November 16-23, no security official was killed or 
injured.  The total number of security force casualties for the year 
through November 23, is 53 killed and 90 injured. 
 
¶3. (U) During the same period, two PKKers were captured in Sanliurfa 
and Hatay provinces.  According to official TGS website figures, 
through the third week of November, the number of PKK militants 
killed this year is 45, the number of those captured is 71.  The 
number of those who surrendered reached 119. 
 
¶4. (U) During the reporting period no civilian was killed or injured 
due to PKK actions.  The total number of civilian casualties for the 
year through the end of the third week of November, is 15 killed and 
25 wounded.
