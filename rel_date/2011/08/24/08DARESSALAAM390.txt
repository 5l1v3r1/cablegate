SUBJECT: TANZANIA P5+1 DEMARCHE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 064530 
 
1 (SBU) Poloff delivered reftel to Government of Tanzania (GOT) 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation (MFA) 
Foreign Service Officer Noel Kaganda on June 19.  Kaganda stated 
that the GOT supported the P5+1 proposed assistance to Iran for the 
peaceful development of nuclear power.  He said that the GOT stood 
firm in its commitment that countries have the right to seek nuclear 
power for peaceful means, but not for purposes of aggression or 
military use.  Kaganda said that he would pass the demarche 
information up to his superiors at MFA and Tanzania's mission in New 
York.  He promised to provide the embassy with any further 
feedback.
