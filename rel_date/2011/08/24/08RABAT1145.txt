SUBJECT: MOROCCO CONSIDERS U.S. PROPOSAL ON UN 
HARMONIZATION OF CONDITIONS OF SERVICE 
 
REF: STATE 129988 
 
(SBU)  Poloff delivered reftel message on December 12 to 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Chief of Service of the Principal 
Bodies of the UN Redoune Houssaini regarding harmonization of 
conditions of service at the United Nations.  Houssaini 
agreed to forward the U.S. proposal to his supervisors for 
their consideration in lieu of accepting the other two 
proposals from the Fifth Committee and the UN Secretary 
General.
