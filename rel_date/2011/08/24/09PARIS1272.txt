SUBJECT: FRANCE TO ATTEND FRIENDS OF DEMOCRATIC PAKISTAN 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 96052 
 
¶1. (SBU) SUMMARY: France will attend the September 24 Friends 
of Democratic Pakistan conference in New York, but the GOF 
has not yet determined the composition of their delegation. 
END SUMMARY. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post delivered the information contained in reftel 
September 17 to Yann Braem, Deputy Chief of Staff for French 
Special Representative for Afghanistan/Pakistan Thierry 
Mariani, and MFA Pakistan Desk Officer Annie Evrard.  Braem 
said Mariani and Jasmine Zerinini, Mariani's chief of staff, 
would probably be attending, and it was possible that Foreign 
Minister Bernard Kouchner might also attend.  However, the 
exact composition of the delegation has not yet been 
determined.  He said he would provide post with the 
delegation list when it is confirmed. 
 
¶3. (U) Post will report directly to the POCs when the GOF has 
provided its delegation list.
