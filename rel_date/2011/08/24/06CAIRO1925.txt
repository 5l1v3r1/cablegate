SUBJECT: WORLD ECONOMIC FORUM IN SHARM EL SHEIKH 
 
¶1. (U) This cable is an action request.  Please see paragraph 
¶2. 
 
¶2. (U) Embassy Cairo is preparing for the upcoming World 
Economic Forum on the Middle East, to be held in Sharm El 
Sheikh May 20-22, 2006.  Given the heavy demand for suitable 
hotel space in Sharm during the summit as well as other 
logistical support requirements, Post urges all potential 
participants from all agencies to declare their intentions to 
travel now and begin consultations with the appropriate 
coordinating offices. 
 
¶3. (U) The summit will provide an excellent forum for 
Washington policymakers to confer with their counterparts 
from the region and the world, and Embassy Cairo encourages 
the broadest possible participation.  The State Department 
Middle East Partnership Initiative office (NEA/PI) will be 
coordinating Washington preparations. 
 
¶4. (U) Embassy Cairo Event Control Officer will be Economic 
and Political Counselor John Desrocher (20-2-797-2251, 
desrocherjp@state.gov) and Deputy Event Control Officer will 
be Econoff Kevin Roland (20-2-797-3952, rolandks2@state.gov).
