SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR DECEMBER 2007 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met January 8, 2008.  The meeting was 
chaired by the DCM and was attended by CONS, RAO, LEGATT, DAO, PD, 
POL, and RSO. 
 
¶2. Discussion centered on the sentencing of members of the Ansar Al 
Mahdi cell on January 4 by the Sale court as well as a press report 
in Arabic in the Al Massae newspaper about alleged Moroccan 
jihadists in Iraq.  Legatt was tasked with developing information 
from the press reports to see if enough information can be developed 
so that new Viper submissions may be submitted. 
 
¶3. No names are being submitted for the December 2007 reporting 
period at this time. 
 
¶4. The Mission's Visas Viper Coordinator is Consular Chief Miguel 
Ordonez.
