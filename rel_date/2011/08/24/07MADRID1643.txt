SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: MEETING ON COLOMBIA 
 
REF: STATE 119828 
 
(SBU)  Acting PolCouns delivered reftel demarche on 
participants and agenda for the September Meeting on Colombia 
to Paloma Villa Mateos, interim Chief of Staff to Secretary 
of State for Iberoamerica Trinidad Jimenez.  Villa expressed 
appreciation for the advance notice and said she had no 
objection in principal to the additional participants and 
agenda items.  However, she needed to confirm that position 
with Jimenez and Chief of Staff Juan Carlos Sanchez Alonso, 
currently both on vacation.  She promised to contact post the 
week of September 3 with any additional comments.  On Spain's 
participation, Villa said that the Spanish representative 
would be either Jimenez or Political Director Rafael 
Dezcallar, depending on the date and time of the meeting. 
She noted that Jimenez is currently holding the morning of 
September 28 for a bilateral meeting with Assistant Secretary 
Shannon on broader Latin America.  Post will follow up.
