¶1. (SBU) This is an Action Request, please see para. 3. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Following the Government of Turkey,s formal offer 
of at least one frigate with air element embarked to support 
counter piracy operations off the coast of Somalia, the 
Department concurs with Embassy Ankara,s support for 
Turkey,s request (Ref A) to be included in the Contact Group 
on Somali Piracy (CGSP). 
 
¶3. (SBU) Action Request: Embassy Ankara is instructed to 
approach appropriate GOT officials per the instructions found 
in Ref B. Post should additionally note our appreciation for 
Turkey,s readiness to support the CGSP with military assets.
