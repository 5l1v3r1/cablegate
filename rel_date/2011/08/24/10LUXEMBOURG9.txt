PARTICIPATION IN THE PRT CONFERENCE IN PRAGUE

GOL Defense Director Jean-Jacques Welfring January 19. Welfring 
stated that he expected someone from the MFA would attend the PRT 
conference but who was yet to be determined.

Conference in Prague, an E.U. meeting on Afghanistan in Brussels 
that same day, requires his presence.

delegate to the conference in Prague.
