¶1.   THE VISAS VIPER COMMITTEE MET ON NOV. 8 AND ON NOV. 29, 
  2004, AS PART OF MADRID INTERAGENCY COUNTERTERRORISM WORKING 
  GROUP AND WAS CHAIRED BY THE CHARGE, ROBERT MANZANARES.  THE 
  CHARGE EXPRESSED HIS APPRECIATION FOR THE CONTINUING STRONG 
  COOPERATION AND COMMITMENT TO THE PROGRAM BY ALL AGENCIES AT 
  POST. 
 
¶2.   DURING THE MONTH OF NOVEMBER 2004, MADRID SUBMITTED 
  EIGHT VISAS VIPER CABLES IDENTIFYING SEVENTY-ONE 
  INDIVIDUALS, TWENTY-FOUR SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ETA AND FOURTY- 
  SEVEN SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ISLAMIST TERRORIST GROUPS LINKED 
  TO AL-QAEDA. 
 
¶3.   CABLES REFERENCE NUMBER: MADRID 04322, 04386, 04387, 
  04388, 04389, 04474, 04523, 04524. 
 
¶4.   IN ADDITION, POST HAS SUBMITTED THREE TIPPIX MEMORANDUM 
  CONTAINING TEWNTY-SIX PHOTOGRAPHS OF SUSPECTS.
