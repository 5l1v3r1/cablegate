SUBJECT: LEBANON:  DEMARCHE DELIVERED: U.S. CANDIDACY FOR 
ICAO COUNCIL 
 
REF: STATE 115443 
 
¶1. (SBU)  Post deliverd reftel demarche to both Minister of 
Transportation Mohammed Safadi and Director General of Civil 
Aviation Dr. Hamdi Chouk.  Dr. Chouk said Lebanon is always 
supportive of the USG in ICAO. 
 
¶2. (SBU)  Dr. Chouk will lead the GOL delegation to the ICAO 
meetings in Montreal in September. 
 
¶3. (SBU)  Dr. Chouk asked the USG to support Lebanon's bid 
for reelection to the council in category Part III.  Lebanon 
has been the council member elected to represent its region 
of small countries in NEA, which includes Syria, Jordan and 
Iraq. This region excludes the Gulf countries and Saudi 
Arabia.  Dr. Chouk has a signed letter of support from Syria 
and Jordan.
