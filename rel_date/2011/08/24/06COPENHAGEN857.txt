SUBJECT: MDA: DENMARK SUPPORTIVE OF US POSITION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 95184 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel deQrche June 14 to Ambassador 
Tyge Lehmann, MFA Senior Advisor for International Law. 
Lehmann noted that Denmark was supportive of the U.S. 
position on membership, including the addition of Magen David 
Adom to the International Red Cross and Red Crescent 
Movement.  He noted that Denmark would leave the final 
decision to the national societies.  Denmark will not send a 
special delegation to attend the conference but will be 
represented by its Mission to the UN in Geneva.
