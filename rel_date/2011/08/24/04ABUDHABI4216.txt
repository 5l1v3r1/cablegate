 On November 23, Ambassador presented ref A demarche to MFA 
U/S Abdullah Rashid Al-Noaimi asking the UAEG to commit to 
providing Iraq with comparable debt relief to that provided 
by the Paris Club and providing suggested language for a 
public statement to that effect.  Al-Noaimi thanked 
Ambassador for the suggested language and reiterated that the 
UAEG would provide comparable debt relief to Iraq.  He was 
not able to specify when the UAEG would issue its public 
statement.  Post will continue to follow-up with the UAEG on 
this issue and will report when the UAEG issues its 
statement.
