SUBJECT: Update on PKK Violence (March 24-31, 2009) 
 
REF: ANKARA 478 and previous 
 
¶1. (U) Sensitive but unclassified - please protect accordingly. 
 
¶2. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶3. (U) During the March 24-31, 2009, no security official was 
injured or killed.  The total number of security forces casualties 
for the year through March 31, was zero killed and zero injured. 
 
¶4. (U) During the same period, one PKK terrorist was captured in 
Reyhanli, Hatay on March 25.  Six PKKers surrendered, including 
three in Yuksekova, Hakkari on March 25 and March 26, and three in 
Silopi, Sirnak on March 30.  According to official TGS website 
figures, through the fourth week of March, the number of PKK 
militants killed this year was one, the number of those captured was 
13, and the number of those who surrendered was 24. 
 
¶5. (U) During the reporting period no civilian died or injured. 
The total number of civilian casualties for the year through the end 
of the fourth week of March, was zero killed and five wounded.
