SUBJECT: MOROCCO CONSIDERS PARTICIPATION IN UNESCO 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 46755 
 
On May 9, 2008, Poloff encouraged the Government of Morocco 
to facilitate participation in the UNESCO international 
conference for at risk youth by delivering ref demarche to 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Counselor to the United Nations 
Division Amine Chabi.  Chabi said that MFA would give full 
consideration to participating in the conference but 
otherwise had no immediate feedback.
