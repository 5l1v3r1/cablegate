SUBJECT: ROK SHARES USG PERSPECTIVE ON IRAN FOR BOG MEETING

(SBU) On June 8, poloff delivered reftel points, encouraging 
national statements seeking Iranian cooperation on the 
nuclear issue, to Park Chul-min, Deputy Director of MOFAT's 
Disarmament and Nonproliferation Division.  Park said that 
Seoul welcomed the points, shared the same perspective as 
Washington, and would forward the message to its Embassy in 
Vienna.
