SUBJECT: KYRGYZ RECEPTIVE TO UNSCR 1747 POINTS

(SBU) Poloff raised reftel points with Kyrgyz MFA Department 
of International Organizations and Security Affairs' Ulan 
Umaraliyev on May 18.  Umaraliyev was appreciative of the 
Department's views on implementing operative paragraph 5 of 
UNSCR 1747, and promised to remind his office -- as well as 
their mission in New York -- of the reporting requirements 
attached to the resolution.
