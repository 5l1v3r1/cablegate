¶1. (SBU) Acting on instructions in reftel, Blue Lantern 
Officer checked with the U.S. Naval Attache in DAO Tel Aviv, 
who confirmed that the U.S. Navy's Naval Air Systems Command 
has ordered the defense hardware listed in DTC Case 
05-050053682.  The Naval Attache explained that the items are 
flares produced by Israel Military Industries (IMI), Ltd., 
and that Naval Air Systems Command is purchasing the flares 
as part of a project to improve them for integration into 
aircraft protection systems. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Embassy Tel Aviv views the transaction in question 
as legitimate and recommends a favorable closing of the case.
