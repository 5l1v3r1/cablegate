SUBJECT: CHINA INFORMED OF GLOBAL INITIATIVE MEETING TO 
DEVELOP A WEB-BASED INFORMATION PORTAL 
 
REF: STATE 91275 
 
Poloff informed MFA Arms Control and Disarmament Department 
Missile Division Deputy Director Wang Daxue of the Global 
Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism (GI) meeting in 
Germany on August 26-29 to develop a web-based information 
portal to facilitate information sharing among GI partner 
nations.  Thanking Poloff for the information, Wang said that 
China considers the GI a valuable forum for international 
nonproliferation cooperation and is proud to be a founding 
member.  He noted that China has actively participated in all 
past GI events.
