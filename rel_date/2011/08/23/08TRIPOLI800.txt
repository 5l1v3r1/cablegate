SUBJECT: U.S. AFRICA COMMAND STATUS UPDATE - LIBYA 
 
REF: STATE 104550 
 
¶1.  We informed the Libyan General People's Committee for 
Foreign Liaison and International Cooperation (MFA equivalent) 
on October 6 via diplomatic note of the formal establishment of 
U.S. Africa Command (AFRICOM) as a unified combatant command 
effective October 1. 
 
 
¶2.  There has been no reporting or comment in the Libyan media 
on the establishment of AFRICOM.  We will stay alert for 
significant publiuc or private comment in the future.
