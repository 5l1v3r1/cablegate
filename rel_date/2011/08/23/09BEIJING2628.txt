SUBJECT: Demarche Delivered: China on UNGA Food 
Security Event 
 
REF: State 93894 
 
¶1. (SBU) Post delivered reftel demarche to the 
International Organizations and Conferences 
Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) on 
September 10 and had a follow-up conversation on 
September 14.  MFA officials said they are 
coordinating with the Ministry of Agriculture on the 
invitation and China's position regarding the draft 
joint statement. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post also contacted Steven Sabey, Policy 
Advisor in the Office of the United Nations Resident 
Coordinator in China, who said an invitation to the 
September 26 food security event has also been sent 
from UN Headquarters in New York to MFA.  Sabey said 
MFA informed his office that China plans to attend 
the September 26 event and is currently deciding on 
the appropriate level of participation. 
 
¶3. (U) We will report further information as it 
becomes available.
