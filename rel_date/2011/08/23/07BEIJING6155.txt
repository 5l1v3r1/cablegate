SUBJECT: CHINA APPROVES SHANGHAI PORT FOR MEGAPORTS PILOT 
PROGRAM 
 
¶1. (SBU) The Chinese State Council has approved the deepwater 
port of Yangshen in Shanghai as the Chinese location for the 
Megaports pilot program, MFA International Organizations 
Department Officer Song Jia told Poloff on September 19.  FM 
Yang Jiechi will notify Secretary Rice of the Chinese 
decision during his meeting with the Secretary inWashington 
on September 21, Song said. 
 
¶2. SBU) China Customs delivered a similar message to post's 
DOE China Acting Country Director on September 19.
