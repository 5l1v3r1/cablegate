the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism (GI) to 
include four new members, MFA Arms Control Department 
official Yu Dunhai told poloff June 4.  China's preparations 
for the upcoming Third GI meeting in Kazakhstan are complete 
and it will make a detailed presentation on preparations for 
the Olympics, Yu said.
