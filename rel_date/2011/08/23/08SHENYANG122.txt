SUBJECT: MEETING WITH LIAONING ENTRY EXIT BUREAU RE 
ECUADOR'S VISA WAIVER POLICY 
 
REF: SECSTATE 90800 
 
¶1.  (SBU) On September 5, conoffs met with Liaoning Provincial 
Entry Exit Administration Bureau (EEB) officials to discuss the 
issues and information outlined in State 90800.  Representing 
the EEB, which is a subset of the Public Security Division, 
were Ms. Du Juan, Bureau Chief, and Mr. Liang Qi Min, 
Secretariat Chief. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Du thanked conoffs for the information and said that 
so far they had not seen any impact from the new Ecuadorian visa 
waiver policy.  She expressed EEB,s concerns about the 
situation and emphasized that the Chinese Government's policy 
was to work to prevent illegal immigration.  She said that 
EEB officials would pay greater attention to travelers 
indicating Ecuador as their destination.  Du also said the 
EEB was willing to share relevant information with the 
consulate.
