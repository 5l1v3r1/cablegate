SUBJECT: CITB AGREES TO NOVEMBER 6 MEETING ON ICSN 
 
REF: A. SECSTATE 180586 
 
     ¶B. HONG KONG 4174 
 
¶1.  (U) This is an action request, please see paragraph 4. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Upon receipt of ref a instructions, Post contacted 
Secretary of Commerce, Industry and Technology Joseph Wong's 
 
SIPDIS 
office to arrange a meeting with the Consul General.  Because 
Secretary Wong's schedule precludes a meeting prior to the 
 
SIPDIS 
USG Team's proposed visit, Post delivered ref a non-paper to 
Secretary Wong's staff.  Secretary Wong supports the 
 
SIPDIS 
Department of Homeland Security/Customs and Border Protection 
and Department of Energy/National Nuclear Security 
Administration  Team's visit to Hong Kong to brief the HKG on 
the proposed designation of Hong Kong as a Phase I 
International Container Security Network (ICSN) participant. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) The Commerce, Industry and Technology Bureau (CITB) 
identified Deputy Secretary Christopher Wong as the head of 
the HKG delegation that will meet with the joint USG Team and 
committed to meet all day on November 6, as necessary.  CITB 
officials indicated that the Hong Kong delegation will 
consist of officials from Hong Kong Customs, the Security 
Bureau, the Economic Development and Labor Bureau and the 
Trade and Industry Department. 
 
¶4.  (U) Action Request.  Post requests that the USG Team 
submit country clearance and provide delegation list and 
arrival and departure flights/times as soon as possible.
