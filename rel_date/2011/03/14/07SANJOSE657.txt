On March 30, Poloff sent information regarding the U.S. 
decision under the provisions of E.O. 13382 to designate 
Iran's Defense Industries Organization (ref A) to Alejandro 
Solano Ortiz, deputy foreign policy director at the Costa 
Rican Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Solano responded that 
same day, indicating that the MFA would study the issue.  Per 
ref B, Costa Rica does not have diplomatic relations with 
Iran, no official Iranians reside in Costa Rica, and Costa 
Rica does not have or intend to have official representation 
in Iran.
