SUBJECT: COSTA RICA REQUESTS ENDORSEMENT OF ITS CANDIDACY 
TO UPU POSTAL EXPLOITATION COUNCIL 
 
 
¶1. Per diplomatic note No. DGPE/DOI-C/146-9-07 dated 
09-07-2007, the GOCR has requested endorsement of its 
candidacy to the Postal Exploitation Council of the Universal 
Postal Union (UPU).  The Council,s membership will be 
decided at the Naroibi UPU congress in August 2008. Costa 
Rica has been a member of the UPU since 1983 and is clearly 
aware of the mission and workings of the 
Council. 
 
¶2. The GOCR has been advised that their endorsement request 
has been passed to the Department (faxed to WHA/CEN) and that 
it will be given due consideration.
