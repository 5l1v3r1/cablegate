SUBJECT: COSTA RICAN DELEGATION FOR WIREC 
 
REF: A. STATE 15551 
 
     ¶B. 2007 STATE 1830 
 
¶1.  After much deliberation, the GOCR proposed three 
individuals for travel to the Washington 
International Renewable Energy Conference (WIREC) in 
Washington, DC, on 3-4 March (reftel A): 
 
- Julio Matamoros, Vice Minister of Energy 
- Paola Arias, Ministry of Energy expert in renewable energy 
issues 
- Linyi Baidal Sequeira, technician of the Environmental 
Concerns Section in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
 
¶2.  This is a smaller delegation than the one proposed in 
October 2007 (reftel B).  Neither the Minster of Foreign 
Affairs nor the Minister of Energy can attend. 
 
¶3.  All three attendees will pay for their travel costs.
