(SBU)  Polcouns delivered the demarche on Opposing 
Anti-Israel Bias at the UN (Reftel) to the Foreign Ministry's 
Director of Multilateral Affairs, Julio Garro, and Director 
of UN Affairs, Miguel Barreto, on 10/24.  Garro said Peru has 
abstained on the three resolutions in question the past two 
years, and that he expects Peru will continue to do so when 
these resolutions are brought to a vote during the current 
UNGA session.
