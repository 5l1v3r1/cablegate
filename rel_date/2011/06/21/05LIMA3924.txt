Deputy EconCouns delivered Reftel demarches on 9/7 and 8/29, 
respectively.  Foreign and Health Ministries confirmed to 
Deputy EconCouns on 9/8 that the GOP is committed to joining 
the International Partnership on Avian and Pandemic 
Influenza.  No cases of Avian Flu have been reported in Peru, 
but the GOP has taken steps to combat the threat and is 
highly motivated to collaborate with the USG.  Septel will 
address the GOP's existing Avian Influenza action plan, steps 
GOP has taken so far, and suggestions for collaboration 
between the USG and GOP.
