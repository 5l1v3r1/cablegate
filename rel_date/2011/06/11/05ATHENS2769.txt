SUBJECT: GREEK SHIPMENT OF APCS AND AK-47S EN ROUTE TO IRAQ 
 
 
Classified By: Amb. Charles P. Ries.  Reasons 1.4(b/d). 
 
¶1.  (C) On October 24, Embassy DATT confirmed that the 
"Aegean Star," a Greek-flagged ship contracted to the 
Ministry of Defense, finished loading thirty-six BMPs 
(Russian APCs) and one thousand AK-47s for transshipment to 
Iraq.  The ship already had on board seventy-two Hungarian 
T-72 tanks on board that were loaded in Koper, Slovenia. 
 
¶2.  (C) The "Aegean Star" will depart Souda Bay, Crete at 
1800 local (October 24) for Kuwait.  At this time, DAO is 
assisting Greece to obtain proper clearance to transit the 
Suez Canal.
