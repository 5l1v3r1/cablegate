SUBJECT: TFUS01:  ADDITIONAL PERUVIAN OFFER OF ASSISTANCE 
 
 
The Peruvian Foreign Ministry, by Diplomatic Note dated 9/12, 
informed the Embassy that a local company, Alicorp S.A.A. has 
donated 350 cases of cookies/crackers for Hurricane Katrina 
victims, and that these cookies are currently in the GOP's 
possession.  The cracker/cookie donation consists of: 
 
--  Soda Crackers (95 cases) 
--  Vanilla Cookies (95 cases) 
--  Coconut Cookies (50 cases) 
--  Orange Cookies (50 cases) 
--  Mini-crackers (60 cases) 
 
Total weight:  571.8 kilos.
