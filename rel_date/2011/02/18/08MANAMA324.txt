(SBU) MFA Undersecretary Sheikh Abdulaziz bin Mubarak 
Al-Khalifa confirmed to CDA on May 22 that Bahrain will 
attend the Friends of Iraq conference and that he will be the 
likely head of Bahrain's delegation.  He added that the GOB 
intended to send military representatives, but that he did 
not yet have names.
