¶1. (SBU) This is an action request ) see para 4. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Poloff met with MFA Americas desk officer Magdi 
el-Mofadi on March 1 to discuss the case of Yakub Mohammed 
per reftel.  Poloff asked that the Sudanese Government, 
within 30 days, confirm the nationality of Yakub Mohammed, 
notify any known relatives, and make the relatives aware of 
the pending proceedings and the option of presenting 
information to the Administrative Review Board.  El-Mofadi 
agreed to USG requests. 
 
¶3. (SBU) One question arose from the information provided. 
Reftel lists Yakub Mohamed's place of birth as Omdurman, 
Saudi Arabia.  Since there is also a well known Omdurman, 
Sudan, adjacent to Khartoum.  El-Mofadi asked if the 
information were accurate or if Yakub Mohammed were actually 
born in Omdurman, Sudan. 
 
¶4. (SBU) Action requested:  Post requests the Department 
confirm the place of birth for Yakub Mohammed.
