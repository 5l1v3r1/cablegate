SUBJECT: CANADA'S 60-DAY REPORT TO UN SECURITY COUNCIL ON 
UNSCR 1803 (IRAN SANCTIONS) 
 
REF: STATE 48615 
 
On May 13 Econoff delivered reftel points and a copy of the 
U.S. sixty day report on UNSCR 1803 to Carolyn Knobel, Deputy 
Director, Economic Law Section, Department of Foreign Affairs 
and International Trade.  Knobel confirmed that Canada has 
already submitted their sixty day report.  She said she 
appreciated hearing from the U.S. Government on this issue 
and would circulate the U.S. report and comments to her 
relevant colleagues.
