SUBJECT: SERBIA: DELIVERY OF IRAQ POINTS 
 
REF: STATE 18283 
 
(SBU) The Ambassador shared reftel points on the review of U.S. 
policy in Iraq with MFA Political Director Borislav Stefanovic on 
February 27.  Stefanovic welcomed the information and indicated that 
Serbia would view positively U.S. efforts to draw down troop levels 
in Iraq while maintaining stability.
