SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED TO SUDAN ON U.S. CANDIDACY FOR ICAO 
COUNCIL 
 
REF: SECSTATE 115443 
 
(U) On September 4, PolOff delivered reftel demarche to the Director 
General of the Sudanese Civil Aviation Authority, Abu Bakr Gaafar 
Ahmed, requesting Sudanese support for the U.S. re-election to the 
International Civil Aviation Organization (ICAO) in membership 
category Part I.  Ahmed stated that he would consider this request. 
He added that he will be representing Sudan in Montreal at the 
upcoming ICAO Assembly.
