SUBJECT: T&T REJECTS U.S. POSITION ON EMERGENCY SPECIAL SESSION

REF: STATE 188244 
 
¶1.  Polchief delivered Reftel talking points and accompanying 
resolution, on November 17, to MFA Multilateral Division Director 
Susan Gordon. 
 
¶2.  Gordon told Polchief that T&T would neither oppose this 
resolution nor abstain on it.  She reminded Polchief that 10 out of 
15 members of the Security Council had voted in favor of a similar 
resolution, and said it was her understanding that France and 
Greece, among other countries, are supportive of the resolution.
