FOR "KURDISTAN WORKERS PARTY" ("PARTIYA KERKERAN 
KURDISTAN" IN KURDISH), NOT/NOT "KURDISH WORKERS 
PARTY."  THE DIFFERENCE IS MORE THAN SYNTACTICAL.
