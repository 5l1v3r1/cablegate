SUBJECT: NAMES OF INFLUENTIAL WOMEN - FRANCE 
 
REF: STATE 178055 
 
¶1. SUMMARY: In response reftel, Mission France identified ten (10) 
influential women in politics, business, civil society and media. 
END SUMMARY.
