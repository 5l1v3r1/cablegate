SUBJECT: ECUADOR'S PARTICIPATION IN MULTILATERAL MEETINGS 
AT TRINIDAD AND TOBAGO 
 
REF: A. STATE 36854 
     ¶B. EMBASSY QUITO POLOFF PANNELL APRIL 13 EMAIL 
 
¶1. (SBU) MFA Multilateral Affairs' Alternate Coordinator of 
Summits Marjorie Ulloa confirmed on April 15 that Foreign 
Minister Fander Falconi will attend the April 18 UNASUR 
meeting as the "Plus One" with President Rafael Correa (Ref 
A). 
 
¶2. (SBU) Ulloa also noted that Coordinating Minister of 
Social Development Nathalie Cely would join the GOE's 
delegation to the Summit of the Americas.  As per Ref B, the 
GOE's delegation already includes President Rafael Correa, 
Foreign Minister Fander Falconi, MFA Under Secretary of 
Multilateral Affairs Carlos Jativa and Ecuador's Ambassador 
to the OAS Efren Cocios.
