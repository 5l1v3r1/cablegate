SUBJECT: CHINA OFFERS TRADE ON U.S. CANDIDATE FOR 
RE-ELECTION TO THE UNITED NATIONS COMMITTEE AGAINST TORTURE 
 
REF: A. STATE 108617 
 
     ¶B. BEIJING 5453 
 
¶1. (U) Sensitive but unclassified; please protect accordingly. 
 
¶2. (SBU) On August 22, China provided an official reply to 
ref (a) request for China's support for Ms. Felice Gaer's 
candidacy for re-election to the United Nations Committee 
Against Torture.  China offered to support Ms. Gaer's 
candidacy in return for U.S. support for China's candidate to 
the UN Committee on the Elimination of Racial Discrimination. 
 Poloff later clarified with Ms. Lin Wenhua of the MFA's 
Department of International Organizations and Conferences 
Human Rights Division that the Chinese candidate is Huang 
Yongan.  China has previously requested support for Huang's 
candidacy in New York and Washington. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Please advise on how to reply to China's offer.
