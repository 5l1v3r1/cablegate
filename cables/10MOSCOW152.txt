SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: GUIDANCE REGARDING HAITI AIR 
TRAFFIC 
 
REF: STATE 004807 
 
 Per reftel, on January 19 Post delivered a demarche to 
Nikolai Smirnov of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs' North 
America Department informing GOR that the U.S. Government has 
established temporary air traffic flow management procedures 
for flights into Port-au-Prince-Mais Gate Airport, Haiti. 
Post also contacted several MFA officials who acknowledged 
receiving the demarche and stated that they have no further 
questions or comments at this point.  Post will continue to 
maintain contact with MFA on this issue.
