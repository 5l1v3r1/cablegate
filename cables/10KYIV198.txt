SUBJECT: UKRAINE: RESPONSE TO CITES COP-15 DEMARCHE 
 
REF: STATE 6668 
 
SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED 
 
¶1. (SBU) Post delivered reftel points to Volodymyr Domashlinets, 
Ukraine's CITES representative at the Ministry of Environmental 
Protection, on February 1.  Domashlinets said Ukraine will support 
the U.S. proposals on sharks, corals, polar bears, and a snake 
trade.  The Ministry of Environmental Protection awaits an opinion 
of the Institute of Zoology of the National Academy of Sciences to 
determine whether to support the U.S. bobcat proposal. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Domashlinets (domashlinets@menr.gov.ua) said he will 
represent Ukraine's Ministry of Environmental Protection at the 
March 13-25 CITES COP-15.  The participation of a representative 
from Ukraine's State Committee for Fisheries is still under 
consideration.
