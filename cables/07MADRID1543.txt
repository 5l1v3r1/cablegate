¶1. (SBU)  This is an interim report of Spanish high-level 
attendance at the 62nd UN General Assembly: 
 
¶A. Attendance of the Head of State 
 
-- His Majesty King Juan Carlos will not attend. 
 
¶B. Attendance of the Head of Government 
 
-- Jose Luis Rodriguez Zapatero, President, will attend. 
 
-- Arrival date: September 23, 2007; departure date: 
September 25, 2007.  President Zapatero will not be present 
for the General Debate. 
 
-- Mrs. Zapatero has not yet decided if she will attend. 
 
-- Interpretation into Spanish would be required in any 
bilateral meeting. 
 
¶C. Attendance of the Foreign Minister 
 
-- Mr. Miguel Angel Moratinos, Minister of Foreign Affairs 
and Cooperation 
 
-- Arrival date: September 23, 2007; departure date: 
September 29, 2007.  Foreign Minister Moratinos will be 
present for the General Debate. 
 
-- No information available on travel of spouse. 
 
-- No interpretation required for meetings in English.
