SUBJECT: GOL NOT LIKELY TO MAKE STATEMENTS ABOUT IRAN'S 
COOPERATION WITH THE IAEA 
 
REF: SECSTATE 100153 
 
(SBU) Lebanon is not likely to make any public statements 
urging Iran to cooperate with the International Atomic Energy 
Agency (ref), MFA Americas Desk Officer Majdi Ramadan told 
poloff on September 28.  Ramadan clarified, however, that the 
GOL does not support the existence of nuclear weapons in Iran 
or in other countries in the Middle East region.  He said he 
would convey reftel's points about the newly publicized 
covert uranium enrichment facility in Iran to Foreign 
Minister Fawzi Salloukh upon his return to Beirut from the UN 
General Assembly later the same day.
