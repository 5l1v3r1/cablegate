(SBU) Pol/Econ chief October 19 delivered reftel demarche 
concerning the UNGA Holocaust Remembrance resolution to MFA 
Director for International Organization Affairs Ghassan 
Shaikho.  Shaikho affirmed that Bahrain stands firmly against 
genocide of any kind, be it directed against Jews or anyone 
else.  He said the Holocaust genocide was a humanitarian 
issue that should not be politicized.  The Bahraini mission 
in New York was in contact with GCC, Arab, and Islamic 
delegations at the UN on developing a joint position.  He 
promised to pass the demarche along with an update on the 
state of play in New York to the Foreign Minister.
