SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED; PLEASE PROTECT ACCORDINGLY 
 
¶1. (SBU) Emboffs shared reftel points concerning the upcoming 
Iran-hosted nuclear conference with the Dutch Foreign 
Ministry's Middle East desk and the Non-proliferation 
Division Head as well as with Taco Westerhuis, Coordinator 
for Middle East and Energy Affairs, Ministry of Economic 
Affairs (MEA).  (Note: MEA has lead responsibility for energy 
issues in the Dutch government.  End note.) 
 
¶2. (SBU) Westerhuis told Econoff October 27 "there wasn't 
much enthusiasm" at MEA about the conference.  MEA Trade 
Policy Department Deputy Director, Rene van Hell, followed up 
October 28 to emphasize the Dutch government "won't attend" 
the conference.
