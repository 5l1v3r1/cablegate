SUBJ:  Amman distributes payments of temporary transition 
allowance stipend to former Embassy Baghdad staff 
 
Ref: STATE 13612 
 
¶1. Per reftel, on January 29 Department approved a public interest 
determination authorizing a temporary transition allowance to former 
Embassy Baghdad local staff awaiting refugee processing in Jordan. 
The first of those payments was disbursed by Embassy Amman on 
February 7. 
 
¶2. In coordination with Embassy Baghdad, PRM, and the International 
Organization for Migration, Embassy Amman's Iraq Support Unit 
located and contacted Baghdad staff in Amman undergoing refugee 
processing once Embassy Baghdad determined who was eligible for the 
$840 stipend.  Less than twenty four hours after the first phone 
call was made, the first payment was disbursed by Amman's Management 
Section and distributed by the Iraq Support Unit on February 7. 
Post expects to complete the distribution on February 10. 
 
¶3. Subsequent payments will be disbursed by Embassy Amman at the end 
of each month.  The next payment will be March 31 to those former 
Baghdad LES determined eligible for the stipend by Embassy Baghdad.
