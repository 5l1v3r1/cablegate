(SBU) On January 12, Deputy Economic Counselor shared reftel 
points regarding the designation of additional Iranian 
entities under E.O. 13382 with Magnus Danielsson, Principal 
Administrative Officer, the Inspectorate for Strategic 
Products (ISP); Mira Naumanen, Coordinator, Anti-Money 
Laundering, the Swedish Financial Authority (SFA); and Sofie 
Hilbom, Desk Officer, Department for Security Policy Policy, 
Counter Terrorism Unit at the Swedish MFA.  Naumanen 
commented that SFA continues to inform the Swedish market of 
all EU regulations regarding the freezing of assets via a 
variety of trade associations.  Swedish legislation does not 
permit assets to be frozen unless directed by the UN and/or 
the EU.  Post will report additional information septel.
