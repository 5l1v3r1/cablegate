SUBJECT: GOB TO RESPECT DESIGNATION OF ABU HAQ AKA 
MAIMAITIMING MAIMAITI 
 
REF: STATE 48524 
 
 ¶1. (SBU) PolOff met with the Director of the Department of 
Multilateral Affairs in the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
International Cooperation Mmamosadinyana Punkie Josephine 
Molefe May 22. PolOff discussed the UN Security Council 1267 
Sanctions Committee approval to add Pakistan-based Chinese 
citizen Abdul Haq, aka Maimaitimung Maimaiti to its 
consolidated list of entities associated with al-Qaeda, Usama 
bin Ladin, or the Taliban.  Further, PolOff informed the 
Director that the United States designated this individual 
domestically on April 20, and urged the GOB to take action in 
accordance with its obligations under relevant UN Security 
Council Resolutions. 
 
¶2. (SBU) The Director said that she will ensure that all 
interested parties are informed and that Botswana will 
respect the designation of this individual as Botswana 
considers its obligations under international agreements very 
important.
