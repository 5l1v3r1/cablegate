¶4.  U.S. Embassy Phnom Penh has reviewed its files and finds that, 
as of this date, it possesses no credible information of gross 
violations of human rights by the above-mentioned individuals. 
 
¶5.  The individuals listed above will attend ILEA Clandestine Lab 
Investigations/Chemical Control Course scheduled for January 12 - 
January 23, 2009 with report date of January 11, 2009 in Bangkok, 
Thailand.  These courses will provide the participants with an 
understanding on current approaches to dealing with precursor 
chemicals and clandestine laboratories.  The courses will provide 
concepts to improve investigative effectiveness and detailed 
instruction regarding control strategies used in Southeast Asia and 
in other parts of the world. 
 
¶6.  Please verify whether the Department possesses any credible 
information that the individuals named above have been implicated in 
gross violations of human rights.  Post requests a Leahy vetting 
response from the Department by November 28, 2008.
