SUBJECT: FRENCH PRESIDENT ISSUES CONGRATULATORY MESSAGE TO 
PRESIDENT OBAMA 
 
 ¶1.  On January 20 at 18:19 CET (12:19 EST), the official 
website of the President of the French Republic Nicolas 
Sarkozy included a congratulatory letter to President Obama. 
 Embassy Paris provides the following unofficial translation. 
 
 
¶2.  Begin text. 
 
Mister President, 
 
With your election, the American people expressed with vigor 
its faith in progress and the future, as well as its wish for 
the open, new, strong and united America that you embody. 
 
At the time you take office, please accept my best wishes, 
both personally and on behalf of the French people, for 
success in your mission at the head of the American nation. 
 
I also want to assure you that France is determined to work 
hard in hand with America, its friend and ally, so that we 
can resolve together the immense challenges that confront our 
world today. 
 
Nicolas Sarkozy 
 
End text.
