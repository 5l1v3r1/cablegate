SUBJECT: CANADA REITERATES DESIRE FOR G7 COORDINATION ON 
IRAN SANCTIONS 
 
REF: A. STATE 121356 
     ¶B. STATE 115523 
     ¶C. OTTAWA 1421 
 
(SBU) On November 17, Robert Sample, Senior Project Leader, 
Financial Crimes Division, Finance Canada reiterated the 
GOC's strong desire to ensure G7 countries coordinate 
financial sanctions against Iran.  Sample reported that he 
and his colleagues are aware of the November 6 Treasury 
statement highlighted in ref A.  As reported in ref C, Canada 
still looks forward to receiving more information about the 
USG proposal for a coordinated G7 statement on Iran sanctions.
