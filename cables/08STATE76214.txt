¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Embassy's assistance in 
conducting a post-shipment check on license 05-050090697 for 
the export of 7-45555-5 Landing Gear Lever to Brazil. 
Embassy is requested to complete this Blue Lantern check 
within 45 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern check 
will affect pending and future licenses involving parties to 
this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request:  Questionable routing to Brazil via 
Israel and unfamiliar foreign intermediate consignee, 
International Transportation LTD. 
 
¶4. ACTION Brasilia: Embassy Brasilia is requested to make 
inquiries with appropriate officials to determine if the 
Brazilian Air Force, Ministerio da Defesa do Brasil, Commando 
da Aeronautica, Sub Departmento de Desenvolvimento PGM Deped 
has received the 7-45555-5 Landing Gear Lever and that the 
articles are being used for the purpose stated on the 
license. 
 
End action Brasilia. 
 
ACTION Tel Aviv: Department requests that Embassy Tel Aviv 
conduct a site visit to Intl Transportation LTD in order to 
assess its business operations, on-site security, and general 
reliability as a recipient of United States Munitions List 
(USML) items.  The following questions are offered as 
suggested guidance with Intl Transportation LTD: 
 
- Can the company provide documentation from the Brazilian 
Air Force? (e.g. purchase order, contract, or letter of 
intent) firmly establishing it as the end user? 
 
- What is the company's exact role in this transaction? 
 
- Who are the company's main clients? 
 
- Does the company keep proper, detailed records of its sales 
and customers? 
 
If a site visit is not possible, please inform PM/DTCC case 
officer. 
 
End action Tel Aviv.
