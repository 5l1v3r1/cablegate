¶1.  Embassy received Diplomatic Note No. 202/417/05 dated 
January 11, 2006 from the Cook Islands High Commission 
granting approval for the R/V Roger Revelle to conduct 
scientific research in Cook Islands waters.  The text of the 
diplomatic note follows. 
 
Begin Text: 
 
The Cook Islands High Commission presents its compliments to 
the Embassy of the United States of America and has the 
honour to advise that clearance has been granted for the US 
flagged marine vessel 'Roger Revelle' to conduct marine 
scientific research in Cook Islands waters from 20 January 
to 03 March 2006. 
 
The Cook Islands High Commission avails itself of this 
opportunity to renew to the Embassy of the United States of 
America the assurances of its highest consideration. 
 
Cook Islands High Commission. 
WELLINGTON 
11 January 2006 
 
End Text. 
 
¶2.  Embassy will fax a copy of the Dip Note to OES/OA, and 
will send the original via pouch under registration number 
¶9238731.
