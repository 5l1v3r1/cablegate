SUBJECT: IAEA DONOR CONFERENCE ON DECOMISSIONING OF IRAQ'S 
FORMER NUCLEAR COMPLEX - FRENCH REPLY 
 
REF: SECSTATE 10647 
 
(SBU) In response to reftel demarche request re promoting 
French participation in the upcoming IAEA conference on 
decommissioning Iraq's former nuclear complex, ESTH Counselor 
contacted Hugues de Longevialle, Deputy Director of the 
Subdirectorate for Disarmament and Nuclear Non-Proliferation, 
as well as officials at CEA (Atomic Energy Commission) and 
ANDRA (National Agency for the Management of Radioactive 
Waste).  De Longevialle responded that the French government 
planned for Mr. Yann Poincignon, the Ministry's liaison with 
CEA, to participate in the conference.  Additionally, he 
recalled that in July 2005, following the French-hosted 
conference in Bordeaux on radioactive sources, the GOF had 
"made preliminary contact" with Dr. Mousa Al Atia, President 
of the Iraqi authority with control over radioactive sources.
