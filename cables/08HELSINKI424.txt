¶2. (U) Government of Finland (GoF) briefed on September 11 
that President Halonen's program will include development 
issues, Millenium Development Goals (including side events), 
chairmanship of a panel on environmental sustainability, 
attending a high level meeting on Africa and bilaterals with 
other heads of state.  GoF also briefed that Foreign Minister 
Stubb will attend a number of bilaterals and will focus his 
efforts in his capacity as OSCE Chairman-in-Office addressing 
the Security Council in that role.
