SUBJECT: MOROCCO NOTES OFFICIAL STAND UP OF U.S. AFRICA 
COMMAND 
 
REF: STATE 104550 
 
¶1. (SBU)  On October 3, 2008, Deputy Polcouns informed 
Moroccan Ministry of Foreign Affairs International Security 
Chief Karim Halim of the stand up of the U.S. Africa Command 
per reftel.  Halim had no substantive comment but thanked 
D/Polcouns for the notification and said that he would 
forward the information to appropriate superiors within the 
Government of Morocco (GOM). 
 
¶2. (SBU) In addition, on October 7,2008, Defense Attache 
informed Moroccan Royal Armed Forces Division of Exterior 
Relations Deputy Chief Mohammed Anesrif of the stand up of 
the U.S. Africa Command.  Anesrif asked if there would be any 
formal diplomatic written notification to follow.  DATT 
informed him that the exact same text had been delivered  to 
the Ministry of Foreign Affairs on 3 October.  Anesrif 
thanked DATT for the notification and said that he would 
forward the information to the appropriate superiors within 
the Royal Armed Forces. 
 
¶3. (SBU)  Mission has received no additional GOM comments 
since.  Any future comments will be forwarded as received.
