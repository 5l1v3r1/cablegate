SUBJECT: KUWAIT WELCOMES U.S. STATEMENT ON IRAN 
 
REF: (A) KUWAIT 2028 (B) SECSTATE 87682 
 
¶1. (SBU) MFA U/S Jarallah called the Ambassador the evening 
of May 31 to say that the Amir and Crown Prince had been 
briefed on our statement on Iran and welcomed the U.S. move. 
An official statement had been released to the media. 
 
¶2. Kuwait News Agency is carrying the following official 
reaction in addition to the full text of the Secretary's 
announcement: 
 
begin text 
 
Kuwait has welcomed on Wednesday the positive move announced 
by the U.S. on joining EU-3 negotiations with Iran. 
 
The Foreign Ministry said in a statement released here today 
that the State of Kuwait welcomes the announcement made by 
the United States on joining the EU regarding the 
negotiations with the aim of reaching a diplomatic solution 
to Iran's nuclear file if Iran announces the suspension of 
its uranium enrichment process. 
 
It added, springing from keenness on realizing regional 
security and stability, the State of Kuwait hopes that 
neighboring Iran would respond to this move that would end up 
in positive outcomes. 
 
end text
