¶1. (SBU) Emboff delivered Ref A talking points April 2 to Tuy 
Ri, Director, Department of International Organizations, 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and International Cooperation. 
In previous conversations regarding Kosovo's independence 
declaration, Royal Government of Cambodia (RGC) officials 
have described Kosovo's declaration as a sensitive issue (Ref 
B).  Reflecting this position, Mr. Tuy stated that the RGC 
has no plans to file a brief (either supportive of Serbia or 
Kosovo) in the case.
