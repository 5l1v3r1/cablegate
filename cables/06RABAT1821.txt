Embassy Rabat is pleased to nominate Ilham Bennani, Director 
of Industrial Design at the Moroccan Office of Industrial and 
Commercial Protection (OMPIC) and Benali Harmouch, Director 
of the Patent Department of OMPIC for the U.S. Patent 
Office's (USPTO) Global Intellectual Property Academy program 
on Industrial Design to be held November 27 to December 1 in 
Alexandria, VA.  Both candidates request USG funding to cover 
the cost of travel, per diem and lodging.  Post will contact 
USPTO directly regarding supplemental funding.
