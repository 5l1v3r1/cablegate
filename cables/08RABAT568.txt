SUBJECT: MOROCCO CONSIDERS PRESSING IRAN ON P5+1 PACKAGE 
 
REF: STATE 063960 
 
(SBU) On June 13, 2008, PolOffs asked Moroccan MFA officials 
for the Government of Morocco's assistance in persuading Iran 
to accept the updated Ref P5 1 incentive package in exchange 
for Iranian compliance with its IAEA obligations.  General 
Assembly and Security Council Service Head Amine Chabi said 
he would pass the request to his superiors at MFA and it 
would be given the highest level of consideration.
