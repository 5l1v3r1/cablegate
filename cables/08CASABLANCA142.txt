SUBJECT:  VISAS VIPER FOR JUNE 2008 REPORTING PERIOD 
 
¶1. Members of the Embassy Rabat and Consulate General Casablanca 
joint Visas Viper Committee met July 7, 2008.  The meeting was 
attended by ACG, RAO, LEGATT, CONS, DAO, RSO, POL, ECON and PD. 
 
¶2. Discussion centered on the recent arrests of 35 individuals 
suspected of belonging to a network that recruits volunteers on 
behalf of Al Qaeda in Iraq and Algeria. RAO is working on collecting 
the names, dates and places of birth of the individuals, but had 
nothing to report at this time. 
 
¶3. The Mission's Acting Visas Viper Coordinator was Deputy Consular 
Chief Kathleen Hanako Doherty.
