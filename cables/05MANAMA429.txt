SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  WOLFOWITZ NOMINATION FOR WORLD BANK 
 
REF: STATE 49831 
 
(SBU) Emboffs March 19 delivered reftel demarche concerning 
the nomination of Deputy Secretary of Defense Wolfowitz as 
World Bank president to Ministry of Industry and Commerce 
Assistant Undersecretary of Foreign Trade Shaikh Daij Al 
Khalifa; Ministry of Foreign Affairs Director of Bilateral 
Relations Dr. Dhafer Al Umran; and Ministry of Finance 
Director of Economic Planning Yousif Humood.  Al Umran told 
PolEcon chief that he would pass the information to the 
international organization affairs office for consideration, 
but stated that Bahrain tried to support the United States 
whenever it could.  He said he would get back to us with a 
more formal response.  Shaikh Daij and Humood were aware of 
the nomination but offered no substantive response.
