SUBJECT: IOM INVITATION TO MIGRATION AND MICROECONOMIC 
CONFERENCE IN MADRID 
 
¶1. (U) The International Organization for Migration 
representational office in Spain has sent an invitation 
informing post of the opportunity for two USG representatives 
to attend the second "International Experts Meeting on 
Microeconomics and Migration" (RIEMM) which is to take place 
March 27, 2009, in Madrid.  Post has emailed to EUR/WE 
(Stacie Zerdecki) a copy of the invitation, which contains 
additional information on the event.  Post would be pleased 
to submit names of those interested in participating in this 
one-day conference.  Otherwise, post will plan to send 
emboff/s to represent the USG.  The registration deadline is 
February 15. 
 
¶2. (U) POC at Post is Lourdes Lamela, 011 34 91-587-2399, 
LamelaLM@state.gov.
