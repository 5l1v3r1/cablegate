SUBJECT: TURKEY'S PARTICIPATION IN SEVENTH ANNUAL 
INTERNATIONAL EXPORT CONTROL CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 132303 
 
 ¶1. Post received a diplomatic note in reply to the 
invitation by the Nonproliferation Bureau to attend the 
Seventh Annual International Export Control Conference.  This 
note states that the GOT will send two representatives to 
participate in the Conference in Stockholm, Sweden from 
September 20-22, 2005. 
 
¶2.  The Turkish representatives will be: 
 
¶A.  Ali Riza Oktay; Head of Department, General Directorate 
for Export, Undersecretariat of Foreign Trade; 
 
¶B.  Aynur Ciray; Customs Specialist, Undersecretariat of 
Customs. 
 
This information will also be passed via email to conference 
coordinators with Los Alamos Technical Associates. 
 
¶3.  Point of contact at Post is EXBS Advisor, Kevin Lyon, 
tel. 90-312-455-5555, ext 2525, unclas email lyonk@state.gov.
