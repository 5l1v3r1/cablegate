SUBJECT: Demarche delivered in Kinshasa: CEEAC summit Kinshasa 
 
REF: State 22840 
 
¶1. PolOff delivered reftel demarche March 11 to Minister Alice 
Kimpembe of the Office of International Organizations at the 
Congolese Foreign Ministry. 
 
¶2. Kimpembe stated that while she was not directly involved in the 
preparations for CEEAC (Economic Community of Central African 
States) summit, U.S. and international support of peace initiatives 
is always welcome.  She said she did not have access to CEEAC plans 
related to Chad, Sudan, or issues of regional security, but would 
request the MFA forward a copy of the CEEAC summit conclusions to 
us.
