Weather Service officials Edward H. Young, Jr. and James 
¶C. Weyman to travel to Niue, April 11-18, 2005.  The 
purpose of travel is to attend the Pacific Islands Global 
Climate Observing System meeting and the Regional 
Meteorological Services Directors Meeting.

assistance required.
