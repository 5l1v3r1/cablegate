FINANCE: USG CONCERN OVER ILLICIT CASH COURIERS 
 
REF: STATE 16120 
 
¶1.  DCM raised the problem of illicit cash courier activity 
during a meeting with Financial Secretary Colin Bullock of 
the Ministry of Finance and Planning on February 15, and 
provided background information paragraphs seven through 
thirteen of Reftel. 
 
¶2.  Bullock expressed thanks, assured us the Government of 
Jamaica also took the danger of such transfers seriously, and 
said he would convey the information to appropriate parties 
in the Ministry and at the Bank of Jamaica.
