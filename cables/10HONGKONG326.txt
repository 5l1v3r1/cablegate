SUBJECT: HONG KONG AND MACAU NOTIFIED: FEBRUARY 10 
DESIGNATION OF IRAN-RELATED ENTITIES UNDER E.O. 13382 
 
REF: STATE 15554 
 
¶1. (SBU) On February 24, Post notified appropriate government 
officials in Hong Kong and Macau of the February 10 
designation under Executive Order (E.O.) 13382 of an 
individual and four companies affiliated with Iran's 
Revolutionary Guard (IRGC), per reftel. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Banking regulators from Hong Kong and Macau welcomed 
the notification and stated they required locally licensed 
banks to monitor the US Department of Treasury's website for 
US financial sanctions list updates.  Regulator guidelines 
also mandate the reporting of identified suspicious 
transactions and/or business relationships to respective 
financial intelligence units.
