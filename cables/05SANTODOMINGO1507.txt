SUBJECT: DOMINICAN REPUBLIC LISTENS ON UNCHR 61 AND DRAFT 
CUBA RESOLUTION 
 
REF: A. A. STATE 42847 
 
     ¶B. B. STATE 44494 
 
¶1. (SBU) Poloffs delivered reftel A and B talking points and 
draft Cuba resolution to MFA Ambassador for UN/OAS Affairs 
Michelle Cohen and Counselor Johanna Calderon on March 15. 
Cohen said that the Dominican Republic "has not yet arrived 
at a position on these issues."  According to Cohen, the 
Dominican Republic's current priority is to decide what 
position it will take on issues in reftel and others, and 
Cohen indicated that she would advise Embassy when a decision 
is reached.
