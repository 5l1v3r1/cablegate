SUBJECT: NUCLEAR NONPROLIFERATION TREATY (NPT) NON-PAPER 
ON TECHNICAL WORKSHOP FOR EGYPT AND ISRAEL 
 
REF: STATE 103725 
 
¶1.  Reftel requested that a non-paper be given to appropriate 
government officials in Cairo and Jerusalem providing further 
details on a U.S.-proposed technical workshop for Egyptian 
and Israeli participation on nonproliferation matters, and 
that a reply be requested by October 17.  Israel has agreed 
to participate in the proposed workshop.  Embassy Cairo is 
requested to remind appropriate host government officials of 
the proposed workshop and to request a reply regarding 
Egypt's willingness to take part. 
 
¶2.  POC is Scott Davis of ISN/MNSA, available at 
davisms@state.gov or by phone at 202-647-1141.
