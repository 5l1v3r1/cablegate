SUBJECT: EMBASSY VALLETTA MARKS DAY OF SOLIDATIRY WITH 
CUBAN PEOPLE 
 
REF: STATE 51536 
 
Embassy Valletta marked the Day of Solidarity with the Cuban 
People with an editorial by Ambassdor Bordonaro.  The 
editorial ran today, May 21, in the independent, 
English-language daily The Times, Malta's largest newspaper 
(circulation 96,000).  Post is placing the text of the 
editorial on the Embassy website, which also features a 
prominent link to www.solidaridadcuba.org.
