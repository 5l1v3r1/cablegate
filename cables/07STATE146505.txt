¶1. (U) This is an Action Request. See paragraphs 2 - 4. 
 
¶2. (U) The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a post-shipment check on license 05-1003329 for the export of 
sonar equipment to Turkey. Post is requested to complete this 
Blue Lantern check within 45 days. Lack of response to a Blue 
Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. (U) Reason for Request: Purchase of United States 
Munitions List (USML)-controlled military electronics by 
Turkish civilian end-user, Institute of Marine Sciences at 
the Middle East Technical University. Institute has never 
been subject to a Blue Lantern before as this is its first 
license. 
 
¶4. (U) Action Request: Post should make appropriate inquiries 
to determine if the end-user, Institute of Marine Sciences at 
the Middle East Technical University, received the 
USML-controlled articles listed below and that the items are 
being used for the purpose stated in the approved license. In 
its reply, Post may also comment on any other information 
pertinent to this transaction such as security of the 
facility, concerns about the Institute, and the end-user,s 
understanding of restrictions on re-export or retransfer of 
USML items.
