SUBJECT: ANDORRA SUPPORTS US CANDIDATE FOR UN ANTI-DISCRIMINATION 
COMMITTEE 
 
REF: STATE 157598 
 
¶1. (U) On September 19, 2007, post sent a dip note to Andorra's 
FM seeking its support for the US candidate, Pierre-Richard 
Prosper, for the United Nations Committee on the Elimination of 
Racial Discrimination (CERD). 
 
¶2. (U) On January 17, 2008, the Government of Andorra informed 
us that Andorra would support the US candidate.
