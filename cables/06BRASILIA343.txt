SUBJECT: ICAO: MISSION RAISES VOSS CANDICACY WITH U/S  AT MFA 
 
¶1.  (U) On February 13, per instructions from Washington, Charge 
d'Affaires  delivered talking points, the biography and the vision 
paper of ICAO Secretary General Candidate Bill Voss to Brazilian U/S 
for Latin American Affairs Jose Eduardo Martins Felicio.  Felicio 
thanked Charge for the information and said he would pass the 
information to the proper authorities within the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs (MFA). 
 
¶2.  (U)  Embassy will continue to monitor this issue and report any 
subsequent response from the MFA.
