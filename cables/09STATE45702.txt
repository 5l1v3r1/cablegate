¶1.  The updated operational guidance for the Overseas 
Seasonal Hire Program will be issued via PERNET and soon 
thereafter, published on the DOS HR/OE intranet website. 
 
¶2.  Operational guidance for the OverseasSeasonal Hire 
Program (OSHP) will no longer be issued as a yearly 
publication.  In the event that future program criteria 
or guidance requires that information be updated, such 
changes will be announced via ALDAC, posted on our 
website, and published in our Omnibus newsletter. 
Please reference the current cable number and retain 
this operational guidance as a general notice in 
administering your post's OSHP. 
 
¶3.  The third and final phase of the minimum wage 
increase to $7.25/hr will be effective on July 24, 2009. 
This increase will affect OSHP hires at the FP-EE Level. 
Refer to the OSHP advisory guidance, Compensation, Item 
B, for further information. 
 
¶4.  In the event of limited budgets, posts are reminded 
that gratuitous service remains an option permitted only 
under the auspices of the OSHP.  Refer to the OSHP 
advisory guidance, Hiring Methods, paragraph 4 for more 
details.  Also, contact your regional bureaus for 
further administrative guidance. 
 
¶5.  Should you have any questions concerning the OSHP, 
please contact your International HR Manager in 
HR/OE/HRM. 
 
¶6.  MINIMIZE CONSIDERED.
