SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON IMPLEMENTING UNSCR 1747 
 
REF: STATE 67462 
 
(SBU) On May 22, Poloff met with the Ministry of Foreign 
Affair's director of international organizations and 
delivered reftel demarche.  The director responded that the 
GOB was still drafting its report on the GOB's implementation 
of UNSCR 1747.  He explained that MFA is working in 
conjunction with the Ministry of Defense on the report and 
that the GOB hoped to meet the May 24 deadline for delivery.
