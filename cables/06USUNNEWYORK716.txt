SUBJECT: ECUADOR:  HUMAN RIGHTS COUNCIL CANDIDACY 
 
 
 ¶1.  Mission received a note from the Permanent Mission of 
Ecuador to the United Nations requesting USG support for its 
candidature to the Human Rights Council.  The elections will 
be held in New York in May 2006. 
 
¶2.  Mission will respond that careful consideration will be 
given by the United States to the candidature of Ecuador.
