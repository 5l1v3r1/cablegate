¶2.   INFORMATION RECEIVED AT THIS POST INDICATES THAT 
  AS OF LATE JULY 2004, THE SEVENTH BODY FOUND IN THE 
  APARTMENT IN LEGANES, A SUBURB IN THE OUSKIRTS OF 
  MADRID, SPAIN, WHERE SIX OTHER 11 MARCH 2004 BOMBING 
  SUSPECTS COMMITTED SUICIDE ON 3 APRIL 2004, REMAINED 
  UNIDENTIFIED.  SPANISH AUTHORITIES WERE CONSIDERING THE 
  POSSIBILITY THAT THE BODY MIGHT BE THAT OF ALGERIAN 
  NATIONAL ALLEKEMA LAMARI, DPOB: JULY 10, 1965, ALGERIA. 
  ALTHOUGH SOME OFFICIALS WERE DUBIOUS, LAMARI WAS 
  ARRESTED BY THE SPANISH NATIONAL POLICE IN APRIL 1997 
  AND SERVED A FIVE-YEAR JAIL TERM FOR MEMBERSHIP IN THE 
  ALGERIAN ARMED ISLAMIC GROUP IN SPAIN, AS WELL AS FOR 
  ILLEGAL POSSESSION OF WEAPONS AND POSSESSION OF 
  MATERIAL AND EQUIPMENT USED TO FALSIFY DOCUMENTS.
