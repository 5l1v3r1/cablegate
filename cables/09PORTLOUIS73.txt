SUBJECT: DURBAN REVIEW CONFERENCE: POINTS DELIVERED TO SEYCHELLES

(SBU) On February 24, Acting Poloff delivered reftel talking points 
regarding the Durban Review Conference and the meeting of the 
Intergovernmental Working Group in Geneva on February 16-19 to U.S. 
Desk Officer Claudette Harrison at the Seychelles Ministry of 
Foreign Affairs.  Harrison was non-committal.  Post notes that 
Seychelles often does not vote at UN events and has been 
unresponsive in the past regarding UN issues.
