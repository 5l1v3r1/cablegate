SUBJECT: FIRST H1N1 FLU CASE CONFIRMED IN EGYPT 
 
Sensitive but Unclassified. Please handle accordingly. (SBU): The Ministry of Health (MOH)reported yesterday, June 2, the first H1N1 flu case in Egypt. A 12-year old Egyptian-American girl, who arrived on a May 31 direct flight from New York City with her mother, was stopped at the airport by MOH authorities who discovered she had a high fever. She was transferred to Cairo's Abbassia Hospital, where she remains in quarantine. Central Public Health Laboratory tests later confirmed the girl has the H1N1 virus. The girl is reported to be in good condition. Scobey
