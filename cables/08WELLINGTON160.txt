SUBJECT: NEW ZEALAND SUPPORTS AUSTRALIA FOR WIPO DIRECTOR GENERAL 
 
REFTEL:  STATE 47560 
 
Econoff contacted the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade 
regarding their support for the WIPO Director General election.  New 
Zealand responded that they are supporting the Australian candidate, 
Dr Francis Gurry, for Director General of WIPO.  Japan is also very 
highly ranked in their list of preferred candidates.
