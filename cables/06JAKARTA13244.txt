SUBJECT: INDONESIA AND US SIGN MULTI-HAZARD MOU 
 
 
¶1.  State Minister of Research and Technology Kusmayanto 
Kadiman and Ambassador B. Lynn Pascoe signed a Memorandum of 
Understanding on "General Cooperation in Science and 
Technology for Natural Hazard Assessment, Analysis, Warning, 
Preparedness and Mitigation" on November 20.  We have 
scanned and e-mailed the full text to OES, L and EAP/MTS, 
and will pouch notarized copies to the Department on 
November 21. 
 
¶2.  We appreciate very much the outstanding support for the 
MOU negotiations, including through the November 18-19 
weekend, by OES, L, EAP, and the National Oceanographic and 
Atmospheric Administration (NOAA).
