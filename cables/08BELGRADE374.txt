SUBJECT: SERBIA:  GOS WILL IMPLEMENT THE UN TRAVEL BAN 
 
REF: STATE 33674 
 
Summary 
 
------- 
 
 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Serbia's Ministry for Foreign Affairs confirmed on 
April 14 that the Government of Serbia will fully implement the 
travel bans on designated Iranian proliferators, as authorized 
by UNSCRs 1737, 1747, and 1803.  End Summary. 
 
 
 
Serbia will Implement Travel Ban 
 
-------------------------------- 
 
 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Serbia will "cooperate and fulfill its obligations" 
under UNSCR 1803 and will implement the travel bans for the 
designated Iranian proliferators, the MFA's Section Head for 
Iran, Vasilije Petkovic told poloff on April 14, in response to 
reftel.  Petkovic said the MFA would cooperate with both the 
Ministry of Justice and the Ministry of Interior to make certain 
that the travel ban was enforced.  He said this was the same 
process Serbia had used to enforce the previous resolutions 
UNSCR 1737 and 1747. 
 
 
 
Comment 
 
------- 
 
 
 
¶3.  (SBU) Petkovic was aware of the new UN Resolution and 
understood the importance for Serbia to fulfill its obligations 
to enact such a travel ban.  Serbia appears willing to fulfill 
its international obligations and to cooperate on issues of 
mutual concern regarding Iran.  End Comment.
