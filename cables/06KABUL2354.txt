SUBJECT: COUNTRY CLEARANCE GRANTED TO U.S. GOVERNORS TRAVEL 
TO NEA, SA, EUR REGIONS 
 
REF: STATE 75442 
 
¶1.  (U) Embassy Kabul welcomes Governor Mitt Romney, 
Massachusets; Governor Matt Blunt, Missouri; Lieutenant 
General H. Steven Blum, US Army; Governor Brian Schweitzer, 
Montana; Anthony Welcher, Director of Intergovernmental 
Affairs, DOS; Dan Wilmot, Director of Intergovernmental 
Affairs, DOD; LTC Kurt Vogel, US Air Force; Captain Ernest 
Barringer, US Air Force; Mr. Eddie Johnson; Ms. Barbara 
Davis; and Major Matt Green, US Marine Corps, and grants 
country clearance for their visit to Afghanistan on May 
25-26, 2006, to visit troops from their home states.  Control 
Officer is Gwen Pascoe, Refugee Cordinator, Political 
Section, email address: PascoeGJ@state.gov, telephone 
011-937-010-8156 or cell number 011-937-997-5567.  All 
travelers should bring USG ID for identification purposes and 
to obtain an Embassy ID. 
 
¶2.  (U) Embassy Kabul will provide transportation, security, 
escorts and lodging. 
 
¶3.  (U) Travelers should note that business attire is 
appropriate for meetings with high level GOA officials.  As 
Afghanistan is a very socially conservative country, female 
travelers are recommended to dress modestly. 
 
¶4.  (U) Post recommends all travelers to review information 
about traveling to Kabul on post's website: 
http://kabul.state.gov.
