    ¶1.  The Turkish Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) 
informed Embassy's Blue Lantern officer that unmanned aerial 
vehicle (UAV) parts under license 05-879186 that were shipped 
from the United States on September 29, 2003 were received 
and checked by reftel recipients.  All but one canceled item 
were accounted for.  The MFA provided an inventory listing 
substantiating UAV parts received and indicated that an order 
for part number UXP31013, for "shocks", fitting a GNAT-750 
model was canceled and the item not supplied. 
 
     ¶2.  Post will fax the list of received/stored items to 
Blue Lantern Coordinator Anna Magoulas.
