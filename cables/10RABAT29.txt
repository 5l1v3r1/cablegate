SUBJECT: TFHA01: MOROCCAN OFFER OF ASSISTANCE FOR HAITI 
 
REF: STATE 004208 
 
On January 13, King Mohammed VI of Morocco offered to send 
two Moroccan Royal Air Force C-130s with 20 metric tons of 
medical supplies worth approximately USD 1 million to Haiti. 
Since then, the Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Cooperation 
and the Inspector General (CHOD) of the Royal Armed Forces, 
General Abdelaziz Bennani, have demarched the Embassy several 
times seeking information on how to coordinate arrival and 
delivery of these supplies.  The Government would like the 
aircraft to be airborne by 18:00 GMT on January 15.  Please 
advise soonest.
