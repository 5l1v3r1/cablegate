SUBJECT: ABUJA APPROVES RESEARCH PROJECT NO. AI069919 01 
 
REF:  STATE 61346 
 
¶1. The U.S. Mission Abuja appreciates the National 
Institutes of Health's (NIH) patience concerning the 
Mission's reply to ref proposed research project. 
 
¶2. The U.S. Mission Abuja has reviewed ref proposed NIH 
Research Project No. AI069919 01 -- to be carried out in 
part in Nigeria.  The Mission agrees to the study with the 
provision that the Principal Investigator (PI) of the 
project first receives written clearance from the Government 
of Nigeria (GON) at the federal level and that the PI shares 
via an NIH cable to the Mission the outcome of his or her 
discussions with the GON.
