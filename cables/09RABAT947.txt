SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: AMPLIFYING THE PRESIDENT'S 
MESSAGE ON AFGHANISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 122234 
 
 (SBU) Polcouns and poloff delivered subject demarche to 
Inspector General for Arab and Islamic Relations Mohammed 
Azeroual on Dec. 1.  Azeroual welcomed the efforts that the 
Obama Administration had put into developing a multi-faceted 
new approach to a very difficult problem.  He noted that U.S. 
partners like Morocco were pleased to see the heavy emphasis 
on tangible development on the ground, which he characterized 
as essential in Afghanistan, especially in the areas of 
education, health, and infrastructure.  He opined that the 
U.S. mission in Afghanistan will be challenging and expressed 
his government's hope that the new strategy would succeed.
