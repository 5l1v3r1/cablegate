SUBJECT: BRAZILIAN RESPONSES TO INVITATIONS TO PARTICIPATE ON PANELS 
AT WIREC 2008 
 
 
¶1.  Post has received the following responses to the invitations to 
Brazilians to participate on a panel or in a plenary session at the 
Washington International Renewable Energy Conference (WIREC 2008): 
 
ACCEPTED:  Jose Sergio Gabrielli de Azevedo, President of Petrobras, 
Brazil's state-owned oil company 
 
ACCEPTED:  Silvio Crestana, President-Director of EMBRAPA, the 
Brazilian Agricultural Research Corporation 
 
REGRETED:  Professor Jose Goldemberg 
 
REGRETED:  Minister of Science and Technology Sergio Rezende 
 
REGRETED:  Minister of Agriculture Reinhold Stephanes 
 
¶3.  Agriculture Minister Stephanes has proposed that Manoel Vicente 
¶F. Bertone, the Secretary of Production and Agroenergy, Ministry of 
Agriculture, replace him at WIREC.  The Embassy supports this 
proposal.
