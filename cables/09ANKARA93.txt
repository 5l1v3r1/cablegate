SUBJECT: Update on PKK Violence (January 8-15, 2009) 
 
REF: ANKARA 41 and previous 
 
(U) Sensitive but unclassified - please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶2. (U) During the January 8-15, 2009 reporting period, no security 
official was killed or injured.  The total number of security force 
casualties for the year through January 15, 2009 was zero killed and 
zero injured. 
 
¶3. (U) During the same period, no PKK terrorist was killed in 
clashes.  Three PKKers were captured in Mersin, Mus, and Van on 
January 12.  According to official TGS website figures, through the 
second week of January, the number of PKK militants killed this year 
was zero, the number of those captured was four, and the number of 
those who surrendered was zero. 
 
¶4. (U) During the reporting period no civilian was killed in 
PKK-linked violence.  The total number of civilian casualties for 
the year through the end of the second week of January was zero 
killed and zero wounded.
