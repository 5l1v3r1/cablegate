¶1. (U) Embassy has received the following 
congratulatory message for President-elect Obama. 
Original copies will be sent via diplomatic pouch 
under Registry No. 4460364. 
 
¶2. (U) Begin text: 
 
To: Mr. Asif J. Chaudhry 
Ambassador of the United States of America 
to the Republic of Moldova 
 
Your Excellency, 
 
On behalf of the Moldovan Research and Development 
Association, I would like to congratulate you and 
all the American people with the election of the 
new President of the United States of America. 
With this victory, America proved once again that 
it stands for democracy and prosperity at home and 
overseas. 
 
We share with you this victory and truly hope that 
this is a big step towards consolidating your 
nation and building up a secure future worldwide. 
 
Sincerely, 
 
/s/ 
Dr. Lidia Romanciuc 
Executive Director 
Moldovan Research and Development Association 
 
End text.
