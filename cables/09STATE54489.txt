¶1. This is an Action Request.  See paragraphs 2-4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests AIT Taipei's assistance in 
conducting a post-shipment check on license number 050096839 
for the export of defense hardware to Taiwan.  It is 
requested that you complete this Blue Lantern inquiry within 
45 days.  Lack of response to a Blue Lantern inquiry will 
affect pending and future licenses involving parties to this 
license. 
 
¶3. Reason for request: To confirm bona fides of Foreign 
Consignee Gon Cheng Precision Instruments Co. Ltd. in this 
transaction. Prior unfavorable Blue Lantern results. 
 
¶4. Action AIT Taipei: Department requests that AIT Taipei 
make inquiries to verify Gon Cheng Precision Instruments Co. 
Ltd's role in the listed transaction. Conduct a site visit to 
assess the company's reliability as a recipient of United 
States Munitions List (USML) items as well as its operations 
and on-site security.  Emphasize that the unauthorized 
re-export or retransfer of USML items is strictly prohibited. 
 Confirm with the end-user that all of the USML items were 
received and that they are being used for the authorized 
purpose. 
 
The following questions provide suggested guidance: 
 
(To Gon Cheng Precision Instruments Co. Ltd.) 
 
 - Please confirm an order that was shipped under license 
¶050096839. 
 
 - Who are your regular customers and where are they located? 
 
 - Do you conduct business involving USML items with any 
countries besides Taiwan? If so, where? 
 
 - If your company has offices overseas, where are they 
located? 
 
 - Are you familiar with the restrictions against the 
unauthorized re-export or retransfer of U.S.-origin defense 
articles? 
 
 
 (To End User) 
 
 - Please confirm an order that was shipped under license 
¶050096839. 
 
 - How long has Gon Cheng Precision Instruments Co. Ltd. done 
business with Taiwan, R.O.C. Air Force? 
 
 - What was its specific role in the licensed transaction? 
 
 
End Action Request.
