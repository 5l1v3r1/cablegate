SUBJECT: MOZAMBIQUE: SUPPORT AT SEPTEMBER 12 - 13 MDA 
CONSULTATIONS IN GENEVA 
 
REF: STATE 149915 
 
On August 19 econ/poloff met with Faruque Faquira, Head of 
Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs' Directorate for 
International Organizations and Conferences (DOIC), to 
deliver reftel talking points. Faquira stated that the GRM 
was interested in seeing the consultations take place, but 
admitted that it had not been following the issue closely 
over the last month. Faquira said he recognized the 
importance of the issue and promised to deliver the message 
to the GRM's mission in Geneva.
