(SBU) PolOff delivered the diplomatic note, resume and points 
in reftel on the candidacy of Michael Matheson for the ILC to 
MFA Director General for Multilateral Affairs Lourdes Puma on 
November 8.  Puma said that Ecuador had its own candidate for 
the ILC, Marcelo Vasquez, and asked whether it would be 
possible to exchange votes.  PolOff noted USG policy not to 
reveal or trade USG candidate votes.  Puma responded that 
Matheson's candidacy would be given full consideration by the 
GOE.
