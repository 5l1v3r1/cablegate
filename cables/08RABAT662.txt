SUBJECT: MOROCCO LIKELY TO SUPPORT INDIA'S SAFEGUARD 
AGREEMENT 
 
REF: STATE 74896 
 
(SBU) On July 13, Poloffs delivered ref demarche regarding 
India's safeguards agreement to Moroccan Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs (MFA), International Organizations Division, Chief of 
Security and Disarmament Eloumni Bouchaib.  Bouchaib advised 
that in principle, Morocco is supportive of any safeguard 
implementation and he would recommend, to both his superiors 
in the MFA and to the Moroccan delegation in Vienna, Moroccan 
approval of the India safeguard agreement during the 1 Aug 
Board of Governors meeting.
