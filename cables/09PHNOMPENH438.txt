Classified By: AMBASSADOR CAROL A. RODLEY FOR REASONS 1.4 (B,D) 
 
¶1. (C) Acting Pol/Econ Chief delivered reftel demarch and 
talking points June 30 to Hos Sereythong, Director, Asia 1 
Department, Ministry of Foreign Affairs an International 
Cooperation.  He noted that while he was familiar with press 
reports regarding the vessel in question, he had not yet 
received any official information from his government 
regarding its response.  He said that he would share this 
information with the relevant officials. 
 
¶2. (C) Hos Sereythong acknowledged that Burmese cooperation 
in this matter is a positive development.  However, when 
encouraged to urge the Burmese to accept third party 
involvement in inspections, he reiterated the Royal 
Government of Cambodia's policy to adhere to ASEAN's 
principles of non-intervention and respect for national 
sovereignty.
