(C) Foreign Minister Norman Caldera told the Ambassador on 
March 7 that he believes that recognizing India's good 
behavior on non-proliferation is the right course to take. 
The Foreign Minister also opined that forging closer ties 
with India as a counterweight to China is a sensible step for 
U.S. strategic policy.
