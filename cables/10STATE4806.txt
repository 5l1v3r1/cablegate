COMMODITY FLOW 
-------------- 
 
¶3.  (SBU) The Chevron convoy that departed Santo Domingo at 
0430 arrived safely in Port-au-Prince at 1230. The convoy is 
on its way back to Santo Domingo. The operation was a success, 
slowed only by the vehicular congestion on the roads to Port- 
au-Prince. There were no security incidents reported en route 
to the Haitian capital.  (TF1/Embassy Port-au-Prince e-mail)

POLITICAL DEVELOPMENTS 
---------------------- 
 
¶9. (U) Ottawa will expedite immigration applications from 
Haitians with family in Canada and give immigration priority 
to Haitians affected by the earthquake.  Haitians currently in 
Canada temporarily will be allowed to extend their stay.  (AP) 
 
¶10. (U) Senegalese President Wade has offered "voluntary 
repatriation" and land to any interested Haitians.  (AP)
