¶1. (SBU) We provided the U.S. response to Ukrainian views on 
the draft text of the PSI Shipboarding Agreement, with 
accompanying Ukrainian language translation, to MFA Arms 
Control Department Counselor Ruslan Nymchynsky January 27. 
Nymchynsky promised to study our views, provided in the form 
of non-papers, and said he would contact the Embassy later 
with any reaction.
