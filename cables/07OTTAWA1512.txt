¶1.  (SBU) We conveyed reftel demarche and strategy document 
to Department of Foreign Affairs and International Trade 
(DFAIT) Afghanistan Task Force Senior Advisor Gallit Dobner 
on August 8.  Dobner expressed appreciation for the detailed 
briefing.  She said it tracked with the "very useful and 
highly appreciated" pre-brief Ambassador Schweich had shared 
with Associate Deputy Minister (ADM) and G8 Sherpa David 
Mulroney and other Canadian senior officials on July 13. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Dobner offered no immediate substantive reaction 
to the demarche (septel).  Rather, she referred us to the 
extensive, detailed remarks made by ADM Mulroney and others 
on July 13 (Ottawa 1420).
