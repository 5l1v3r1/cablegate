SUBJECT: IRISH UNDECIDED ON PLEDGE FOR AFGHANISTAN COMPACT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 226670 
 
On December 28, emboff delivered reftel points to Development 
Cooperation Ireland (Aid mechanism of the Department of 
Foreign Affairs) Assistant Principal Cait Moran.  Moran said 
that the GOI has not yet determined the amount or the method 
that Ireland will pledge to the upcoming international 
conference on Afghanistan ("Afghanistan Compact") in London. 
She said that this decision will be taken by mid-January 
after consultations with the EU.  She added that Ireland is 
looking for new partners on the ground since several Irish 
NGOs who were partnered with the GOI pulled out of 
Afghanistan due to the difficulties of working there.
