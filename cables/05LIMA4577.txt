¶1.  Action Request - see paragraph 4. 
 
¶2.  Polcouns delivered the demarche on the October 3-7 Summit 
of the Americas Negotiating Session (Reftel) to the Foreign 
Ministry's Director of Multilateral Affairs, Julio Garro, and 
Action Officer for the OAS and Summit of the Americas, Romulo 
Acurio, on 10/24. 
 
¶3.  Garro said that Peru has been working pro-actively to 
push for consensus in the SIRG process and would continue to 
do so.  With respect to the USG's proposal to eliminate the 
paragraph on "Institutional framework for decent work" 
(number 26), Garro requested more information on the specific 
objections we have to the issues raised and "problematic 
language" used. 
 
¶4.  ACTION REQUEST:  Please provide guidance to respond to 
Garro's questions regarding the USG's specific objections to 
the substance and language of the paragraph on "Institutional 
framework for decent work."
