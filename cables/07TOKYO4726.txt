SUBJECT: JAPAN PLANS CONTRIBUTION FOR IRAQ REFUGEE SUPPORT 
 
REF: STATE 138123 
 
Embassy Tokyo Political Officer delivered reftel demarche 
October 4 and followed up October 9 with MOFA Humanitarian 
Assistance Division Principal Deputy Director Hisanobu 
Mochizuki.  Mochizuki said that Japan shares U.S. concerns 
about the growing Iraq refugee and IDP problem, and will 
definitely make a financial contribution from fiscal 2007 ODA 
funds.  The Japanese government will determine the exact 
amount of the contribution as well as which program to 
contribute to "during the next months," said Mochizuki, 
cautioning that the amount would not be as large as the U.S. 
contribution.
