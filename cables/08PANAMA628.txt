SUBJECT: PANAMA: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON UNMEE MISSION 
 
REF: SECSTATE 79539 
 
Classified By: POL ERIC GEELAN: REASONS 1.4 (d) 
 
POL delivered Reftel demarche on the UN Mission in Ethiopia 
and Eritrea (UNMEE) to MFA working level politicial advisor 
Janio Tunon on July 24. Tunon noted that Panama agreed with 
the U.S. position to terminate UNMEE if Ethiopia and Eritrea 
were not cooperating. POL double tracked with MFA advisor 
Ernesto Cerrud and confirmed Panama's position on July 29.
