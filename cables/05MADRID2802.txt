     ¶B. STATE 132303 
 
¶1.  MFA International Disarmament Affairs Ambassador Tomas 
Rodriguez Pantoja informed ESTHOFF August 1 that Industry 
Ministry Deputy Director General for Foreign Trade in Defense 
and Double Use Equipment Antonio Segura will be one of the 
two GOS representatives to attend the September 20-22 
International Export Control Conference in Stockholm.  Segura 
can be reached at: 
 
EMail:  antonio.segura@sscc.mcx.es 
Phone:  (34) (91) 583-5066 
Fax:    (34) (91) 583-5619. 
 
¶2.  Rodriguez Pantoja said the second GOS participant would 
come from the Spanish Customs Service but that the 
July/August vacation period had delayed the nomination of the 
participant.  Embassy will forward the name and contact 
details when they become available.
