SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  FOLLOW UP TO SECRETARY RICE'S STRATEGY 
SESSION ON WOMEN'S EMPOWERMENT 
 
REF: STATE 178055 
 
(U) Pol/Econ Chief October 26 delivered reftel fact sheet 
concerning follow up to the Secretary's session on women's 
empowerment to MFA Director for International Organization 
Affairs Ghassan Shaikho.  Shaikho appreciated receiving the 
fact sheet and pledged to pass it to Bahraini organizations 
working on women's empowerment.  In response to reftel 
question, there are currently two women in the Bahraini 
cabinet who would be interested in pursuing women's 
empowerment programs - Minister of Health Dr. Nada Haffadh 
and Minister of Social Development Dr. Fatima Al Baloushi. 
Post has partnered with several Bahraini women's 
organizations in support of MEPI and other programming, 
including the Supreme Council for Women, Bahrain Business 
Women's Society, Bahrain Women's Society, and Al Sharaka 
Society, among others.
