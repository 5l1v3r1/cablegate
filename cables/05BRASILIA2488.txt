SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: LIST 72T 
 
REF: A) STATE 172703, B) STATE 145406 (NOTAL) 
 
¶1. (SBU) EmbOff delivered reftel designation of Dr. Abdul 
Latif Saleh (List 72T) to Luisa Lopes da Silva of the 
Foreign Ministry's office for the Combat of Illicit 
International Activities and to Jaime Freitas of Brazil's 
Financial Intelligence Unit (COAF).  Freitas undertook to 
search COAF's databases and share the list with Central Bank 
and the Brazilian intelligence agency (ABIN), for them to 
conduct similar searches.  Lopes da Silva will likewise 
follow up. 
 
¶2. (U) We will report septel should we receive any 
substantive response.
