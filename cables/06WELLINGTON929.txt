SUBJECT: COOK ISLANDS: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON TRADE TRACKING 
PROGRAM FOR SWORDFISH 
 
REF: SECSTATE 187003 
 
(U) On November 16, Poloff delivered reftel points regarding 
the International Commission for the Conservation of Atlantic 
Tunas (ICCAT) swordfish statistical document to James Waite, 
Policy Officer, New Zealand Ministry of Foreign Affairs and 
Trade (MFAT).  Poloff also delivered points to Sonya Kamana, 
Acting High Commissioner, High Commission of the Cooks 
Islands in Wellington.  On November 24, Waite responded that 
reftel points were conveyed to the New Zealand High 
Commission in Rarotonga and through the High Commission to 
the Government of the Cook Islands.  On November 24, Kamana 
confirmed that she had forwarded reftel points to the Cook 
Islands Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Immigration.
