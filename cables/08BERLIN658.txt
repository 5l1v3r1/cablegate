¶1.  (U) Per reftel B, Mission Germany plans to issue an Op-Ed 
under the Ambassador's by-line in recognition of the Day of 
Solidarity with the Cuban People on May 21.  The Op-Ed will 
appear in a national daily newspaper, The Tagesspiegel.  We 
are working with the Chancellor's office and the Foreign 
Ministry to encourage supportive statements as well.  Results 
of this outreach will be reported in a follow-up cable after 
May 21.
