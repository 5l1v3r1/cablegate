SUBJECT: BLUE LANTERN LEVEL 3: PRE-LICENSE END-USE CHECK 
ON LICENSE 05-050079420 
 
¶1. This is an Action Request. See paragraphs 2 - 4. 
 
¶2. The Department's Office of Defense Trade Controls 
Compliance (PM/DTCC) requests Post's assistance in conducting 
a pre-license check on license 05-050079420 for the export of 
ammunition to Iraq. Post is requested to complete this Blue 
Lantern check within 30 days. Lack of response to a Blue 
Lantern check will affect pending and future licenses 
involving parties to this license. 
 
¶3. Reason for Request: Large volume of ammunition is being 
exported directly to the Iraqi government end-user rather 
than going through USG. Caliber of this ammunition is 
uncommon for the Iraqi Army. 
 
¶4. Action Request: Post is requested to contact appropriate 
officials in order to establish the bona fides of the 
end-user and the legitimacy of this order. Post is also 
requested to establish the bona fides of foreign consignee, 
Stuart Watkins, and verify his affiliations with those party 
of this transaction.
