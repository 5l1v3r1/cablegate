SUBJECT: POINTS DELIVERED TO ROK ON IAEA DECISION ON IRAN 
 
REF: STATE 19516 
 
On February 8, poloff conveyed reftel points on the February 
4 IAEA Board resolution reporting Iran to the UN Security 
Council to Yeo Sung-jun, Deputy Director of the Disarmament 
and Nonproliferation Division at the Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs and Trade.  Poloff emphasized U.S. appreciation for 
South Korean support for the IAEA resolution and provided 
copies of the President's and Secretary's statements.  Deputy 
Director Yeo expressed his thanks for the materials.
