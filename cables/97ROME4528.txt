SUBJECT:  QADHAFI JUST WANTS TO BE FRIENDS WITH EUROPE 
 
THE ITALIAN NEWS SERVICE ANSA DATELINED TRIPOLI 
MAY 19 REPORTED THAT LIBYAN LEADER QADHAFI 
PUBLICLY SAID HE IS READY TO UPHOLD A ''FRIENDSHIP 
WITH EUROPE'' BASED ON RECIPROCAL RESPECT AND 
INDEPENDENCE FROM THE U.S.  ACCORDING TO ANSA, 
QADHAFI SAID THAT HE IS ''IN LINE WITH THE 
THINKING OF EUROPEAN LEADERS WHO WOULD LIKE 
INDEPENDENCE FROM THE UNITED STATES.''  HE ADDED 
THAT HE ''SUPPORTS THE FRENCH PRESIDENT AND THE 
GERMAN CHANCELLOR WHO ARE AT THE HEAD OF THIS 
EUROPEAN MOVEMENT AWAY FROM AMERICAN CONTROL, AND 
WHO REPRESENT THE NUCLEUS OF THE EUROPEAN 
MILITARY FORCE (EUROFORCE).''  QADHAFI ALSO AGAIN 
CALLED FOR ARAB UNITY, SAYING THAT ''IT WOULD BE 
GOOD FOR ARAB COUNTRIES TO CREATE THEIR OWN 
POLITICAL, ECONOMIC AND MILITARY FORCE, AND TO 
ESTABLISH RELATIONS WITH EUROPE.  WE WILL SUPPORT 
THIS PROCESS.''
