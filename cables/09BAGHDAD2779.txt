Republic of Iraq 
Nori K. Al Maliky 
Prime Minster 
 
His Excellency President Barack Obama, 
President of the United States of America 
 
I am pleased to present my congratulations to you for 
receiving the Nobel Peace Prize.  I hope that the honor of 
your receiving this prize will give an impetus to the success 
of your vision in promoting freedom, justice, and peace among 
nations to maintain the security and stability of the world 
and preserve the rights and dignity of mankind. 
 
Please accept my highest regard and appreciation. 
 
(Signed) 
Nori Kamil Al Maliky, 
Prime Minister, Republic of Iraq
