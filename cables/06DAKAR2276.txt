SUBJECT: WADE MEETS WITH IRANIAN PRESIDENT AND OFFERS 
PEACEKEEPERS FOR UNIFIL 
 
¶1.  Before departing for the UNGA, President Wade met with 
Iranian President Mahmoud Ahmedinejad in Dakar on September 
13 and offered peacekeepers for the UN force in Lebanon 
(UNIFIL) on September 14.  Following the Iranian President,s 
visit, Senegalese President Abdoulaye Wade insisted that 
dialogue without preconditions remains the best vehicle for 
ending the stand-off over uranium enrichment. 
 
¶2.  As for UNIFIL, Wade said he has offered to contribute 150 
Senegalese troops.  (COMMENT: This would be in addition to 
Senegalese peacekeepers and civilian police in 
Bosnia-Herzegovina, Cote d,Ivoire, the Democratic Republic 
of the Congo, Haiti, Liberia and Sudan.  END COMMENT.)
