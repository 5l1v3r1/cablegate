SUBJECT: SAG APPROVES RETURN OF U.S.-ORIGIN SPENT FUEL 
 
Department of Minerals and Energy Directorate Nuclear Safety 
official Ditebogo Kgomo informed Energy Officer on January 15 
that the SAG cabinet approved the recommendations of the 
feasibility study on participation in the Foreign Research 
Reactor Spent Nuclear Fuel Acceptance Program and 
repatriation of the spent fuel back to the U.S.  This cable 
provides formal notification to DOE.  According to the study, 
the next step would be for DOE National Nuclear Security 
Administration to send a technical team to South Africa. 
Embassy is pleased that this key and long-awaited bilateral 
cooperation step is proceeding.
