SUBJECT: START FOLLOW-ON NEGOTIATIONS, GENEVA (SFO-GVA-I): 
DRAFT PRESS RELEASE BY RUSSIAN DELEGATION TO START 
FOLLOW-ON NEGOTIATIONS 
 
¶1.  (U) This is SFO-GVA-I-004. 
 
¶2.  The text at paragraph 3 is the official translation of 
the press release the Russian START Follow-on Negotiations 
Delegation will release at the conclusion of this round of 
negotiations.  The press release is provided to agencies for 
informational purposes only, as the U.S. Delegation has no 
plans to issue a press release. 
 
¶3.  Begin text (Russian Press Release): 
 
                                    Official Translation 
 
                        Press Release 
 
     The second round of Russian-U.S. negotiations to work 
out a new, comprehensive agreement to replace the START 
Treaty was held in Geneva on June 1-3. 
 
     There was continued discussion of a broad range of 
issues related to preparation of the future agreement, as 
well as consideration of possible parameters of the report on 
the course of the negotiating process, which is to be 
submitted to the Presidents of the two countries in July of 
this year. 
 
     The sides agreed to hold the next round of negotiations 
in Geneva during the second half of June 2009. 
 
End text.
