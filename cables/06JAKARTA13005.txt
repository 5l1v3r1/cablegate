SUBJECT: DEMARCHE TO INDONESIA ON ARF STATEMENT ON DPRK 
NUCLEAR TEST 
 
REF: STATE 178680 (DEMARCHE REQUEST AND INFORMATION 
     FOR ASEAN REGIONAL FORUM HOST GOVERNMENTS) 
 
(SBU) On November 3, we met with Fahmi Jamaludin, Desk 
Officer for Korea at Indonesia's Department of Foreign 
Affairs, to urge that the ASEAN Regional Forum Intersessional 
Working Group meeting at Batam include clear language 
expressing concern over the DPRK's recent nuclear test.  We 
stressed our support for Australia's proposed statement on 
this subject.  We left talking points contained reftel paras 
5 and 8.  Jamaludin undertook to coordinate the matter with 
the GOI's delegation at the ARF.
