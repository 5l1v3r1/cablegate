SUBJECT: REQUEST HUMAN RIGHTS VETTING FOR ISRAELI DEFENSE 
FORCES PERSONNEL ATTENDING MAY INTERNATIONAL SPECIAL 
OPERATIONS FORCES WEEK EVENTS 
 
¶1. (SBU) Embassy Tel Aviv's Defense Attache Office (DAO) has 
nominated the following individuals for participation in 
events during International Special Operations Forces week, 
scheduled to run during the last week of May in Tampa, 
Florida.  Embassy Tel Aviv has no derogatory information in 
its records on the individuals, or any other indication that 
they have been involved in gross violations of human rights.
