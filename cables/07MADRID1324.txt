SUBJECT: SPAIN NOTIFIED OF USG SANCTIONS ON WINTER AIRCRAFT 
PRODUCTS SA 
 
REF: SECSTATE 90408 
 
(SBU) Per reftel instructions, Post notified the Spanish 
Ministry of Defense (MOD) of the USG's decision to place 
sanctions on Winter Aircraft Products for violations of the 
Export Administration Regulations (EAR). On July 2, poloff 
delivered reftel points and a copy of the Commerce 
Department's Final Decison Order to CAPT Eladio Ferreiro of 
the MOD's Directorate General for Armaments and 
Material/International Relations section.  Ferreiro agreed to 
distribute the information among the members of the 
Inter-Ministerial Regulatory Board on Foreign Trade in 
Defense or Dual-Use Items (JIMDDU), though he said that the 
GOS had already received notice of the USG action via the 
Foreign Ministry.
