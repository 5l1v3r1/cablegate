SUBJECT: JENIN: PASF CONCENTRATES ON TUBAS AREA; MULTIPLE 
IDF INCURSIONS 
 
REF: A. JERUSALEM 827 
     ¶B. JERUSALEM 817 
 
¶1.  (SBU) National Security Forces (NSF) commanders report 
detaining 31 criminal suspects and seizing 21 
stolen/unregistered vehicles in Tubas and nearby villages May 
17-19.  Security contacts report that PIJ spokesman Abd 
al-Fatah Khzaymiah turned himself over to the PA General 
Intelligence (Mukhabarat) May 18 after he learned the IDF had 
been searching for him during a May 17 incursion.  Of seven 
PIJ militants/affiliates taken into PA custody during the 
Jenin operation, all remain in custody or at a hospital under 
police guard. 
 
 
Multiple Israeli Incursions 
in Jenin and Qabatiya 
--------------------------- 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Jenin security contacts and local press report four 
IDF incursions into Jenin May 17-19.  Early May 19, the IDF 
entered Jenin Refugee Camp, arrested PIJ militant Ammar Abu 
Ghalyun and detained a local imam for questioning.  Security 
contacts report that the IDF entered Jenin and the Refugee 
camp twice May 17-18, and entered Qabatiya on May 17, taking 
control of and damaging two houses before leaving without 
making arrests.
