Special Assistant to the French MFA Director for the Middle

Parliamentary hearings before he learned about the BMENA 
Forum.  The MFA has asked Parliamentary leaders to delay the 
hearings, Roche reported, and they expect a response 
imminently.  Roche promised to inform us as soon as the MFA 
receives final confirmation that he will attend.  Post will 
keep the department informed.
