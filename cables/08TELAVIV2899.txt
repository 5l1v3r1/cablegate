SUBJECT: ISRAEL: PLANNING TO PARTICIPATE IN FIFTH GLOBAL 
INITIATIVE MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 132539 
 
Per reftel, Post informed GOI Global Initiative to Combat 
Nuclear Terrorism (GICNT) points of contact on December 22 
that the 5th Global Initiative Plenary Meeting will be hosted 
by The Hague in June 2009.  MFA Arms Control Officer Israel 
Tikochinski indicated December 23 that the GOI intends to 
participate in the plenary but has yet to determine which of 
its officials will attend.
