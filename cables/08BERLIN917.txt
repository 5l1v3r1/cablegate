SUBJECT: INVITATION DELIVERED: INTERNATIONAL WORKSHOP ON 
NEXT GENERATION NUCLEAR SAFEGUARDS (NGSI) 
 
REF: STATE 71246 
 
(SBU) On July 2, Global Affairs officer delivered reftel 
invitation to German MFA Export Control Division Desk Officer 
Mark Hinrichsen.  Hinrichsen emphasized Germany's commitment 
to strengthening worldwide nuclear safeguards and delivered 
the reftel invitation to Dietrich Becker, the MFA Head of 
Unit for Nuclear Energy and Non-Proliferation, and Martin 
Langer, the MFA IAEA Head of Unit.  Hinrichsen expressed 
confidence that Germany will have representation at the 
workshop, but admitted he is unsure whether this 
representation will come from the MFA or from the German 
Embassy in Washington.  Post will report any substantive 
response septel.
