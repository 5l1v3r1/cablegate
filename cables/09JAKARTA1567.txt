SUBJECT: INDONESIAN FM WILL ATTEND FOOD SECURITY MEETING 
 
REF:  STATE 93894 
 
The Indonesian Ministry of Foreign affairs has informed Post that 
Minister of Foreign Affairs N. Hassan Wirajuda will attend the first 
half-hour of the meeting on Partnering for Food Security in New York 
(reftel).  After the opening of the meeting, Director General for 
Multilateral Affairs Rezlan Ishar Jenie will replace Wirajuda.
