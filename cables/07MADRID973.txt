REF: A. STATE 62145 
     ¶B. STATE 58978 
 
¶1. (SBU) ESTOFF and POLOFF delivered REFTEL A points on May 
10 to MFA Disarmament Affairs Deputy Director General Ignacio 
Sanchez and met again on May 18 with Sanchez and MFA 
Counselor for Nuclear Non-Proliferation and Disarmament 
Carlos Torres to discuss Spain's participation in the Global 
Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism.  EMBOFFs welcomed the 
Government of Spain into the Initiative, and provided the MFA 
with the most current version of the Plan of Work, Terms of 
Reference, Statement of Principles, and other related 
documents, including U.S. and Russian Government POC 
information.  We encouraged Spain to participate actively in 
the Initiative, and requested government-wide point of 
contact information for Spain.  Sanchez replied that Madrid 
views the initiative positively and looks forward to active 
participation.  Spain will be represented at the third 
meeting of the Global Initiative by Angel Lossada, MFA 
Director General (A/S equivalent) for Terrorism, 
Non-proliferation and Disarmament Affairs.  Post has also 
been in contact with the Russian Embassy in Madrid and we are 
determining how to work together to support Spain's 
participation in the initiative.
