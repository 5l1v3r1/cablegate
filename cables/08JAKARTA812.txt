The Government of Indonesia nominated the following participants to 
take part in the Department of Energy's National Nuclear Security 
Administration seminar on nuclear infrastructure. 
 
¶1. Foreign Ministry participant: Carolina Tinangon, Head of Nuclear 
Affairs Section of Directorate of International Security and 
Disarmament. 
 
¶2. State Ministry of Research and Technology participant: Carunia 
Mulya Firdausy, Deputy Minister of Research and Technology for 
Social Dynamics. 
 
¶3. Nuclear Research Agency participant: Dr. As Natio Lasman, 
Chairman of Nuclear Energy Regulatory Agency (BAPETEN). 
 
Further to reftel request, we will assist the participants in 
completing the visitor access forms for submission to NNSA via 
email.
