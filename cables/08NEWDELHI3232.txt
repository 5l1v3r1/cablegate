demarche on the U.S. domestic designation of the additional 
Iranian entities, Assa Corp., and Assa Co., Ltd., to the GOI, 
including Ministry of Finance Joint Secretary K.P. Krishnan, 
Ministry of External Affairs (MEA) Joint Secretary T.K.A. 
Raghavan, and Ministry of Finance (MOF) Director Anand Bajaj. 
 In addition, Post provided GOI officials with copies of the
