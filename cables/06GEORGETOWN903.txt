SUBJECT: DEMARCHES DELIVERED: NAM SUMMIT AND IRANIAN NUCLEAR PROGRAM 
(GUYANA) 
 
REFS: A) STATE 149133, B) STATE 145242 
 
¶1. EmbOff delivered Iran NAM demarche using talking points in Ref A 
to Jackie Hamer, Director for Multilateral and Global Affairs, 
Guyana Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA). Hamer said she will convey 
the points to Minister Rudy Insanally, who is currently attending 
the NAM ministerial in Havana. Hamer also said that Insanally did 
not leave Guyana with specific instructions regarding a possible NAM 
statement on the Iranian nuclear program. 
 
¶2. DCM delivered general NAM points from Ref B to Ambassador 
Elisabeth Harper, MFA Director General, on September 8 and 
Ambassador reviewed with Foreign Minister Insanally on September 9. 
Insanally offered no substantial reaction.
