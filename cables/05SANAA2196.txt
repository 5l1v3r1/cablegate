Econoff delivered reftel demarche August 9 to Ibrahim 
al-Nahari, Director General of External Relations at the 
Ministry of Finance, on the UN Sanctions Committee 
designation of individuals associated with the Moroccan 
Islamic Combatant Group.  Nahari promised to share the 
information with the ROYG's inter-ministerial Terrorist 
Finance Committee, and to update Post about progress in 
locating and freezing assets belonging to the listed 
individuals.
