SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON GLOBAL MARITIME PARTNERSHIP 
 
REF: STATE 50488 
 
On June 22, PolOff delivered reftel demarche to Yemeni 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs Two Americas First Secretary 
Abdullah al-Socotry.  Al-Socotry asked PolOff to clarify the 
requirements for Yemen's participation in the Global Maritime 
Partnership, which PolOff answered from talking points. 
Al-Socotry said he would forward the suggestion to the 
appropriate committee.
