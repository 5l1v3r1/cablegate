SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON THE UN DECLARATION ON THE RIGHTS OF 
INDIGENOUS PEOPLES 
 
REF: STATE 169257 
 
Reftel demarche was delivered to Nasser Bourita, Director of 
United Nations and International Organizations, in the 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs on October 13.  Bourita said that 
the GOM is in full agreement with the US position.  According 
to Bourita, the GOM view concerning the text is that it is 
unclear, controversial and governments should have the 
opportunity to discuss the text.  He said the GOM will 
abstain when the text comes to a vote.
