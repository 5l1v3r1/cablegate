SUBJECT: GOL CONTINUES TO SUPPORT US-EU EFFORTS ON IRAN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 16171 
 
We delivered reftel points to Vaclovas Salkauskas, Head of 
the MFA's Middle East and Africa Division, February 1. 
Salkauskas said that the GOL will continue to support joint 
US-EU efforts to find a diplomatic solution to the Iran 
question.  He added that it is imperative that Iran 
understand the seriousness of the international community's 
concern about its actions.
