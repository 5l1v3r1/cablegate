SUBJECT: BRIEFING AZERBAIJAN ON U.S. PROPOSALS ON MD, CFE 
 
REF: STATE 159773 
 
(SBU) Per reftel, Emboffs briefed Azerbaijani Government 
officials on recent discussions with Russia regarding Missile 
Defense and the Conventional Forces in Europe (CFE) treaty, 
leaving the points in paras five and six as nonpapers.  In a 
November 30 meeting, Presidential Foreign Policy Advisor 
Novruz Mammadov said that he appreciated the United States' 
efforts to keep Azerbaijan informed of its missile defense 
discussions with Russia, particularly those that involve any 
plans to incorporate Azerbaijan's Gabala radar station into a 
new regional missile defense architecture.  He said that he 
would appreciate a readout of the November 26 informal 
discussions in Washington, and also noted that Russian 
Defense Minister Serdyukov had visited the Gabala radar 
station on November 28.  Mammadov said he did not yet have a 
readout of the visit.  (We will provide septel a readout of 
Serdyukov's visit from the Russian Ambassador.)  Poloff 
separately briefed MFA International Security Department 
Deputy Director Yusuf Mammadaliyev on the Missile Defense and 
CFE discussions in a November 28 meeting.  Mammadaliyev had 
no substantive comment, but promised to share the nonpapers 
with Deputy Foreign Minister Araz Azimov, who oversees 
Azerbaijani policy in these areas.
