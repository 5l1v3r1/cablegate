SUBJECT: UAE SUPPORT FOR WAY FORWARD IN AFGHANISTAN

(SBU) Ambassador delivered reftel demarche to Foreign Minister 
Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed Al Nahyan (AbZ) and discussed the 
President's announcement of the way forward in Afghanistan.  AbZ 
reaffirmed the UAE's active support for the mission in Afghanistan 
and noted that the newly-appointed UAE Ambassador is expected to 
arrive in Kabul soon.
