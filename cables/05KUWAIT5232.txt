SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED:  "AFGHANISTAN COMPACT" 
CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 226670 
 
(SBU) On December 21, Charge d' Affaires and Econoff 
(notetaker) met with Ministry of Foreign Affairs Asia 
Department Head Ambassador Abdul Mohsin Al-Haroun to deliver 
reftel talking points.  CDA thanked Al-Haroun for Kuwait's 
previous support for Afghanistan and other causes, and urged 
that the GOK consider new financial pledges at the upcoming 
conference.  Al-Haroun stated that the GOK was "trying our 
best to help develop Afghanistan," and said that he was 
certain the GOK would participate in the conference, but 
offered no specifics about possible pledges.
