SUBJECT: GOK REQUESTS DETAILS ON IRAQ NEIGHBORS CONFERENCE 
PARTICIPANTS 
 
 
¶1. (U) Summary and Action Request: On February 20, post 
received a circular from the Kuwait MFA requesting 
information concerning the U.S. delegation attending the 
April 22 Iraq Neighbors Conference in Kuwait.  Specifically, 
the MFA is requesting the names of the delegation members, as 
well as the specifics of their travel arrangements.  Post 
requests that the Department provide this information for 
relay to the MFA.  End summary. 
 
¶2. (U) Begin text of circular: 
 
Complimentary opening 
 
"With reference to the third Conference of Iraq Neighboring 
Countries' Foreign Ministers, the Permanent Members of the 
Security Council, and the Group of Eight Major Industrial 
Countries, which Kuwait is hosting on 22 April 2008, please 
provide us with details of the date of arrival and departure 
of the head of the participant delegation and the names of 
the accompanying delegation as soon as possible. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs avails itself of this 
opportunity to express its consideration to the Embassy." 
 
End text.
