SUBJECT: TUNISIA'S DELEGATION TO 63RD UNGA 
 
REF: STATE 72078 
 
Ambassador met August 29 with Minister of Foreign Affairs 
Abdelwaheb Abdallah who confirmed he will lead Tunisia's 
delegation to UNGA.  He plans to depart August 24 and stay in 
New York for a week.  He would welcome a meeting with A/S 
David Welch.  He expressed hope there will be an Arab Maghreb 
Union (AMU) Foreign Ministers' meeting with the Under 
Secretary for Political Affairs as happened last year.  He 
said the Tunisian speech before the General Assembly is 
scheduled for 29 August.
