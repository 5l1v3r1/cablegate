SUBJECT: GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT NUCLEAR TERRORISM 
 
REF: STATE 21535 
 
¶1.  PolCouns, accompanied by Russian Counselor Sergei 
Lobanov, delivered reftel demarche March 14 to Mohammad Koba, 
Head of Section for Itra-State Conflict in the Department of 
Foreign Affairs Directorate of International Security and 
Disarmament.  Koba was assigned to receive the demarche at 
the last minute and thus was not knowledgeable about the 
issue.  He was accompanied by Adhyanti Sardanarini Wirajuda, 
who works on nuclear issues within the directorate, and is a 
daughter of the Foreign Minister.  They took on board the 
suggestion that the contents of the statement of principles 
were very much in line with Government of Indonesia foreign 
policy and promised to study it closely. 
 
¶2.  Bio Notes:  Kobas has been with the Directorate two 
months.  Ms. Wirajuda has been assigned there for some time, 
but recently returned from a leave of absence connected to 
the birth of a son.
