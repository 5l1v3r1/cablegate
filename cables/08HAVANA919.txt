SUBJECT: USINT Havana Holiday Security Planning 
 
REF:  STATE 129801 
 
¶1. Post's Emergency Action Committee(EAC) convened December 11, 2008 
to discuss reftel, local threat reporting, and security planning for 
USINT holiday events. 
 
¶2. A canvas of USINT representatives determined that there will be a 
total of four small-to-medium sized events from December 18-20, 2008 
for Section employees.  All will be held at private residences and 
existing security support has been deemed adequate by the EAC.
