SUBJECT: CZECH REPUBLIC APPROVES $150,000 FOR UNRWA/LEBANON 
 
REF: STATE 78944 
 
 Econoff delivered reftel demarche to Jiri Slavik at the 
Czech MFA Department for Development Cooperation June 13. 
Slavik confirmed that the GOCR is fully aware of the critical 
situation of Palestinian refugees in Lebanon and said on June 
11, the MFA approved CZK 3 million (approximately US$ 
150,000) for funding the UNRWA Flash Appeal.  The Czech 
Embassy in Beirut is in direct contact with UNRWA to 
implement that commitment.  The GOCR has also approved CZK 2 
million (about US$ 100,000) as its annual contribution to 
UNRWA.
