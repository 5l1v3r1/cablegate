SUBJECT: RUSSIAN FEDERATION:  UN COMMITTEE ON ECONOMIC, 
SOCIAL AND CULTURAL RIGHTS CANDIDACY 
 
¶1.  Mission has received a note from the Permanent Mission of 
the Russian Federation to the United Nations requesting USG 
support for the candidature of Professor Yuri M. Kolosov for 
re-election to the Committee on Economic, Social and Cultural 
Rights (CESCR).  The elections will be held during the 
resumed organizational session of ECOSOC in May 2006. 
 
¶2.  Mission will respond that careful consideration will be 
given by the United States to the candidature of Professor 
Kolosov.
