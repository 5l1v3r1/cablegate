¶1.  Embassy is in receipt of a letter from the New Zealand 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs and Trade (MFAT) with copies of 
clearances for the research vessel Kaimikai-o-Kanaloa from 
the NZ Ministry of Fisheries, NZ Ministry of Research, 
Science, and Technology, Land Information New Zealand, NZ 
Ministry for the Environment, NZ Maritime Safety Authority, 
and the NZ Ministry of Defense. 
 
¶2.  The letter from MFAT advises that written clearance from 
the New Zealand Immigration Service will be passed 
separately. 
 
¶3.  The letter and clearances from the various agencies was 
faxed to OES/OA on February 23, 2005.
