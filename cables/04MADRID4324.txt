¶1.   THE VISAS VIPER COMMITTEE MET ON OCT. 25, 2004, AS 
  PART OF MADRID INTERAGENCY COUNTERTERRORISM WORKING 
  GROUP AND WAS CHAIRED BY THE DCM, ROBERT MANZANARES. 
  THE CHARGE EXPRESSED HIS APPRECIATION FOR THE 
  CONTINUING STRONG COOPERATION AND COMMITMENT TO THE 
  PROGRAM BY ALL AGENCIES AT POST. 
 
¶2.   DURING THE MONTH OF OCTOBER 2004, MADRID SUBMITTED 
  FOUR VISAS VIPER CABLES IDENTIFYING ONE HUNDRED AND 
  THREE INDIVIDUALS, SEVENTEEN SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ETA 
  AND EIGHTY-SIX SUSPECTED MEMBERS OF ISLAMIST TERRORIST 
  GROUPS LINKED TO AL-QAEDA.
