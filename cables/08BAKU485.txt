SUBJECT: DEMARCHE ON UNSCR 1803 60-DAY REPORT DELIVERED TO

REF: STATE 48615 
 
¶1. (SBU) On May 19, the DCM delivered reftel points to Gabil 
Israfilov, Head of Regional and International Security in the 
MFA's Security Affairs Department.  Poloff also delivered the 
points to MFA Security Affairs Department Officer Yalchin 
Rafiyev in a May 20 meeting.  Rafiyev explained that the MFA 
was close to finishing its 60-day report but was waiting on 
information from two ministries - the Ministry of Emergency 
Situations and the Ministry of Economic Development.  Rafiyev 
believed that the report would be ready by the end of May, if 
not earlier. 
 
¶2.  (U) We will follow up with the MFA at the end of May and 
report any further information septel.
