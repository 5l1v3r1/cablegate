SUBJECT: Canadian Representation at PRT Conference in Prague 
 
REF: 10 STATE 3595; 09 PRAGUE 731; 10 STATE 4108 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  In response to a January 14 demarche by PolMinCouns 
concerning high level Canadian participation at the Prague PRT 
conference (ref a), Acting Director General Jamie Christoff of the 
Afghanistan Task Force at the Department of Foreign Affairs and 
International Trade confirmed that Jess Dutton, Director of the 
Kandahar PRT and Sean Jorgensen, Political Advisor to Task Force 
Kandahar, will represent Canada. 
 
 
 
¶2. (SBU)  Embassy separately continues to solicit Canadian views on 
the transition to Afghan lead at the provincial level (ref c).
