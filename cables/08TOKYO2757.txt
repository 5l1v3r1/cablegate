SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: IRAN SPECIFIC EXPORT CONTROL

SENSITIVE BUT UNCLASSIFIED 
¶1.  (SBU)  On October 2, EconOff delivered points contained 
in ref to Ministry of Foreign Affairs Export Controls Deputy 
Director Shinichi Sato and Ministry of Economy, Trade and 
Industry Export Controls Deputy Director Atsushi Tanizawa. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Sato expressed thanks for the non-paper.  Tanizawa 
noted the GOJ's continued deep concern with front companies, 
and that it regularly meets with industry to encourage 
vigilance.  He provided EmbOff a Japanese language copy of 
the Ministry's guidance for industry, which has many of the 
same "red flag" points listed in the demarche.  Tanizawa said 
METI will redouble efforts to keep industry officials aware 
of ongoing concerns regarding Iran's illicit programs.
