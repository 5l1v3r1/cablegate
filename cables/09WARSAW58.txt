SUBJECT: U.S.-POLAND STRATEGIC COOPERATION - RESPONSE TO 
POLAND'S NON-PAPER 
 
REF: SECSTATE 4079 
 
On January 16, Post delivered the U.S. response to the Polish 
non-paper on bilateral strategic cooperation 16 to Adam 
Kobieracki, Director of the MFA's Security Policy Department, 
and Artur Orzechowski, Director of the Americas Department. 
Orzechowski promised to provide the text to Deputy Foreign 
Minister Grudzinski upon Grudzinski's return to Warsaw 
Monday, and said the MFA would need a little time to analyze 
the U.S. text before discussing it with us in detail.
