SUBJECT:  ALBANIAN ACKNOWLEDGES U.S. POSITION ON TURKEY'S EBRD 
CHANGE OF STATUS REQUEST 
 
REF: SECSTATE 41999 
 
(SBU) On 24 April 2008, POLOFF delivered ref demarche to Mr. Samir 
Repishti in the Office of Multilateral Affairs at the Albanian 
Ministry of Foreign Affairs.  Mr. Repishti appreciated the work of 
the EBRD in Albania, and had not received other information 
concerning Turkey's request to change status to a country of 
operation within the EBRD.  Mr. Repishti will ensure all the 
relevant offices within the Albanian government are aware of the 
U.S. position and will advise accordingly.
