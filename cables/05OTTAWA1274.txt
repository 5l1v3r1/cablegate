SUBJECT: Malone nomination for IRC directors conference, May 
16-20, 2005, Santo Domingo 
 
Ref: State 212109Z 
 
¶1.  Post wishes to nominate PA Ottawa IRC Director Kyle 
Malone to attend the IRC Directors Conference for Caribbean, 
Central America, Canada and Venezuela from 05/16/2005 
through 05/20/2005. 
 
¶2. Ms. Malone will be arriving Continental 1950 at 1250 
hours on May 16, 2005 and departing on May 20 at 1405 hours, 
Continental 1951. 
 
¶3.  Ms. Malone manages Mission Canada's reference services, 
electronic outreach, websites, and special outreach projects 
to information professionals and targeted audiences.  She 
can offer Conference participants views on Mission Canada's 
use of Goldmine contact management software to advance 
electronic outreach effectiveness and reporting; Mission 
Canada website design, coordination and marketing 
experience; and recent initiatives to communicate with 
audiences in remote areas (Canada's far north) through 
Virtual Presence Post websites, web-based online dialogue, 
and other program tools.
