SUBJECT:  COSTA RICA'S PARTICIPATION IN THE CODEX TASK FORCE 
ON BIOTECHNOLOGY 
 
REF: STATE 147159 
 
¶1. On August 22, 2005 AgAtt conveyed reftel demarche to 
Isabel Cristina Araya, Technical Secretary of the National 
CODEX Committee. 
 
¶2. By means of a letter dated August 29, 2005, Ms. Araya 
indicated that Costa Rica would not be represented at the 
CODEX Intergovernmental Task Force on Biotechnology in 
Chibas, Japan, September 19-23, due to a lack of resources. 
She further indicated that she was arranging for Costa Rica 
to be represented at the task force meeting by an officer 
from the Costa Rican embassy in Japan, who would be provided 
with Costa Rica's positions on the issues that will arise at 
the meeting.  Lastly, she added that Costa Rica's 
representative would be pleased to share Costa Rica's 
position with the United States.
