SUBJECT: NETHERLANDS/PSI: DUTCH ATTENDANCE AT PSI 
HIGH-LEVEL POLITICAL MEETING 
 
REF: STATE 84640 
 
Polmiloff discussed reftel with MFA Senior Advisor on the 
Proliferation Security Initiative (PSI) Frank van Beuningen 
on June 6.  Van Beuningen appreciated the information 
regarding U/S Joseph's and U/S Levey's attendance at the PSI 
High-Level Meeting in Warsaw on June 23.  He said that the 
GONL had not yet decided on the composition of the Dutch 
delegation, but suspected that MFA Director General for 
Political Affairs Hugo Siblesz and MFA Director for Security 
and Defense Policy Robert de Groot would attend.
