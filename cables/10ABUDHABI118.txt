SUBJECT: OUTREACH ON LONDON CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 6355; ABU DHABI 33 
 
(SBU) On January 26, the Ambassador delivered Ref A demarche 
encouraging UAE support for the London Conference on Afghanistan, 
particularly the reintegration trust fund, to MFA Assistant 
Minister for Political Affairs Tariq Al Haidan.  Post also conveyed 
reftel points to the Ministry of Foreign Affairs. Although there 
was no substantive UAEG response to the demarche, as reported in 
Ref B, Amb. Holbrooke recently discussed the London Conference in 
detail with Foreign Minister Sheikh Abdullah bin Zayed, who will 
lead the UAE delegation to London.
