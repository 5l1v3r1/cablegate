SUBJECT: AUSTRIA ON THE ADOPTION OF UNSCR 1747 AND THE 
IMPOSITION OF SANCTIONS ON IRAN 
 
REF: A. STATE 37801 
 
     ¶B. VIENNA 810 
     ¶C. STATE 38553 
 
¶1.  EconPolCouns delivered ref (a) demarche on March 29 to 
Ralph Scheide, the Austrian MFA's Director for the Middle 
East and Africa.  Per ref (b), on March 28, EconUnit Chief 
had delivered ref (c) demarche the implementation of UNSCR 
1747 to the Austrian Finance Ministry. 
 
¶2.  Scheide echoed ref (b) comments that Austria would 
implement UNSCR 1747 speedily and fully. 
 
¶3.  Scheide mused about the need for a strategy regarding the 
next steps to take in the event that Iran did not respond 
positively to the latest Security Council action.  He said 
contacts between EU High Representative Javier Solana and 
Iranian nuclear negotiator Ali Larijani would be extremely 
important in the effort to move the Iranians in the right 
direction.
