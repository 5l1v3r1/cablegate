SUBJECT: MOROCCO SUPPORTS GLOBAL INITIATIVE TO COMBAT 
NUCLEAR TERRORISM WEB PORTAL 
 
REF: STATE 109152 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel message on October 24 to MFA 
Head of United Nations Division Azzedine Farhane who just 
this week returned from UNGA meetings in New York.  Farhane 
expressed Morocco's sincere support for the Global Initiative 
to Combat Nuclear Terrorism (GICNT) and promised to provide 
an update on the status of Morocco's Global Initiative 
Internet Portal (GIIP) as well as verify Morocco's GI POC 
list and GIIP manager as soon as possible.  Post will follow 
up with an email to the Department as instructed in reftel.
