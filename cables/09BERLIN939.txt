SUBJECT: (C) GERMANY LIKELY TO DELIVER ADDITIONAL 
PRESENTATIONS AT THE MTCR PLENARY IN RIO 
 
REF: STATE 80072 
 
Classified By: Acting Global Affairs Unit Chief David L. Fisher 
for reasons 1.4 (b) and (d). 
 
(C) On August 4, EconOff delivered ref A talking points to 
MFA Export Control Division Desk Officer Nancy Reck 
encouraging Germany to present at the 2009 Missile Technology 
Control Regime Information Exchange in Brazil.  Reck said 
that Germany would likely be willing to deliver "one or two" 
presentations in addition to what they agreed to present last 
April at the Reinforced Point Of Contact meeting in Paris. 
However, Reck said a final decision would not be made until 
mid-August when decision makers in her office return from 
vacation.  Reck speculated that her office would be inclined 
to present on risk assessment in licensing.  Post will report 
any additional German response septel.
