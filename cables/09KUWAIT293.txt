SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED: SEEKING SUPPORT FOR 
AFGHANISTAN CONFERENCE 
 
REF: STATE 24422 
 
¶1. A/DCM on March 22 delivered reftel points urging the GOK 
to participate in a March 31 Dutch-hosted International 
Conference on Afghanistan to Ambassador Khaled Muqames, 
Director of MFA's Follow-up and Coordination Department. 
 
¶2. Muqames thanked A/DCM for the information and promised to 
convey the demarche up his chain of command, but was unable 
to confirm GOK participation.  (Note: The GOK FonMin has 
announced publicly that official travel would be greatly 
curtailed pending election of a new parliament, scheduled NLT 
mid-May.  End Note.)
