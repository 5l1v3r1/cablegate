¶1. Post contacted the Foreign End User, the Indonesian Air Force 
(IAF), regarding licenses 050185190.  IAF Colonel Amrullah Asnawi, 
Chief of the Aeronautics Directorate, confirmed the procurement 
order for the gyroscopes listed in reftel.  Asnawi said that the 
gyroscopes were ordered by for the purpose listed on the license but 
have not yet been received.  Col. Asnawi also confirmed that the MOD 
routinely does business with the Foreign Consignee, Staraero PTE Ltd 
and it is a legitimate firm.
