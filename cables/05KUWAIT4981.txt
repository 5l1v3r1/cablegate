(SBU)   On December 3, Econcouns and Econoff met with 
Americas Department Director Ambassador Khalid Al-Babtain and 
his deputy, Mohammad Shamlan, to deliver the para 5 talking 
points (Ref A) relating to the Mehlis report; points were 
previously sent via diplomatic note on November 29 (Ref B). 
Al-Babtain stated that the Central Bank would not act on a 
list provided by the U.S., but would do so only on a list 
from the U.N.  He stated that he would check with the MFA 
International Organizations Department to see if they had 
received a similar list from the U.N., and would report back 
to the Embassy as appropriate.
