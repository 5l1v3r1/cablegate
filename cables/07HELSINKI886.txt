SUBJECT: FINLAND PROVIDES ASSURANCES ON USEC'S AMERICAN 
CENTRIFUGE PLANT 
 
REF: SECSTATE 144645 
 
¶1. (SBU) In response to reftel, the Finnish Ministry for 
Foreign Affairs issued the following verbal note covering 
USEC's intent to purchase autoclaves from Platom Oy (Mikkeli, 
Finland).  The full text of the verbal note follows. 
 
BEGIN TEXT 
The Ministry for Foreign Affairs present their compliments to 
the Embassy of the United States of American and have the 
honour to acknowledge receipt of the Note No. 100 dated 4 
December 2007 in regard to the request of technology transfer 
assurances from the Government of Finland. 
 
The Ministry hereby confirms, on behalf of the Government of 
the Republic of Finland that: 
 
¶1. All gas centrifuge enrichment plant design information 
transferred by USEC to Platom Oy will be used only for 
civilian nuclear power and fuel activities and not for any 
nuclear explosive or other military purpose, and 
 
¶2. No gas centrifuge enrichment plant design information 
transferred by USEC to Platom Oy will be retransferred to any 
country other than the United States without the prior 
consent of the Government of the United States. 
 
The Ministry for Foreign Affairs avail themselves of this 
opportunity to renew to the Embassy of the Unites States of 
America the assurance of their highest consideration. 
 
Helsinki, 13 December 2007 
END TEXT. 
 
¶2. (SBU) As necessary, post is happy to assist further with 
this process in the future.
