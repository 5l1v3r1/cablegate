SUBJECT: BURMA: FRANCE SUPPORTS SECURITY COUNCIL FOR 
RESOLUTION TO AID CYCLONE VICTIMS 
 
REF: STATE 48651 
 
(SBU)  MFA desk officer for Burma, Jeremie Petite reported 
that France is in complete agreement with the reftel 
demarche.  France has 3 million dollars (2 million euros) in 
aid ready to assist victims.  As for next steps, the GOF is 
currently devising a strategy to deal with recent reports 
that the Burmese regime seized UN aid shipments.  While the 
French have not yet determined how they will react to the 
seizure of aid, MFA desk officer Jeremie Petite told poloff 
that he believes France will increase pressure for a Security 
Council resolution that would allow humanitarian assistance 
regardless of Burmese objections.
