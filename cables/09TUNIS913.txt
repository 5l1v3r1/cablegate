SUBJECT:  TUNISIA:  THE 2010 NUCLEAR NON-PROLIFERATION 
TREATY REVIEW CONFERENCE

On December 17 the Embassy delivered to the Tunisian Ministry 
of Foreign Affairs the points outlined reftel along with a 
non-paper summarizing the views of the U.S. Government on the 
status of the Treaty on the Non-Proliferation of Nuclear 
Weapons and preparations for the upcoming international 
review conference.  The Embassy will report any substantive 
reaction we receive.
