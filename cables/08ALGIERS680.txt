SUBJECT: FRIENDS OF IRAQ CONFERENCE JUNE 17-19 
 
REF: STATE 50592 
 
Counselor to Foreign Minister Medelci Ambassador Abdellah 
Baali told Ambassador June 10 that Algeria will attend the 
Friends of Iraq Conference scheduled to be held in the United 
Arab Emirates on June 17-19.  Baali will be the senior 
representative, and will be joined by Algerian Ambassador to 
Iraq Mustafa Bouterfa, currently resident in Jordan. 
Ambassador Baali plans to depart Algeria on June 16, and 
Bouterfa will join him from Amman.
