¶1. Post warmly welcomes and grants country clearance for 
David Broome, Deputy Assistant Secretary of Defense, and 
John Klemmer, Senate Photographer, Sergeant at Arms, 
and, to travel to Tallinn, Estonia on October 1, 2006 as 
part of CODEL Frist.  This will be a refueling stop 
only; all members of delegation will remain at Tallinn 
airport for the duration of the visit. 
 
¶2. No visa is required for holders of American 
diplomatic, official, or tourist passports for a visit 
of up to 90 days.  Delegation will not go through 
passport control in Estonia. 
 
¶3. Control officer for the visit is Economics Officer 
Robert Hollister (office tel +372-668-8107; cell phone: 
+372-514-5240; e-mail: hollisterrm@state.gov). 
 
¶4. CODEL Frist will meet with Minister of Defense Jurgen 
Ligi and with Foreign Minister Urmas Paet, from 1415 to 
1445 in the VIP Lounge at the Tallinn Airport. 
Ambassador Aldona Wos, the Defense Attache and the 
Control Officer will also participate in the meeting.
