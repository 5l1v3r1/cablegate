SUBJECT: FRENCH ATTENDANCE AT PSI HIGH-LEVEL POLITICAL 
MEETING 
 
REF: SECSTATE 84640 
 
On June 6 poloff spoke with French MFA desk officer for the 
Proliferation Security Initiative (PSI), Sebastian Jaunet, 
about the importance of the upcoming High Level Political 
Meeting (HLPM) in Warsaw.  Jaunet said that the GoF planned 
on sending representatives from the MFA and MOD to the event, 
to include MFA Deputy Director for Strategic Affairs, Paul 
Dahan.  Jaunet said the MOD planned on sending two 
representatives but that he did not yet know who they would 
be.  Jaunet noted that U.S. representation at the meeting 
would include U/S Joseph and U/S Levey.
