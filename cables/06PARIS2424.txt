SUBJECT: POTENTIAL FOR MISUSE OF LIFE SCIENCES RESEARCH 
DEMARCH DELIVERD 
 
REF: STATE 56179 
 
Embassy ESTH officer delivered demarche contained in REFTEL 
to Frederic Jung, officer for biological disarmament at the 
French MFA, on April 12, 2006. Mr. Jung noted that France 
gave a presentation on the subject of biosecurity for dual 
use research during the last AG plenary, to which Department 
may wish to refer.  MFA offered to compile answers to 
questions contained in REFTEL, including appropriate French 
contacts, and forward them by the date requested.
