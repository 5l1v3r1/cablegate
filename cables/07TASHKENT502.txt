SUBJECT: UZBEKISTAN SAYS IT WILL FULLY IMPLEMENT UNSCR 1747 
 
REF: STATE 37801 
 
Poloff delivered reftel information on UNSCR 1747 and the 
imposition of sanctions on Iran to MFA U.S. Division Chief 
Ismat Fayzzulaev on March 28.  Fayzzulaev said that the 
Government of Uzbekistan has no interest in a nuclear-armed 
Iran and would take necessary steps to fully implement the 
resolution.  Fayzullaev also reminded poloff that Uzbekistan 
had demonstrated its commitment to nonproliferation, 
particularly through its participation in the Central Asian 
Nuclear Weapons Free Zone (CANWFZ).  He said that Uzbekistan 
continues to hope that the United States eventually will 
recognize CANWFZ's value for the international 
nonproliferation regime and support the treaty.
