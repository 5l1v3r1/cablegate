SUBJECT: COUNTER-PIRACY: EXCHANGE OF MARITIME COMMAND FOR 
OPERATION OCEAN SHIELD 
 
¶1. (U) On November 9, Standing NATO Maritime Group 1 (SNMG1) 
took over responsibility for NATO's counter-piracy mission, 
Operation Ocean Shield, from Standing NATO Maritime Group 2 
(SNMG2).  SMNG1 is commanded by Portuguese Rear Admiral Jose 
Domingos Pereira de Cunha. 
 
¶2. (U) The four ships currently in SNMG1 are: 
 
-- NRP Alvares Cabral (flagship, Portuguese Navy); 
 
-- ITS Libeccio (Italy); 
 
-- USS Donald Cook (United States); and 
 
-- USS Stephen W Groves (United States). 
 
¶3. (U) A press release with more information on the exchange 
of maritime command for Operation Ocean Shield can be found 
at the Allied Maritime Component Command Headquarters 
Northwood website (www.manw.nato.int).
