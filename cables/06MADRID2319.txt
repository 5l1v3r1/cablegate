SUBJECT: SPAIN AGREES ON DARFUR AND NAM SUMMIT 
 
REF: STATE 151212 
 
(SBU)  Poloff delivered reftel demarche to Spanish 
Subdirector General for Sub-Saharan Africa Antonio 
Sanchez-Benedito Gaspar on September 13.  Sanchez-Benedito 
told Emboff that the Spanish government is in complete 
agreement with the U.S. position.  Also September 13, 
Polcouns raised the issue during a meeting on other issues 
(septels) with Alvaro Iranzo, Director General for the Middle 
East and Africa, and asked that Spain make clear its position 
to NAM members at the Havana Summit.  Iranzo agreed with the 
U.S. position and said the Ministry would relay reftel points 
to the Spanish delegation, present in an observer status.
