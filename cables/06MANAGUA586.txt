SUBJECT: NICARAGUA WILLING TO CO-SPONSOR US PROPOSAL ON THE 
MULTILATERAL SYSTEM FOR GI WINES AND SPIRITS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 37657 
 
On March 13, Saramelia Rosales, Director of International 
Organizations for the Ministry of Trade (MIFIC) reaffirmed 
Nicaragua's support for the US position on geographic 
indicators.  She further noted that the Minister of MIFIC has 
instructed the Nicaraguan delegation to co-sponsor the US 
proposal on the multilateral system for GI wines and spirits 
in the WTO.
