¶2.   ON DECEMBER 22, 2004, THE SPANISH NATIONAL POLICE 
  IN CONJUNCTION WITH THE SPANISH CIVIL GUARD, DETAINED 
  SIX MOROCCAN NATIONALS IN BARCELONA, SPAIN, FOR THEIR 
  APPARENT INVOLVEMENT IN AN EXPLOSIVES NETWORK.  THE 
  ARREST IS THE RESULT OF AN ON-GOING SPANISH POLICE 
  INVESTIGATION IN COOPERATION WITH SEVERAL EASTERN 
  EUROPEAN COUNTRIES AND THE INTELLIGENCE UNIT OF THE 
  MOROCCAN POLICE. THE GROUP ALLEGEDLY HAD MADE 
  ARRANGEMENTS TO ACQUIRE AROUND 400 KILOGRAMS OF HIGH 
  QUALITY INDUSTRIAL-USE EXPLOSIVE THROUGH AN 
  INTERMEDIARY IN THE CZECH REPUBLIC.
