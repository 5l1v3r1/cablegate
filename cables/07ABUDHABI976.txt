SUBJECT: REQUEST FOR INFORMATION ON USG ASSISTANCE TO 
LEBANON 
 
¶1. (U) This is an action request.  Please see para 
 
¶2. (SBU) Yousef Al-Otaiba, Director of International Affairs 
for Abu Dhabi Crown Prince, Sheikh Mohammed bin Zayed 
Al-Nahyan asked Ambassador for detailed information on U.S. 
humanitarian and military assistance to Lebanon.  This 
request for information has come in response to USG requests 
for UAE support to the Lebanese Armed Forces, and in an 
effort to determine specific itemized requirements for 
Lebanon.  Al-Otaiba specifically requested information that 
could be passed to the UAEG electronically. 
 
¶3. (U) Action request:  Please provide -- at the unclassified 
level -- a consolidated breakdown of USG military and 
humanitarian/economic assistance to Lebanon for the last 12 
months.  Post would appreciate as detailed a breakdown as 
possible that we can pass to the UAEG.  End Action Request.
