SUBJECT: GOVERNMENT OF MOROCCO TO REVIEW DRAFT MEGAPORTS 
AGREEMENT 
 
REF: STATE 76562 
 
¶1.  (U) EconOff delivered reftel draft Megaports Memorandum 
of Understanding (MOU) to Nabigha Hajji, Counselor in the 
United Nations Division of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs 
and Cooperation (MFA) on July 28.  Hajji promised to share 
the draft with the MFA official responsible for discussing 
the Megaports initiative, Bouchaib Eloumni, who is currently 
on leave. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Hajji explained that the MFA's Legal division would 
first review the proposed MOU, followed by an interagency 
consultation to determine which Government of Morocco entity 
would be the appropriate signatory of the MOU and eventual 
partner in the Megaports initiative for the Department of 
Energy's National Nuclear Security Administration.  The MFA 
will provide comments on the draft to the USG following these 
internal and interagency reviews, Hajji concluded, although 
he was unable to predict a timetable for the review process.
