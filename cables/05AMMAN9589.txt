SUBJECT: PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR DECEMBER 15 IRAQI ELECTION - 
JORDAN 
 
REF: STATE 220728 
 
(SBU) Poloff delivered reftel points on December 12 to Eihab 
Omaish, Deputy Director of the Foreign Minister's private 
office.  Poloff thanked Omaish for Jordan's consistently 
strong support for the Iraqi political process, and asked 
that the GOJ issue positive public statements regarding the 
December 15 elections in Iraq.  Omaish replied that Jordan 
would continue its efforts to aid Iraq, and that he would 
pass our points directly to Foreign Minister Khatib.
