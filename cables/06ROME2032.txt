Relations, Camillo Pirozzi, informed us July 14 of 
Finmeccanica's decision not/not to pursue the sale of naval 
guns to the Venezuelan navy, reported reftels.  The guns were 
to have been installed on patrol boats being manufactured by 
Navantia, a Spanish company.
