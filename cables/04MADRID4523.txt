  PERPETRATORS SAMIR AZZOUZ, 18 YEARS OLD, FROM MOROCCO

  RANKING AL-QAEDA MEMBER, ALSO INVOLVED IN THE

  AMSTERDAM AIRPORT COMING FROM MALAYSIA AND HOLDING A 
  FALSE SPANISH PASSPORT.  ABDELADIM AKOUAD WAS ARRESTED

  ALLEGED ASSOCIATION WITH THE RADICAL ISLAMIST GROUP "AL
