SUBJECT: ITALY - REQUEST FOR INDENTIFIER AND BACKGROUND 
INFO ON ENTITIES AND INDIVIDUALS LISTED IN THE ANNEXES OF 
UNSCRS 1737 AND 1747 
 
REF: STATE 89511 
 
¶1.  (SBU)  Econoff delivered July 3 reftel non-paper 
requesting additional background information on individuals 
and entities listed in UNSCRs 1737 and 1747 to the MFA's 
Office for International Cooperation on Counterterrorism and 
the Ministry of Finance's Office for the Prevention of 
Financial Crimes. 
 
¶2.  (SBU)  Italian officials noted they would pass the 
information to the GOI's interagency body which oversees 
sanctions enforcement, the Financial Security Committee. 
Post will report by septel any substantive information 
received or GOI actions taken.
