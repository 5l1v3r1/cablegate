SUBJECT: BAHRAIN:  UN INTERNAL ADMINISTRATION OF JUSTICE

Sensitive but unclassified; please protect accordingly.  Not

(SBU) Pol/Econ Chief March 18 delivered reftel demarche 
concerning reforming the UN's internal administration of 
justice to MFA International Organizations First Secretary 
Salah Al Malki.  Al Malki was unaware of the issue but, in 
response to the demarche, promised to pass the information to 
the Bahraini Mission in New York and encourage the Mission to 
consult on the issue with USUN.
