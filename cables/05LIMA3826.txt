Polcouns delivered the demarche on Development Issues in the 
UN High Level General Assembly (Ref B) to Julio Garro, the 
Foreign Ministry's Director of Multilateral Affairs, on 9/2. 
Garro said that Peru shares the USG's concerns on the length 
of the draft outcome document, but had no/no comment to make 
regarding our substantive concerns.  He noted that U.S. 
Permrep Amb. John Bolton and Peruvian Permrep Amb. Oswaldo de 
Rivero had discussed this issue on 8/30 in New York (Ref A), 
indicated that Amb. de Rivero had significant latitude to 
negotiate on the composition of the outcome document, and 
suggested that the U.S. Mission in New York continue to 
engage its Peruvian counterpart on these issues.
