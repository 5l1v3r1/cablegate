SUBJECT: EXBS: INVITATION TO STOCKHOLM INTERNATIONAL EXPORT 
CONTROL CONFERENCE PASSED TO SPAIN 
 
REF: SECSTATE 132303 
 
¶1. ESTHOFF passed reftel invitation letter July 20 to MFA 
International Disarmament Affairs Ambassador Tomas Rodriguez 
Pantoja, urging Spain to send two experts to the September 
20-22 International Export Control Conference in Stockholm, 
Sweden.  Rodriguez Pantoja said he would coordinate with the 
Industry and Commerce Ministry's export control office, the 
Spanish intelligence services, and Spanish customs, and hoped 
to be able to provide the Embassy the names of the Spanish 
attendees prior to our August 15 deadline. 
 
¶2. Per reftel request, Embassy POC for future interaction 
regarding this conference is ESTHOFF Ken Forder, (phone) 
33-91-587-2385; (fax) 34-91-587-2292; (unclassified e-mail) 
forderk@state.gov.
