REF: 47531 
 
¶1. On May 6, we delivered refel demarche to Juan Salas, UNSC 
Desk Officer at the MFA. Salas commented that he had also 
received a letter from Spain on the subject. Due to a state 
visit by the Chinese Vice Premier, the MFA had been tied up 
in meetings, and Salas stated he would review the matter and 
get back to us the next day. 
 
¶2. In a follow-up call to Salas on May 7, Salas stated that 
he had talked to the GOCR Mission to the UN in New York, 
which was analyzing the resolution; no decision has been 
made. Post will advise when/if we receive a more detailed 
response.
