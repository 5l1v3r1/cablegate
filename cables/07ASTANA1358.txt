SUBJECT: KAZAKHSTAN SUPPORTS BROAD DEFINITION OF UNSCR 1747 
 
REF: STATE 67462 
 
In response to reftel points, MFA International Security 
Section Advisor Kayrat Sarzhanov told Poloff that Kazakhstan 
supports a broad interpretation of UNSCR 1747, operative 
paragraph 5.  He added that Kazakhstan is preparing its 
report on UNSCR 1747 implementation and expects to submit it 
by the May 24 deadline.
