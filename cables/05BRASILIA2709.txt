SUBJECT: TERRORIST FINANCE: LIST 72U 
 
REF: STATE 183729 
 
¶1. (SBU) EmbOff delivered reftel designation of seven names 
(List 72U) to Luisa Lopes da Silva of the Foreign Ministry's 
office for the Combat of Illicit International Activities 
and to Jaime Freitas of Brazil's Financial Intelligence Unit 
(COAF).  Freitas undertook to search COAF's databases and 
share the list with Central Bank and the Brazilian 
intelligence agency (ABIN), so they can conduct similar 
searches.  Lopes da Silva will likewise follow up. 
 
¶2. (U) We will report septel should we receive any 
substantive response.
