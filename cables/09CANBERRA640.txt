¶1. (U) On July 10, embassy informed Damien Miller, Director 
of the Afghanistan Section in the Australian Department of 
Foreign Affairs and Trade (DFAT) of the agreement to continue 
using Manas airbase to supply ISAF troops in Afghanistan. 
 
¶2. (U) Mr. Miller stated that the GOA is very pleased that 
the Kyrgyz Republic has agreed to again provide logistical 
support to the NATO/ISAF forces operating in Afghanistan.
