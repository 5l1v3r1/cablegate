¶1.  On June 16, PolOff delivered ref A demarche, outlining 
the publication of the annual TIP report, to An Gang, 
Director of the MFA's North American and Oceanian Division. 
An told PolOff that China hoped that the United States would 
"stop releasing such annual reports."  An informed PolOff 
that he would instruct his staff to study the report and 
would contact the embassy with any specific concerns.
