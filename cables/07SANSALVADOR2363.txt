(SBU) On November 20, Poloff delivered reftel demarche and 
points to Jose Francisco Gonzalez, MFA Official for Security 
Affairs and Defense.  Gonzalez highlighted Salvadoran 
President Saca's meeting with President Bush the day before 
and reiterated the excellent relationship between the US and 
El Salvador on military and defense matters.  He added that 
he was confident in El Salvador's commitment to the MNF-I 
through at least 2008.  Gonzalez promised to discuss the 
US-Iraqi Declaration with Ambassador Leon in advance of the 
December 6 Iraq Forum.
