SUBJECT: SUDAN: ATTENDEES FOR UNGA 
 
REF: A. KHARTOUM 01829 
 
     ¶B. STATE 112656 
 
¶1.  (SBU) In a September 11 phone call with Poloff, MFA 
Acting Deputy Director for the Americas Magdi Al Mofadal 
provided a list of Sudanese delegates to the United Nations 
General Assembly (UNGA).  This list supersedes the one 
provided in Ref A. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) The following delegates will attend the UNGA 
Opening Session: 
 
--Foreign Minister Lam Akol; 
--Director of the Cabinet of the Foreign Minister Amb. 
Soleiman Mohammed Mustafa; 
--MFA Director General for Bilateral and Regional Affairs 
Mahjoub Al Basha; 
--MFA Director of the Department of International 
Organizations Amb. Awad Mohammed Hassan; 
--MFA Deputy Director of Department of International 
Organizations and Minister Plenipotentiary Aleir Deng Raway; 
--MFA Deputy Director for Technical and Economic Cooperation 
and Minister Plenipotentiary Abdulrahim Zubig Mohammed Omar; 
--Acting Permanent Representative to the United Nations Omar 
Bashir Maneef; and 
--A representative from the Ministry of Finance and National 
Economy yet to be determined. 
 
¶3.  (SBU) The following delegates will attend the UNGA 
Opening Session and the subsequent committee meetings, 
remaining in New York for approximately two months: 
 
--Abdulaziz Hassan Salih (Counselor); 
--Ahmed Abdulrahman Mohammed Hassan (Counselor); 
--Mohammed Alballa Osman (Counselor); 
--Khalid Abbas Ahmed (Counselor); 
--Osman Mahjoub Hassan (First Secretary); 
--Christopher Leonardo Jada (First Secretary); 
--Osama Hassan Salman (First Secretary); and 
--Magdi Ahmed Mofadal (First Secretary). 
 
¶4.  (SBU) Minister Plenipotentiary Abdulrahman Sharfi will 
serve as MFA Acting Deputy Director for the Americas in 
Mofadal's absence.
