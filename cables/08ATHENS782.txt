SUBJECT: GREEK REP AT FRIENDS OF IRAQ CONFERENCE 
 
REF: SECSTATE 56560 
 
¶1. (SBU) DepPolCouns passed reftel agenda for the June 17-19 
Friends of Iraq Conference in the UAE to MFA A6 Directorate 
for Middle East Affairs deputy head Giorgos Ayfantis. 
PolMilOff also passed the agenda to the office of Ambassador 
Stoidis, Diplomatic Advisor to the Minister of Defense. 
 
¶2. (SBU) While Ayfantis initially believed Deputy FM Doukas 
would attend, he later informed us that the Greek ambassador 
in Abu Dhabi would attend.  Stoidis' office later confirmed 
the ambassador in Abu Dhabi's attendance and added that if 
there were a last-minute change, the GOG would contact the 
conference organizers directly.
