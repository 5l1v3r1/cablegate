SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST:  INL TRAINING ATTORNEY GENERAL'S 
SECURITY DETAIL 
 
¶1. (U) This is a time sensitive action request; please see para 3. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Background: In order to comply with Leahy Amendment vetting 
requirements, this cable forwards (in para 5) the names and biodata 
of 21 candidates for INL-funded training as members of a personal 
security detail (PSD) for Attorney General Abdul Jabbar Sabit. The 
Attorney General was asked to nominate candidates whom he trusts 
fully. The Attorney General has received threats, due to his 
position and because of his prosecution of corruption. Post is 
therefore eager to have these PSD candidates complete this training 
as soon as possible, so that they can begin to provide Attorney 
General Sabit protection. 
 
¶3. (U) Action Request: In light of this background, post requests 
that the Department complete its Leahy review of the candidates by 
April 20. Post wishes to thank the Department in advance for its 
support on this matter. 
 
¶4. (U) INL has conducted in country vetting and has possesses no 
evidence of human rights violations on the individuals. Post has 
made good faith effort to ensure that the individuals who provided 
only one name truly only have one name.
