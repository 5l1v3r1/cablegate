SUBJECT: BAHRAIN: G8 BMENA/OECD MENA DEMARCHE 
 
REF: STATE 64389 
 
Ambassador delivered reftel demarche to Minister of Finance 
Shaikh Ahmed bin Mohammed Al Khalifa April 12.  Al Khalifa 
was fully aware of G8/BMENA and OECD/MENA initiatives and 
said he would attend the G8/MENA ministerial session on the 
periphery of the World Bank/IMF meetings.  (Note:  Manama was 
just named regional headquarters for the OECD/MENA investment 
initiative at the Paris Steering Committee Meeting April 4. 
End Note).  The Ambassador noted that the Ministry of Finance 
had taken a leading role on these projects and thanked him 
for his efforts.
