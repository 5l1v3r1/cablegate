SUBJECT: IAEA COMMITTEE ON SAFEGUARDS AND VERIFICATION 
(PERU) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 159113 
 
¶1.  Polcouns delivered the demarche on the IAEA Committee on 
Safeguards and Verification (Reftel) to Julio Garro, the 
Foreign Ministry's Director of Multilateral Affairs, on 9/2. 
Garro expressed satisfaction on the creation of the 
Committee. He added that while Peru will leave the IAEA Board 
at the end of the month, it will continue to have a keen 
interest in IAEA Board developments as it expects to win a 
seat on the UN Security Council in 2006, and wants to be 
fully briefed should the question of Iran's compliance with 
nuclear non-proliferation obligations come up before the UNSC. 
 
¶2.  Per Reftel para 6, Embassy is unaware of a Peruvian 
official capable of effectively chairing the new IAEA 
Committee on Safeguards and Verification.
