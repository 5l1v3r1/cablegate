VENEZUELA - INTERIM REPORT 
 
REF: SECSTAE 072078 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Venezuelan Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) 
officials reported to us that President Chavez' attendance at 
the United Nations General Assembley is as yet undetermined. 
The Venezuelan delegation will likely include Foreign 
Minister Maduro and Vice Foreign Minister for North America 
and Multicultural Affairs and UN Ambassador Jorge Valero. 
Polcouns reminded the MFA officials of the need to apply for 
visas well in advance of the event.
