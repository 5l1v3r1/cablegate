SUBJECT: CANADA WILL CONTRIBUTE $35 MILLION TO SUPPORT 
AFGHAN ELECTIONS 
 
REF: A. OTTAWA 1331 
     ¶B. STATE 108855 
 
¶1.  (SBU) Department of Foreign Affairs and International 
Trade (DFAIT) Afghanistan Task Force Policy Advisor Jennifer 
Kleniewski told pol/miloff on October 20 that Canada has 
budgeted $35 million to support the 2009 and 2010 elections 
in Afghanistan.  In her follow-up to ref a, she also 
specified that $25 million will go to the UNDP Basket Fund, 
and $10 million to elections support specifically in 
Kandahar. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) Kleniewski added that Canada will consider other 
election-related funding options if Kandahar-based projects 
are unable to absorb that entire $10 million.  She noted that 
Canada will not make the figure public until after Prime 
Minister Stephen Harper selects a new Minister for 
International Cooperation following the Conservative victory 
in the October 14 federal election.
