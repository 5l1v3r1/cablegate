SUBJECT:  NOAA KA'IMIMOANA MARINE RESEARCH CLEARANCE REQUEST, 
10/5/2006 - 12/4/2006, FILE NO. 2006079 
 
REF:  A.) WELLINGOTN 637; B.) STATE 130870 
 
¶1.  Embassy is in receipt of a letter from the Office of the 
Administrator of Tokelau at the New Zealand Ministry of Foreign 
Affairs and Trade, granting vessel clearance for the Ka'imimoana 
during the period of October 5 - December 4, 2006.  A copy of the 
letter has been faxed to OES/OA.  The text of the letter is as 
follows: 
 
¶2.  Begin text: 
 
"I refer to your letter of 15 August 2006 requesting a vessel 
clearance for Tokelau waters for the 'Ka'imimoana' during the period 
5 October 2006 - 4 December 2006. 
 
Tokelau has considered the request and accepted it.  Consent is 
granted on the condition that New Zealand scientific representatives 
and Tokelau officials have access to all data and samples collected, 
and that research information is submitted after the conclusion of 
the research voyage. 
 
The vessel should also inform the Fisheries Communication Centre for 
the New Zealand Ministry of Fisheries (fcc@fish.govt.nz) of the 
position and times it enters and exits Tokelau's EEZ."
