Classified By: Classified by POLCOUNS Brian R. Naranjo for reasons 1.4

¶1.  (C) POLOFF delivered Reftel demarche on UN General 
Assembly Third Committee 2008 Human Rights Priorities to MFA 
Human Rights Officer Janio Tunon on September 10.  Tunon 
generally refused to answer directly whether Panama would 
support certain resolutions without specific text in hand. 
However, Tunon indicated it likely that Panama would favor 
resolutions about the human rights situation in Iran and DPRK 
but would not commit to Panama co-sponsoring such 
resolutions.  Regarding Zimbabwe, Tunon expressed the need 
for support from South Africa for any resolution. He 
expressed Panama's support for the Special Rapporteur on 
Sudan. 
 
¶2.  (C) Regarding thematic resolutions, Tunon offered no 
information about Panama's intentions, but promised to 
consult with his superiors on specific resolutions.  Post 
will report any additional information SEPTEL.
