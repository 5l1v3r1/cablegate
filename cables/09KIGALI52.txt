SUBJECT: MESSAGE FROM FOREIGN MINISTER MUSEMINALI OF RWANDA TO 
SECRETARY OF STATE CLINTON 
 
¶1.  The following text was received on January 28, 2009 from the 
Foreign Minister of Rwanda, Rosemary Museminali, to Secretary of 
State Hillary Clinton: 
 
Honourable Hillary Rodham Clinton 
Secretary of State 
Washington 
 
Excellency Madam Secretary of State, 
 
On the occasion of your appointment as the Secretary of State of 
your Country, I wish to convey to you warm congratulations and my 
personal best wishes! 
 
Relations between the United States of America and Rwanda have 
continued to grow over the past years.  May I take this opportunity 
to assure your Excellency of our commitment to strengthen the 
current good relations of friendship and cooperation existing 
between our two countries and peoples. 
 
As you embark on tackling the current global challenges, we look 
forward to working together. 
 
May I wish, Your Excellency, success and personal good health in 
your noble duty. 
 
Yours, 
/S/ 
Rosemary Museminali 
Minister of Foreign Affair and Cooperation 
 
¶2.  The signed original will follow via diplomatic pouch mail
