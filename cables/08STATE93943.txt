SUBJECT: APPLICATION DEADLINE EXTENDED FOR IRAN SMALL 
GRANTS PROGRAM 
 
REF: 08 STATE 33385 
 
¶1.  (SBU) The Office of Iranian Affairs (NEA/IR) announces 
that the application deadline for the Iran Small Grants 
program has been extended to November 15, 2008. 
 
¶2.  (SBU) The Office of Iranian Affairs has funds available 
for small grants at addressee posts to support civil society, 
human rights, and rule of law in Iran.  Interested posts 
should encourage appropriate local NGOs and other 
organizations to submit proposals that fit within the 
parameters of program (REFTEL). 
 
¶3.  (U) Please contact Nate Swanson at SwansonNL@state.gov or 
Carolyn Coberly at CoberlyCH@state.gov for any questions 
concerning the small grants or Iran democracy program.
