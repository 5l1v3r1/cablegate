SUBJECT: ACTION REQUEST: USG COMMENTS ON PRC DRAFT BID AND 
TENDER REGS 
 
¶1. (SBU) China's State Council Legislative Affairs Office 
published draft implementing regulations for the Bidding and 
Tendering Law on September 30, 2009.  The draft regulations 
are available at the following website:  www.chinalaw.gov. 
Comments are due October 26, 2009.  Post is preparing a 
summary translation and will forward the full translation of 
the draft to the interagency as soon as available. 
 
¶2. (SBU) ACTION REQUEST:  Interested agencies are requested 
to coordinate consolidated USG comments on the draft law and 
provide those to State EAP/CM Econ Director William Klein 
(kleinwh@state.gov) by October 21 for onward transmission to 
post in time to allow translation and official submission to 
the PRC by the October 26 deadline.
