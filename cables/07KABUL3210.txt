SUBJECT: KABUL'S PLANS TO USE 2007 INTERNATIONAL RELIGIOUS 
FREEDOM REPORT 
 
REF: STATE 128772 
 
¶1.  (U) Per Reftel, Embassy Kabul confirms that the 2007 
International Religious Freedom (IRF) Report has been posted 
on Post,s website in English.  Copies of the report are 
being circulated to key contacts in the Afghan government, to 
other international actors, and to religious contacts. 
 
¶2.  (U) Post is preparing a translation into Dari for posting 
on the website as well as to be shared with religious 
leaders.  Post plans to make the IRF a facet of our ongoing 
outreach to religious 
communities.
