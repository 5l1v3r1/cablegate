SUBJECT: JCIC-XXXIII:  STATUS OF HEAVY BOMBERS AT THE 
CONVERSION OR ELIMINATION FACILITY AT DAVIS MONTHAN AIR 
FORCE BASE 
 
¶1.   This is JCIC-XXXIII-024. 
 
¶2.   The text at Paragraph 3 below is the Unclassified Annex 
to the U.S. Closing Plenary Statement for JCIC-XXXIII on 
Status of Heavy Bombers at the Conversion or Elimination 
Facility at Davis Monthan Air Force Base.  The Statement was 
delivered at the Closing Plenary Meeting at the U.S. Mission 
on November 21, 2008. 
 
¶3. Begin text: 
 
 
UNCLASSIFIED ANNEX 
 
TO U.S. Closing Plenary Statement 
November 21, 2008 
JCIC Unilateral Plenary Statement 
on 
 
Status of Heavy Bombers at the Conversion or Elimination 
Facility at Davis Monthan Air Force Base 
 
     The United States would like to inform the other Parties 
that there are a number of heavy bombers undergoing aging and 
surveillance activities at the Davis-Monthan Conversion or 
Elimination Facility.  As a result of these activities, these 
heavy bombers are in various stages of disassembly.  These 
heavy bombers remain accountable under the Treaty and are 
subject to inspection, as provided for in the Tenth Agreed 
Statement.  The United States assures the other Parties that 
if a decision is made to eliminate these heavy bombers in the 
future their elimination shall be conducted in accordance 
with the provisions of Section VI of the Conversion or 
Elimination Protocol, including all required notifications. 
 
End text.
