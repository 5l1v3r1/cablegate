SUBJECT: AU CHARTER ON DEMOCRACY, ELECTIONS AND GOVERNANCE 
 
REF: STATE 10434 
 
On January 30, Ambassador delivered reftel demarche regarding 
the African Charter on Democracy, Elections and Governance to 
MFA Secretary of State for the Americas and Asia Saida 
Chtioui.  Ambassador conveyed our strong support for this 
African Union charter.  Chtioui said she understood that the 
GOT has a positive view on the charter, but that another MFA 
colleague is responsible for African affairs.  Post will 
attempt to obtain additional details from the MFA Africa 
division and report any further information septel.
