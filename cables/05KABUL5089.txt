SUBJECT: IRAQ:  SEEKING PUBLIC SUPPORT FOR ELECTION 
 
REF: STATE 220728 
 
POLOFF GAVE REFTEL DEMARCHE TO MR. NAWABI, DIRECTOR GENERAL 
OF THE SECOND (MIDDLE EASTERN) DIVISION AT THE MINISTRY OF 
FOREIGN AFFAIRS.  NAWABI EXPRESSED HIS GOVERNMENT'S SUPPORT 
OF THE DEMOCRATIC PROCESS IN IRAQ AND SAID HE WOULD SPEAK TO 
THE FOREIGN MINISTER ABOUT ARRANGING A PUBLIC ANNOUNCEMENT OF 
CONGRATULATIONS TO THE IRAQI PEOPLE.  AT THE SAME TIME, 
EMBASSY PUBLIC AFFAIRS SECTION HAS GIVEN WIDE MEDIA 
DISTRIBUTION TO MATERIAL ON THE IRAQI ELECTION.
