SUBJECT: GULF IRAQI EXPO SET FOR NOVEMBER 2007 
 
REF: MANAMA 165 
 
¶1. The "Gulf Iraqi Expo 2007" trade show, postponed from 
March 2007 (reftel) has been rescheduled for November 1-3, 
2007 in Manama, Bahrain at the Bahrain International 
Exhibition Center.  The event is being organized under the 
patronage of the Bahraini Ministry of Industry and Commerce 
and the Iraqi Ministry of Trade and is the first of its kind 
to take place in the Gulf region.  A diplomatic note from the 
Iraqi Embassy in Bahrain advises that ministers and officials 
will be present from several ministries including planning, 
oil, trade, communication, electricity, housing, 
transportation, and water.  Activities include an exhibition, 
a conference, and bilateral meetings run by the United 
Nations Development Program in which companies from various 
sectors will participate. 
 
¶2. Post has advised the local AmCham regarding the new date 
and requests the support of regional posts in soliciting 
participation among local and US expat business entities.
