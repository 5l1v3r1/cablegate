SUBJECT: GOT REQUESTS EXCHANGE OF SUPPORT FOR UN HUMAN 
RIGHTS COMMITTEE CANDIDATES 
 
REF: A. STATE 85758 
 
     ¶B. TUNIS 1275 
 
¶1. This is an action request.  Post received a dipnote from 
MFA June 12 seeking an exchange of support for Tunisian 
candidate to the UN Human Rights Committee Abdelfattah Amor 
and US candidate Prof. Ruth Wedgwood.  Post seeks Department 
guidance in responding to this request. 
 
¶2. Relevant text of the dipnote follows (translation from 
French): 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs presents its compliments to 
the Embassy of the United States of America in Tunis and has 
the honor to propose an exchange of support between the 
candidacy of Mr. Abdelfattah Amor for the Human Rights 
Committee and that of Prof. Ruth Wedgewood for the same 
organization, whose elections will take place September 7, 
2006 in New York, during the meeting of State Parties to the 
International Covenant on Civil and Political Rights. 
 
The Ministry of Foreign Affairs would be grateful to the 
Embassy of the United States of America if it would 
communicate its response to this proposal.   End text.
