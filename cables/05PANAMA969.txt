SUBJECT: PANAMA TO CONSIDER SUPPORT FOR U.S. CANDIDATE FOR 
INTER-AMERICAN COMMISSION ON HUMAN RIGHTS 
 
REF: STATE 75344 
 
(SBU) PolOff demarched MFA Deputy Director of International 
Organizations and Commissions Nubia Lezcano on April 29, 
stressing the qualifications of U.S. candidate for 
Inter-American Commission on Human Rights Paolo G. Carozza. 
PolOff delivered talking points and c.v.  Lezcano said she 
would inform PolOff when GOP had made a decision on support 
for Carozza.  Lezcano said the decision would be made by 
Foreign Minister Samuel Lewis Navarro and Director of 
International Organizations and Commissions Javier Bonagas. 
Embassy will follow-up with MFA and report any further 
developments to Department.
