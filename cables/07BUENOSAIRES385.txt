SUBJECT: ARGENTINA AND THE U.S. "ON THE SAME PAGE" 
REGARDING IRAN'S NUCLEAR AMBITIONS 
 
REF: STATE 023001 
 
¶1. (U) This message is sensitive but unclassified, and not 
for Internet distribution. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Emboff delivered reftel points regarding Iran's 
non-compliance with UNSCR 1737 February 28 to Carlos 
Hernandez, acting director of the MFA's Office of 
International Security, Nuclear, and Space Affairs. 
Hernandez stressed that Argentina and the U.S. "are on the 
same page as regards Iran and the IAEA," and said that 
Argentina will continue to support diplomatic efforts to 
press Iran to cooperate fully with the IAEA and abandon its 
pursuit of nuclear weapons.
