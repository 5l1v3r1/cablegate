(SBU)  Post relayed reftel points to Michel Tilemans, Adviser 
for European Affairs at the Belgian Foreign Ministry.  The 
Belgians see Kosovo as a priority issue for discussion, 
although they do not believe much progress will be made in 
this month's European Council (GAERC) meeting.  (Belgian 
position on Kosovo status process reported septel.)  Belgium 
also intends to urge adoption of a strong EU implementation 
framework for UNSCR 1737 Iran sanctions at this month's GAERC 
meeting.
