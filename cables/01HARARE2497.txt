ECONIMIC RECOVERY ACT 
 
¶1.   THE GOVERNMENT-CONTROLLED DAILY "THE HERALD" REPORTS 
    ON AUGUST 23 THAT THE RULING ZANU PF PARTY WILL PRESENT A 
    MOTION IN PARLIAMENT ON AUGUST 28 TO "CONDEMN AND OPPOSE" 
    THE ZIMBABWE DEMOCRACY AND ECONOMIC RECOVERY ACT. 
 
¶2.   UNDER THE HEADLINE "GUMBO TO MOVE MOTION ON ZIMBABWE 
    DEMOCRACY BILL," ZANU-PF CHIEF WHIP AND MBERENGWA WEST 
    MEMBER OF PARLIAMENT JORAM GUMBO IS QUOTED AS SAYING THAT 
    THE ZDERA IS "RACIST AND BENT ON UNDERMINING THE 
    SOVEREIGNTY AND INDEPENDENCE OF ZIMBABWE."
