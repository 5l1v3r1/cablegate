SUBJECT: ASEAN REGIONAL FORUM -- ENCOURAGING GOI 
PARTICIPATION IN BIOLOGICAL THREAT WORKSHOP 
 
REF: STATE 49659 
 
¶1.  (U) Per reftel instruction, poloff delivered invitation 
and related materials for the June 10-11 U.S.-Philippines 
ASEAN Regional Forum workshop on biological threat reduction 
on May 18 to Ibnu Hadi, Director for Intra-Regional 
Cooperation at the Department of Foreign Affairs (DEPLU). 
Poloff urged GOI participation in the workshop and noted that 
we hoped the GOI delegation would provide a 10-15 minute 
presentation on its efforts in this area. 
 
¶2.   (U) Hadi said the workshop was important and Indonesia 
hoped to participate.  He promised to consult with GOI 
interagency colleagues regarding the Indonesian delegation 
and get back to us as soon as these plans were finalized.
