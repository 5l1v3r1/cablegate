TELECOM TRADE AGREEMENT COMMENTS 
 
REF: SECSTATE 202978 
 
¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, on December 29 post requested comments 
from the South African Department of Communications (DOC) and 
the telecommunications regulator ICASA (Independent 
Communications Authority of South Africa) regarding SAG 
performance under various multilateral telecommunications 
agreements.  On January 11 post received a request from both 
DOC and ICASA for an extenstion of the January 16 deadline 
for comments. 
 
¶2. (SBU) South African government departments either close or 
are minimally staffed between December 15 and January 15 due 
to summer school holidays.  If possible, post recommends a 
three week extension of the deadline for comments.
