SUBJECT: URUGUAY: DEMARCHE DELIVERED ON U.S. STATEMENT ON 
IRAN 
 
REF: STATE 87682 
 
Classified By: Charge D'Affaires James D. Nealon 
for reasons 1.4 (B) and (D) 
 
(C) On May 31, Charge delivered reftel demarche to Deputy 
Foreign Minister Maria "Belela" Herrera, per instructions 
reftel.  With respect to a public statement of support, 
encouraging Iran to fully and verifiably suspend its 
enrichment and reprocessing activities and keeping the 
spotlight on Iran's choice, Herrera said she would raise the 
issues with the Foreign Minister as soon as this evening.  On 
a related issue, Herrera described how the Iranian Embassy 
had "heavily pressured" the MFA to back its bid for a seat on 
the UN Human Rights Council. She said that the GOU had 
resisted voting for Iran and she highlighted the GOU's 
preoccupation over Iran's nuclear ambitions.
