SUBJECT: Update on PKK Violence (March 8-15, 2009) 
 
REF: ANKARA 371 and previous 
 
(U) Sensitive but unclassified - please protect accordingly. 
 
¶1. (SBU) This is another in a series of weekly reports on PKK 
violence in Turkey.  Our primary source is the Turkish General Staff 
(TGS) website, which documents contacts and clashes with the PKK, as 
well as casualties incurred. 
 
¶2. (U) During the March 8-15, 2009, no security official was injured 
or killed.  The total number of security force casualties for the 
year through March 15, was zero killed and zero injured. 
 
¶3. (U) During the same period, one PKK terrorist was killed in 
clashes in Nusaybin, Mardin Province on March 12.  Two PKKers were 
captured, one in Mardin and a second in Idil, Sirnak Province. 
According to official TGS website figures, through the second week 
of March, the number of PKK militants killed this year was one, the 
number of those captured was 10, and the number of those who 
surrendered was 17. 
 
¶4. (U) During the reporting period no civilian died or was injured. 
The total number of civilian casualties for the year through the end 
of the second week of March, was zero killed and five wounded.
