SUBJECT: TERRORISM FINANCE:  DESIGNATION OF FOUR 
SYRIA-BASED MEMBERS OF ABU GHADIYAH NETWORK (LIST 97) 
 
REF: SECSTATE 22757 
 
Sensitive but unclassified 
 
Econcouns delivered reftel information regarding the U.S. 
designaiton of four Syria-based members of the Abu Ghadiyah 
Network to Yves Beaulieu, Deputy Director (Counter-Terrorism) 
for International Crime and Terrorism at the Department of 
Foreign Affairs and International Trade (DFAIT).  Beaulieu 
expressed appreciation for the names and background, but 
stated that DFAIT had already received the information 
through an automatic notification from the Department of 
Treasury website.  He stated that the this notification 
system helps Canada take immediate appropriate action 
regarding designations.
