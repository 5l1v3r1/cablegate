SUBJECT: EGYPT CONSIDERING SUPPORT FOR INDEPENDENT 
ASSESSMENT OF EVENTS IN UZBEKISTAN 
 
REF: STATE 100623 
 
In response to our demarche regarding events in Uzbekistan 
(reftel), MFA Cabinet staff member Mohamed Aboulkheir made no 
substantive response, but committed to sharing the USG position 
with his Minister and the Asian Affairs Department within the 
Ministry.  Post made the same points to the MFA Cabinet staff 
member responsible for Asian issues, Ahmed Shaheen, who also had 
no immediate reply.  Post will follow up and report any 
substantive replies from the GOE.
