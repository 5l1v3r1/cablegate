SUBJECT: LET'S SWAP SUPPORT - CANDIDATES FOR THE

REF: A. TUNIS 390 
     ¶B. TUNIS 694 
     ¶C. STATE 66814 
 
¶1. (U)  On July 2,2008 the Embassy received Diplomatic Note 
2367 from the Ministry of Foreign Affairs (MFA) replying to 
U.S. Diplomatic Note 840 (Ref B & C). In the note, the GOT 
proposes an exchange of support for each other's candidacy 
for election to the Administration Council and the Postal 
Operations Council of the UniversalPostal Union.  The 24th 
session of the Universal Postal Congress will take place from 
July 23 to August 12 in Geneva.  The text of the diplomatic 
note will be faxed 
to NEA/MAG - Virginia Stewart.
