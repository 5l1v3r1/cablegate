SUBJECT: GLOBAL INITIATIVE: DEMARCHES ON EXPANSION, 
POSSIBLE CHINESE WORKSHOP DELIVERED 
 
REF: A. STATE 50177 (EXPANSION OF INITIATIVE) 
     ¶B. NOTAL BEIJING 2484 
     ¶C. NOTAL STATE 50432 (LETTER TO DG ZHANG) 
 
¶1. (SBU) China welcomes ref A information about the expansion 
of the Global Initiative to Combat Nuclear Terrorism to 
include Armenia, Romania, Cambodia and Macedonia, MFA Arms 
Control Department Deputy Director Zhang Junan told poloff 
April 17.  Poloff noted that we did not yet have any 
information in response to Zhang's questions about refining 
the process for receiving new members (ref B). 
 
¶2. (SBU) Poloff also delivered to Zhang Junan a letter from 
Department of Energy Undersecretary Aoki to MFA Arms Control 
DG Zhang Yan asking China to consider hosting a workshop on 
radiological response procedures (ref C).  Zhang Junan said 
he would convey the letter to DG Zhang Yan and respond to the 
Embassy.
