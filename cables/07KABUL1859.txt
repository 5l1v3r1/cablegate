SUBJECT: LEAHY VETTING REQUEST: MOI IMET Candidates for NIU Training 
¶1. This is an action request - see paragraph 2. 
 
¶2. In order to comply with Leahy human rights vetting requirements, 
this cable forwards the names and biodata of 50 Afghan Ministry of 
Interior (MOI) officers to be trained for the Narcotics 
Investigative Unit, Counter-Narcotics Police of Afghanistan.  Post 
has made a good faith effort to ensure that the individuals with one 
name actually only have one name.  Post has reviewed the information 
available on these individuals and has found no derogatory 
information.  Post requests Department's review of these candidates 
and response by June 27, 2007.
