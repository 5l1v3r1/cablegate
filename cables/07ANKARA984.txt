SUBJECT: TURKEY: NPT PREPCOM DEMARCHE DELIVERED 
 
REF: STATE 54147 
 
(SBU) We delivered reftel points on the NPT PrepCom I as a 
nonpaper to Serhan Yigit, Head of Section, MFA Department of 
Arms Control and Disarmament, on April 26.  Yigit expressed 
thanks, advised that his Department would study them, and 
pledged to provide MFA comments, if any.  Yigit told us that 
the MFA will share our points with the Geneva-based diplomat 
who will represent Turkey at the Vienna conference.
