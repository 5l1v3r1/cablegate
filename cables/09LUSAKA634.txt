SUBJECT: BANDA TO ATTEND POTUS LUNCH 
 
REF: SECSTATE 92202 
 
¶1.  (SBU) On September 11, President Rupiah Banda told the 
Ambassador that he would be delighted to attend the September 
22 POTUS lunch for sub-Saharan African heads of state. 
 
¶2.  (U) President Banda's official title is His Excellency 
Mr. Rupiah Bwezani Banda, President of the Republic of 
Zambia.  When addressed, his middle name is usually omitted. 
His name is otherwise pronounced "ROO-pe-ah Bwe-ZAH-nee 
BAHN-da." 
 
¶3.  (SBU) President Banda does not have any dietary 
restrictions nor will he require translation assistance. 
Zambia's Permanent Representative to the United Nations, 
Lazarous Kapambwe, will serve as a point of contact for 
President Banda, who will arrive in New York on September 19.
