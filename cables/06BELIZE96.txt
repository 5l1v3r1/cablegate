SUBJECT: BELIZE: BIO OF NEW TAIWAN AMBASSADOR 
 
 
¶1. (SBU) Summary.  The Embassy of Taiwan announced the 
appointment Joseph Ting Shih as the new Ambassador to 
Belize.  Shih comes from the Taipei Economic and Cultural 
Office in New Zealand, and is expected to arrive within the 
next week.  End Summary. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Shih's curriculum vitae is: 
Education: 
1975 - Bachelor's Degree, National Chengchi University, 
Taiwan 
1983 - M.A., Foreign Service, Georgetown University, USA 
 
Foreign Postings: 
South Africa 1984-88 and 1995-1999; Saudi Arabia 1988-91; 
South Korea 1991-1994 (Director of Mission 1992-94); New 
Zealand 2002-2005 
 
¶3. (SBU) During their sixth annual tourism awards and 
awareness week January 16-22, the state run Belize Tourism 
Board awarded the outgoing Taiwanese Ambassador Charles Tsai 
a "Special Recognition Award." The highly respected Tsai has 
served in Belize for nearly six years, and will next be 
posted in Taiwan's Ministry of Foreign Affairs.
