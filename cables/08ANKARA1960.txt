SUBJECT: TURKEY: CHP CONGRATULATIONS TO PRESIDENT ELECT 
OBAMA 
 
¶1. (U) The following congratulatory message was received 
November 6 for the President-elect from the Chairman of the 
opposition Republican Peoples' Party (CHP). 
 
Begin text: 
 
Dear Mr. President-Elect, 
 
I warmly congratulate your election as the President of the 
United States of America.  I am confident that your 
presidency will start a new era, an era of hope and 
cooperation in the United States and in the world.  I also 
believe that Turkish-American relations based on friendship 
and confidence will be enhanced.  I wish you a great success 
in the performance of important duties that you are going to 
assume. 
 
Sincerely yours, 
Deniz Baykal 
Chairman 
 
End text.
