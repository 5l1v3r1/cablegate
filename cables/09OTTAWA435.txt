SUBJECT: GOC AGREES WITH USG APPROACH TO UN CONFERENCE ON 
DEVELOPMENT IMPACT 
 
REF: STATE 50892 
 
(SBU) In response to reftel points, James Haley, Director 
General, International Trade and Finance, Finance Canada, 
told Econmincouns that the Government of Canada shares USG 
concern with the draft outcome document for the UN Conference 
on the World Financial and Economic Crisis and its Impact on 
Development.  Haley added that Canada is interested in 
continuing to work with the United States to enhance the 
relevancy of this Conference.
