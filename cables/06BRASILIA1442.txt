SUBJECT:  FOLLOW-UP TO BRAZILIAN ENV TECHNICAL ASSISTANCE 
INITIATIVE 
 
REF:  BRASILIA 1292 
 
¶1.  Reftel outlines Government of Brazil, Ministry of Environment 
(MMA) requests for technical assistance in several environmental 
areas. 
 
¶2.  After consulting again with Fernando Lyrio, Minister Sylvia's 
Director for International Relations briefly last week about the 
type of agreement be sought to cover the activities, Post was told 
that Environment Minister Marina Silva was seeking an umbrella 
environmental agreement.  Minister Silva believes she discussed this 
concept with U/S Dobriansky last year during a meeting at the 
Department, September 2005.  Under this umbrella agreement, MOU's or 
other instruments could be developed to govern the collaborations 
and group them together. 
 
¶3.  ESTH Counselor Norman would like to discuss next steps during 
consultations in Washington July 24-26, 2006.  Will contact Larry 
Sperling to set up a meeting.
