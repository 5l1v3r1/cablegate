SUBJECT: DEMARCHE DELIVERED:  URGING IRAN TO RESOLVE AMCIT 
CASES 
 
REF: STATE 129480 
 
¶1. (SBU) Per reftel, post demarched Bosnian MFA Director of 
Bilateral Affairs Jasmina Pasalic on January 5, soliciting 
Bosnia's assistance in encouraging Iran to free Amcits who 
have been imprisoned.  Post provided para 7 of reftel as 
non-paper to Pasalic. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Pasalic had no substantive reply to the cases in 
question, but advised that Bosnian MFA would continue to 
raise human rights issues with the Iranians in periodic 
meetings, and would consider the Amcit cases as a possible 
topic for use in such human rights talks.  Pasalic expressed 
her concern generally about the use of detention and other 
practices in Iran against students, protesters, and others 
the regime seeks to pressure, and appeared knowledgeable 
about Iran-related issues generally. 
 
¶3. (SBU) Pasalic advised she would meet on January 5 with the 
BiH ambassador on his way out to Tehran, and committed to 
providing him the non-paper regarding the Amcit cases. 
 
¶4. (SBU) Post will report on any further developments in the 
matter.
