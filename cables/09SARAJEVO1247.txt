SUBJECT: NO SUBSTANTIVE REPLY FROM BOSNIA ON GOLDSTONE 
REPORT 
 
REF: STATE 112828 
 
¶1. (SBU) Post demarched Bosnian MFA contacts Amb. Milenko 
Misic, Head of Department for Human Rights, and Sanja 
Kenig-Corovic, Head of Department for UN and International 
Organizations, on November 3 regarding the UN General 
Assembly's upcoming debate on the Goldstone Report.  Misic 
and Kenig-Corovic had no substantive reply at this time. 
 
¶2. (SBU) Post notes Bosnia's likely abstention from any UNGA 
action related to the Goldstone Report, based on past Bosnian 
pattern of voting in the UNHRC.
