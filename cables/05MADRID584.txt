¶2.   DAILY PRESS OF FEB. 14, 2005 REPORTED THAT THE 
  SPANISH GUARDIA CIVIL ARRESTED ON FEB. 11, 2005 TWO 
  SUSPECTED ETA ACTIVISTS IN BASAURI, VIZCAYA AND 
  CAMPELLO, ALICANTE, SPAIN.  DURING A LATER SEARCH IN 
  THE HOUSE OF ONE OF THE SUSPECTS, THE GUARDIA CIVIL 
  SEIZED A PISTOL GUN, A RIFLE WITH TELESCOPIC SIGHT, 
  AMMUNITION FOR BOTH, TIMERS, DETONATORS, FALSE SPANISH 
  I.D. CARDS, AND ABUNDANT INFORMATION FOR POSSIBLE 
  TERRORIST TARGETS, MAINLY ON SPANISH POLICE MEMBERS AND 
  ELECTED CITY COUNCILLORS OF THE BASQUE COUNTRY AREA OF 
  SPAIN.
