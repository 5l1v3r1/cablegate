On July 18, poloff delivered the talking points in reftel to 
Cecilia Contreras, Deputy Director of the Foreign Ministry's 
International Organizations Section.  On July 25, poloff 
delivered the three draft U.N. resolutions in reftel to 
Contreras.  Contreras stated that the GON would carefully 
study the USG position and draft resolutions on the UN 
Peacebuilding Commission.  Post will forward the Department 
any additional commentary or statement of position received 
from the GON.
